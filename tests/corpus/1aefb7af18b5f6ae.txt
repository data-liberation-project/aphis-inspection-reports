2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at approximately
11:45 am on 07/01/2015.
The inspector sat at the facility gate, honked the horn of his vehicle, and called the provided contact number in an
attempt to reach the license holder. All attempts were unsuccessful. The inspector was contacted by the license
holder after leaving the facility. The license holder indicated that she was away from the facility and would not be
returning for several hours.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
Note: This inspection report was sent by mail.
