2.40(b)(2) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
*****The facility had medications that were past the expiration date. There was a bottle of amikacin sulfate that
expired March 2014. There was a 50 ml bottle of clindamycin that expired December 2014. There were two 2 ml
bottles of clindamycin that expired September 2014. These medications were intermingled with other
pharmaceuticals that are currently being used and are therefore ready for use. The facility must ensure that all
medications used in the facility are not expired and are labeled properly in accordance with standard veterinary
practices and manufacturer's recommendations to ensure efficacy of the medications. The expired medications
must be not be used on covered species.
To be corrected from this date forward.
3.75(a)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
*****There were at least two animals that were not contained within their enclosures. Two ring-tailed lemurs were
observed outside their enclosure. The lemurs were observed entering and leaving the enclosure through the wire
enclosure fencing. Inadequate enclosures can endanger the health of the animals. These animals must be placed
in enclosures that will contain them.
To be corrected by: March 4, 2015.
An exit briefing was conducted with a facility representative.
This was a focused inspection conducted on the walk through portion of the facility, and the big cat and fox
enclosures in the drive through area of the facility.
