2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 9:30-10:00 am
on 07/15/2014.
Inspector knocked on front door of residence several times. After no response, a call was made to the Licensee and
a voice message was left. Licensee called back and advised Inspector of optimal business hours. Inspector waited
at the facility for 30 minutes.
