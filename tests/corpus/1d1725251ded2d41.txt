2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The pigs at this facility have had a chronic issue with skin parasites and treatments occur on a regular schedule
however the written program of veterinary care does not address the dosage of medication or treatment intervals.
There is also no information on the program of veterinary care concerning treatment for ear mites in rabbits or
vaccinations /de worming of the sheep and goats .The program of veterinary care must be updated to reflect the
treatments and preventative care provided to the animals. This must include names of any medications used ,
dosage amounts and frequency of treatment . A detailed written plan will ensure that correct medications , dosages
and frequency of treatments are given. The updated plan must be followed to ensure adequate veterinary care is
provided to the animals.
