Primary enclosures.
In the Chimpanzee bedrooms, there were areas of chipped paint that exposed the porous concrete block wall. The
porous concrete can not be readily cleaned and sanitized. Surfaces in nonhuman primate primary enclosures must be
maintained to allow for the required cleaning and sanitization.
