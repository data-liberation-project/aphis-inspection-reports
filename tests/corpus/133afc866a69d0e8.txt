2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC conducted a review of the program for humane care and use of animals and the inspection of animal
facilities on February 27, 2015; however the reports of its evaluations were not submitted to the Institutional Official
of the research facility. The IACUC, with respect to activities involving animals and as an agent of the research
facility, must perform the function of preparing its semiannual evaluations and then submitting them to the
Institutional Official in a timely manner to keep the Institutional Official adequately informed and to comply with the
regulatory requirements. The IACUC report of its February 27, 2015 evaluations must be submitted to the
Institutional Official.

Correct by October 1, 2015.
