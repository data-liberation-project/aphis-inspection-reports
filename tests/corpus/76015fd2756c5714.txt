3.125(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Storage. There are bags of feed on the floor of the food storage shed and open bags of feed. Supplies of food and
bedding need to be stored in facilities which adequately protect supplies against deterioration, molding, or
contamination by vermin. Bags of feed need to be stored off the floor such as on pallets or shelves and open feed
needs to be stored in closed containers with lids to protect supplies from contamination, insects, and vermin.
