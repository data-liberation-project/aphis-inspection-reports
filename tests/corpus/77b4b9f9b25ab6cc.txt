2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
THE CURRENT WRITTEN PROGRAM OF VETERINARY CARE NEEDS MORE INFORMATION FROM THE
ATTENDING VETERINRIAN. UNDER THE SECTION ADDRESSING CAPTURE AND RESTRANT METHODS.
THE DRUGS USED AND THE SPECIES THAT THESE DRUGS ARE TO BE USED ON NEEDS TO BE ADDED
TO THE DOCUMENT. THE DOCUMENT SHOULD ALSO ADDRESS THE DOSAGE, THE ADMINISTRATION
METHOD, AND WHO IS ADMINISTER THE DRUGS. ALL OF THIS SHOULD BE SPELLED OUT.
CORRECT BY: JULY 30, 2014
2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
EACH EXHIBITOR SHALL KEEP AND MAINTAIN RECORDS THAT FULLY AND CORRECTLY DISCLOSE ALL
ANIMALS OWNED, HELD, LEASED OR OTHER WISE IN HIS OR HER POSSESSION. RECORDS OF ANIMALS
OFF THE PREMISES THAT ARE BEING BOTTLE FED ARE NOT BEING KEPT. RECORDS MUST BE
MAINTAINED ON ALL ANIMALS.
CORRECT BY: JULY 28, 2014
2.131(d)(2)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
A READILY IDENTIFIABLE ATTENDENT MUST BE PRESENT AT ALL TIMES DURING PERIODS OF PUBLIC
CONTACT WITH ANIMALS. NO ATTENDENT IS PRESENT IN THE PETTING ZOO. AN EMPLOYEE OR
ATTENDENT MUST BE PRESENT DURING PERIODS THAT THE PUBLIC IS IN THE PETTING ZOO.
CORRECT BY: JULY 14, 2014
