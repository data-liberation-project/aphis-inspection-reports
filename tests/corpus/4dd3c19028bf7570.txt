Veterinary care for dogs.
The facility has an attending veterinarian; however, a written Program of Veterinary Care for dogs was not completed.
Each facility with dogs must follow an appropriate program of veterinary care for dogs that is developed, documented in
writing, and signed by the attending veterinarian. The written program of veterinary care for dogs is required and is
important for the health and well-being of the animals and ensures clear and accurate communication between the facility
and the attending veterinarian. Ensure a written Program of Veterinary care for dogs is developed and addresses all the
requirements in this section. The program must also be approved and signed by the attending veterinarian and made
available for all inspections.
