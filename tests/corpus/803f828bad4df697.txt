2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The male short-tailed opossum "Otis" was observed to have a pink, swollen area, approximately 3 mm in diameter,
on his anus. No active bleeding or discharge was observed during examination. The facility representative provided
documentation showing that the veterinarian had examined and treated Otis on September 26, 2017.
Otis needs to be reevaluated by a veterinarian and an appropriate plan of treatment established to address the
issue. The program for providing adequate veterinary care shall include the use of appropriate methods to prevent,
control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries. The licensee is required to follow the guidance of their attending
veterinarian.
During the inspection, the facility representative was able to make an appointment with the veterinarian for February
2, 2018.
