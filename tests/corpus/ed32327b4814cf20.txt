3.51(d)

Facilities, indoor.
The new rabbit building has bare wood on the front and back wall. Interior building surfaces of indoor rabbit facilities must
be constructed and maintained so that the wall surfaces are substantially impervious to moisture so that the surfaces can
be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized, will not retain odors, and so that moisture will not absorb into the surface material.
The bare wood on the front and back wall of the rabbit building must sealed or covered with an impervious surface.
