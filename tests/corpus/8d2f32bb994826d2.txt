2.36(b)(1)
ANNUAL REPORT.
The annual report filed in 2015 had guinea pigs listed in category C which should have been listed in category D
according to the use of the animal in the protocol.
The annual report shall assure acceptable standards governing the care, treatment, and use of animals, including
appropriate use of anesthetic, analgesic, and tranquilizing drugs, prior to, during, and following actual research.
In order to comply with this section of the regulations animals must be reported in the appropriate category
according to the procedures conducted with the animals and the use of anesthesia, analgesics, etc.
To be corrected by 7-15-2016.
3.1(c)(2)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The igloo houses inside the dog enclosures had chewed/rough edges and a toy ball was also chewed.
In order to properly sanitize these objects and surfaces and to prevent injury to the animals from swallowing a piece
of the chewed surface or cutting their gums with the rough edges, all surfaces must be maintained or replaced on a
regular basis.
It was corrected while conducting the inspection.
