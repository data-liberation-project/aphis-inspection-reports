3.25(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Structural strength.The door on the north side of the buidling is being left open without a screen or barrier to prevent
other animals from entering the guinea pig building. Housing facilities for guinea pigs must be structurally sound and
maintained in good repair, to protect the animals from injury, to contain the animals, and to restrict the entrance of
other animals. If the door is left open, a screen door or other barrier must be provided to restrict other animals from
entering the guinea pig building and to prevent escape of the guinea pigs. Licensee must ensure that the building
and doors are structurally sound and kept in good repair to prevent disease risks and injury to the animals by
restricting other animals from entering and to contain the guinea pigs in the building.
