Facilities, general.
Storage. There were open bags of feed in the two Syrian/golden hamster trailers. The food storage room had an open
bag of rodent feed and a wheelbarrow full of rodent feed. Open food supplies must be stored in containers with tightly
fitted lids or covers to protect supplies from infestation or contamination by vermin. Licensee must ensure that open feed
is stored in containers with lids or covers to protect supplies. Correct by November 10, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
