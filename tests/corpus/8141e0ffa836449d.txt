2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC reviewed the research facility's program for humane care and use of animals on 5 June 2014. The next
review of the facility's program for humane care and use of animals occurred on 27 April 2015. The IACUC is
responsible for ensuring that the research facility's program uses and cares for animals in a humane manner
according to the Animal Welfare Act. An IACUC that does not meet to review the program at least once every six
months does not fulfill these responsibilities. The IACUC must review the research facility's program for humane
care and use of animals at least once every six months. To be corrected with the next program view of the facility.
2.31(c)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC reviewed the research facility's animal facility 5 June 2014. The next review of the animal use facility
occurred on 27 April 2015. The IACUC is responsible for ensuring that the research facility's animal facility is safe
and appropriate for animal use and compliant according to the Animal Welfare Act. An IACUC that does not meet to
review the facility at least once every six months does not fulfill these responsibilities. The IACUC must review the
research facility's animal facility at least once every six months. To be corrected with the next review of the facility.
The inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representatives and Margaret A Shaver, VMO in
attendance.
