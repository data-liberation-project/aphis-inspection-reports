2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Item IACUC 2.31 c 3
For 2016 the IACUC only met once, that being in April 2016 to perform a Semiannual inspection and report. Since
that time the IACUC failed to meet and perform an inspection, program review and semiannual report as required
under the act.
The IACUC needs to insure it meets at least once every 6 months to perform an inspection, program review and
completion of a semiannual report signed by a majority of IACUC members
Correct from this day 1/ 11/2017
