2.75(b) REPEAT
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Acquisition and disposition information was not provided for all of the animals. The animal records must include the
name and address from whom the animals were purchased or acquired, the USDA license or registration number,
and the vehicle license number and state and driver's license number and state if not licensed or registered. In
addition, the information must include the name and address of the person to whom an animal was sold or given,
the date of purchase, acquisition, sale or disposal of an animal, the species of the animal, and the number of
animals in the shipment.

Records enable the inspection process and provide traceability of animals, which can help protect their welfare.
The records must be updated and maintained for all regulated animals at the facility.
During the inspection, the Record of Animals on Hand was partially updated.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the facility representative.
