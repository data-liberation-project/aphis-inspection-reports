2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC did not conduct a review of the facility’s program for humane care and use of animals at least once
every six months in 2019. There was only one program review held on April 16, 2019.
The IACUC must review the animal use program, at least once every six months, in order to assure the proper care
and welfare of animals with respect to the activities involving animals.
The IACUC did resume review of the facility's program in April 2020. The next review shall occur at least once, six
months from this date.
2.31(c)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC did not conduct an inspection of the animal facilities at least once every six months in 2019. Only one
facility inspection occurred on April 16, 2019.
The IACUC must inspect the animal facilities, at least once every six months, in order to assure the proper care and
welfare of animals with respect to the activities involving animals.
The IACUC did resume inspection of the animal facilities in April 2020. The next review shall occur at least once, six
months from this date.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Program Coordinator and facility representative.
