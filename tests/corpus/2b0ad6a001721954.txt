2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
* A facility representative was not available at 3 PM to do a facility inspection.
* Inspector knocked on front door to the house of the licensee. Inspector then honked GOV horn and called
telephone numbers given by licensee. Licensee stated per phone conservation that he had prior commitments and
would not be available to do a facility inspection.
* Copy of inspection report was sent by regular mail.
