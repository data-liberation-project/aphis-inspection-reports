Date: 28-APR-2022

2.131(b)(1) Critical

Handling of animals.
While shifting the guinea pigs from one enclosure to another, a facility employee accidentally closed the shift door on top
of a guinea pig attempting to return to the initial enclosure. The animal died as a result of the injuries from the incident.
Animals must be handled in a manner that doesn't cause them physical harm.

Note: The facility has taken action to assure the shift doors do not injure the animals.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
