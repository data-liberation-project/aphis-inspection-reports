2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This pet shore has sugar gliders and hedgehogs on exhibit to the public for sale. Pocket pets have been sold at this
pet store. The owner Christine Mandia stated she was unaware of needing a USDA license for this new pet store
and that she was informed the previous owners USDA license was allowed. A new application was given and Mrs.
Mandia stated she would apply for a USDA license.
Any person operating as a dealer or exhibitor must have a valid license. Until a USDA is obtained no regulated
animals can be exhibited or sold.
