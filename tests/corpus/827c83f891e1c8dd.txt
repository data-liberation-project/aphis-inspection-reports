2.131(b)(1) Critical

Handling of animals.
On March 31,2022, six (6) two-toed sloths were shipped from this broker in SW Florida to an exhibitor in Canada. Upon
arrival, one of the sloths was observed to be in poor body condition with open wounds showing exposed bone on both
tarsi.

Failure to implement proper handling procedures exposes the animals to potential injuries and undue stress.

Handling of all animals shall be done as expeditiously and carefully as possible in a manner that does not cause trauma,
overheating, excessive cooling, behavioral stress, physical harm, or unnecessary discomfort.
To be corrected by 5/18/2022.
3.129(a) Critical

Feeding.
On March 31,2022, six (6) two-toed sloths were shipped from this broker in SW Florida to an exhibitor in Canada. Upon
arrival, at least three of the sloths were identified as being in poor body condition and two of the sloths were humanely
euthanized due to complications from emaciation.



Animals that are not provided with essential nutrients are more susceptible to sickness, disease, and in extreme cases,
death.

The food shall be wholesome, palatable, and free from contamination and of sufficient quantity and nutritive value to
maintain all animals in good health.
To be corrected by 5/18/2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
