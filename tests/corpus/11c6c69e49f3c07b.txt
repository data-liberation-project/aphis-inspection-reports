3.1(c)(1)(1)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
(c) Surfaces--(1) General requirements.(i)
There were three sheltered enclosures at the end of the housing area that had rusted expanded metal flooring in the
outdoor runs. Rusted flooring cannot be properly cleaned and sanitized and may break. The licensee shall provide
surfaces that are free from excessive rust.

3.6(a)(2)(10)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
3.6Primary enclosures for dogs and cats must meet the following minimum requirements:
(a) General requirements.(2)(x)
There was a litter of un-weaned puppies housed with their mother in the indoor whelping area that were observed
with their feet and legs passing through the flooring of the enclosure. Young animals housed on flooring that does
not properly support them may not learn to gait properly or may sustain injury. The licensee shall provide enclosures
that are constructed so that the animal's feet cannot pass through any openings of the floor.
TO BE CORRECTED BY: 4/21/14

PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Sec. 3.6 Primary enclosures.(2)(iii)
There were two adult females ID 919 and 986 housed in the indoor housing area that did not have 6 inches of head
