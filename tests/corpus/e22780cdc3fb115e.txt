2.1
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
*** This person sold a ring-tailed lemur at an exotic animal auction without a valid USDA license. Contact by phone
with this person by USDA inspector revealed that the USDA license number provided to the auction belonged to
another USDA licensed facility. The sale of all exotic species including primates requires a valid USDA license.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITY MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
