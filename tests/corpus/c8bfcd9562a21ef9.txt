2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11am on
02/13/2014. APHIS official waited for forty five minutes at facility, and checked at the residence but no one was
home. A message was left on Licensee's voice mail. He returned the call at 12:30pm. He had been at the dentist
that morning, had not yet returned home, and was unable to return home until evening. Optimal hours for
inspection were established.
