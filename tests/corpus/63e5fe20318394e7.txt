Facilities, general.
Storage. There is spilled feed on the floor underneath the wooden feed bin in the food storage shed. There are rodent
droppings on shelf above the feed bin. An opened bag of dry dog food was present on the floor in the food storage shed.
Supplies of food and bedding must be stored in facilities which adequately protect the supplies from spoilage, infestation
or contamination by rodents. Correct by ensuring that opened feed is stored in closed containers, spilled feed is removed
from the floor, and food supplies are protected from contamination by rodents. Correct by January 30, 2021
3.31(b)

Sanitation.
Housekeeping. There are accumulations of cobwebs, dust, dirt, and debris on the ceiling, walls, fans, and racks. Animal
facilities must be kept clean to ensure good quality of air for the animals and to prevent unnecessary disease risks.
Ineffective cleaning exposes the animals to disease hazards and health problem. Correct by ensuring the premises are
kept clean, including: removing cobwebs, dust, dirt, and debris from ceiling, walls, fans, and racks; routinely dusting off
and cleaning all surfaces in the inside of the guinea pig room; and maintaining a level of general cleanliness to support
good animal husbandry and health. Correct by January 30, 2021.
3.31(c)

Sanitation.

Pest control. There are rodent droppings on the shelf above the wooden feed bin in the guinea pig shed. There are plastic
containers, cardboard boxes, and miscellaneous items stored on the shelf which can provide hiding and living areas for
rodents. There was spilled feed on the floor underneath a wooded feed bin. There were no pest control measures in use

contamination of feed and to protect the health of the guinea pigs. Correct by February 4, 2021.
