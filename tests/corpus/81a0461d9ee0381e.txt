2.126(b)

Access and inspection of records and property; submission of itineraries.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11:31 am on June
07, 2021. The inspector arrived at 11:31 am, phoned the contact number on file, rang the doorbell and knocked on the
door, and waited for 30 minutes. The licensee returned the call and replied that they were traveling and not available for
inspection. Licensees shall ensure that a responsible adult is available to accompany an inspector for inspection during
standard business hours.
