2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The facility inventory records of animals on hand are not complete or up to date. Animals can not be properly traced or
tracked when animal inventory records are not kept current with all required information. The license must make, keep
and maintain inventory records of animals on hand that contain all required information. Correct by September 30, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
