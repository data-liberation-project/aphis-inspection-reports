2.75(a)(1)(vi)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
Adult dogs are listed in the record of dogs in kennel by their name. The official USDA tag number, tattoo, or microchip
number must be listed on the Record of Acquisition of Dogs and Cats on Hand (APHIS form 7005) to help identify and
keep track of dogs. Licensee must ensure all dogs are identified in records with an official tag number, tattoo, or microchip
to help keep track of animals. Correct by April 10, 2022.
2.75(a)(2)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
Dogs and puppies are currently being listed on PA state records instead of the Record of Acquisition of Dogs and Cats on
Hand (APHIS form 7005). Licensed dealers are required to use APHIS form 7005 and APHIS form 7006 to keep records
unless a variance is requested and obtained to use a computerized record keeping system. Licensee must use APHIS
form 7005 to keep track of dogs and puppies. Correct by April 14, 2022.
