3.6(a)(2)(ix)

Primary enclosures.
In an enclosure housing two Bengal cats, the concrete entryway and several of the vertical wooden supports around the
enclosure as well as the surfaces of a climbing tree were unsealed preventing those surfaces from being readily cleaned
and sanitized.
The surfaces of housing facilities including houses, dens, and other furniture -type fixtures and objects within the facility
must be constructed in a manner and made of materials that allow them to be readily cleaned and sanitized, or removed
and replaced when worn or soiled.
