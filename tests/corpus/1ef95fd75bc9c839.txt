3.31(a)
SANITATION.
All enclosures have a build up of manure which leave the pigs with no clean resting area. Dirty pens are not a
healthy environment for the animals and can lead to an increase in disease , odors and pests, all which may result
in pain and distress to the animals. All pens must be cleaned with such frequency to prevent a build up of manure.
