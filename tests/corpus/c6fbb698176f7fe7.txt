2.31(b)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
IACUC Membership.
1. The research facility's Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC) does not have a Chairman. The
Chairman of the IACUC left the college in August 2015 but a replacement has not been appointed by the
Institutional Official (IO).
Per this Section of the Regulations, the IACUC shall be composed of a Chairman and at least 2 other members who
are appointed by the Chief Executive Officer of the research facility. A properly constituted IACUC is important and
is required in order to assess a research facility'(cid:25)s animal program, animal facilities, and procedures to ensure
compliance with the AWA and Regulations.
The IO needs to appoint a Chairman to the IACUC.
Correct by 4/15/16.

2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
IACUC functions.
1. The IACUC has not updated their reports to the Institutional Official (IO) at least once every six months as
required. The last Semi-annual IACUC Report submitted to the IO was dated 4/20/15 and pertained to the IACUC's
April 2015 semi-annual reviews of the program for animal care and animal facility inspection. Almost 12 months
have elapsed since the IACUC prepared and updated their semi-annual report to the IO.
Per this Section of the Regulations, reports of the IACUC semi-annual evaluations of the program for animal care
and
animal facility inspection should be updated at least once every 6 months upon completion of the required
semiannual evaluations and the report should be submitted to the Institutional Official. Compliance with the
requirements outlined in this Section is important to ensure the IO is updated regarding the status of the animal use
program and the condition of the animal facilities to ensure that the research facility is adhering to the standards and
regulations specified under the Act.
The IACUC needs to update their report to the IO after completing the semi-annual review of the program for animal
use and the inspection of the animal facilities.Correct by 5/1/16.
