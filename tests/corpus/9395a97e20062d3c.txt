2.8
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME, ADDRESS, CONTROL, OR OWNERSHIP OF BUSINESS.
Inspectors attempted to perform a routine inspection on Jurassic Pet's Thornton location. Upon arrival they were
notified by the new store owner, Mr. Josiah X, that Jurassic Pets had been sold to him on September 1, 2016 and
renamed Exotic Pets and Fish Co. USDA had not been notified about the change of ownership of the business by
the licensee.
The licensee is required to promptly notify the AC Regional Director by certified mail of any change in the name,
address, management, or substantial control or ownership of is business or operation within ten days of any
change.
