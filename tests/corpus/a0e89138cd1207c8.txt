2.40(a)(2)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The vaccinations and deworming in the written program of veterinary care are not being followed by the licensee. The
veterinarian has a rabies vaccine, ferret distemper vaccine, deworming with Pyrantel, and selamectin for topical parasites
listed for the ferret on hand. Neither the vaccines, the deworming, or selamectin have been administered to the ferret.
The attending veterinarian recommended Pyrantel for deworming, and selamectin for topical parasites be given to the 2
hedgehogs, and sugar glider. These medications have not been administered to the hedgehogs or sugar glider.
Each dealer shall assure that the attending veterinarian has appropriate authority to ensure the provision of adequate
veterinary care.
The attending veterinarians written recommendations must be followed to ensure that the animals held at the facility are
maintained in good health.
