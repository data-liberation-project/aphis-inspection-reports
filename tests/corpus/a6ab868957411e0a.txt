2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC met on March 9, 2015 and conducted a program review and facility inspection. The reports of that
review and inspection were not submitted to the Institutional Official(IO). The IO is responsible for ensuring all
deficiencies are corrected in a timely fashion, and for reporting any uncorrected significant deficiencies to APHIS
within 15 business days of the elapsed correction date. It is important that the IO be kept apprised of IACUC
activities so that the humane care and use of animals at the facility is ensured and maintained in adherence with the
Animal Welfare Act. Correct by preparing a report of the IACUC's semi-annual program review and facility
inspection and send the report to the IO by May 19, 2015. Furthermore, send all future program review and facility
inspection reports conducted by the IACUC to the IO in a timely manner this day forward.
The inspection was conducted with Animal Resources staff and the Director of Lab Animal Resources and the exit
interview was conducted with the IO and the Director of Lab Animal Resources.
