2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Any person operating or intending to operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except persons
who are exempted from the licensing requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section, must have a valid license.
The owners of the facility have a petting farm with regulated species on exhibit to the public.
A USDA, APHIS, AC license is required to exhibit animals to the public.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONCUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Exit conducted with a facility representative.
