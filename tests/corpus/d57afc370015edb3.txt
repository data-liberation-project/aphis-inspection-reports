HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The licensee feeds meat/chicken directly on the cats wooden elevated resting surfaces. The surfaces in the back
area cat enclosure as well as the right enclosure in the middle section were soiled with old dried blood and meat
remnants (there were numerous ants on the back area resting surface that were over the food).
All surfaces which are used for feeding and animal contact shall be cleaned and sanitized when necessary to satisfy
generally accepted husbandry standards/practices and minimize the attraction of insects and vermin.
Correct immediately upon receipt of this inspection report.
