INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
IACUC functions. The IACUC did not review the research facility's program for humane care and use of animals,
inspect all of the animal facilities, or prepare and submit reports of these evaluations to the Institutional Official (I.O.)
