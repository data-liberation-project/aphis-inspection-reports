2.131(c)(1)

Handling of animals.
The tapir has been moved to a new enclosure that does not have a barrier fence . The public could easily reach the
animal when he is in the pool or at the feeder which are located along the fence. Tapirs have been known to bite. During
public exhibition, there must be sufficient distance and /or barriers between the animal and the public to assure the safety
of the animals and the public. A barrier that prevents public contact with the animal must be constructed in this area .
Fence panels were added during the inspection . CORRECTED AT TIME OF INSPECTION

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility employee.
