Requirements and application
Upon arrival to the facility it was noted that the property gates were locked. In contacting the licensee, the Aphis Official
was informed that the licensees moved from this location back in September 2022 and now have a new location. The
licensee failed to notify Animal Care of said move in less than 90 days nor did they obtain a new license before this
change of location.

A licensee shall notify Animal Care no fewer than 90 days and obtain a new license before any change in the name,
address, substantial control or ownership of his business or operation, locations, activities, and number or type of animals
described in paragraph (b)(2) of this section.
