3.131(c)
SANITATION.
*** Poles used to enclose the camels and the Scottish Highlander had recently been painted. Small amounts of the
paint had fallen on the grass beneath. The animals were seen to be grazing on the grass in the area of the paint. If
the paint were ingested toxicity could result.
For the health of the animals, premises must be kept clean and foreign material properly disposed of.
This non-compliance was corrected immediately.
An exit interview was cconducted with the Licensee on 8/7/14.
This inspection was conducted at the Skowhegan Maine State Fairgrounds on 8/7/2014
