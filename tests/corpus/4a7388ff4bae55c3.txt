2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This facility is exhibiting USDA regulated animals without a USDA license. The animals on exhibit to the public
during this inspection include a fox, two raccoons and a skunk. The USDA license for this facility was cancelled on
August 31, 2017, due to failing to renew. The new Director stated he was unaware that their USDA license had
expired and has contacted USDA for an application packet.
Any person operating or intending to operate exhibitor must have a valid USDA license.
UNTIL A USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED THERE MUST NOT BE ANY PUBLIC EXHIBITING OF USDA COVERED
ANIMALS
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Director.
