2.33(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
The following expired drugs were identified in the large animal barn and were being stored with in-date drugs:
Trimethoprim Sulfa, Yohimbe, Lutalyse, Prevail, Lidocaine, Dexamethasone and Xylazine.
Expired drugs should be clearly labeled and stored in a manner that prevents their accidental use.The use of
expired drugs could negatively impact the health and well-being of the animals.
Each research facility shall establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that include the use of
appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries.
To be corrected by July 22, 2015
An exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
