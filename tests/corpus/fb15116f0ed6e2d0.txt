3.26(a)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
The ambient temperature of the facility within which the guinea pigs were housed was maintained at 51 degrees
Fahrenheit, according to the thermostat and validated verbally by the facility representative. A thermometer kept
several feet from the thermostat indicated the temperature of the facility was approximately 48 degrees Fahrenheit.
The ambient temperature shall not be allowed to fall below 60 degrees Fahrenheit, nor exceed 85 degrees
Fahrenheit. Sufficient heating is required to protect the animals from the cold and to provide for their health and
comfort.
The temperature of the facility must be corrected to so that the facility has an ambient temperature of at least 60
degrees Fahrenheit, not to exceed 85 degrees Fahrenheit.
Correct by February 14, 2019.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
