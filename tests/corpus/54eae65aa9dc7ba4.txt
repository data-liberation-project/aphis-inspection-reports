3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The outdoor enclosure housing 3 cows had a shelter structure with two nails exposed on the southwest corner near
the roof. The metal roofing material on the northwest corner of this shelter was bent and unsecured from the
decking. The nails and unsecured, bent metal have sharp edges and pose a risk of injury to the animals.
Facilities shall be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury.
To be corrected by 2/18/2015.
Accompanied on the inspection by the Recreation Supervisor, the Farm Technician, an APHIS AC Student, and the
AC VMO. Exit interview conducted on site with facility representatives.
