HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
(a) Structure; construction.
***** At time of inspection the houses located in the pens known to the owner as "Dons" and "Einstiens" pens are no
longer structurally sound and need to be repaired or replaced. The side and back walls have started separating from
the roof section and the roofing material covering the roof has become torn and is in need of replacement.
Repair or replacement of these housing units will provide the protection needed for the animals in these pens during
periods of strong winds, rain and snow or ice which all are experienced in this location depending on the time of
year.
