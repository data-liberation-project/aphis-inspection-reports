2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.

02/01/2016.
Inspector arrived at 2:25 pm and spoke with a household member who said that the licensee was unavailable and
there was no other responsible adult available for the inspection. The inspector left her phone number for the
