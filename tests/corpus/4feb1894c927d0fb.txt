3.131(a)
SANITATION.
The enclosures in rooms 4 and 5 had just been cleaned, but there was still wet and obviously soiled bedding in the
bottom corners of the enclosures, as well as under and behind the nest boxes. Failure to clean thoroughly on the
days the enclosures are scheduled to be stripped and rebedded can contribute to disease transmission, soiling of
the animals, and odors.
Correct by ensuring all employees are properly trained and supervised in the cleaning procedures from now on.
The inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the licensee.
