Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
A bottle of pills labeled "Metronidazole 500 mg" has no instructions or expiration date. The licensee says they received

3.1(e)

Housing facilities, general.
The container holding wood shaving bedding for the dogs did not have a lid on it. All open supplies of food and bedding
must be kept in leakproof containers with tightly fitting lids to prevent contamination and spoilage. The facility must put the
open bedding material in a container with a tight fitting lid.
3.1(f)

Housing facilities, general.
The trash container in the dog building did not have a lid on it. Open trash can attract insects and other pests that can be
a threat to the health and well being of the dogs. Trash containers in housing facilities must be leakproof and must have

tightly fitted lids on them at all times. The facility must use a trash container with a tight fitting lid in the this area where the
dogs are housed.
3.13(a)(3)

Veterinary care for dogs.
The written program of veterinary care is incomplete. There is no plan for sampling for blood and intestinal parasites.
There is also no treatment plan for blood parasites. Parasites are a common cause of disease in dogs and puppies and if
undetected and properly treated, can lead to serious illness and even death. The program of veterinary care must be
completed and include plans for sampling and treatment for blood and intestinal parasites.
3.13(a)(4)

Veterinary care for dogs.
The written program of veterinary care does not include any plans for preventative care and treatment to ensure healthy
and unmatted hair coats, properly trimmed nails, and clean and healthy eyes, ears, skin, and teeth. A plan for the care
and appropriate treatment of disease in these areas is important for the health and well being of the dogs. The program
of veterinary care must be completed to include preventative care and treatment to ensure healthy and unmatted hair
coats, properly trimmed nails, and clean and healthy eyes, ears, skin, and teeth.
