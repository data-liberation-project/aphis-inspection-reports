2.33(b)(3)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care.
In discussion and records review, a lack of daily observation for the cats was noted on weekends. The facility records
document daily observations for Monday through Friday, but not Saturday and Sunday, the owner reports checking on the
cats either Saturday or Sunday on the weekends, but it is not completed daily. Daily observation is essential to detect
possible problems, including disease and abnormal behavior and to ensure animal health and well-being.
