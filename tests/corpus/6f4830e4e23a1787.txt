3.129(b)
FEEDING.
At the time of inspection, there was dried blood spilled on the interior of the holding refrigerator in the cougar night
house. The spilled blood can pose a risk of contamination on other food products and transmitting disease. Food
receptacles shall be kept clean and sanitary at all times. The refrigerator must be kept sanitary and cleaned to
prevent this risk.
Correct by: December 19, 2014
The inspection and exit interview were conducted December 17 - 18 2014 with the Curator of Collections and the
Senior Veterinarian.
