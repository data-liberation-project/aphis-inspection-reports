3.26(d)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
Interior surfaces. There is bare wood on walls, shelves, work bench, and areas of the floor where wood is present.
Interior surfaces of indoor guinea pigs rooms must be constructed and maintained so that all surfaces are
impervious to moisture so that they can be readily cleaned and sanitized and won't retain odors. The bare wood on
walls, shelves, work bench, and wooden areas of the floor must be sealed or covered with an impervious surface so
they the surfaces can be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized. Correct by May 18, 2018.
Note: Adult female (tan and white) smooth haired guinea pig housed in the top enclosure in row 6, was examined by
the attending veterinarian and treated. Animal had no signs of hair loss on this inspection.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
