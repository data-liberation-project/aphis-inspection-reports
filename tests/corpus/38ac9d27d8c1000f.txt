3.125(a) REPEAT
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Structural strength. The following structural items were present:
1) The petting zoo fence on the west side still has protruding wire, bent wire, and loose wire strands.
2) There is loose and detached siding on the barn with protruding nails and screws on the inside walls.
Facilities must be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury and to contain
the animals. The fence on the west side of the petting zoo area must be repaired, removed, or replaced to prevent
possible injury to the animals and to contain the animals securely. The loose siding on the barn and the protruding
nails and screws must be repaired, removed, or replaced to prevent possible injury to the animals.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
