3.1(e)

Housing facilities, general.
A bag of bedding at the premises was being kept on the floor, and an opened bag of food and an opened bag of bedding
were not being kept in containers with lids.

Supplies of bedding kept on the floor, as well as open bags of food and bedding, can be spoiled, contaminated, and/or
infested with vermin, creating potentially hazardous situations for dogs.

Supplies of bedding and food must be stored in a manner that protects the supplies from spoilage, contamination, and
vermin infestation. Supplies of bedding and food must be stored off the floor to allow for cleaning underneath and around
the supplies.

Correct by August 28, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
