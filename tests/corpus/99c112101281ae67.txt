2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 1:30PM on
22-Jul-19.
The inspector arrived at 1:30 PM on 7/22/19 to conduct an inspection but there was no one on the premises. The
inspector contacted the Licensee by phone and learned that he/she was at a distant off-site location and would not
return until later that day.
An AWA compliance inspection could not be conducted because there was no other facility representative available
to accompany the inspector.
Unannounced inspections are used to evaluate compliance with the AWA Regulations and Standards.
