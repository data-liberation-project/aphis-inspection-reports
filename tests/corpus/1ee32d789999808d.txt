3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The back corner of the wall of the arctic fox enclosure was not securely fixed to the frame, and the wood was worn,
had been chewed, and a hole was present. The animal may escape from an enclosure that is not structurally
sound. The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair
to protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
Correct by date: 30 May 2019
