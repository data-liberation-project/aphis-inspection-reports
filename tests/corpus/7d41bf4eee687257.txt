3.31(b)
SANITATION.
Housekeeping. There is clutter and supplies in the back corner on the floor of the guinea pig room. Animal areas
inside of housing facilities must be kept neat and free of clutter, but may contain materials actually used and
necessary for cleaning the area, and equipment and supplies needed to care for the animals. Clutter and
unnecessary supplies must be removed from the back corner of the animal room. Animal facilities must be kept
clean to ensure the health and well-being of the animals.
