3.28(c)(2)(ii)

Primary enclosures.
(c)(2)(ii) The interior height of any primary enclosure used to confine hamsters shall be at least 6 inches (15.24 cm).

Six rodent enclosures, measuring 9.25" wide x 21" long x 3.5 " in high, were observed to be housing 32 Russian dwarf, 18
Chinese dwarf, and 20 Roborovski dwarf hamsters. All of the enclosures provided the appropriate floor space for the
hamsters. However, the interior height of these enclosures did not meet the required height of 6 inches. All hamsters
shall be housed in primary enclosures that have an interior height of at least 6 inches. Staff immediately removed the
hamsters and placed them in enclosures that meet the space and height requirements for hamsters.

CORRECTED AT TIME OF INSPECTION 4-26-22.

This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the licensee.
