ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 9:15am to
9:45am on 06/20/2014 or at 12:50pm to 1:20pm on 05/05/2014. Attempts to make contact with the licensee were
done each visit by ringing the doorbell and knocking on the door, waiting in the driveway for 30 minutes, honking the
vehicle horn, and calling the licensee on the phone and leaving a message when there was no answer.
