2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
A current records of guinea pigs was not available for review during this inspection. A total of 204 adult guinea pigs, 108
weanlings, and 109 newborns were counted during this inspection. There were no inventory records kept since March
2022. Failure to maintain these records can make it difficult to assess the health of the caviary by identifying animal
losses by death or decreased numbers of animals born and to track animals being sold. The licensee must maintain
records containing the required information for animals which disclose the number of guinea pigs held, owned, acquired
or disposed of including births and deaths. Correct by June 2, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
