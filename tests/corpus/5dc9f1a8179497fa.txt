2.40(a)(1) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee does not have a written program of veterinary care from his attending veterinarian. A complete and
accurate written program of veterinary care is required to ensure that the animals are receiving veterinary care
consistent with currently accepted veterinary medical practices. Each dealer and exhibitor shall employ an attending
veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a part-time attending veterinarian or consultant
arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled
visits to the premises of the dealer or exhibitor. The facility must have a veterinarian visit the facility and complete
the program of veterinary care.
3.1(a) REPEAT
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
- There are structures in the facility that were not being properly maintained and repaired. Two metal vertical
support poles in the first run of the outdoor area are in disrepair. The bottom section of the poles are rusted,
jagged, and not structurally sound. In the second run the shelter door has been excessively chewed, leaving the
wood in poor condition and exposing the head of a screw.
In the indoor housing area, an enclosure with a single female and one puppy has hard plastic walls that are
damaged and broken in two places.
Damaged structures are a potential source of injury to the animals, and cannot be cleaned and sanitized as needed.
Housing facilities for dogs must be designed and constructed so that they are structurally sound. They must be kept
in good repair, and they must protect the animals from injury.
3.1(c)(3) REPEAT
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
- In the sheltered section of the facility, an outdoor run housing four dogs has an excessive accumulation of feces
near the gate. The fecal matter is dry and whitish in color. Accumulation of waste material is a source of potential
contamination to the animals. This area must be cleaned as often as necessary to ensure the animals are being
adequately protected.This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
