3.56(d)

Sanitation.
In the small animal room located at the back of the facility where Animal Encounters are held there was a cockroach
infestation. Cockroaches were found on the floor and walls of the room where a cage containing four weanling rabbits was
kept.

Cockroaches can carry or transmit bacterial diseases that can affect the health and well-being of animals. It is important
to have an effective pest control program in place to minimize potential disease-causing organisms that can be spread by
cockroaches.

An effective program for the control of insects, ectoparasites, and avian and mammalian pests shall be established and
maintained.

Correct from this day forward.
3.84(d)

Cleaning, sanitization, housekeeping, and pest control.


In the small animal room located at the back of the facility where Animal Encounters are held there was a cockroach
infestation. Cockroaches were found on the floor and walls of the room where a Black-and-white Ruffed Lemur was
housed.

Cockroaches can carry or transmit bacterial diseases that can affect the health and well-being of nonhuman primates. It
is important to have an effective pest control program in place to minimize potential disease-causing organisms spread by
cockroaches.

An effective program for control of insects, external parasites affecting nonhuman primates, and birds and mammals that
are pests, must be established and maintained to promote the health and well-being of the animals and reduce
contamination by pests in animal areas.

Correct from this day forward.
3.131(d)

Sanitation.
In the small animal room located at the back of the facility where Animal Encounters are held there was a cockroach
infestation. Cockroaches were found on the floor and walls of the room where enclosures and cages containing a
Kinkajou, a hedgehog, two chinchillas, and a skunk, were kept. One cockroach was observed on the floor of the skunk
enclosure, and several were observed at the bottom of the Kinkajou's metal cage on feces.

Cockroaches can carry or transmit bacterial diseases that can affect the health and well-being of animals. It is important
to have an effective pest control program in place to minimize potential disease-causing organisms that can be spread by


cockroaches.

A safe and effective program for the control of insects, ectoparasites, and avian and mammalian pests shall be
established and maintained.

Correct from this day forward.
