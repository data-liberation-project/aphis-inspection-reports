2.75(a)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
There are five records of disposition from an unlicensed source confirming the acquisition of 24 dogs/puppies that are
missing information. Four documents do not have the date of sale. One document is missing the date of birth or
approximate age for four animals. One document is missing the color and any distinctive markings of four animals. On two
different records, the dates of birth do not match the electronic records provided by the facility, encompassing six animals
in total. On one record, the sexes of four dogs do not match the electronic records provided by the facility. Four animals
on one acquisition record are missing from the electronic records provided by the facility. This information is required to
ensure animal tracking and the accountability of acquisition/disposition is possible. Correct this by ensuring that full and
accurate information on all acquisition and disposition records are provided.
Correct By: 12/15/2021
2.132(d) Critical

Procurement of dogs, cats, and other animals; dealers.
The licensee is knowingly acquiring animals from an unlicensed business who is required to have a USDA license. There
are five disposition records that confirm the transfer of 24 dogs/puppies from the un-licensed source and acquired by the
facility. The licensee stated he is on the board of the unlicensed business. The unlicensed business acquires animals
from several different locations, they are not born and raised on the unlicensed facilities premise. After acquiring the

dogs/puppies, the animals are transferred to his facility for re-sale or re-distribution to other LPO locations within the US.
Brokers shall not knowingly obtain any dog from any person/business who is required to be licensed but who does not
hold a current, valid, and unsuspended license. Correct by ensuring all animals are acquired from locations that maintain
a USDA License or are exempt from requiring a USDA License.
Correct By Close of business on 12/14/2021
