3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
The facility recently acquired 2 young bobcats. On Friday December 17, 2021 the female bobcat escaped from the
outside/exhibit portion of its primary enclosure.

The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the
animals from injury and to contain the animals.

Primary enclosures for wild animals must be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to prevent escape from the
enclosure. Primary enclosures must contain the animals to prevent injury or death. The bobcat was livetraped off
grounds on December 19, 2021. The bobcats are currently being housed in the inside/sheltered portion of their
enclosure.

To be corrected January 22, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
