HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* An estimated six inch gap was observed along the bottom when the enclosure gate was closed. One dog is in
contact with the affected gap area which is located in outdoor housing. The potential for injury, stress or discomfort
exists if the dog gets caught in the gap area. Enclosure gates that properly close at all times is necessary to ensure
the safety, health & well-being of the dog. The licensee must ensure all enclosures contain the dogs securely at all
times. Correct by 4/2/14.
3.1(e)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* There was one open sack of dog feed located in the storage building being fed to nine dogs. All open feed
supplies must be stored in a manner that protects it from spoilage, contamination or vermin infestation. The
nutritive value of the feed could be affected if it becomes spoiled or contaminated. Spoiled or contaminated feed is
not healthy for dogs to consume and could lead to health issues. The licensee must keep all open feed supplies in
leak proof containers with tightly fitting lids at all times. Correct by 3/26/14.
