3.127(d)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Two red kangaroos are housed in an enclosure in which one side of the enclosure fence line is the perimeter fence.
The perimeter fence serves to protect the animals from unwanted animals and persons from contacting the animals
and also serves as a secondary containment system. Animals such as kangaroos must be housed in an enclosure
that is at least 3 feet from the perimeter fence .
