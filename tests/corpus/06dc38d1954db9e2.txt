2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 1:45 PM on
24-Mar-18.The inspector arrived at 1:45 PM on 3/24/18 to conduct an inspection but there was no one on the
premises. The inspector contacted the Licensee by phone and learned that the Licensee was at a distant off-site
location and would not be available to accompany the inspector on an inspection.
An Animal Welfare Act compliance inspection could not be conducted because there was no other facility
representative available to accompany the inspector.
A responsible individual must be available for inspection during regular business hours.
