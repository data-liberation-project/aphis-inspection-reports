3.131(c)
SANITATION.
There is an excess accumulation of materials being stored next to the wallaby and kangaroo enclosure. Inside the
entrance to the building and next to the outdoor enclosure is travel crates, bird cages, boards, hay, building
materials and other items sitting on the ground. Piles of excess materials can harbor pests which pose a risk to the
health and well-being of the animals and does not facilitate proper husbandry practices. All buildings and grounds
shall be kept clean and in good repair to facilitate proper husbandry practices.
