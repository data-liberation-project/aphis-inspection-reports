2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Section 2.75(b)(1) Records: There are no current birth, death and euthanasia records for the hamsters. Birth, death
and euthanasia records are reviewed by APHIS officials during the inspection as part of the review of the overall
animal care program for the facility. Every dealer must make, keep and maintain records concerning animals
acquired, sold, euthanized, or otherwise disposed of by the dealer. The records shall include any offspring born of
any animal while in his or her possession. Correct by keeping records on birth, death and euthanasia for the
hamsters. Correct from this day forward.
