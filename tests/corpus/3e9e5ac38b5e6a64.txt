Housing facilities, general.

past normal wear and tear and can no longer be adequately cleaned and sanitized. The floors have not been maintained

replaced when it cannot be readily cleaned and sanitized. Correct by following a schedule for replacement and
developing a plan to ensure proper maintenance of the floors on a regular basis. Correct by March 31, 2022.
