2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
No responsible adult was available to accompany the inspector for an inspection. The inspector arrived at the facility
at 10:10 AM. The licensee was not at the facility. The inspector called the licensee who answered and said he
would not be available during normal business hours this day. The licensee stated there was no one else available
to accompany the inspector.
