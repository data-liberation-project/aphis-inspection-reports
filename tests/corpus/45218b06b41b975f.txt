2.126(c) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
At the time of this inspection a group of animals that included 5 tigers, were off site at a traveling location. The
animals had been housed overnight at this location for at least 5 days. As of the date of this inspection no itinerary
regarding the overnight travel and off site exhibition had been filed with the regional office. The last itinerary
received by the regional office indicated all animals would be at the home site after September 2015.
Itineraries must be received by the AC regional director no fewer than 2 days in advance of any travel and shall
contain complete and accurate information concerning the whereabouts of any animal intended for overnight
exhibition at any location other than the licensee's approved site.
The inspection and exit interview were conducted with Drs. M. Moore, E. Pannilll and the facility representative
designated by the licensee.
