2.75(b)(1)(iv)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Sale records to a new buyer/dealer were incomplete. The address of the new buyer/dealer was not present on sale
records on several transactions. Several sale dates to the same buyer/dealer were listed on the same receipt. The
sale records must include the full name and address of the buyer to help keep track of animals and to document
changes in ownership. A new sale record must be completed for each transaction to document the sale to the
buyer. APHIS forms 7020 were left with facility during this inspection to help owner with sale records. Correct by
September 9, 2019.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
