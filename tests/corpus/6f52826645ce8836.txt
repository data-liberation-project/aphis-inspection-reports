3.131(d)
SANITATION.
There are numerous fruit flies in the rooms housing the ferrets, chinchillas, and hedgehogs. The fly strip present
was covered with fruit flies. Additonal measures are necessary to reduce or eliminate the fruit fly problem.
Increasing the cleaning frequency, covering trash all times and establishing an effective treatment for the control of
fruit flies is needed. Flies can spread contamination and disease between animals in separate enclosures. An
effective program for insects shall be established and maintained.
