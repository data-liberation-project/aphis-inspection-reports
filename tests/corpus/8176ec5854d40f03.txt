3.84(d)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
The outside primary enclosure that houses two capuchins has a wood floor with a 1/2 inch space in-between each
wood board. The inspector noted two rats underneath the wood boards in the outside primary enclosure. Pests may
cause contamination and spread disease. The facility needs to establish and maintain an effective program for
controlling pests. Correct by 08/17/2016.
Exit briefing was conducted with Zoo Manager.
