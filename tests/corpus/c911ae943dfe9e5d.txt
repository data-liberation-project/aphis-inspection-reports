3.106(b)(3)
WATER QUALITY.
Water quality records presented indicate that water samples are not being collected and tested at least daily for ph.
According to the records and a facility representative, it is being done at least 4 times per week. Monitoring water
quality for ph on a daily basis is important for the health and well-being of the animals.
Water samples shall be taken and tested at least weekly for coliform count and at least daily for pH and any
chemical additives (e.g. chlorine and copper) that are added to the water to maintain water quality standards.
A system should be in place to ensure that samples are collected and tested as required.

SANITATION.
