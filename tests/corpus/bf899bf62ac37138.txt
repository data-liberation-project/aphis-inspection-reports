3.54(b)
FEEDING.
The metal feeders in the outdoor rabbit hutches have several areas of rust. The rusting of this material prevents the
feeders from being properly sanitized which can affect the health and well-being of the animals. The feeders must
be repaired or replaced and maintained to ensure the health of the rabbits.
Correct by October 16th, 2019.
3.56(b)(1)
SANITATION.
The walls and doors of the three outdoor rabbit hutches have several areas that have been chewed which has
resulted in the exposure of the underlying porous wood material. This damaged material cannot be properly
sanitized which can affect the health and well-being of the animals. The damaged wood must be repaired or
replaced and maintained to ensure the health of the rabbits.
Correct by October 16th, 2019.
