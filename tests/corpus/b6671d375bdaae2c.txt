2.126(a)(2)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
The licensee stated that the Program of Veterinary Care, Record of Animals on Hand and a record of disposition for
each animal were kept at a site away from the animals. This site is approximately twenty miles away and the
licensee was unable to retrieve these records for the inspectors during the inspection. Each dealer, exhibitor,
intermediate handler, or carrier, shall, during business hours, allow APHIS officials to examine all records that are
required to be kept by the Animal Welfare Act. Licensee must ensure that all required records are accessible to the
inspector at the time of the inspection.
Correction date: July 14, 2015
No regulated animals were present at the time of the inspection.
Exit interview conducted with Kelly Maxwell, ACI, Kate Ziegerer, DVM and the facility representative.
