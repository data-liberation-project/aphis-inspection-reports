3.131(a)
SANITATION.
The multi level climbing structure in the enclosure has a large accumulation of old feces, hay, and other dirt on the
floors.
These types of accumulations do not minimize disease hazards, nor do they reduce odors & pests (especially
during the summer months).
The enclosure shall be cleaned entirely as often as necessary to minimize these risks to the animals.
