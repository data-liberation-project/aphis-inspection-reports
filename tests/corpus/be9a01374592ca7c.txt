Cleaning, sanitization, housekeeping, and pest control.
The licensee is not using any cleaner prior to sanitizing the kennel. The licensee is using water only prior to using bleach
to sanitize the kennel.


Hard surfaces of primary enclosures and food and water receptacles must be sanitized using one of the following
methods: Washing all soiled surfaces with appropriate detergent solutions and disinfectants, or by using a combination
detergent/disinfectant product that accomplishes the same purpose, with a thorough cleaning of the surfaces to remove
organic material, so as to remove all organic material and mineral buildup, and to provide sanitization followed by a clean
water rinse.

A cleaner to remove all organic materials must be utilized prior to using a disinfectant.
