3.1(c)(1)(1)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* Excessive rust was observed on one wire enclosure gate. One dog and five puppies are in contact with the
affected area which is located in indoor housing. Rust can erode and deteriorate all metal surfaces to the point that
the metal surfaces can no longer be readily cleaned and sanitized. Excessive rust affects the structural integrity of
all metal surfaces. Licensee must ensure that this and all other surfaces are free of excessive rust and be of good
structural strength. Correct by 7/7/14.

HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* There is a mixture of dirt, grease and other excreta on the fiberglass wallboards in two enclosures. There are
four dogs in contact with the affected area located in indoor housing. The affected area covers approximately 25 to
30 percent of the total surface area. Sanitization of surfaces is necessary to prevent an accumulation of excreta

accordance with 3.11 (b). Correct by 7/28/14.
