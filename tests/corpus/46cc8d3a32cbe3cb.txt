ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).

arrangements. In the case of a part-time attending veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal
arrangements shall include a written program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the
