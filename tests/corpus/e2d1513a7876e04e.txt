2.1(b)(1)

Requirements and application
Upon arriving to the facility to conduct an inspection it was determined that the licensee had moved operations to a
different physical location. The licensee did not notify USDA of the location change. Accurate information regarding the
license is important for ensuring the facility meets the standards and an inspection can be conducted. A licensee shall
notify Animal Care no fewer than 90 days of any changes in the name, address, substantial control or ownership,
locations, activities, and number or types of animals. The licensee needs to notify Animal Care before any future changes
are made regarding the license.
