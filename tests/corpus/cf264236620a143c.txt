2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 3:15 pm on
06/04/2015. The gate leading to the house was closed. Honked the vehicle horn multiple times and called out to
the licensee. Due to having no cell phone service in the area left the facility to make a call to the licensee. A
message was left on the number that was provided by the licensee. Returned to the facility and waited until 3:45
pm when inspector departed.
