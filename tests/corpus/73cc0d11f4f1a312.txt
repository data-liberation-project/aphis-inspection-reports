3.58(a) REPEAT
CLASSIFICATION AND SEPARATION.
A group of 10 rabbits is housed with two guinea pigs. The rabbits are housed in a pen in the petting area barn with a
two-story hutch/nesting structure. The guinea pigs had a separate cage in that pen but it was open and they were
intermingled with the rabbits. Rabbits shall not be housed in the same primary enclosure with any other species of
animals unless required for scientific reasons.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
The inspection was started on Sept. 6, 2016 and completed on Sept. 7, 2016.
