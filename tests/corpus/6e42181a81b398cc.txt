3.81(c)(4)

Environment enhancement to promote psychological well-being.
A recent death of of a ring tailed lemur left a single ring tailed lemur at the facility. This individual is unable to see and
hear nonhuman primates of their own or compatible species and this circumstance is not addressed in the environmental
enhancement plan to promote psychological well-being of nonhuman primates. All nonhuman primates meeting the
special considerations must be addressed in the plan or exempted by the veterinarian. The plan must be accordance with
the currently accepted professional standards as cited in appropriate professional journals or reference guides and must
address all points in this section (3.81).
