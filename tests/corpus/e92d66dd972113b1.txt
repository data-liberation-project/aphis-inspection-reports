2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The attending veterinarian has not visited the site for over a year.
The attending veterinarian should visit the site regularly, and at minimum once per year. The written program of
veterinary care should be reviewed and updated regularly with the attending veterinarian. This serves to review all
aspects of animal husbandry and preventive medical care for the animals at the facility.
Failure to carry out regularly scheduled visits with the attending veterinarian could be detrimental to the health and
well-being of the animals.
