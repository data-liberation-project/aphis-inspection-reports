2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Two bottles of expired medications were found in the facility refrigerator. Since it cannot be certain that expired
drugs will have the intended medical effect they are not considered appropriate veterinary care. There should be a
schedule of checking all drugs for expiration dates and expired drugs removed from use.
Correct by: expired drugs were removed during inspection.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
