2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
There are no acquisition records on one or more shipments of hedgehogs.
Every dealer shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the following
information concerning animals purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, or otherwise in his or her
possession or under his or her control, or which is transported, sold, euthanized, or otherwise disposed of by that
dealer. The records shall include any offspring born of any animal while in his or her possession or under his or her
control.
