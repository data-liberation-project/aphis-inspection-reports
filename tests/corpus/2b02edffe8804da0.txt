ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
A white and black male goat back tag #334 was observed not putting weight on it's right rear leg. While standing,
the goats right rear leg was observed shaking and the goat seemed unsteady on his feet. The attending
veterinarian examined the goat found and old wound on the left rear leg. The intake paper work stated that the
animal arrived with a injury and was documented by the auction personnel. When animals arrive at the auction
facility with previous injuries, they must be examined by the attending veterinarian to determine if they are healthy
enough to go through the auction. Owner must ensure that all of the animal taken into the auction are healthy
enough to participate or examined by the attending veterinarian and deemed acceptable to go through the auction.
Correction date: The goat was removed from the auction and provide veterinarian care.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with facility representative.
