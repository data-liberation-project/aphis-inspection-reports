INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
IACUC functions.
1. The IACUC did not update their reports to the Institutional Official (IO) at least once every six months as required
per this Section of the Regulations. The IACUC prepared a semi-annual report to the Institutional Official on 1/21/13
following their August 2012 semi-annual reviews of the Program and facility inspection. The IACUC's next update
to their semi-annual report to the IO was prepared 9 months later on 10/24/13 (following their February 2013
semi-annual reviews of Program and facility inspection).
Per this Section of the Regulations, reports of the IACUC evaluations of the program for humane care and use of
animals and the inspection of the animal facilities should be updated at least once every 6 months upon completion
of the required semi-annual evaluations and the report submitted to the Institutional Official of the research facility.
Compliance with the requirements outlined in this Section is important to ensure the Institutional Official is aware of
and updated regarding the status of the animal use program and the condition of the animal facilities to ensure that
the facility is adhering to the standards and regulations specified under the Act.
