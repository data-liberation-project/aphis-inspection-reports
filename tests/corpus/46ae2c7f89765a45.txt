2.8
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME, ADDRESS, CONTROL, OR OWNERSHIP OF BUSINESS.
While interviewing facility personnel, it was noted by this inspector that USDA regulated activity (staging animals for
customer pickup) is occurring at different location owned by the licensee. A licensee must promptly notify the USDA
Animal Care office at Raleigh, North Carolina, by certified mail and within 10 days, of any additional sites where
business and/or transactions with regulated animals occur. The licensee must immediately request adding the
additional site as required by the Animal Welfare Act.
Correct by: April 16, 2018
2.76(a)(1)
RECORDS: OPERATORS OF AUCTION SALES AND BROKERS.
At the time of inspection, there were no records of acquisition for two regulated animals, a Fennec fox and a
Patagonian cavy. Every dealer must make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose
information concerning the acquisition of animals for sale by the facility; name and address of the person and/or
entity who consigned/sold the animal to the facility, date of acquisition, USDA license number (if licensed with
USDA) of the person/entity who sold the animals to the facility, and specific description of the animals. The facility
must obtain the required animal acquisition information, and must have it available for USDA inspection at all times.
Correct by: April 16, 2018
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
