2.134

Contingency planning.
At the time of inspection, the licensee does not have a written contingency/ emergency plan in place. A written plan shall
be developed and maintained to provide for the humane handling, treatment, transportation, housing and care of their
animals in the event of an emergency or disaster. The plan shall address the potential situations, response, personnel
responsible for the actions to be taken, chain of command, etc., as required in Section 2.134 of the animal welfare
regulations.
