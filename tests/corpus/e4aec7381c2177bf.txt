ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The refrigerator contained an outdated bottle of penicillin, electrolytes and tetracycline .Expired medications may no
longer be effective in the treatment of diseases and conditions. Adequate veterinary care includes the use of in date
medications. All outdated medications must be discarded or marked expired and stored separately to ensure that
adequate veterinary care is provided. All the above described medications were discarded at time of the inspection .
