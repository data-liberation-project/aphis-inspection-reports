2.40(b)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
One bottle of Uniprim was found to be expired at the time of inspection but was not currently in use. The use of
expired medications is not considered appropriate to comply with the provisions of the Animal Welfare Act (AWA)
as outlined in the above noted section of 9 Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). Expired medications may loose
their effectiveness or become harmful to the animals. Correct by assuring all medications are discarded or replaced
so that they can not be used on the animals. To be corrected by 6 January 2016
3.25(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
An open bag of food was present in the facility next to the enclosed food container. The owner stated that this was
what did not fit in the storage container. Leaving food in open bags without further protection can lead to
deterioration of the nutrient value and possible contamination by pests and the environment. Correct by assuring
food supplies are stored in containers with tightly fitting lids or covers when no longer sealed in the original
containers as received from the commercial sources of supply. To be corrected by: 6 January 2017
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
