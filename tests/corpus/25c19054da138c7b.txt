2.40(b)(3)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).

the animals were weak and covered with ticks. The deaths were communicated to the attending veterinarian

All

3.125(d)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
According to the licensee 3 dead kudu and 1 dead gemsbok were disposed of in an unapproved watershed area.
This type of disposal could facilitate spread of disease. Disposal of dead animals must comply with applicable
Federal, State, and local laws. All future animal carcass disposals must occur in approved areas.
To be corrected immediately.
