2.131(c)(1)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
The gate at the entrance to the armadillo and African crested porcupine exhibit is low enough that a member of the
public would be able to reach and contact the animals. The porcupine was laying next to the door at time of the
inspection, and I was able to easily reach over the door and touch the animal. Unsupervised public contact with
these animals may result in injury to the animals or to the public Sufficient distance and or barriers must be present
during public exhibition to assure the safety of the animals and viewing public. CORRECT BY : 7/31/19
The inspection and exit briefing was conducted with the husbandry manager.
