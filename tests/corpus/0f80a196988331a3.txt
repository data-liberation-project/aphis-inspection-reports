2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The last semi-annual report to the Institutional Official was not signed by a majority of the IACUC members as
required by the regulations. In order to assure a fully functioning and participatory committee and meet regulatory
requirements, all future reports to the IO must be signed by a majority of committee members.
An exit interview was conducted with facility representatives.
