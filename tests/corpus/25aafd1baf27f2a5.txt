2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
At time of inspection, the licensee could not provide a written program of veterinary care. Formal arrangements with an
attending veterinarian , that include a written program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises,
are important to ensure animal health. The licensee shall employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements to
include a written program of veterinary care. Licensee has been provided with a program of veterinary care.

To be corrected by: 6/17/2021
2.75(b)(2)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The licensee could not provide a form or documentation of inventory on hand. Keeping an inventory of animals on hand
aid in animal/herd health management. The licensee shall implement a regimen to keep an accurate record of animals on
hand. The licensee has been provided with a 7019 form.

To be corrected on 6/4/2021
2.76

Records: Operators of auction sales and brokers.

The licensee could not provide records or forms for animals that were sold/dispersed of or acquired. Keeping records or
forms of disposition and acquisition are important tools to keep track of inventory. The licensee shall implement a record
keeping regimen to include the acquisition and disposition of all USDA regulated animals.

To be corrected: 6/4/2021
