2.12.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
On March 21, 2021 Lisa Lopez, doing business as, “All Things Wild” was confirmed to be conducting regulated activity as
“The Royal White Tiger Discovery” at the “Faux Real Trade Days” event at the Trinity Valley Exposition Fairgrounds in
Liberty, TX. Two white tigers were being exhibited for viewing and photo opportunities.
Lisa Lopez and All Things Wild does not have an active USDA license to exhibit. No person shall operate as a dealer,
exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, without a valid license. All Things Wild must cease and desist from conducting
regulated activity without a license.

This inspection was conducted with a facility representative.
