3.131(d)
SANITATION.
Section 3.131(d) Pest control: During this inspection a groundhog was observed in the feeder for the enclosure
housing 10 ibex. Groundhogs may be a source of bacteria and/or parasites that could cause the ibex to become ill.
There must be a safe and effective program for the control of groundhogs to maintain the health of the animals.
Correct by July 14, 2014.
An exit briefing was conducted with the caretaker.
