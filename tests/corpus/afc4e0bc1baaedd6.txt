2.126(c)

Access and inspection of records and property; submission of itineraries.
The licensee did not submit a written itinerary for events involving overnight stays from May - November 2021.
Itineraries enable effective tracking of animal movement, biosecurity monitoring, and permit inspections to be conducted
in accordance with the AWA when animals are away from the home site.
Traveling exhibitors must submit an itinerary to APHIS no fewer than 2 days in advance of any travel and shall contain
complete and accurate information concerning the whereabouts of any animal intended for exhibition at any location other
than the person's approved site.
