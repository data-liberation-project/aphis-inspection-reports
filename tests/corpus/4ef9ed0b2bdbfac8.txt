2.38(b)
MISCELLANEOUS.
There was no one available during business hours to allow APHIS Officials to enter and inspect the facilities,
property, records, and animals on05-OCT-17 at 11:37 AM.
At 11:37 AM on October 5, 2017, APHIS inspector arrived at the facility to conduct an unannounced inspection.
After knocking on the door and receiving no response, the inspector called the phone number provided by the
facility and spoke with the licensee. He informed the inspector that he was not at the facility and unable to return for
an inspection. APHIS inspector notified the licensee that this would be considered an attempted inspection.
