2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
There were multiple drugs (inject-able, topical, tablets, etc.) that were expired from 2011 to 2016.
In order to assure proper treatment of animals drugs to be used must not be expired.
A program of veterinary care was not available for inspection at the time of inspection.
Exhibitors shall establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that includes the use of appropriate
methods to treat diseases and injuries as recommended by the attending veterinarian.
To be corrected by 7-12-2017.
3.125
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The vertical pole of the small mammal enclosure (Skunk, Opossum), were rusted at the bottom where it comes in
contact with the concrete floor. The rust was flaky and there was complete gap in some poles which cross the whole
diameter of the pole affecting the structural strength of the enclosure.
The wood fence of the deer exhibit was in disrepair and had broken wood that could entrap the animals.
Housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the animals from
injury and to contain the animals.
To be corrected by 7-30-2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
