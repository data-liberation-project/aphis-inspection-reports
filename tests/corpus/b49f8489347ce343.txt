WATERING.
The pond that functions as the source of drinking water for the white tail deer has frozen over and no other source
of drinking water has been provided. Lack of drinking water does not ensure for the health and comfort of the deer.
Provide an alternate source of drinking water for the deer. Corrected at the time of inspection.
This inspection was a focused inspection of the white tail deer.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the facility representative.
End of report.
