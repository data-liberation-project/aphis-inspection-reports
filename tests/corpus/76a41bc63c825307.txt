Date: 26-APR-2021

3.56(d)

Sanitation.
There was an excessive accumulation of rodent feces on the surface of a work counter located in the garage above rabbit
cages containing nine rabbits.

Rodent droppings can harbor viruses and bacteria harmful to other mammals, including humans. The droppings and wild
mice may potentially contaminate the rabbits and/or their cages located on the floor below the counter.

An effective program for the control of insects, ectoparasites, and avian and mammalian pests shall be established and
maintained.

Correct by May 3, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with licensee.
