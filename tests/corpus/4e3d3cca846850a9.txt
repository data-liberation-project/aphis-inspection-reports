2.126(a)(2) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
During inspection, the facility representative was unable to locate the facility records, including the written Program
of Veterinary Care (PVC) and the Record of Acquisition of Dogs and Cats form. These records are required to be
kept for examination by APHIS officials during business hours for the necessary enforcement of regulations.
An exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
