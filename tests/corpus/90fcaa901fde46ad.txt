3.130 REPEAT
WATERING.
The water receptacles in the large cat enclosures, the black Leopard and the Cougar, were green in color. Green
water indicates that the receptacles are not cleaned or sanitized often enough. Fresh potable water must be
accessible to the animals as often as needed for the health and comfort of the animal. Licensee shall implement a
proactive regimen to clean and sanitize the water receptacles as often as necessary so as to provide potable water.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
