3.75(e)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The lids of the galvanized food storage bins were rusted, impacting the ability to protect the food within them.
Supplies of food must be stored in a manner which protects the supplies from contamination and vermin infestation.
Adulteration of food can cause disease or injury in the animals.
The food must be protected by either repair or replacement of the existing affected food storage bins.
Correct by 10/21/2017.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with a facility representative.
