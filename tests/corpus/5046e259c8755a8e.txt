3.25(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Six Guinea pigs were noted loose on the floor but contained within the elevated building. The enclosures within the
facility must be constructed to contain the animals. Loose animals may pick up and transmit disease to the other
animals. Correct by: 24 November 2014

FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The feed bin in the facility did not have a tight fitting lid as required by the regulations. Feed and bedding were
exposed at the time of inspection which could lead to contamination or deterioration that would adversely affect the
animals. Correct by: 24 November 2014
