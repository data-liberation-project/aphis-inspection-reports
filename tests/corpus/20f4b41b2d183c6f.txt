INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Review of Protocol #1328: The protocol does not contain a complete description of the use of rabbits for the
parasite colony maintenance procedure.
Per this Section of the Regulations, a proposal to conduct an activity involving animals must contain a complete
description of the proposed use of the animals in order for the IACUC to review the proposal to determine that the
proposed activities are in accordance with all of the requirements of the AWA.
The IACUC needs to address this issue identified for this protocol.
