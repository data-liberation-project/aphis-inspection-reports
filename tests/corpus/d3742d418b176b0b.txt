3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
At the time of inspection, a prairie dog was kept in wooden enclosure approximately 6 feet by 6 feet. The wood was
untreated and water from the animal's water source was noticed dripping and seeping into the bottom floor of the
enclosure. The material used for the animal's primary enclosure was not treated with paint or other sealant material.
Interior structures for animals must be impervious to moisture and be able to be properly sanitized.
Correct by: April 23, 2018
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility manager.
