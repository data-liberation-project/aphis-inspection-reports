2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The facility manager admits to currently exhibiting animals to the public without a valid USDA license. Any person
operating or intending to operate as an exhibitor must have a valid license. The facility manager must apply for a
USDA exhibitor's license.
No covered activities may be conducted until an USDA license is obtained.
An application packet will be mailed to the facility.
An exit interview was conducted with APHIS personnel, Dr. Simone Tomlinson, VMO and the facility manager.
