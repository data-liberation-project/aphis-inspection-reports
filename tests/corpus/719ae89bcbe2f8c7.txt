REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Sugar gliders and hedgehogs were on display and offered for sale in this pet store. The exhibition and/or sale of
these regulated species requires a valid USDA license. No USDA license has been issued to cover regulated
activities at this store.
The owner indicated willingness to comply with the regulations and standards under the Animal Welfare Act.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED WITHOUT A VALID USDA LICENSE.
