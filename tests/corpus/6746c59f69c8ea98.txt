2.31(c)(1)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC failed to conduct the April 2020 and October 2020 review of the program for humane care and use of animals.
The IACUC should conduct a review of the animal care and use program at least every 6 months to ensure established
policies and processes have been implemented and that such are in compliance with the Animal Welfare Act standards
and regulations. The IACUC must conduct a program review to ensure animals are used humanely and receive adequate
care. Correct by November 30, 2020.
2.31(c)(2)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
Two representatives of the IACUC conducted the semi-annual facility inspection on April 24, 2020 but the IACUC did not
meet to review the inspection findings. Also, The October 2020 semi-annual facility inspection was not conducted. A semi-
annual facility inspection should be conducted every 6 months to ensure facilities and animals areas are maintained in
accordance with established facility policies and the Animal Welfare Act. The IACUC must conducted a facility inspection
in accordance with the regulations to provide assurance of the welfare of the animals. Correct by November 30, 2020.

An IACUC meeting is scheduled for November 20, 2020. This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the
IACUC Representative.
