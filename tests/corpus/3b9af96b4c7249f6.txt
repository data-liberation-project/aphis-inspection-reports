3.131(c)
SANITATION.
There are large piles of discarded materials and cages in close proximity to the enclosures housing the pigs , coati
mundi, sheep and goats . Hay string is on the ground in the sheep /goat enclosures and one goat with her young
kid are laying in a pile of old metal cages. Trash piles serve as habitats for pests that may harm the health and well
being of the animals . Hay string may be ingested or become tangled on an animals body. The goat and her kid are
at risk for injury by laying in the pile of old cages. All discarded materials , cages and hay string must be removed
from close proximity to animal housing areas. The premise must be maintained in a clean condition to protect the
animals from injury .
