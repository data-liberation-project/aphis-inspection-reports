3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The inside holding area in the moat exhibits, which includes mongoose, porcupine, bat eared fox and meerkats and
the inside holding for the tiger has peeling and chipped paint.
The indoor and outdoor housing facilities must be maintained in good repair.
Repair or replace the peeling and chipping paint to protect the animals from injury.

3.129(a)
FEEDING.
The refrigerators in the discovery building have excessive dirt and food wast in them. The refrigerator in the Bongo
holding has rust in it.
Food shall be wholesome, palatable and free from contamination to maintain the animals in good health.
Clean the refrigerators and freezers as often as necessary to prevent contamination of the food for the health of the
animals. Replace the refrigerator in Bongo holding so that it can be properly cleaned and sanitized.
To be corrected by July 1, 2016.

FEEDING.

SANITATION.
