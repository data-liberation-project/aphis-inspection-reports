2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The written program of veterianry care lacks sufficient detail. The ectoparasite control program states that the
animal will be monitored monthly and treated as required, The treatment for ectoparasites must include the drug
name, dosage, route of administration and frequency so that licenesee can treat animals appropriately. The
licensee must establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that include the appropriate method to
prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries.The program of veterinary care must be complete to
provide adequate guidance to the licensee to maintain the health and well-being of the animals. All medications
used on the guinea pigs must be approved by the attending veterinarian.
