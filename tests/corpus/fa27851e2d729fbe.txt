3.127(d)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
There is no perimeter fence surrounding the 2 fox enclosures. To protect the animals and the public, a perimeter
fence must be contructed which restricts other animals and unauthorized persons from going through it or under it
and having contact with the foxes. It must be a minimum of 6 feet tall and at least 3 feet from the primary enclosure
fencing. Correct by: November 22, 2014
An exit interview was conducted with the licensee.
