SANITATION.
THE PREMISES SHALL BE KEPT CLEAN TO PROTECT ANIMALS FROM INJURY. THE ENCLOSURE
HOUSING GOATS HAS A LARGE AREA IN THE MIDDLE OF THE PEN WITH HALF BURIED ROUND BALE
STRING. AN ANIMAL COULD BECOME ENTANGLED IN THE STRING AND BECOME INJURED. THIS STRING
NEEDS TO BE REMOVED FROM THE ENCLOSURE.
