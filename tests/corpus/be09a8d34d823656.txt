2.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
A petting zoo with exotic mammals was observed at the Heart of Texas Fair, Waco, Texas on 10/11/2022. The owner
stated he had applied for a license but has not yet had a pre license inspection but had already scheduled exhibitions with
the petting zoo .
No person shall operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, without a valid license. Conducting
regulated activity without a license is a violation of the Animal Welfare Act.
No regulated activity may be conducted until a USDA license is obtained.

This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the petting zoo owner.
