3.127(d)

Facilities, outdoor.
The facility perimeter fence is not fully constructed leaving a section of the facility exposed. All non-dangerous animals
housed in an outdoor facility must be enclosed by an appropriate perimeter fence. The facility must finish construction of
a perimeter fence to serve as secondary containment for the animals, and prevent access by unauthorized persons and
animals.
Correct by 24 March 2022

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with licensee.
