2.40(b)(2) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The facility is using a product called Permectirn II for fly control on the dogs. This product is not documented on the
program of veterinary care for use against ectoparasites. The licensee does not have any other directions for it's
dilution and use in dogs that have been approved by the attending veterinarian. In addition, while the licensee is
using vaccines and deworming medication in puppies, the program of veterinary care does not have a documented
plan for vaccinations or use of deworming medications in puppies. The use of medications and vaccines without
directions from the attending veterinarian can lead to incorrect use and unintended side effects.The licensee must
ensure that all medications are used in a manner consistent with the provision of adequate veterinary care to protect
the health of the animals and ensure drugs are used in an appropriate manner. The applicant must ensure that all
medication used at the facility have directions for correct use from a veterinarian. To be corrected by 1 January
2015.
3.1(c)(2)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
There are three "igloo-type" shelter structures in that have chewed edges. The chewed areas on these structures
are deeply pitted and with dirt in the pitted areas. The chewed edges are difficult to sanitize and could play a role in
disease transmission. The licensee must maintain all surfaces on a regular basis. Surfaces of housing facilities,
including houses and dens, that cannot be readily cleaned and sanitized, must be replaced when worn or soiled. To
be corrected by 1 Jan 2015.
