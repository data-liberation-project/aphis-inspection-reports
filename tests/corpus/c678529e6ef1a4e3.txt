INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
**Protocol #419971-9 describes utilizing analgesics and anesthetics in order to facilitate performing various health
assessments of the animals. Although the protocol was categorized as having the potential for more than
momentary pain and/or distress, no alternative search was conducted by the prinicipal investigator. The principal
investigator must provide a written narrative indicating an alternative search to the potentially painful and/or
distressful procedures was conducted. It is the responsibility of the IACUC to ensure that the principal investigator
has considered alternatives to potentially painful and/or distressful procedures as set forth in this section.
