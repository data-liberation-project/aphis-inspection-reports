3.127(a)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
At the time of this inspection the llama, nilgai, and older camel where being housed in areas without access to
shade. The temperature and humidity at the time were approximately 80 F and 84% humidity. Animals must have
access to shade at all times when sunlight is likely to cause discomfort or overheating.
To be corrected immediately.
3.127(c)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
The pen the older camel was being housed in was all mud. The animal had no areas of dry ground to escape the
mud. Animals must have access to shelter and dry ground at all times whether they choose to use it or not.
To be corrected immediately.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with the licensee.
