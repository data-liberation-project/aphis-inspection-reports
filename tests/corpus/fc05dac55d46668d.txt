FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Item Facilities General 3.125 a
At the time of inspection it was noticed that there were several exhibits that had areas of exhibit fencing that need to
be repaired.
The zebra exhibit which houses 2 zebras and a goat, has areas of bowed,bent, and broken fencing that needs to
be needs to be repaired.
The Alpaca exhibit has a space running the length of the exhibit fence that permits the alpacas to get their heads
thru the fence .
Exhibit fencing that is bent, broken or has gaps does not adequately protect the animal from injury.
Facility needs to insure exhibits areas are structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals
from injury.
The inspection and exit interview were conducted with the VP.
