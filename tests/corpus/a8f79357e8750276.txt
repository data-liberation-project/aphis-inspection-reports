3.53(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Section 3.53(a)(1) General: The edges of the wire mesh floor for the enclosure housing the tan rabbit are pulling up
away from the wood supports. Some of the wire is broken at the edges of the floor and wire strands are protruding
up into the enclosure. The rabbit may be injured by the ends of the wire strands and the floor is not structurally
sound. The wire floor must be repaired or replaced to protect the rabbit from injury. When the condition of the floor
of the rabbit enclosure was identified by the APHIS official, facility personnel replaced the floor during the
inspection. Corrected during the inspection.
An exit briefing was conducted with the owner.
