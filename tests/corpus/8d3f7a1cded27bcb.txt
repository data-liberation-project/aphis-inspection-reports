2.131(c)(1)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
A child was injured by a camel at the zoo on 9-21-15. During an allowed feeding, the camel placed its head over
the barrier and bit the child(cid:25)s head. Sufficient distance and/or barriers must be present to assure the safety of the
animals and the public.
Assure that all allowed animal feedings are conducted with sufficient distance and/or barriers between the animals
and the public.
To be corrected: From this day forward.
Note: This was a focused inspection.
The inspection exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
