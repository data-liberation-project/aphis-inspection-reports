2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The program of veterinary care is not available today.
Each exhibitor shall employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a part-time
attending veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written program of
veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the dealer or exhibitor;
The program of veterinary must be available for inspection to ensure all animals are covered and cared for by an
attending veterinarian.
3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
There is a fallow deer that is housed by itself that has parts of its primary enclosure in disrepair. The fencing is bent
under at the bottom in several areas leaving a gap at the bottom.
The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to
protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
Repair the primary enclosure fence to properly contain the deer, prevent predators from entering and keep the deer
safe from injury.
