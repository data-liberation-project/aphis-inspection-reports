3.55
WATERING.
Most of the water bottles for 22 rabbits had varying amounts of mold adhered to the inside of the bottles.
Water bottles should be sanitized when dirty to prevent health issues.
Correct by the end of the day of inspection.
3.56(a)
SANITATION.
Two rabbit enclosures contained excessive amounts of excreta on the enclosure flooring. The tray under one
enclosure also contained an excessive amount of excreta.
Rabbit enclosures should be cleaned as often as necessary to prevent a buildup of excreta that cannot be avoided
by the animal.
Correct by the end of the day of inspection.
An exit briefing was conducted with a facility representative.
