PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
(a) General requirements.(2) (i) Have no sharps
****
Inside an outdoor shelter, affecting 2 dogs, there was a hanging heat lamp with a metal reflector that the dogs have
bent creating a sharp edge and point. Sharp points or edges could cause injury the dogs. The licensee must repair
or remove the metal reflector and ensure that all enclosures are free of sharp points and edges. Licensee corrected
after this was pointed out by the inspector.
To be corrected by: May 15, 2016
Inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative, ACI Stephanie Osborne and ACI Chad
Proctor.
