3.125(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
A large bag of feed additive used as a supplement for alpacas was left open on a counter in the kitchen leaving it
unprotected from vermin or other pests.
Supplies of food must be stored in facilities which adequately protect such supplies against deterioration, molding,
or contamination by vermin to ensure the health and well-being of all animals. A system of food storage which
adequately protects the supplies of food for all animals must be in place.
