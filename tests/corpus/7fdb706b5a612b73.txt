3.25(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
During today's inspection there was one guinea pig that was noticed running loose within the facility. The
Licensee's wife stated they she had noticed it the previous day but was unable to catch it at that time. Loose guinea
pigs can escape the facility all together or get hurt or injured while they are running loose. In addition, they could
get hurt or injured by falling/jumping to the ground from the enclosure. Primary enclosures shall be constructed and
maintained so that the guinea pigs contained and not able get out of it's enclosure.
Correct By: 9/1/2019
3.29(d)
FEEDING.
On today's inspection around 75% of the self feeders had rust around the lip where the guinea pigs access their
food. Areas with rust can not be properly cleaned or sanitized. All food receptacles shall be kept in a manner and
constructed of a material that allows them to be clean and shall be sanitized at least once every 2 weeks. All
feeders with rust must be repaired, resurfaced or replaced to allow proper cleaning and sanitation.
Correct By: 9/1/2019
