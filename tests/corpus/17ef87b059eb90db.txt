2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 12:00 pm on
18-Oct-16. The inspector called the contact number for the license holder, honked the horn, and waited at the
facility gate for approximately 30 minutes. The license holder later called back indicating that she was out of town.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
