Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The written program of veterinary care indicates the attending veterinarian will conduct visits annually. The attending
veterinarian last visited 30NOV2020. Regular visits by the attending veterinarian help assure optimal animal welfare is
attained. Licensee must ensure regularly scheduled visits by the attending veterinarian are completed.

This is a 1st re-license inspection for a class "A" license. Inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the applicant.

All items must be in compliance within 2 more inspections or by 26MAR2022 or the applicant will forfeit the license fee
and must wait 6 months to re-apply. Please contact your inspector to schedule your next re-license inspection.

Regulated activity can continue under your existing USDA license until it expires on 27JAN2022.
