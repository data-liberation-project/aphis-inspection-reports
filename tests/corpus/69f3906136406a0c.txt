2.131(c)(1)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
The exhibit housing the cavies and the muntjac has no public barrier. There is concern the public could contact the
animals thru the fence . A public barrier to restrict access between the animals and the public must be present at all
exhibits with covered species to assure the safety of the public and the animals.
