INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Section 2.31(d)(1)(iii) IACUC: The two animal proposals (protocols) using small animals for teaching purposes do
not have written assurance provided by their principal investigator that the activities do not unnecessarily duplicate
previous experiments. The principal investigator must provide written assurance that the animal activities in the
protocols do not unnecessarily duplicate previous experiments to comply with the animal welfare regulations and to
help prevent the unnecessary use of animals. Correct by September 15, 2016.
