2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The licensee employs a part-time Attending Veterinarian who has visited the premises and provides the veterinary care
for the domestic farm species, but there is no written Program of Veterinary Care.

Each exhibitor shall employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a part-time attending
veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written program of veterinary care. The
licensee needs a written program of veterinary care as part of the formal arrangements with the Attending Veterinarian.
