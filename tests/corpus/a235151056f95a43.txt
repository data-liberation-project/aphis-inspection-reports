2.40(b)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
There are outdated medications ( Liquimycin, Excede and B Vitamins ) in the container with other in date supplies.
Expired medications may lose their effectiveness when used past the expiration date. An adequate and effective
program of veterinary care includes the use of effective medications . Expired medications must be removed and
not used for treatment of covered species.
