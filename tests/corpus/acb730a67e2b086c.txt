3.31(a)(1) REPEAT
SANITATION.
There is a buildup of brown roughened scale and debris on the floors and plastic sides of enclosures. Licensee
stated that the guinea pig enclosures are being sanitized weekly. Additional measures are needed to remove
organic matter, scale, and debris on floors and sides of enclosures prior to using a disinfectant. The accumulation of
brown scale and debris exposes the guinea pigs to potential infections with bacteria, fungi, and parasites. Correct
by ensuring that primary enclosures are clean and sanitized often enough to prevent and accumulation of scale and
debris. Additionally, ensure that primary enclosures are sanitized at least once very two weeks.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
