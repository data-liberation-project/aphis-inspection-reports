2.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
This facility is a breeder of dogs. The facility representative acknowledged sales via websites with some dogs being sold
not face-to-face, which is a regulated activity for which a license is required under the Animal Welfare Act (AWA).

Being licensed is necessary for USDA Animal Care to assure compliance with the AWA and the humane care of animals
covered by these regulations.

The facility must apply for and become licensed before any future regulated activity.
Correct by: The application for license must be submitted to Animal Care by 31 December 2021.

No regulated activities may be conducted until a valid USDA license is obtained.
