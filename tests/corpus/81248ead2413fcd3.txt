3.1(c)(3)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
*The dog door in an sheltered enclosure (containing 1 dog) had a build up of dirt and grime. The sheltered building
has two outdoor portions with a build up of old, dried and fresh feces (affecting 2 dogs total). Three outdoor
enclosures (containing three dogs total) have a build up of dried and fresh feces.These same enclosures also have
cobwebs between the fencing and the doghouses within. This type of waste, dirt and grime build up can soil the
dogs, increase opportunity for pests and disease organisms. Licensee must ensure that all hard surfaces in contact
with the dogs are spot-cleaned daily and sanitized in accordance to Sec. 3.11 to prevent any accumulations of dirt,
grime or feces to minimize/delete soiling of the dogs and disease hazards.
