2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
In the north building in the refrigerator with other ready-to-use vaccinations were a pack of expired Rabies
vaccinations (expiration date: September 4, 2014) and Distemper vaccinations (expiration date: June 5, 2015). The
licensee stated that he had recently used the rabies vaccination on his "hound dogs".
In the cabinet storing ready-to-use medications in the north building there were several expired or unlabeled
medications. There was a bottle of "Albon" with an expiration date of July 2015, a bottle of "metronidazole" with the
expiration date scraped off, and several small bottles of unknown medications with unknown expiration dates.
The use of expired medications and vaccinations may be ineffective and cause unintended reactions and results.
The licensee must not use expired medications and vaccinations, and must use appropriate methods prevent,
control, diagnose and treat diseases and injuries.
