3.13(a)(2)

Veterinary care for dogs.
During inspection, there were no records available from the licensee showing each regulated adult dog had received a
complete physical exam by a licensed veterinarian during the past twelve months.

Annual physical examination of each adult dog by a licensed veterinarian is important in helping to detect potential issues
affecting animal health.

All regulated adult dogs must receive an annual physical exam conducted by a licensed veterinarian and findings from
each annual exam documented in writing.

Correct by 02 MARCH 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
