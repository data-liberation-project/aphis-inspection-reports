2.31(c)(3) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
**A report of the semi annual evaluations of the facility in addition to the animal care and use program had not been
submitted to the Institutional Official. This does not allow the Institutional Official to be kept informed on IACUC
functions at the frequency required by the regulations. Prepare and submit reports of the facility and humane care
and use program evaluations to the Institutional Official at least once every 6 months.
Inspection and exit briefing conducted with the Institutional Official.
