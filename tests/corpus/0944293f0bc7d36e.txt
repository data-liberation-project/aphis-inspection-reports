Primary enclosures.

Veterinary care for dogs.

The written program of veterinary care (PVC) is not signed by the attending veterinarian (AV). Lack of the AV's signature
does not ensure that the AV is in agreement that appropriate veterinary care is being provided to the dogs. The PVC
must be signed by the AV.
