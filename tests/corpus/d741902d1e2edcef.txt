2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
A written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) was not available for inspection. In order to comply with this section of
the Animal Welfare Act (AWA) a written PVC must be in place, and available to APHIS inspectors upon request.
The facility must identify and employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements, who could write a PVC
plan which would include regularly scheduled visits to the premises where the animals are kept. The attending
veterinarian should conduct at a minimum an annual visit, to ensure adequate veterinary medical care to animals.
Correct by: March 3, 2020
3.81
ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
An appropriate plan for environment enhancement adequate to promote the psychological well-being of nonhuman
primates was not available for inspection; plan should be available to APHIS inspectors upon request. The plan
must be in accordance with the currently accepted professional standards, and as directed by the attending
veterinarian.
Correct by: March 3, 2020
