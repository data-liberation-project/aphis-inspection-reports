2.132(d)
PROCUREMENT OF DOGS, CATS, AND OTHER ANIMALS; DEALERS.
Licensee purchased over $500 worth of rabbits from at least one unlicensed dealer in the current calendar year. No
dealer shall knowingly obtain any dog, cat, or other animal from any person who is required to be licensed, but does
not hold a current valid USDA license. Licensee must not purchase animals from unlicensed breeders or dealers
who sell more than $500 in gross income from the sale of animals during any calendar year. Correct by October 25,
2016.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
