2.25(a)
REQUIREMENTS AND PROCEDURES.
Air carrier was found to be operating by transporting regulated animals with a cancelled certificate at the MIA site
since March 29, 2007. Aircraft belonging to the registrant have been transporting regulated animals using another
carrier's registration certificate. In accordance with the Animal Welfare Act, Part 2, Subpart B, each carrier shall be
registered with the Secretary of the US Department of Agriculture (USDA) and list all business locations. The air
carrier should add the MIA site as a place of business by contacting the USDA's Animal Care Regional Director in
Raleigh, North Carolina. The inspector provided all needed information for adding the new site to the facility
representative.
