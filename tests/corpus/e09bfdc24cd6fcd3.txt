2.31(c)(1)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC completed a review of the facility's program for humane care and use of animals only once between April 3,
2019 and May 13, 2021. This Review was conducted on June 18th, 2020 and is a period longer than six months from
either date.

The IACUC is responsible for ensuring that the research facility's program uses and cares for animals in a humane
manner according to the Animal Welfare Act. An IACUC that does not meet to review the program in a timely manner
does not fulfill these responsibilities. The IACUC must review the research facility's program for humane care and use of
animals at least once every six months.

Correct from this day forward.
2.31(c)(2)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC completed a review of the facility's animal facilities only once between April 3, 2019 and May 13, 2021. This
review was conducted on June 18th, 2020 and is a period longer than six months from either date.



The IACUC is responsible for ensuring that the animal facilities, including the animal study areas are appropriate for the
species being held and meet all of the requirements of the Animal Welfare Act. An IACUC that does not meet to review
the animal facilities in a timely manner does not fulfill these responsibilities. The IACUC must review the research facility's
animal facilities at least once every six months.

Correct from this day forward.
