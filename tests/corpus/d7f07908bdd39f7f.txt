3.1(c)(3)

Housing facilities, general.
** The whelping building's general area surfaces have a buildup of hair, dirt and grime. The main building has dog shelters
in the enclosures that have a buildup of dirt and grime on the inside and outside surfaces. The housing facilities must be
cleaned and sanitized to remove the organic buildup. All surfaces in contact with animals must be spot cleaned daily and
sanitized at least every two weeks, all other surfaces must be cleaned and sanitized on a regular basis to avoid a buildup
of organic material, prevent odors, pest and disease hazards. To be corrected by: 01-15-2023.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
