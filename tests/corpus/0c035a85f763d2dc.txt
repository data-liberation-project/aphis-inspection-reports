Sanitation.

currently cleaning enclosures once every two weeks. The cleaning process (method and frequency) utilized is not
effective in removing accumulations of wet and soiled bedding. Ineffective cleaning and decreases in the cleaning
frequency can expose the guinea pigs to disease and health risks. Correct by ensuring that primary enclosures are
cleaned often enough to prevent accumulations of wet and soiled bedding. Correct by December 9, 2021.
