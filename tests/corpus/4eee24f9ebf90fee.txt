2.40(a)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
No Program of Veterinary Care was completed at the time of the inspection.The applicant should have written
guidance from the AV regarding preventive care, treatment for routine and common veterinary problems and
emergency care for all animals intended for exhibition. The form should be signed by the AV to ensure his/her
approval of the program.
This first pre-license inspection and exit briefing for a Class C License was conducted with the applicant.
All items must be in compliance within 2 more inspection(s) or by 90 days or the applicant will forfeit the application
fee and must wait 6 months to reapply.
Conducting regulated activities without a valid USDA license is a violation of the Animal Welfare Act.
