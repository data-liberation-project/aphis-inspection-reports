3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
There are accumulations of excreta and soiled bedding in guinea pig enclosures. Licensee stated that enclosures
are cleaned about every 2 weeks. Accumulations of excreta and soiled bedding exposes the guinea pigs to potential
infections with bacteria, fungi, and parasites. Correct by ensuring that all primary enclosures are cleaned and
sanitized often enough to prevent an accumulation of excreta, soiled bedding and debris, and that enclosures are
sanitized at least once every 2 weeks. Correct by January 11, 2018.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
