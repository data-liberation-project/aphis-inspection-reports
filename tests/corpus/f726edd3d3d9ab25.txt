FEEDING.
(b) ***** At time of inspection the self feeders throughout the kennel did not have lids on the portion of the feeder
keeping the surplus feed in storage until it flows to the bottom of the feeder for consumption by the dogs. This
allows for the potential of this food becoming contaminated from the fluids used in the day to day cleaning of the
pens as well as allowing access to this feed to pests. Lids need to be put onto these self feeders to prevent
contamination of the feed which can affect the health and well being of the animals in the kennel.
