Sanitation.
The outside of the rabbit cages and the cage racks in the back area have accumulated a brown sticky substance
appearing to be from flies. The sticky substance attracts pests which can raise the risk of disease hazard. The cages and
racks shall be fully cleaned and sanitized as required in section 3.56 to promote the health and well being of the rabbits.

3.131(d)

Sanitation.
There are swarms of gnats or flies surrounding the sugar glider enclosures and in the kitchen area. Swarms of flying
insects may cause discomfort for the sugar gliders and accumulations of flying insects in areas where food is being
prepared may cause contamination of the food. A safe a effective pest control program shall be developed to protect the
sugar gliders from discomfort and protect the food from contamination.
Correct by October 1, 2021.
