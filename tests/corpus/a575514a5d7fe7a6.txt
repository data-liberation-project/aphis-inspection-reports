2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
No available records of the birth of offspring or disposition of the offspring were available. Complete and accurate
information for the disposition of animals is required for tracking and tracing purposes for the health of the animal in
case of disease outbreak. The licensee shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly
disclose information concerning the disposition of all animals. The records shall include all offspring born of any
animal while in their possession or under their control. The Record of Animals on Hand (APHIS Form 7019) and
Record of Acquisition, Disposition, or Transport of Animals (APHIS Form 7020) are forms which may be used by the
licensee to keep and maintain the information required by federal regulations. As required by federal regulations,
the licensee needs to obtain/document the pertinent information on the missing transactions of the regulated
animals identified above and for all future animals. To be corrected from this date forward.
