SANITATION.
There were 12 enclosures housing a total of 100 adult guinea pigs with their offspring that had accumulations of
feces and dirty bedding. The enclosures were last cleaned 2 weeks ago.The accumulation of feces and dirty
bedding exposes the guinea pigs to potential infections with bacteria, fungi, and parasites. Correct by ensuring that
primary enclosures are cleaned and sanitized often enough to prevent an accumulation of feces, dirty bedding, and
debris. Additionally, ensure that the primary enclosures are sanitized at least once every two weeks. Correct by May
7, 2019.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
