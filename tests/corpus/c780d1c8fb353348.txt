3.25(c)

Facilities, general.
Storage. There were accumulations of spilled feed, shavings, and debris under the pallet for the guinea pig feed outside
the entrance to the animal room. Loose hay was scattered on the floor in the food storage area near the stored feed.

Supplies of food and bedding must be stored in areas that protect supplies against spoilage, deterioration, and
contamination from insects or rodents. Loose hay and spilled feed can attract insects and rodents. The licensee must
ensure that the food storage area is kept clean to prevent contamination of the feed by insects and vermin. Correct
August 7, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
