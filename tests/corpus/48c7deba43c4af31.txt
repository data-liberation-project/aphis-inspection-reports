REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This is a search inspection.

*The facility currently exhibits twelve elk as part of a seasonal Christmas Lights display without a valid USDA Animal
Welfare Act license. The facility advertises on websites and in on-line media that their facility will have live animals
on display and includes photos and video of the elk. The animals on exhibit are in their pens for the ticketed public
to view. The facility is open on weekends in Nov. and Dec. to the public on an entrance fee basis. The facility was
previously contacted in 2012 and 2013 and advised that a license was required to continue exhibiting the elk.
*A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations.
*The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities. An application packet was left with
the facility representative.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
