Watering
Eight adult dogs have 20 oz cans for watering receptacles. These containers are small and narrow and do not allow the
dogs to have water continuously available. The applicant was working on completing the continuous water system during
inspection. Ensure that if containers are used for water that they are large enough to provide continuous access to
potable water or ensure that the continuous water system functioning for all dogs.

Veterinary care for dogs.
