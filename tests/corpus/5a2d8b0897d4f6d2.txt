2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 13:00 on
11-SEP-18.
Inspector arrived at the facility at 13:00. Knocked on the main house door and the side door of the garage, there
was no answer. Inspector honked the car horn and called both numbers listed as contact numbers. One number
was unavailable to receive calls and the other rang 15 times and then asks for voicemail password. Inspector
redialed the numbers with the same results. Departed facility at 13:45 after honking the horn and attempting to call
both numbers once more prior to leaving. No contact was made.
