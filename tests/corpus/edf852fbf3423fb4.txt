3.55 REPEAT
WATERING.
Five rabbit hutches containing 12 rabbits had water bottles that had green scum growing on the interior of the bottle.
Rabbits must be provided with sufficient potable water daily. All water receptacles shall be sanitized when dirty and
at least every two weeks.
3.56(a) REPEAT
SANITATION.
Four rabbit enclosure containing nine rabbits have excessive amount of fecal material and fur built up in the bottom
of the cage. Unsanitary conditions can lead to disease or infestation of pests. Cages must be cleaned as needed
to prevent fecal and debris buildup. Cages should be sanitized at least once every 30 days.
