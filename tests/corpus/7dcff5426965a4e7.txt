2.40(a)(1) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
***The licensee uses a veterinarian under consulting arrangements to provide veterinary care for the animals but
does not have a formal agreement nor a written program of veterinary care. The licensee does not have written
guidance from the attending veterinarian to provide preventative care or treatment for routine and common
veterinary problems. In order to ensure problems of animal health are addressed adequately and timely, a written
program of veterinary care should be maintained and followed.
Each dealer shall employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a part-time attending
veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written program of veterinary care
and regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the dealer or exhibitor.
2.75(b)(1) REPEAT
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
***The licensee did not have records that contained the required information regarding the acquisition and
deposition of animals used or co-mingled with animals used in regulated activities. There were no records of
acquisition for 2 regulated steers. Records containing the required information for animals acquired for use in
regulated activities, including those offspring born on premise, need to be made and maintained in order to properly
track the animal numbers and use in those activities.
