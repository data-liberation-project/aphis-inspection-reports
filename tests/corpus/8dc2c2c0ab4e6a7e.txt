2.31(c)(1)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The facility IACUC usually does their semi-annual review of the program of humane care and use of animals in the spring
and fall of each year. It was completed on April 14, 2021 and again on May 19, 2022, but the IACUC was unable to
complete a review between these dates.

A review of the facilities program of humane care and use of animals by the IACUC, at least once every six months, is
critical in assuring that documented protocols and procedures, put in place to protect the health and welling of animals,
are bring followed and are effective and adequate.

The IACUC, as an agent of the research facility, shall review, at least once every six months, the research facility's
program for humane care and use of animals, using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A--Animal Welfare, as a basis for
evaluation.

To be corrected by: November 19, 2022
2.31(c)(2)



Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The facility IACUC usually does their semi-annual inspection of all of the facility's animal facilities in the spring and fall of
each year. It was completed on April 14, 2021 and again on May 19, 2022, but the IACUC was unable to complete an
inspection between these dates.

An inspection of all of the facility's animal facilities by the IACUC, at least once every six months, is critical in assuring that
these areas are safe, properly equipped, and in adequate condition to protect the health and welling of the animals.

The IACUC, as an agent of the research facility, shall Inspect, at least once every six months, all of the research facility's
animal facilities, including animal study areas, using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A-Animal Welfare, as a basis for
evaluation.
