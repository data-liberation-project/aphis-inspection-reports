3.53(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Several mesh-wire doors with sharps ends were identified at enclosures on barns A, B, M and N; wire was rusted
and deteriorated. Primary enclosures for rabbits shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair,
to protect the animals from injury, to contain the animals, and to restrict the entrance of other animals. The
deteriorated doors of all affected enclosures should be repaired or replaced to avoid potential injuries to rabbits.
