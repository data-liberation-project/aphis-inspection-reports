REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This facility is open to the public and allows patrons to tour, interact, & feed domesticated farm animals (reportedly
approximately: 3 bovines, 7 goats, & 20 pigs & their website also shows rabbits.) This is considered an exhibition of
regulated mammals which requires a USDA Exhibitor license.
You may not exhibit any regulated mammals (including, but not limited to: goats, pigs, bovines, & rabbits) without a
valid USDA Exhibitor license. A pre license packet was ordered and will be sent to you from the Raleigh Regional
Office. If you wish to continue to exhibit farms animals you must complete the application for license and submit it
with an application fee of $10 to the Raleigh Regional Office.
You must then pass a pre license inspection before a USDA license will be issued.
The exit interview was conducted on-site with the Farm Manager. The process for obtaining a USDA Exhibitor
license was discussed at that time.
