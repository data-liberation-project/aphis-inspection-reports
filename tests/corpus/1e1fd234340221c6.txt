2.40(a)(1) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) does not include all species covered by the Animal Welfare Act (AWA) that
are currently on the premises. To ensure appropriate care is provided for the animals, the PVC must be updated to
include all mammal species in the store except rats of the genus Rattus and mice of the genus Mus. Have the
Attending Veterinarian(s) update the PVC so that it describes a program of care for each AWA covered species on
the premises.
NOTE: This was a focused inspection on non-compliant items identified on the April 24, 2014 inspection report. The
non-compliant item cited under 2.40(b)(2) on the April 24, 2014 report has been corrected.

Exit interview conducted with facility representative.
