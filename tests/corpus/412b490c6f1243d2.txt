2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The written program of veterinary care was not available for review by USDA at time of the inspection . A written
program of veterinary care, signed by the attending veterinarian must be maintained at the facility and followed to
ensure adequate veterinary care is provided to the animals . It must be made available for review at time of the
inspection .
