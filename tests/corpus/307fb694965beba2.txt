2.1(a)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This is a search inspection. The facility is currently conducting regulated activity without a valid USDA license. The
facility admits to the shipment of puppies from the facility to the buyer of the puppy sight unseen. A valid USDA
license is necessary to protect the health and well being of the animals and to comply with the Animal Welfare Act.
This facility must obtain a USDA license.
No covered activities may be conducted until a USDA license is obtained.
An application packet will be mailed to the facility.
An exit interview was conducted with APHIS personnel, Dr. Simone Tomlinson, VMO and the facility manager.
