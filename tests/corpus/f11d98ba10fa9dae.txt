2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Farm animals are being exhibited at this facility named Saddlewood Horse Club, during birthday parties with the
public without a valid USDA license. There are numerous ads on the internet showing this facility is exhibiting farm
animals to the public for numberous years. The owner was unaware of needing a USDA license and will be
applying to obtain one. Until a USDA license is obtained, no USDA covered animals can be exhibited to the public.
Exit interview was conducted with the owner of Saddlewood Horse Club.
