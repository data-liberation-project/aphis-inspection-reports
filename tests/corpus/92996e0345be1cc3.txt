2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
Records of acquisition/disposition for the facility’s animals were unavailable or incomplete. Complete and accurate animal
records must be kept current to facilitate identification and tracking of animals as well as ensure their health and well-
being. Records must be maintained which fully and correctly disclose all the required information concerning all animals
purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, transported, sold, euthanized or otherwise disposed of and shall
include any offspring born while in the licensee’s possession.
