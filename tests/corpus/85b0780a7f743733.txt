3.130

Watering.
In the paddock that enclosed the rams and cull sheep from the flock the water receptacle was found to contain an
excessive amount of plant debri and algae. The water was a a brownish-green color, and the bottom of the receptacle
was not visible.

Failure to maintain water receptacles in clean and sanitary condition could be harmful to the well-being of the animals.

To be corrected by 11/25/2021, with clean water to be provided to the animals immediately.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
