2.38(f)(1) CRITICAL
MISCELLANEOUS.
On 7/24/18 a rabbit with ID # 71434 was being handled during a dermal scoring procedure when it jumped off the
table and fell five feet to the ground. It was immediately apparent that the animal had lost function of its right hind
limb. It was determined that the animal had a fractured leg.
Handling of all animals shall be done as expeditiously and carefully as possible in a manner that does not result in
injury. The facility should take steps immediately to ensure that all handling of animals is appropriate to safeguard
the animals from harm. To be corrected from this time forward.
3.53(a)(4) CRITICAL
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
On 5/1/18 a rabbit with ID # 70849 was observed to catch its front leg in the floor grate of its enclosure, and then
quickly turn its body, twisting the leg in a manner that caused a fracture in the forelimb.
The facility must ensure that the primary enclosures are suitable for the animals in order to safeguard the animals
from injury. The facility must provide appropriate flooring for all animals from this time forward.
This inspection was conducted on 11/13/18 and 11/15/18.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
