2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 1:45 PM on
15-Apr-19. Upon arrival at the facility, the inspector knocked at the door, called three licensee contact numbers,
and sat in the driveway for approx thirty minutes in attempts to reach the licensees. The licensee contacted the
inspector and indicated they were unavailable to accompany inspection.
A responsible adult shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
