2.126(c)

Access and inspection of records and property; submission of itineraries.
At time of the inspection , 3 camels were off site on exhibition in San Antonio . The exhibition is planned to last for almost
2 months. There is no record that an itinerary has been sent to the Animal Care office. A written itinerary must be
submitted no fewer than 2 days in advance of travel and must contain accurate information concerning the whereabouts
of any animal intended for exhibition other than at the approved site. The itinerary may be emailed to
animalcare@usda.gov
