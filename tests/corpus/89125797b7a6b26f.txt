3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The fencing around the stream area for the primary enclosure housing the water buffalo and cattle is leaning. The
fencing is standing and is appropriately containing the animals. Over time this fencing may continue to fail, affecting
the structural integrity of the enclosure. The facility must be constructed of such material and of such strength as
appropriate for the animals involved.
Correct by securing the fencing before November 23, 2015.
3.130
WATERING.
The bucket used for drinking water within the wolf enclosure was chewed around the edges. These chewed areas
may be able to hold dirt or debris and cannot be appropriately cleaned and sanitized. All water receptacles shall be
kept clean and sanitary.
Correct by replacing the water bucket before November 6, 2015.

Dr. Michael Neafsey at the time of the inspection.
