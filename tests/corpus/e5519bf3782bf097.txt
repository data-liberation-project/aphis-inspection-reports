FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The metal panels utilized as fencing for the enclosure housing 5 goats has multiple areas where the paint is chipped
or peeling off the panels. The chipped or peeling paint can be ingested by the animals and could potentially cause
health issue/injuries. Maintain all facilities so that they are structurally sound and in good repair to protect the
animals from injuries.
