The exceutive director stated to the USDA inspectors that within the last few months a paid tour had been given to a
group who came to make primate enrichment toys. Texas Country Reporter filmed a segment of the TV show about
the animals at this facility. The TV show is scheduled to air this week in Texas . The above activities are considered

for license approximately 5- 6 months ago but recently withdrew the application before a prelicense inspection was
ever conducted. At the time application was made, phone contact was made with the facility/executive director and
the requirements for licensing was discussed as well as the fact that no covered activity was to be conducted until
the facility was in compliance and license had been issued. Any person operating or intending to operate as a
dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except persons who are exempted from the licensing requirements
must have a valid license.
