2.36(a)

Annual report.
The registrants annual reports for fiscal year 2020 and 2021 were submitted late. It is essential that these reports be
submitted by the annual December 1st deadline for APHIS inspectors to evaluate the information contained in the reports
to help determine compliance with the Animal Welfare Act regulations. Submit all future annual reports by the required
deadline.
