3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The kinkajou enclosure has an area of the wire mesh wall located in the sheltered portion where the mesh is
broken, unraveling and has sharp, rusty points protruding into the enclosure. There are two screws or nails
protruding at the top portion of the exterior wall of the enclosure near a platform that is pulling away from the wall
and appears unstable. The kinkajous may injure themselves on the sharp points and unsecured platform. The
indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the
animals from injury.
Correct by July 25, 2014.
Exit interview conducted with licensee.
