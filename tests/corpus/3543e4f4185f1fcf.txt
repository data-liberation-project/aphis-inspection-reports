2.40(x)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee has not had the attending veterinarian conduct a site visit per the program of veterinary care plan as
outlined. The minimum of once per year is overdue. The licensee has scheduled with the veterinarian for this
week.
Each dealer and exhibitor shall employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a
part-time attending veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written
program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the dealer.
