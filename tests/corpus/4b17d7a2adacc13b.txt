3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
3.125(a) FACILITIES, GENERAL. At the front of the cougars exhibit there is a chain mesh overhang that has
become separated from the support pole. This needs to be re-attached so that it is structurally sound and then
maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
Correct by: 10-22-2016.
3.131
SANITATION.
3.131(d) SANITATION. Approximately 100 vultures were observed in and around the bear’s enclosure. The birds
were in the swimming area, on fences and all over the ground of the exhibit. Twice while inspecting the area the
birds were harassing the bear that was out on exhibit. A safe and effective program for the control of avian pests
shall be established and maintained.
Correct by: 11-20-2016.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Manager.
