3.61(c)

Primary enclosures used to transport live rabbits.
There were several enclosures that did not give the rabbits sufficient space. The enclosures were not large enough for
the rabbit to turn about freely and make normal postural adjustments. Licensee must ensure that primary enclosures are
large enough to ensure that each rabbit contained therein has sufficient space to turn about freely and to make normal
postural adjustments at all time.
To be corrected from this day, 18 June 22.
3.61(d)

Primary enclosures used to transport live rabbits.
There was one enclosure housing 16 rabbits. Primary enclosures used to transport rabbits must not contain more than 15
at one time to allow all of the animals in the enclosure to move about freely at all times.
To be corrected from this day forward, 18 June 22.
3.36(a)(5)

Primary enclosures used to transport live guinea pigs and hamsters.
There were 17 enclosures containing 17 hamsters that did not have enough ventilation. All of the containers had 1 - 3
small holes in the top. Lack of enough ventilation could lead to health issues. The licensee must ensure that all
enclosures have enough ventilation to allow the animals to have proper air flow for their health and well-being and ensure

there are ventilation openings located on two opposite walls of the primary enclosure and the ventilation openings on
each such wall shall be at least 16 percent of the total surface area of each such wall, or there are ventilation openings
located on all four walls of the primary enclosure and the ventilation openings on each such wall shall be at least 8
percent of the total surface area of each such wall: Provided, however, That at least one-third of the total minimum area
required for ventilation of the primary enclosure shall be located on the lower one-half of the primary enclosure and at
least one-third of the total minimum area required for ventilation of the primary enclosure shall be located on the upper
one-half of the primary enclosure.
To be corrected from this day forward, 18 June 22 .
