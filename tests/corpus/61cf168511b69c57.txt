3.106(b)(3)
WATER QUALITY.
Coliform testing records were not readily available for inspection. Water samples shall be taken and tested at least
weekly for coliform count to maintain water quality standards. Records must be kept documenting the time when
water samples were taken and the results of the sampling. Weekly documentation / records of coliform count test
results shall be maintained by management for a 1-year period and must be made available for inspection purposes
on request.
