2.33(b)(3) DIRECT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
Female rabbit number 24, chip #839-020-616 appeared to be hunched in the corner of her enclosure when the
inspector approached the enclosure. Her vertebrae and hip bones appear prominent. There is hair loss on her face
and nape of neck. The facility representative pulled the rabbit from the enclosure to allow further inspection. Upon
inspection the inspector observed that the toe nails on all feet are excessively long and the skin on the top of her
feet, hocks and inner thighs are reddened and very moist. The condition had not been observed prior to the
inspection. The individual animal record for this rabbit does not indicate there has been any abnormal conditions
requiring treatment. This rabbit shall be examined and appropriate treatment provided.
Corrected by end of inspection.
The inspection and exit briefing conducted with the Institutional Official, Senior QA manager, Quality Specialist and
Senior Animal Handler.
