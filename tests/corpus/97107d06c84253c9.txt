Housing facilities, general.
The four "big dog" enclosures off the garage, housing a eleven dogs, had brown organic material on the inside portion of
the wall with the egress doors. The four enclosure on the west wall of the main building had brown residue on the outside
portion of the egress doors.

The build-up of dirt, feces, and debris can harbor diseases. Hard surfaces with which the dogs or cats come in contact
must be spot-cleaned daily and sanitized in accordance with § 3.11(b) of this subpart to prevent accumulation of excreta
and reduce disease hazards.
