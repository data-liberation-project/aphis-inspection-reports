3.11(c)

Cleaning, sanitization, housekeeping, and pest control.

storage under refrigerated conditions, had multiple patches of black material on the rubber seals closing the doors.
Refrigerators that are contaminated may also contaminate food and/or testing supplies. The facility must ensure
cleanliness of refrigerators to facilitate good husbandry practices and maintain wholesome diets.
