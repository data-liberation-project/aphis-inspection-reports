2.36(a)

Annual report.
The facility failed to submit the required annual report which specifies numbers of regulated animals/species to be used
on research protocols. The reporting facility must submit an annual report to the USDA-APHIS-AC Director of Animal
Welfare Operations at Fort Collins, CO, on or before December 1 of each calendar year. The facility must ensure the
annual report is signed and certified by the CEO or Institutional Official, and that it covers the previous Federal fiscal year.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with two facility representatives.
