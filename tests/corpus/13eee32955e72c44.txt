2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at approximately
12:00 pm on 04/30/2015.
The inspector contacted a employee upon arrival at the facility. The employee informed the inspector that the
facility owner was away and would not be returning for the day. The employee was also in the process of the
leaving the facility himself.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
Note: This inspection was sent by mail.
