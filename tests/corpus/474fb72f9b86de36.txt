3.130

Watering.
The water receptacle in the enclosure housing the goats, sheep, pig, and miniature horses has a green algae-like
substance covering the bottom of the receptacle. Water receptacles that are not cleaned frequently enough can lead to
decreased water drinkability and subsequent health problems of the animals. Correct by cleaning as often as needed to
ensure clean and sanitary water receptacles. Correct by: December 6, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Park Manager.
