2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) was not available at time of inspection. The PVC is a written agreement
between the exhibitor and the attending veterinarian establishing standards of care for the animals. Failure to have
a PVC could jeopardize the health and well being of the animals. The facility should establish and maintain a PVC
to include all the regulated animals at the facility.
To be corrected for all future inspections.
2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
There were no records of acquisition/disposition for the animals at the facility. Records of acquisition/disposition
must be maintained and available to the inspector at time of inspection.
To be corrected for all future inspections.
An exit briefing was conducted with a facility representative.
