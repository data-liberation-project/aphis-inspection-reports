FACILITIES, GENERAL.

holes in the tiles ranged from dime sized to approximately 5 inches in length. These holes in the tiles make it very
difficult to keep the floor clean and sanitized. It is the facilities responsibility to keep the buildings (floors, walls, ect.)
in good repair.
