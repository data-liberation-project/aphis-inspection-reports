3.25(c)

Facilities, general.
Storage. There are bags of feed and bedding on the floor along the walls of the guinea pig room. There is an open bag of
shavings on the floor in the rear corner. Food and bedding supplies must be stored in a manner that protects supplies
from spoilage, deterioration, insects, and vermin. Storing bags of feed on the floor next to walls can allow contamination of
the supplies. Licensee must ensure that food and bedding supplies are stored in a manner that protects supplies from
contamination such as on pallets or platforms and away from walls. Correct by December 21, 2021.

This inspection was conducted with the licensee and the exit interview was conducted with a family member.
