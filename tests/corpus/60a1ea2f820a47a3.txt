3.11(a)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
Enclosures that house dogs must be cleaned at least once each day and more often if needed. This enclosure that
houses dogs has feces, leaves, and dirt that is more than a day old. Daily cleaning can help in the prevention of
diseases and sickness. The owner must clean each enclosure daily to help ensure the good health and well-being
of all dogs. Correct by 07-28-2017
