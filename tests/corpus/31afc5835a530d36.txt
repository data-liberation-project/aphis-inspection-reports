2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The current written program of veterinary care was not available for review at the time of inspection. Having a written,
current program of veterinary care ensures that the facility is prepared for routine care as well as emergencies in the
absence of the attending veterinarian. The facility must ensure that the program of veterinary care is available for review
at the time of inspection.
Correct by April 30, 2022.
3.81

Environment enhancement to promote psychological well-being.
A current primate environmental enhancement plan including details on individual housing for aged or debilitated animals
was not available for review at the time of inspection. Environmental enhancement plans that address social grouping
and environmental enrichment, such as objects to manipulate, dietary variety, positive human interaction, and housing
items to provide complexity to the environment are important for the psychological and physical wellbeing of the animals.
The facility must ensure that the plan is available for review at the time of inspection.
Correct by April 30, 2022.
