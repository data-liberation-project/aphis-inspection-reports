ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
At time of inspection, the facility did not have an up-to-date, written Program of Veterinary Care(PVC) signed by the
current attending veterinarian. The facility had documentation of veterinary care and visits to the premise, but the

and regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the dealer or exhibitor
