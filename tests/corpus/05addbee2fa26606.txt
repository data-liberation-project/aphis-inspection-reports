HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The marmoset enclosure in the tan building had a extensive buildup of debris. The facility must ensure that
enclosures are sanitized as needed in order to prevent build up of debris. In addition, areas of rust were noted on
the enclosure. These conditions can affect the ability of the enclosure to be effectively sanitized. The facility must
ensure that enclosures are maintained in good repair or replaced when worn.
