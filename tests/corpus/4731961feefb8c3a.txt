2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at approximately
1:30 pm on 02/17/16.
The inspector waited in the driveway for thirty minutes and left a message by telephone with the license holder. The
license holder called later and indicated that he was away from the facility and would not be returning for a couple of
hours.
A responsible person shall be available to conduct an inspection during business hours.
Note: This inspection was sent by standard mail.
