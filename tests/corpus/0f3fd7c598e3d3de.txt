2.131(d)(2)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
A large number of people were present in the petting area feeding the animals at time of the inspection .There was
no employee present to monitor the interaction of the public with the animals. Close monitoring will ensure the
safety/well being of both the animals and public. A responsible, knowledgeable, and readily identifiable employee
or attendant must be present at all times during periods of public contact.
