3.75(c)(1)(i)

Housing facilities, general.
Excessive rust was noted around the access door to the tunnel into the Nonhuman Primate night enclosure, as well as on
the brackets supporting the elevated resting surface within the night enclosure. Any surface that comes into contact with
nonhuman primates must be free of excessive rust that prevents the required cleaning and sanitization. Correct by
12/3/2021.
3.81

Environment enhancement to promote psychological well-being.
The facility is lacking a documented appropriate environmental enhancement plan. Although the facility provides
appropriate environmental enhancement to promote the psychological well-being of nonhuman primates, they do not have
a written plan that addresses each of the required subparts within this regulation. An appropriate plan for environment
enhancement adequate to promote the psychological well-being of nonhuman primates must be developed and
documented. The plan, at a minimum, must address each of the following: social grouping, environmental enrichment,
special considerations, restraint devices, and exemptions. Correct by 11/30/2021.
3.131(c)

Sanitation.


The backsplash behind the cooktop, sink, and food preparation counter had crumbling, peeling, flaking paint that could
contaminate the food and or food receptacles being used and prepared in the area. Premises shall be kept clean and in
good repair in order to protect the health of the animals. Correct by 12/3/2021.
