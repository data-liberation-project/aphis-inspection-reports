3.130

Watering.
The water trough, for the white-tailed deer, has soil, algae and fallen leaves present in the bottom. Algae is also present
on the interior walls and bottom of this receptacle. This trough shall be cleaned and potable water provided as often as
necessary for the health and comfort of the deer.

All water receptacles shall be kept clean and sanitary.
