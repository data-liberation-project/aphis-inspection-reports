2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
There was no written program of veterinary care at time of inspection and the attending veterinarian had not visited the
facility since August 21, 2019.

An attending veterinarian's regularly scheduled visits and a written program of veterinary care must be provided for the
animals to ensure their continued health and well-being is monitored appropriately.

Each dealer shall employ an attending veterinarian that will examine every breeding animal head to tail, document
findings, provide husbandry advice, healthcare screening, and compose a thorough written program of veterinary care
with regularly scheduled visits to be followed by the dealer.

Correct by October 1, 2021

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
