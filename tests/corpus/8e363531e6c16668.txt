3.25(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Storage. There was an open bag of feed and and an open bag of shavings on the floor of the animal building.
Opened food and bedding supplies must be stored in containers with tightly fitting lids or covers or in the original
containers as received from the commercial sources of supply. Opened feed and bedding must be stored in
containers with lids or covers to protect supplies from contamination, spoilage, and pests. Correct by April 6, 2018.
3.29(d)
FEEDING.
At least 3 feeders were rusted to the point that they can no longer be appropriately cleaned and sanitized. Food
receptacles must be kept clean and sanitized at least once every two weeks. Rusty feeders must be repaired or
replaced to that they can be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized. Correct by April 6, 2018.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
