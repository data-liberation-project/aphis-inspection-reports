SANITATION.
There is brown scale buildup on the sides of floors of the guinea pig enclosures. Brown scale buildup exposes the
guinea pigs to potential infections with bacteria, fungi, and parasites. Correct by ensuring that primary enclosures
are cleaned and sanitized often enough to prevent a buildup of brown scale. Additionally, ensure that the primary
enclosures are sanitized once every two weeks. Correct by May 20, 2017.
