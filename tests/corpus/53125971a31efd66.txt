This was a FOCUSED inspection report, following up on the 2.40(a)(2) and 3.106(b)(3) DIRECT citations issued on the 6-
8-21 inspection.
The health and welfare of citation 2.40(a)(2)’s affected animals were re-evaluated: Squirt, Bimini, Toki, Samantha “Sam,”
Lii, Catalina, Loke, and Elelo. The new attending veterinarian’s program is being implemented and it was determined to
be providing adequate veterinary care at the time of inspection. All of the facility’s pools were re-visited to assess the
health and welfare of the animals. The facility’s water quality staff and veterinary staff communicated long term goals for
improving water quality for the animals. At the time of inspection, the facility had implemented a daily schedule of
recording total chlorine and adopted a newer method of weekly coliform testing.

No non-compliant items identified during this inspection.

This inspection took place over three days (July 12th-14th) with the General Manager, Facility Curator, Training Curator
and Attending Veterinarian. They were also present at the exit interview.
