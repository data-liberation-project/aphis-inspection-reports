2.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
This is a search inspection. The facility is exhibiting the following species to the public today without a valid USDA license:
African crested porcupine, red fox, coyote, American porcupine, wallaby, coatimundi, prehensile tail porcupine, rabbits,
goats, cats and guinea pigs.
A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal Welfare Act
and Regulations.
The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities. No regulated activities may be conducted
until a USDA license is obtained.

This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the facility representatives.
