2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
A current records of guinea pigs on hand was not available for review. Approximately 201 guinea pigs were counted
during this inspection including adults, weanlings, and newborns. There were no inventory records indicating the number
of adults, weanlings, or newborns present for the 2021 calendar year. Failure to maintain these records can make it
difficult to assess the health of the caviary by identifying animal losses by deaths, number of animals born, and to track
animals being used in regulated purposes. The licensee must maintain records containing the required information which
fully and correctly discloses the number of animals held, owned, acquired or disposed of including births and deaths.
Correct by August 21, 2021.
