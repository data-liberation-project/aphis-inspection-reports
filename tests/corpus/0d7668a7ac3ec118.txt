3.84(b)(3)(iii)

Cleaning, sanitization, housekeeping, and pest control.

hazards and provide to the health and well-being of the animals. To be corrected by: 12-31-2022.

Facilities, general.

be repaired so that it does not continue to deteriorate. The licensee must ensure that all housing facilities are structurally
sound and in good repair. To be corrected by: 04-15-2023.

Sanitation.
