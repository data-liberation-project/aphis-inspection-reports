3.131(c)
SANITATION.
At the time of inspection, there was an exposed wire bed within the dirt floor of the enclosure for the facility's hyena.
The exposed wire bed wrapped around the entire perimeter of the hyena's enclosure. The exposed wire poses a
threat of harm and injury to the hyena. The facility must keep the group premises clean and
in good repair in order to protect the animals from injury.
Correct by: April 26, 2019
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility director.
