2.36(a)
ANNUAL REPORT.
The facility failed to submit their 2014 annual report to the AC Regional Director. The facility must submit an annual
report to the AC Regional Director at Raleigh, North Carolina on or before December 1 of each calendar year. The
report must be signed and certified by the Institutional Official, and must cover the previous Federal fiscal year.
