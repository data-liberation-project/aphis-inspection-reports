2.75(b)(1) Repeat

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The facility did not have complete acquisition and disposition records. There were no acquisition records for the African
serval and red kangaroo. Additionally, no disposition records were available for the zebra, rabbits, guinea pigs, aoudad
sheep, alpaca, and gray fox.

Acquisition and disposition records, including offspring born at the facility, are necessary to accurately track animals being
used in regulated activities. The licensee shall make, keep, and maintain records of acquisition/disposition of all animals
that fully and correctly disclose the required information.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
