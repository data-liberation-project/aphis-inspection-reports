2.131(c)(1) Critical Repeat

Handling of animals.
On August 27th, 2022, a boy was bitten on the hand by an adult female kinkajou during an animal encounter. The boy
was accompanied by an adult and a facility attendant was present during the encounter. The facility was informed that
the boy had to receive medical attention after the incident. The animal is being quarantined and has been temporarily
removed from animal encounters. The facility allows petting and sometimes feeding of the animals during an encounter.
Visitors are allowed to pet the animal on the back with two fingers. Animals must be exhibited and handled in a manner
and with sufficient barriers or distance between animals and the public to assure the safety of the animal and the public.
Correct from this day forward.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
