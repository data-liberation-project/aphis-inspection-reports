3.10(a)

Watering
There was a Labradoodle and her 4 pups that had contaminated water. There were also two Pugs that did not have
water available. The dogs drank some when presented with fresh water, The contaminated water could decrease the
animals' acceptability of the water, as well as increase the risk of disease. Water is important to the health of the dogs
and is necessary for their well being. The licensee must ensure all dogs have potable water available at all times.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
