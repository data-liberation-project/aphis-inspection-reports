2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 3:45 pm on
18-NOV-19. Upon arrival at the facility, the inspector tried to contact the registrant by knocking on the facility door,
calling the provided contact number, and waiting at the facility for thirty minutes. All attempts to contact the
registrant failed.
A responsible person shall be available to conduct an inspection during business hours.
