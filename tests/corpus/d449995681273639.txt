facility had conducted research on rabbits in the past but they were no longer at the facility. During the visit to the
facility, dogs were heard barking in the back area but Animal Care was not given permission by the facility to
observe the dogs. A representative of Los Angeles County Animal Control conducted an inspection. The facility
representative was informed by USDA Animal Care that the facility may not conduct covered activity on covered
species per the Animal Welfare Act. They were provided with contact information for registration.
Registration is required so that regulatory oversight, including scheduling of inspections, can be maintained to
assure compliance with the Animal Welfare Act.
