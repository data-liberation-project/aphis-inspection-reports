2.75(b)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
There are no records available to review regarding the acquisition and disposition of the sugar gliders currently on hand.
Acquisition and disposition records regarding animals purchased, sold, donated, deaths and offspring born at the facility
are required for animals being used in regulated activities. The licensee shall make, keep, and maintain records of
animals on hand including acquisitions and dispositions of all animals The records shall fully and correctly disclose the
required information.
3.126(b)

Facilities, indoor.
There is a very strong odor in the housing room for the sugar gliders. There is very little ventilation or fresh air currently
provided leaving a strong odor of ammonia, scent marking, and other waste material. Heavy concentrations of ammonia
like odors can lead to respiratory illness, attract flies and vermin. The room shall be provided additional ventilation to
protect the sugar gliders from disease hazards. Correct by May 27, 2022.
3.131

Sanitation.
There are several cages that have become rusty allowing waste material to collect on the enclosure wires. The
enclosures shall be cleaned in a manner that prevents such build up, reducing the odors and insect attractant to provide


for the health and well-being of the sugar gliders. The rusty enclosures shall be repaired or replaced to facilitate
appropriate cleaning and sanitation. Correct by May 27, 2022.
