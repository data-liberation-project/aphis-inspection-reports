3.78(d)
OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
***The perimeter fence around the outdoor enclosure for the capuchin is incomplete on two sides of the enclosure.
A perimeter fence at least 6 feet in height and no closer than 3 feet is required around the outdoor enclosure to
restrict access of unauthorized people and animals and to act as a secondary containment for the non-human
primates housed within. Correct by May 26, 2020.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the President and the Treasurer.
