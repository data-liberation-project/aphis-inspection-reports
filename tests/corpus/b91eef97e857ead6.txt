Facilities, general.
The pen housing eight goats is not constructed to contain juvenile goats. The pen is constructed of metal posts and metal
panels. The spacing at and near the bottom of the panels is large enough to let juvenile goats through it. A brown/tan
juvenile goat was observed outside of the pen during the inspection. All of the animal pens and enclosures are
surrounded by a perimeter fence that is constructed to prevent the animals from escaping. Assure the juvenile goats are
housed in an enclosure that is constructed to prevent their escape.
