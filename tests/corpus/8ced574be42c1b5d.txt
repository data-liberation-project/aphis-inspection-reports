3.6(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
The kennel facility consists of one occupied sheltered building. There are wooden surfaces on the interior portion of
the individual enclosures. All of the fourteen enclosures have chewed wood surfaces which need to be repaired or
replaced to facilitate husbandry practices and to insure their structural soundness. Primary enclosures must be
designed and constructed of suitable materials so that they are structurally sound. The primary enclosures must be
kept in good repair to protect the health and welfare of the animals.
Affects twenty-two adults and three puppies. Correct by February 6, 2015
The inspection and exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
