3.75(a)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
In the marmoset enclosure there are three areas in need of repair.
*A metal spring that was used to close the entry door has rusted and broken away from the support. This spring
needs to be replaced or eliminated for the animals.protection.
*There is a small patch on the wire mesh on the south side of the enclosure. The patch was done so that the wires
are fastened on the inside and sharp points protrude in towards the animals. This needs to be repaired or replaced.
*At the bottom of the enclosure there is a wood climbing structure that is fastened to metal supports. A long screw
is sticking out more that two inches. This needs to be made flush or removed to protect the animals from possible
injury.
Housing facilities for nonhuman primates must be kept in good repair, and they must protect the animals from injury,
contain the animals securely, and restrict other animals from entering.
