Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
No alternative search was conducted for Protocol 15-013-02.

The regulations require principal investigators to consider alternatives to procedures that may cause more than
momentary or slight pain and distress to the animals and to provide a written narrative of the methods used and sources
consulted to determine availability of alternatives.

The Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC) shall ensure that a proposal to conduct an animal activity
includes an adequate search for alternatives to assure minimal pain and distress.
