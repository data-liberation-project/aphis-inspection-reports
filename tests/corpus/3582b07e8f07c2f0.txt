HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
One metal self-feeder is rusted through in the bottom. One lower metal support pipe in one enclosure in the west
side of the kennel is rusted to the point that sections are missing. Rusted surfaces cannot be adequately cleaned
and sanitized and can affect the structural strength of the material. All surfaces must be constructed in a manner
that allows them to be readily cleaned and sanitized and remain structurally sound. Correct by 13 Aug. 2015.

HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
There are wire points sticking into two dog enclosures at the height of the dogs. Wire points could cause injury to
the dogs. All surfaces of housing facilities must be constructed in manner that are free from sharp points that might
injure the dogs. Correct by 20 July 2015.
