3.79(d)
MOBILE OR TRAVELING HOUSING FACILITIES.
On July 3, 2015 a family relative of the licensee was bit by a ring-tailed lemur while at a public property in
Wisconsin. The licensee reported being present at the time of the incident however was not in direct control of the
lemur. Non-human primates must be under the direct control and supervision of an experienced handler or trainer
at all times during physical contact with the public. If an experienced handler or trainer is not present, sufficient
barriers and/or distances must be in place to protect the public from harm.
Correction: Take appropriate actions to prevent future incidents.
Exit interview conducted with facility representative via phone on 7/17/2015.
