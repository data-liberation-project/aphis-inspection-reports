3.54(b)

Feeding.
Multiple rabbit feed receptacles were rusted and had urine and fur adhered to the outsides. Feed receptacles that are not
clean are a potential source of contamination of feed that can be detrimental to animal welfare. The registrant must
ensure that the feed receptacles are kept clean and sanitized at least once every two weeks.

Correct by: October 25, 2022

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
