HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.

a moderate to excessive amount of rust. Rusted surfaces do not allow for proper cleaning and sensitization
requirements that are set forth in 3.11 of this subpart/regulation. The Licensee must ensure all surfaces to include
the enclosure doors are maintained without excessive rusting and to allow for proper cleaning and sensitization.
