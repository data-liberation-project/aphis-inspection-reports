3.16(c)
FOOD AND WATER REQUIREMENTS.
An adult, mixed breed, male dog was housed in a transport carrier that only had one water container attached.
There must be food and water receptacles securely attached inside the transport carrier prior to accepting animals
for shipment. Airline must ensure that animals being received for transport have food and water containers that are
securely attached to the transport carriers.
Corrected at the time of inspection.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
