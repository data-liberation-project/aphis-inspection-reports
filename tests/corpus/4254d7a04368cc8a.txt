2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at approximately
2:15 pm on 04/06/2015. The inspector knocked on the facility door and called two different contact numbers in an
attempt to reach the license holder. Additionally, the inspector waited in the facility driveway for approximately 30
minutes. All attempts to contact the license holder were unsuccessful.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
