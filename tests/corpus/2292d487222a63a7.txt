3.8
EXERCISE FOR DOGS.
There was no formal written exercise plan for the dogs in place and made available for review at time of this
inspection. Exhibitors must develop, document, and follow an appropriate plan to provide dogs with the opportunity
for exercise and the plan must be approved by the attending veterinarian. The plan must include written standard
procedures to be followed in providing the opportunity for exercise. The plan must be made available to APHIS
upon request.
Correct by 11-15-2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility owner.
