2.131(c)(1)

Handling of animals.
One prairie dog "Jellybean " is on display to the public in the store. Plexiglass covers one end of his enclosure but 2 sides
are wire with spaces large enough to allow the public to easily touch the animal. Close contact with the prairie dog may
result in a bite to a member of the public . There must be sufficient distance and /or barriers between the animal and the
viewing public reduce risk and assure the safety of the animals and the public. CORRECT BY : 6/30/2022

This inspection was conducted with store employees and the exit briefing was conducted with the licensee by phone.
