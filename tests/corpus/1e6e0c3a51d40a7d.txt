3.131(c)
SANITATION.
The grain storage area has a large amount of spilled animal feed on the ground. This spilled feed can serve to
attract pests that could be a potential source of disease for the animals that consume the feed. A large amount of
bird feces was present in the area. Clean up this spilled feed in order to control the pests in the area.
