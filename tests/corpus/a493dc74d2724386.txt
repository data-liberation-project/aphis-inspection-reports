3.56(a)(1)
SANITATION.
An excessive amount of cobwebs were observed in the rabbit enclosure.
Failure to remove cobwebs increases animal exposure to vermin such as spiders and insects and the disease
causing agents they carry. The licensee should ensure that animal enclosures are kept reasonably free from
cobwebs.
Correct by July 17, 2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
