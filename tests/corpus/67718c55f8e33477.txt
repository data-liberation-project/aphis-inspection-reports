2.1(a)

Requirements and application
The applicant has been operating as a dealer after the license had expired on September 23, 2022. Conducting regulated
activity without a valid license prevents APHIS officials from determining compliance with the Animal Welfare Act. Correct
by ensuring that a valid license is obtained before operating as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction or sale.
3.6(a)(2)(i)

Primary enclosures.
The fence containing the dogs in the outdoor portion of the facility is constructed of barbed wire. The facility also
implementing a hot wire around the bottom of the barbed wire fence to contain the animals. Using barbed wire as primary
enclosure exposes the dogs to an excessive amount of sharp points that could injure the animals. Correct by ensuring
that primary enclosures are constructed and maintained in a way that they have no sharp points or edges that could injure
the dogs.
3.13(a)(3)

Veterinary care for dogs.
Fecal testing has not been performed in accordance with the schedule that was approved by the Attending Veterinarian.
Failure to follow a written and Attending Veterinarian approved schedule for sampling and treatment of parasites and

other pests can lead to a delay in treatment for the animals in the facility. Correct by ensuring that sampling and treatment
of parasites and other pests in accordance with a schedule approved by the Attending Veterinarian is followed.
