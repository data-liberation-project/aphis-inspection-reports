3.127(d)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Back perimeter fence measured between 7 feet 6 inches to 7 feet 8 inches. Fences less than 8 feet high for
potentially dangerous animals, such as wolves, must be approved in writing by the Administrator. The fence must
be constructed in such a way so that it can function as a secondary containment system for the animals in the
facility. A system of maintenance must be in place to ensure that the perimeter fence is maintained with a minimum
effective height of 8 feet.
Correction date 4 February 2016
