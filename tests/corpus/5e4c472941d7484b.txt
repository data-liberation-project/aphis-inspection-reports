This was a focused inspection to look at an adult ram and a red haired, juvenile, male pig who had been cited on
the previous inspection report dated July 12, 2017 under 2.40(b)(3).
APHIS VMO observed the ram and pig; both animals appeared to be healthy and did not display the clinical signs
that the inspector noticed on July 12. The inspector reviewed the veterinary records for the ram and pig which
indicate that both animals had received adequate veterinary care on July 12 and July 13.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
