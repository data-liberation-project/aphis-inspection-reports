REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
2.1(a)(1) Sugar gliders and hedge hogs are being sold at this pet shop without a USDA license.
Any person operating as a dealer must have a valid USDA license. The owner of this pet shop was notified about
the standards and requirements of obtaining a USDA license and a USDA packet was issued.
This inspection and exit interview was accompanied by Florida Fish & Game Commission and owner of this pet
shop.
