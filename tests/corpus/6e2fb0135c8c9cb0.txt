3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
*** The primary enclosure fence surrounding the alpaca, sheep, and goats is loose from the structural supports in
several areas. Enclosure fencing must be maintained in good repair and structurally strong to ensure the animals
are contained and protected from injury. Correct this by repairing or replacing these areas of fence.
Correct by: 25 JULY 2015
3.130
WATERING.
*** The water receptacle for the alpaca, sheep, and goats is dirty and requires cleaning. There is a build-up of
brown green silt on the bottom and sides of the receptacle. Water receptacles must be kept clean and sanitary to
ensure a safe water supply for the animals. Correct this by cleaning and maintaining all water receptacles in a
sanitary condition.
Correct by: 25 July 2015
3.138(a)
PRIMARY CONVEYANCES (MOTOR VEHICLE, RAIL, AIR, AND MARINE).
*** The metal side walls of the stock trailer used to transport the petting zoo animals is rusted where the walls meet
the floor. These walls are rusted completely through in several areas in the animal holding area where there are
now holes large enough for animal feet to potentially fall through. These areas are a potential injury hazard to the
animals during transport. Correct this by repairing or replacing these areas.
Correct by: 15 AUG 2015 and before transport of any regulated animals.
