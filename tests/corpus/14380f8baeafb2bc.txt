2.1(a)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This was a search inspection. The Small Animal Manager was available at this inspection.
At the time of inspection, there were 3 hedgehogs in the store with a for sale sign on their enclosure. In addition,
there as an advertisement for the hedgehogs outside of the store. The Small Animal Manage admitted that they
have also sold other covered species in the past.
The facility is currently conducting regulated activity without a valid USDA license. A valid license is necessary to
protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal Welfare Act and Regulations. The
facility must obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities.
The regulations were explained in great detail and a Licensing packet was provided.
At the time of exit, all advertisements for the hedgehogs were removed and a "Not for Sale" sign was posted on the
hedgehog enclosure.
Exit briefing was conducted with the Small Animal Manager.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
