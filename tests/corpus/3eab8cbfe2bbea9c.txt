2.27(a)
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF OPERATION.
A registrant shall notify the animal care regional office by certified mail of any change of address, or other change in
operations affecting its status, within 10 days after making the change. A registrant is in violation of the Animal
Welfare Act if notification is not relayed to the animal care regional office in accordance with this regulation.
