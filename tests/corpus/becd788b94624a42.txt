2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
A female Bichon #193 has two circular lesions of scabbed and crusted skin with a reddened appearance to the skin
underneath; one lesion is under the neck and one on the left ear. Skin lesions with scabs and redness can be itchy
or painful, and also are potentially contagious between dogs depending on the case. Correct by having this animal
examined by the veterinarian and treated accordingly. The licensee must maintain a record of this dog's diagnosis
and treatment. In addition the licensee must incorporate appropriate preventive measures if instructed by the
attending veterinarian in the event of an infectious disease.
