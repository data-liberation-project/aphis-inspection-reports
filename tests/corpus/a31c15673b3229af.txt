Watering.
Water receptacles for the hoof stock were found to have a thick layer of green algae growth and other debris. Failure to
keep the water receptacles clean and sanitary could result in water that is not healthy for consumption.

The facility should institute a regular cleaning schedule for the water receptacles so that they are maintained in clean and
sanitary condition at all times.
