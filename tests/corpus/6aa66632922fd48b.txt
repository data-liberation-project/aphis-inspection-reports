2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 10:50am on
10-FEB-20.
The APHIS Inspector arrived at the facility at 10:50am but no one answered the door. The contact number was
then called but the representative who answered stated that the individual who could conduct the inspection was not
available and that no one else was able to lead the inspection. When asked if the individual who was able to lead
the inspection would be available in an hour, the representative answered no. The APHIS Inspector then left the
premises at 11:00am.
