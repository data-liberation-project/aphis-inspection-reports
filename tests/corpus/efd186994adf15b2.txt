3.127(d)

Facilities, outdoor.
There was no perimeter fence around the enclosure housing two capybaras.

Without a perimeter fence there is no restriction of unwanted animals or unauthorized persons and it does not provide a
secondary containment systems for the animals. A perimeter fence of at least 6ft in height and at least 3ft away from the
enclosure, that restricts unauthorized persons and animals the size of dogs, skunks and raccoons from going through it or
under it and having contact with the capybaras, must be installed, unless a variance has been granted.
