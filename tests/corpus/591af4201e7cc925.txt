2.75(b)(1) REPEAT
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The record of animals on hand did not include all the required information concerning acquisition and disposition for
the covered species at this facility. Complete and accurate animal records must be kept in order to facilitate
tracking of animals. Records must be maintained that fully and correctly disclose information concerning all animals
purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, transported, sold, euthanized or otherwise disposed of and
shall include any offspring born while in the licensee's possession.
