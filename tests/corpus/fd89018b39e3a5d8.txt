2.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
At the time of this inspection it was noted that the following species were being exhibited to the general public along with a
variety of other domestic farm animals: dromedary camel, watusi, yak, water buffalo, fallow deer, jacob sheep, and mini
zebu. These species are not exempt, or excluded, from the definition of an Animal Welfare exhibitor. Therefore, a USDA
Class "C" exhibitor license is needed. An application will need to be submitted to the USDA - Animal Care office.

No regulated activities can be conducted until a USDA license is obtained.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
