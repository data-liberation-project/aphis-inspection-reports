2.126(c)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
The licensee is currently exhibiting off site and is away overnight from the home site with several animals. An
itinerary must be furnished to the AC Regional Director no fewer than 2 days prior to travel . The itinerary must
include the name of the person exhibiting /transporting the animals, name and ID of each animal species exhibited,
names , dates and locations of exhibition ( including stop overs ).
