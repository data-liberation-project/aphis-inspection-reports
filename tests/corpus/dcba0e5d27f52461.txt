2.40(b)(2) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The following dogs were listed under 2.40 (b) (2) on the inspection report dated July 11, 2017. The inspection
report stated that the dogs must be evaluated and treated by a licensed veterinarian for poor dental health. As of
the September 26, 2017 neither dog has been evaluated or treated by a licensed veterinarian.
Female Chihuahua (microchip #4832771D7C) has a buildup of brown tartar on the upper left canine tooth and
upper left cheek teeth and the front teeth.
Female Chihuahua (microchip #48331C1201) has a buildup of brown tartar on both upper canine teeth and the
upper front teeth.
Poor dental health could cause pain and discomfort to the dog as well as lead to further health issues. The above
mentioned dogs must be evaluated by a licensed veterinarian for a proper diagnosis and to establish an effective
treatment plan. The diagnosis and treatment plan must be documented in writing and available to the inspector
upon request. The licensee must ensure that all dogs receive adequate veterinary care at all times.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with ACI, Kelly Maxwell and the facility representative.
