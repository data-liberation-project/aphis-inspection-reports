3.125(d) REPEAT
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Waste disposal
A large heap of animal waste, bedding, and other material remains in the drive through animal enclosure. Animals
enclosed in this area of the facility have free access to the waste. This exposes the animals to disease hazards.
Licensee must provide for the removal and disposal of animal and food wastes, bedding, and debris.

WATERING.
There continues to be no potable water source for the animals in the drive through area. Ponds present in this area
are frozen. A small amount of water is running from a drain pipe that feeds into one of the ponds. Animals must
have access to clean, potable water for optimal health and well-being. Clean and potable water must be provided at
