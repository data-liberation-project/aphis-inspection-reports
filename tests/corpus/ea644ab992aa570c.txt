2.75(a)(1)(vi)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Puppies are currently being listed their name in records instead of an official USDA tag number. Records must
include the official USDA tag number or tattoo assigned to a dog or puppy. Licensee must record the official
identification number assigned to a puppy to help identify and keep track of animals. Correct by November 15,
2018.
