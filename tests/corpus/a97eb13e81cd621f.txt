2.33(b)(2)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care.
Between May 12, 2022 and November 8, 2022, 9 procedures were done on cats using a bottle of Ketamine Hydrochloride
Injection that had an expiration date of April 2022. There were no adverse effects observed due to use of the expired
medication.
Manufacturers do not guarantee the full potency and safety of medications used beyond their expiration dates.
Each research facility shall establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that include the use of
appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases. Procedures must be followed that insure
medications used for these purposes are safe and effective, including the timely removal of expired medications from use.
The expired medication was removed at the time of inspection.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.

No regulated species were present at the time of inspection.
