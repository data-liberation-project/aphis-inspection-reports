3.131(d)
SANITATION.
***There are numerous small black flies swarming throughout the pig housing area. The flies are on the gates, rails,
feeders, etc. There are also flies on the pigs. Flies can pester the pigs and cause discomfort, they may also
contaminate the feed. An effective means of minimizing the presence of the flies shall be developed and
maintained to prevent discomfort and protect the food from contamination. Correct by August 8, 2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Animal Caretaker.
