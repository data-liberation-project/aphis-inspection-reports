INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
** Upon inspection the facility's IACUC had not met since December 2017. Since they had not met in over a year
they also had not reviewed the facility's program of humane care and use of the animals, inspected the facility or
reported to the IO since December 2017. IACUC is supposed to meet, review the research facility's program for
humane care and use of animals, inspect the facility and report findings to the IO at least once every six months for
the well-being of the animals involved in the research activity. Correct by June 1, 2019
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the authorized person.
