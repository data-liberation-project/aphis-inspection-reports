2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
Acquisition/Disposition records for one patas monkey and one Nubian Ibex are not complete. There is no address for the
person donating the ibex or for the person to whom the patas monkey was donated. There is also no vehicle license
number and drivers license number listed for either since they were not licensed.
Acquisition /Disposition records must be contain all information required :The USDA license or registration number of the
person if he or she is licensed or registered under the Act; The vehicle license number and State, and the driver's license
number (or photographic identification card for non-drivers issued by a State) and State of the person, if he or she is not
licensed or registered under the Act; The name and address of the person to whom an animal was sold or given; The date
of purchase, acquisition, sale, or disposal of the animal(s);The species of the animal(s); and the number of animals in the
shipment. CORRECT BY : 10/1/2022
