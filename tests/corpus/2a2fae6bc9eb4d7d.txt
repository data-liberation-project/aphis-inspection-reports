2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
APHIS VMO conducted a search inspection of Kodiak Ranch Petting Zoo at the Adams County Fair. The public was
feeding and interacting with the goats, sheep, alpacas, and calves brought by Kodiak Ranch. The authorized
representative informed APHIS VMO that the Brahman bull he brought would be posing for photos with the public.
All animal exhibitors, whose business involves the showing or displaying of animals to the public, must have a valid
USDA license in order to conduct USDA regulated activity.
During the inspection the authorized representative of Kodiak Ranch filled out a USDA license application and gave
the inspector a check for the application fee.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the authorized representative of Kodiak Ranch.
No regulated activities may be conducted until USDA license is obtained.
