2.40(a)(1) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The museum program of veterinary care needs to be updated and signed by the current veterinarian. A current
program of veterinary care is essential to comply with USDA regulations and to provide regulated animals at the
museum with adequate veterinary care. Documentation needs to be maintained verifying at least annual
inspections
by the current veterinarian.
