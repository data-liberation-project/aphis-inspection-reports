3.53(c)(2) REPEAT
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
One adult nursing female and 7 nursing young rabbits weighing less than 4.4lbs. are housed in a cage measuring
24x20 in. which provides 3.33 sq. ft. of floor space. The minimum space requirement for a 4.4 lb. or less nursing
female rabbit is 4 sq. ft. of floor space. It is important to make sure all rabbits have adequate floor space to make
normal postural movements and to have adequate freedom of movement.
