REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
An unlicensed person is exhibiting regulated animals in a petting zoo. Exhibiting regulated animals in a petting zoo
requires a valid USDA license. No further regulated activity can be conducted until a valid USDA license has been
obtained.
Inspection and exit briefing conducted with the unlicensed person and Animal Care Inspectors Miller and Wilken.
End of report.
