2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Program of Veterinary Care has not been updated, reviewed and approved by the attending veterinarian. The
written program of Veterinary Care must reflect the current program of animal care. An example of this is that the
veterinarians preventive medicine protocols have changed and are not reflected in the written program. Program
must b kept current and approved on a regular basis.
