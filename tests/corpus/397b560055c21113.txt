2.50(c)(2)(2)
TIME AND METHOD OF IDENTIFICATION.
The licensee does not have a record book containing each animal's tag number, or microchip number, a written
description of each animal, the data required by Sec. 2.75(a), and a clear photograph of each animal.
The required identification for dogs, including a clear photograph of each animal, is necessary to ensure all dogs
can be properly identified for oversight of their care and use in transport and in exhibition.
The licensee shall make a record book that contains all of the required identification information including a clear
photograph of each animal.
