SHELTERED HOUSING FACILITIES.
Surfaces.
The wood surfaces on the dog access doorways for the enclosures with indoor/outdoor access are no longer
impervious to moisture. Unsealed surfaces could deteriorate or harbor bacteria and other disease hazards. Cover,
replace or seal all bare wood to provide for thorough cleaning and disinfecting practices and to protect the materials
from deterioration. Correct by October 1, 2014.
Inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
