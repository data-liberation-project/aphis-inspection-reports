2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee does not have a current written program of veterinary care. The written program of veterinary care is
out of date, has inaccurate information, and has not been reviewed for over a year.
The licensee and the attending veterinarian should review the program of veterinary care at least once a year.
Failure to have a current program of veterinary care could have a negative impact on the health and well-being of
the animals.
An updated version of the written program of veterinary care should be created and made available for inspection
by 6/1/15.
AN exit interview was conducted with the licensee.
