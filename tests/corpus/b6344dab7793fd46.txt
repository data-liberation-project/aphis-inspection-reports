HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* Two dug holes were observed in the dirt ground floor directly under the enclosure fencing. Four dog are in
contact with the affected areas located in outdoor housing. The holes were estimated to be six inches wide by six
inches deep. This affects the ability of enclosure fencing to contain the dog securely. It could lead to potential injury
affecting the safety and welfare of the dog. The licensee must keep facilities in good repair to protect the animals
from injury and contain the animals securely. Correct by 3/18/15.
* Twisted wire ends with sharp points were observed in the enclosure fencing. In addition, hail screen wiring
attached to the enclosure gate in the same enclosure also had broken, protruding wires with sharp points. Four
dogs are in contact with the affected wiring located in outdoor housing. Sharp points could injure the dogs. The
licensee must ensure that all enclosure wires are maintained in good repair at all times for the safety of the dogs.
Correct by 3/18/15.
* The facility inspection done on 2/19/15 and the exit briefing done on 2/20/15 were conducted with the licensee.
