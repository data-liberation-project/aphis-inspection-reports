2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The kennel facility consists of one indoor housing building and one outdoor housing area. During the inspection a
three year old white male English Bulldog, chip #0A02091138 was observed with crusty green matter around it's left
eye. The dealer did not have any written documentation the animal had been evaluated and or treated by the
attending veterinarian for this condition. The dealer did not have any written documentation of treating the animal.
The animal must be evaluated by the attending veterinarian. The dealer must maintain written documentation of the
evaluation and all treatment.
Affects one adult. Correct by July 13, 2015
3.1(f)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The outdoor housing area is composed of ground enclosures with a compacted earth floor surface. During the
inspection four of the outdoor enclosures had standing water and mud causing the contained animals to stay wet
and muddy. The enclosure floor surfaces need to be repaired and maintained to minimizes contamination and
disease risks. The enclosures must have better drainage to insure the animals can stay clean and dry, to protect
their health and welfare.
Affects four adults. July 17, 2015
3.11(a)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
The outdoor housing area consists of one row of attached enclosures and a lone enclosures attached to a trailer.
The lone enclosure contained one small white French Bulldog during the inspection. The primary enclosure
contained more than one days accumulation of waste material. The dealer stated she had last removed waste
material from the enclosure four days prior. Excreta must be removed from primary enclosures daily to prevent an
excessive accumulation of feces and food waste, to prevent soiling of the dogs contained in the primary enclosures,
and to reduce disease hazards, insects, pests and odors. Affects four adults. July 10, 2015
The inspection and exit briefing was conducted with the facility representatives.
