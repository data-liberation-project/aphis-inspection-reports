3.125(a) REPEAT
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
***The metal mesh panels on the walls in the main pig room are covered with rust and the surfaces are pitted.
Severe rust leads to corrosion which weakens the structural strength. These surfaces cannot be appropriately
cleaned and sanitized and shall be repaired or replaced to protect the integrity of the structure as well as allow
appropriate husbandry practice.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Technician.
