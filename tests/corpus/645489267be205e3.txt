2.75(a)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
* The cage card information attached to four enclosures containing 24 unweaned puppies was incomplete. The
identification, breed and sex of the puppies were missing. Complete and accurate records are needed to ensure
each puppy's identity can be known with certainty and to facilitate the ability to trace all puppies. The licensee must
maintain complete and accurate records of all puppies at their facility. Correct by 10/28/15.
3.11(c)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
* Cobwebs and dust were observed collecting on the outside of three plastic containers containing medical
supplies. The affected area is located in the whelp building housing three dogs and 17 puppies. It indicates a living
area for rodents, other pests and other vermin exists. Unsanitary living conditions exist if the affected area is not
kept free of accumulations of trash, junk, waste products and discarded matter. This could impact the health of the
dogs and puppies. The licensee must ensure the premises where housing facilities are located including buildings
and surrounding grounds are kept clean to facilitate normal required husbandry practices. Correct by 10/28/15.
