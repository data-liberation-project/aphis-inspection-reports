2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 2:15 pm on
27-SEP-18. The applicant's contact number was called. There was no answer and the voicemail box was full. The
applicant was texted to inform her I was there for inspection. There was no response. I waited at the entrance gate
to the facility for 45 minutes and left.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
