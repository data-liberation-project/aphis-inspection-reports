2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
A male poodle (DOB 02-07-2006, tag #55) has a thick buildup of brown tartar encasing the majority of the surface of
the upper canine teeth on both sides of the mouth and the gums have become slightly reddened.
A male Yorkshire terrier (DOB 2011, tag #47) has a thick buildup of dark brown tartar encasing nearly the entire
surface of the cheek teeth, front teeth and canine teeth. The gums have become slightly reddened.
Poor dental health can cause pain and discomfort to the dog and could lead to further health complications.
The licensee must have the above mentioned dogs evaluated by a licensed veterinarian for a proper diagnosis and
to establish an effective treatment plan. The diagnosis and treatment plan must be documented in writing and
available to the inspector upon request. The licensee must provide all dogs with adequate veterinary care at all
times.
