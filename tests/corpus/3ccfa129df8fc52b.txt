3.28(c)(1)(iii)

Primary enclosures.
Space. Insufficient space was being provided to approximately 173 weanling guinea pigs in 10 enclosures with the rubber
pans. Enclosure measure 32 inches by 22 inches or 704 square inches of floor space. Weanling guinea pigs weighing up
to 350 grams require at least 60 inches of floor space per animal. Ten enclosures were housing 13 or more weanling
guinea pigs in each cage. Can not house more than 11 weanling guinea pigs in this size enclosure.

Inadequate space can cause stress, overcrowding, and aggression in animals. Licensee must ensure that guinea pigs are
provided with at least the minimum amount space per animal to prevent unnecessary stress and discomfort. Correct by
March 30, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
