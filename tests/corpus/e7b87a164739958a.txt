3.11(b)(2)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
There are approximately 10 metal automatic feeders that have an accumulation of caked feed and dirt on the inside.
Uncleaned feeders may lead to contamination of the feed. All food receptacles must be cleaned and sanitized as
least once every two weeks to prevent accumulation of dirt, debris, and food waste. Correct by 3 April 2014.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with the licensee.
