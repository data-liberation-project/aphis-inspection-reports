3.131(c)

Sanitation.
There are three separate white-tail deer pens. The pens have a heavy and tall weed burden. This needs to be taken
down to allow visual inspection of the animals, enclosures, and to help prevent vermin infestation. Premises (buildings
and grounds) shall be kept clean and in good repair in order to protect the animals from injury and to facilitate the
prescribed husbandry practices set forth in this subpart.
