3.58(a)

Classification and separation.
Two of the rabbits share a primary enclosure with the Patagonian Cavy.

Rabbits cannot be housed in the same primary enclosure with any other species of animal. Co-housing rabbits with other
species can lead to stress and can increase the risk for the spread of disease. Correct by separating the rabbits from the
Patagonian Cavy.
