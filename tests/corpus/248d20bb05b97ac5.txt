2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Since the previous inspection report on May 11, 2016, there were no records of adult animals sold. Owner stated
during the inspection that he recently sold adult animals to a dealer, but there was no record of the sale. Records
must be maintained on the disposition of all animals to keep track of animals, document changes in ownership and
document the sale of animals. Licensee must ensure that records on the disposition of animals are kept and
maintained. Correct from this day forward.
