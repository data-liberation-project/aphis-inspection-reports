3.11(d)

Cleaning, sanitization, housekeeping, and pest control.
Bird droppings were observed next to walls within several enclosures where dogs were housed. Rodent droppings were
additionally observed within the facility on shelves containing kennel supplies. Birds and rodents can transmit diseases to
the dogs and contaminate the areas. An effective pest control program must be established and maintained so as to
promote the health and well-being of the animals and reduce contamination by pests in animal areas.

Correct by December 6, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
