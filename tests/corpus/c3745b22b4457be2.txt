FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The roof of the large shelter in the center of the Patagonian cavy enclosure has caved in. There are boards with the
sharp points of the nails exposed, and the structural strength of the shelter has been compromised. The Patagonian
Cavy could become injured by the nails and loose boards. The shelter shall be repaired or replaced and all loose
material shall be removed to protect the animals from injury. Correct by April 1, 2015.
The large barn in the camel enclosure is severely leaning and there are loose boards that could fall and injure the
camels, The barn is not structurally sound. The barn shall be repaired or removed and replaced to protect the
camels from injury. Correct by April 15, 2015.

escape into the porcupine enclosure. There is no barrier to prevent the raccoon from digging out. A proper barrier
shall be constructed or the raccoon should be moved to another enclosure that will securely contain the animal
within. Correct by March 12, 2015
The side of the pig barn has boards that are broken and/or separated from the structure. The barn shall be repaired
to protect the pigs from injury. Correct by April 15, 2015.

and young muntjac. When the main perimeter fence on that side meets the enclosure it stops and then the
