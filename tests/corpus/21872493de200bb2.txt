2.33(b)(2)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care.
One opened 5 ml bottle of gentamicin ophthalmic solution with exp date of 11/21 was found in the ocular procedure room
during the inspection.

Per this Section, each research facility shall establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that include the
use of appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries. Medications that are past the
manufacturer’s expiration date on the product’s label may not be as effective and should not be used on the animals.
The bottle of gentamicin ophthalmic was immediately discarded. Corrected at the time of the inspection.

The inspection and exit briefing were conducted 5/23/22 with facility representatives.
