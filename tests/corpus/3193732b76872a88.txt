3.11(d)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
In the outdoor portion of the facility there were an abundance of flies. The flies were landing on the enclosure walls,
the dogs, and the feeders.
Pests can transmit disease and contaminate food. The licensee must establish and maintain an effective program
for the control of insects, external parasites affecting dogs, and birds and mammals that are pests so as to promote
the health and well-being of the animals and reduce contamination by pests in animal areas.
