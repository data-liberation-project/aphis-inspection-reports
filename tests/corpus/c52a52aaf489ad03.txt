2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).

be accomplished. According to the facility representative, the animal could not be found in its enclosure for a period
of time. The carcass was later found in the exhibit, but a necropsy could not be performed. There was not sufficient
information to determine the cause of death of the animal.
An adequate program of veterinary care should prevent, control, diagnose and treat all diseases and injuries to
ensure the health and well-being of all facility animals.
To be corrected from this date forward.
