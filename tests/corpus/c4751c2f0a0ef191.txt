FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The refrigerator storing the mammal food was dirty. The shelving was soiled with old food particles and spilled juice.
The door of the refrigerator had a minor buildup of mold. The food stored in the dirty refrigerator has the potential to
become contaminated and pose a health risk to the animals once ingested. Supplies of food and bedding shall be
stored in facilities which adequately protect such supplies against deterioration, molding, or contamination by
vermin.
