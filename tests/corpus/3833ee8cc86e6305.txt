2.134

Contingency planning.
The facility did not have a documented contingency plan developed to provide for the welfare of their animals in the event
of an emergency or disaster. The facility must develop, document, and follow an appropriate plan to provide for the
humane handling, treatment, transportation, housing, and care of their animals in the event of an emergency or disaster.
