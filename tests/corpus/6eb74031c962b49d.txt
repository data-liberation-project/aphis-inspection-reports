This was a focused inspection to look at a Syrian hamster who had been cited on the previous inspection report
dated April 13, 2018 under 2.40(b)(3) Direct.
APHIS inspector reviewed the veterinary records for the hamster which indicate that the animal had been examined
by the attending veterinarian on April 14, 2018 and received adequate veterinary care.
No non-compliant items were identified during this inspection. This inspection and exit interview were conducted
with facility representatives and Animal Care Program Specialist Katie Whisenton.
