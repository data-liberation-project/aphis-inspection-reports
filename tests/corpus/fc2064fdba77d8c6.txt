2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
There were expired medications ( Dectomax, Noramection , B 12 ) on the shelf with currently dated supplies.
Expired medications lose their potency and may not be effective in treating and preventing diseases. Adequate
veterinary care includes the use of in date drugs and medications. All expired medications must be discarded or
marked expired and kept separately from in date medications to ensure adequate veterinary care is provided.
The medications were moved to the expired shelf at time of the inspection .
