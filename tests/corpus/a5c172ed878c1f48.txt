2.40(b)(2)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
There was a 4 month old male kitten named Sunshine that had been vaccinated by the licensee on June 6, 2021 and July
6, 2021 with an FVRCP vaccine that expired on December 4, 2020. Vaccine manufacturers provide an expiration date to
ensure safety and potency. Expired vaccines may no longer be safe or effective at preventing disease. Correct by using
unexpired vaccines and medications from this day forward.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
