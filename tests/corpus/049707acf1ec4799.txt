2.31(c)(1)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC conducted a program review on March 2, 2021. The next program review was conducted on December 17,
2021. The facility program review should have been conducted in September 2021.

The interval between the program reviews is longer than the required 6 months. The IACUC did not fulfill their
responsibility to conduct a review of the animal use program at the required interval. The IACUC is responsible for
ensuring that the research facility's program uses and cares for animals in a humane manner every 6 months in
accordance with the IACUC responsibilities outlined in the Animal Welfare Act. The IACUC must conduct a review of its
program of animal use to ensure it's procedures and policies are in accordance with the regulations and standards
outlined in the Animal Welfare Act. Correct by March 31, 2022 and subsequently in September 2022.
2.31(c)(2)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC conducted a semi-annual facility inspection on March 2, 2021. The September 2021 facility inspection was
not conducted.



The IACUC did not fulfill their responsibility to conduct an inspection of the animal facility at least once every 6 months.
The IACUC is responsible for ensuring that the research facility's program uses and cares for animals in a humane
manner every 6 months in accordance with the IACUC responsibilities outlined in the Animal Welfare Act. The IACUC
must conduct an inspection of its facilities for animal use every 6 months to ensure animals are used and maintained in
accordance with the the standards in the Animal Welfare Act. Correct by March 31, 2022 and subsequently in September
2022.
