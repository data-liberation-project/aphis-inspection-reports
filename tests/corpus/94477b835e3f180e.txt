ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Licensee is adding "SpectoGard" to the water reservoirs that are then used to water the hedgehogs. This
medication is not labeled for use in hedgehogs and this practice has not been approved in writing from the attending

injurious to the animals. Use of medications in an extra-label manner must be approved in writing by the attending
veterinarian and must include information such as, but not limited to, appropriate dosing, mode of application,
duration, etc. Correct by 15 March 2017.
