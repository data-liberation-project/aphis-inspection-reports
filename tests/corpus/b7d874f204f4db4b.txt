RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.

3.26(a)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
The temperature in the facility according to the facilities thermometer was 86.7*F and 87.8*F on the inspectors
thermometer.
Ambient temperatures of greater than 85*F can cause stress and overheating of the guinea pigs. Indoor housing
facilities for guinea pigs shall not exceed 85*F.
To be corrected by: July 16, 2018
3.28(c)(1)(iii)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
There were 83 weaned young guinea pigs in an enclosure that provided 2160 square inches. Each animal in the
enclosure required 60 square inches totaling 4980 square inches need.
Lack of proper space can cause injury and health issues, and impact growth. The license shall provide a minimum
amount of floor space of 60 square inches for each guinea pig that is weaned to 350 grams in size, and 101 square
inches for each guinea pig greater than 350 grams and nursing females with litters.To be corrected by: July 16, 2018
