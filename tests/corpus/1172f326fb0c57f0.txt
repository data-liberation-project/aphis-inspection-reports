2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11:45 am on
11-Oct-16.
The inspector honked, knocked on the door and called the licensee. The inspector reached the licensee by phone
and the licensee stated that it wasn't a good time to inspect. The inspector requested and received optimal hours for
future inspections.
