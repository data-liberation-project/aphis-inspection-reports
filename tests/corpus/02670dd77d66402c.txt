Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The facility has not had regularly scheduled visits by the attending veterinarian at the time of inspection. The last on-site
visit by the attending veterinarian was conducted in 2019.

Regularly scheduled visits by the attending veterinarian help to ensure that appropriate veterinary care is provided to all
animals in the facility.

In the case of a part-time attending veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal arranges shall include a written
program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises.
