ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
(a)(1) The part time attending veteterinarian has not prepared the written program of veterinary care (PVC) to be
used as a guide in the compliance with the Animal Welfare Act. The facility shall ensure that a current PVC be
prepared by the attending veterinarian as a tool in maintaining adequate veterinary practice in the facility.
