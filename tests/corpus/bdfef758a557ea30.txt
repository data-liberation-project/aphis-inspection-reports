2.30(c)(1)
REGISTRATION.
At 12:23 pm, the USDA inspector received an electronic communication from the contact phone number provided
by the facility. The message stated that the business no longer exists and that it has been dissolved.
VMO Dr. Aaron Rhyner spoke with facility representative several months previously regarding the closure of the
research facility. At that time, he informed the representative that the Regional Director needed to be notified about
the change in operation.
To date, the research facility still has an active USDA license.
A research facility shall notify the AC Regional Director by certified mail of any change in the name, address, or
ownership, or other change in operations affecting its status as a research facility within ten days after making such
change.
