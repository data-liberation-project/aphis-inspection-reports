Facilities, general.
Storage. There was a large bowl with feed on top of a cart and a food container with a cracked lid with holes. Supplies of
food must be stored adequately in a manner that protects supplies from contamination, spoilage, insects, and vermin.
Food supplies must be stored in closed containers with lids or in the original containers received from the feed supplier.
Licensee must ensure that opened food supplies are stored in closed containers with lids to protects supplies from
infestation and rodents. Correct by April 29, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
