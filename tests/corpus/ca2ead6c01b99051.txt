2.40(b)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The method of euthanasia listed on the program of veterinary care has been changed . The program of veterinary
care must be updated by the attending veterinarian to include an AVMA approved method of euthanasia.The
licensee must follow the written program of veterinary care to ensure the animals receive adequate veterinary care.
