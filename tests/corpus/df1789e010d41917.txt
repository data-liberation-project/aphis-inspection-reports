REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The facility is conducting regulated activities without a license. The facility is open to the public and has petting zoo
animals including goats, pot bellied pigs, alpacas, and domestic cats on exhibit. The facility owners were contacted
and made aware that a USDA, APHIS, Animal Care license is needed in order to exhibit the animals to the public.
The facility owner indicated that she will be contacting the Animal Care office in Raleigh, NC, to apply for a license.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES ALLOWED UNTIL A LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Note: This inspection report was sent by certified mail.
