SANITATION.
Housekeeping.There are several dirty, empty enclosures in the adult guinea pig room and the holding room for
guinea pigs. The empty enclosures contain dirty bedding, feed, and debris. Leaving empty enclsoures dirty for days
or weeks increases the risk of disease. Animal facilities must be kept clean to ensure the health of the animals.
Ineffective cleaning exposes the animals to diseases hazards and health problems. Licensee must establish a
program to keep animal facilities clean and sanitary.
