3.56(c)

Sanitation.
The rabbit room had urine staining and dried puddles on the walls and in corners where racks had previously been
placed. Accumulations of urine and waste products contribute to odors that are unpleasant and may produce respiratory
problems in rabbits. Additionally, accumulated waste products contribute to bacterial growth that may adversely impact
animal health and lead to disease. The premises shall be kept clean in order to maintain the health of the animals.
