3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
A guinea pig enclosure containing approximately 25 animals was overdue for cleaning at the time of inspection.
Excess feces and urine accumulation can affect air quality and can expose the animals to transmissible diseases.
The licensee said he would make cleaning the enclosures a priority for his employee due in later in the day on the
day of inspection - he will correct by close of business on the day of inspection.
