3.11(d)

Cleaning, sanitization, housekeeping, and pest control.
Bird droppings were observed on the floor within one enclosure, as well as on the gates of two enclosures, and the inside
of two uncovered feeding receptacles. Birds can transmit diseases to the dogs and contaminate the areas. An effective
pest control program must be established and maintained so as to promote the health and well-being of the animals and
reduce contamination by pests in animal areas.

Correct by May 4, 2021.
