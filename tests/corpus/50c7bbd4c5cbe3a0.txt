3.130
WATERING.
The water receptacles in the pig enclosures are not clean and sanitary. There is a thick buildup of dirt, mud, and
grime on the bottom of the receptacles. Dirty water receptacles can be a source of pathogens and toxins that could
cause the pigs to become ill. All water receptacles shall be kept clean and sanitary at all times.
