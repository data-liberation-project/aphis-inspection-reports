2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
***The facility was unable to provide documentation indicating that the IACUC has been performing semiannual
reviews of the research facility’s program for humane care and use of animals. The facility representative, who is
also a member of the IACUC, verbally informed the inspector that the program has not been reviewed every six
months.
Review of the program for the humane care and use of animals in teaching by the IACUC is required at least every
six months to ensure that any activities involving animals at these facilities are being conducted in a manner to
reduce pain and/or distress and that they meet the current standards for all species. The IACUC, as an agent of the
research facility, the research facility's program for humane care and use of animals, will use title 9, chapter I,
subchapter A--Animal Welfare, as a basis for evaluation.
To be corrected by July 1, 2018 and before animals are utilized by the facility.
2.31(c)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
***The facility was unable to provide documentation indicating that the IACUC as been performing a semiannual
inspection of the research facility’s animal facilities, including animal study areas. The facility representative
informed the inspector that a facility inspection has not been performed within the last six months.
Inspection of the animal facilities by the IACUC is required at least every six months to ensure that any activities
involving animals at these facilities are being conducted in a manner to reduce pain and/or distress and ensure they
meet the current standards for all species.
The IACUC, as an agent of the research facility, shall inspect, at least once every six months, all of the research
facility's animal facilities, including animal study areas, using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A- Animal Welfare, as a
basis for evaluation.To be corrected by July 1, 2018 and before animals are utilized by the facility.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
