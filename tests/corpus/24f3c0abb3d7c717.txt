2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Semi-Annual Program Review
The most recent IACUC meeting and program review were conducted August 13, 2015. According to
the IACUC Chair, a meeting and program review were supposed to be conducted February 2016, but were not due
to scheduling conflicts. At time of inspection, the next meeting and program review was tentatively scheduled for
March 2016. It is important for the IACUC to conduct regular reviews of the program to ensure that it is in
compliance with the Animal Welfare Act. The registrant must ensure that the IACUC reviews, at least once every six
months, the program for humane care and use of animals.
