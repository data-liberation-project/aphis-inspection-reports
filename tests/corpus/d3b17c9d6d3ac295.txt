3.125
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
(a) Structural strength.
The facility must be constructed of such material and of such strength as appropriate for the animals involved.
The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to
protect
the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
***** The west wall inside the lock down area for the Giant Anteaters located in the walk in area off exhibit is
constructed
of plywood covered with plexi-glass. The plexi-glass has become broken and allowed the anteaters access to the
plywood with their long claws. They have damaged the plywood and plexi-glass to the point they need replacement
to
keep this wall maintained in good repair and to prevent possible injury to these animals.
***** The protective grates which covered two openings in the floor drain and the cap to the flush out pipe for this
drain
located in the lockdown area addressed above are missing. These grates and cap need to be replaced so that the
drain
system is maintained in good repair and to prevent possible injury to the animals in this enclosure.
