WATERING.
Five water bottles had an algae buildup on the inside of the water container. Water receptacles must be kept
cleaned and sanitized whenever dirty and at least once every two weeks. Inadequate cleaning of the water
receptacles can result in contamination of the guinea pigs in the same enclosure and health problems. Licensee
must establish and maintain a program to keep the water receptacles clean when dirty, and receptacles must be
sanitized at least once every 2 weeks. Correct by March 29, 2017.
