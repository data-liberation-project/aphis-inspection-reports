3.125(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Food supplies must be stored to prevent contamination . There is one open bag of sweet feed present which has
clothes pins used to close the top of the bag. Pest may gain access to the feed and the feed may be damaged if the
bag becomes wet. The feed must be stored in a manner to prevent contamination or deterioration such as in a
container with a lid .
