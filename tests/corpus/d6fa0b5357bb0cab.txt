3.53(a)(2)

Primary enclosures.
The metal roof of an enclosure housing 4 rabbits had been penetrated by a fallen tree branch. The roof of the enclosure
was no longer intact and could act as an entry point for rain water into the rabbits enclosure and bedding. Primary
enclosures shall be structurally sound and maintained in good repair so as to enable the rabbits to remain dry and clean.
Correct by October 26th, 2021.
3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
An enclosure housing 3 goats had a portion of the fencing damaged by a horse. The damaged section of fence posed a
risk of escape for the animals contained therein. The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and
shall be maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
Correct by November 2nd, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
