2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
***In the ready to use portion of the medication storage area, there were at least six medications that were expired,
had no expiration date or were improperly labeled. Medications that are expired, have an unknown expiration date
and are given to the animals may not produce the desired result. Medications that are improperly labeled leave
room for error in administration. The licensee must to remove all expired medications from the ready to use portion
of the facility, assure that all medications are labeled appropriately, are within the expiration date and assure
adequate veterinary care at all times.
