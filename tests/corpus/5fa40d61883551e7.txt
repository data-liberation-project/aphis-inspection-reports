2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Any person operating or intending to operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except persons
who are exempted from the licensing requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section, must have a valid license.
The owner of the business told the inspector that he exhibited his reindeer to the public at the Surrey Point gas
station and convenience store on 12-21-14. The exhibitors' license for his business was cancelled 5/20/13. A valid
USDA license is required to exhibit covered species to the public. Do not exhibit to the public without a valid USDA
license.
No covered activity is permitted without a valid USDA license.
