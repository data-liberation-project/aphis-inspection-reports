2.40(x)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
At the time of inspection, the facility did not have documentation of meeting the regularly scheduled visits to the
premises by the attending veterinarian in the last two years. Their written Program of Veterinary Care(PVC) signed
by the attending veterinarian in 2011 requires two annual visits by the attending veterinarian. The formal
arrangement with an attending or consulting veterinarian shall include regularly scheduled visits to the premises, in
addition to the written program of veterinary care.
Correction date 20 February, 2016
3.6(a)(2)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
In multiple enclosures, portions of the wire mesh used to reinforce the enclosure fences and prevent digging along
the inside of the fence are exposed (no longer buried). The exposed wire mesh has the possibility of catching paws
and causing injury to the wolf dogs. A system of maintenance must be in place to maintain the enclosures,
including keeping the wire mesh buried.
Correction date 20 March, 2016
