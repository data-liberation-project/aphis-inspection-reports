3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The wire that was previously used as hot wire has become detached from the fencing that divides the the bear
enclosure. This loose wire is in the bear pool and near the bottom of the fencing near the bear enclosure. This
loose wire could become entangled with the bears. The bears are digging under the chain link fence which could get
their body parts caught between the sharp edges of the fence and the ground. The outdoor housing facilities need
be maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury.
TO BE CORRECTED BY: JANUARY 26, 2015.
The steel outdoor enclosure housing the bears has numerous rusted areas and holes at the rear walls and ceiling.
There is paint peeling from the the ceiling and the walls of this rusted enclosure. These rusted areas must be
repaired or replaced with new material. The outdoor housing facilities is structurally sound, however it must be
maintained in good repair to protect the animals health and welfare.
TO BE CORRECTED BY: FEBRUARY 24, 2015.
