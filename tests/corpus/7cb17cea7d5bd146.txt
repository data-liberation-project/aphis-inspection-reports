2.31(e) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Several protocols involve teaching the students venipuncture, IM, or intravenous injections. None of the protocols
(with the exception of the rabbit) indicate how much blood will be withdrawn or the amount of fluid that would be
injected into the animals.
These protocols shall be revised, and approved by the IACUC, to indicate the volumes of blood that will be
withdrawn and the amount of fluids that will be injected into each species of animal.
