3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
The front edges of the plastic lining the deer restraint chute is chipped and no longer smooth. There is concern that the
rough edges may cause injury to the deer when they are in the restraint chute. These areas must be repaired or replaced
prior to using the chute again to prevent injury to the animal .
