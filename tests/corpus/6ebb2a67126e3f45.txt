3.127(b)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
The goats, sheep and camel have been moved to a front pasture that lacks shelter from the elements. Natural or
artificial shelter appropriate to the local climatic conditions for the species concerned shall be provided for all
animals kept outdoors to afford them protection and to prevent discomfort to such animals. A shelter or shelters
that can accommodate all the animals needs to be provided.

3.127(d)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
The facility has recently acquired two camels. The camels are required to have a six foot high perimeter fence. No
perimeter fence is in pace at time of this inspection. Facilities not entirely indoors) must be enclosed by a perimeter
fence that is of sufficient height to keep animals and unauthorized persons out. Fences less than 8 feet high for
potentially dangerous animals, such as, but not limited to, large felines (e.g., lions, tigers, leopards, cougars, etc.),
bears, wolves, rhinoceros, and elephants, or less than 6 feet high for other animals must be approved in writing by
the Administrator.
