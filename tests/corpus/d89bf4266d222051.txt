3.131(c)
SANITATION.
Section 3.131(c) Housekeeping: The interior surfaces of the two white plastic public feeding tubes for the enclosure
housing four goats and two sheep have an accumulation of dirt and food residue. The dirt and food residue may
provide an area for the growth of bacteria and/or mold. The food fed by the public through the tubes may become
contaminated and cause the animals to become ill. The inside of the feeding tubes must be kept clean to protect
the health of the animals.
Correct by May 22, 2014.
An exit briefing was conducted with the facility manager.
