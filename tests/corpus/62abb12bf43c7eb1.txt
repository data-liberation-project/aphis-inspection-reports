3.129(a)
FEEDING.
The bags of deer feed are being stacked directly on a cement floor.
Food shall be wholesome, palatable, and free from contamination and of sufficient quantity and nutritive value to
maintain all animals in good health.
Store feed bags off the floors on shelving or pallets to prevent moisture from wicking into the bags and
contaminating the food with mold and mildew.
To be corrected by end of business today.
Inspection and exit conducted with the caretaker.
