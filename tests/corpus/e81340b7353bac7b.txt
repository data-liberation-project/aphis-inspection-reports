2.131(c)(1)

Handling of animals.
The post and rail barrier fence surrounding the reindeer enclosure has several posts and rails that are broken, missing, or
lying on the ground. Exhibit is not currently open to the public this time of year. Public exhibition of animals must be
handled so that there is minimal risk of harm to the animal and to the public, with sufficient distance and/or barriers
between the animal and the public so as to protect the safety of the animal and pubic. The barrier fence surrounding the
reindeer must be repaired or replaced to protect the health of the animal and public. Correct by October 15, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
