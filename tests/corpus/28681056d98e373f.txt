3.75(a)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Rust that compromised the structural integrity of the pipes holding the cyclone fence in place was observe in
primate enclosures with some sharp edges in the corroded ends of the pipes.
Housing facilities for nonhuman primates must be constructed so that they are structurally sound and kept in good
repair, and they must protect the animals from injury, and contain the animals securely.
To be corrected by 3-15-2020
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
