ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee could not locate the written program of veterinary care form at the time of the inspection. The written

2.75(a)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The Record of Dogs on Hand forms are not complete. The microchip number is missing for 10 dogs. The lack of
complete information on these forms makes it difficult to accurately track and monitor the dogs present in the
kennel. These forms shall accurately and completely include all required information, including but not limited to
dog identification number. Correct by 26 February 2015.
3.4(a)(1)(2)
OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
Currently the licensee is housing 8 dogs of different short-haired breeds in outdoor housing facilities without
approval from the attending veterinarian. Short-haired breeds of dogs may not be able to tolerate the cold
temperatures without causing them stress and discomfort. The attending veterinarian must specifically approve
this practice of housing short-haired breeds of dogs in outdoor housing facilities. Correct by 26 February 2015.
