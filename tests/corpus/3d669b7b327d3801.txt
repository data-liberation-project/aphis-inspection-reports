3.31(b)
SANITATION.
The office trailer has 2 guinea pigs, 1 ferret, 1 prairie dog housed there. There are several floor tiles that are broken
or missing. The floor is not sanitizable with the missing and broken tiles. The fluorescence lights in the office trailer
do not have glass covers. In the event the glass shatters, it can cause injury to the animals.
The building shall be kept clean and in good repair in order to protect the animals from injury and to facilitate the
prescribed husbandry practices. Floors, walls, ceilings that are not impervious to moisture cannot be sanitized. For
the health and wellbeing of the animals, a system to routinely monitor and repair the facility(cid:25)s building is to be
created and/or intensified.
