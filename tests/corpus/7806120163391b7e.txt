3.30
WATERING.
It was found during the inspection that on the left side of the building, the water supply system not supplied water to
the nipples, also was found that that side water tank was empty and dirty. The animals were continually biting the
nipples trying to desperately to obtaining water. When the licensee's wife filled the water tank the animals drank for
a long time.
Potable water shall be provided daily, and the water supply system must be checked and clean with the frequency
that is necessary to assure the health and the well-being of the animals, also all watering receptacles shall be
sanitized when dirty: Provided, however, That such receptacles shall be sanitized at least once every 2 weeks.
Correct by: immediately.
This citation was corrected during the inspection.
