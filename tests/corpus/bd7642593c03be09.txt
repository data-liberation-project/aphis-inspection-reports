3.11(c) REPEAT
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
The indoor whelping building which contains mothers and puppies needs to be cleaned to remove the
accumulations of dead flying inspects from the floors, wash downs, interior building fixtures and window ledges.
Premises where housing facilities are located, including buildings and surrounding grounds, must be kept clean and
in good repair to protect the animals from injury, to facilitate husbandry practices and to reduce or eliminate
breeding and living areas for rodents and other pests and vermin and prevent the spread of disease.
Affects two adults and ten puppies.
The inspection and exit interview was conducted with the facility representative.
