2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 2:00 p.m. on
06-JUL-17.The inspector knocked on the door of the house and the kennel and received no answer. The inspector
then called the phone number on file and left a voicemail. After waiting 30 minutes in the driveway of the facility the
inspector left the area.
The licensee must ensure that a knowledgeable adult is available during normal business hours to allow for
inspection.
