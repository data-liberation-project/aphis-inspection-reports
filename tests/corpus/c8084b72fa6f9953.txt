3.13(a)(2)

Veterinary care for dogs.
The licensee has not had the attending veterinarian perform a complete a physical exam of each adult dog in the past 12
months from head to tail. This requirement is important to assure any potential health issues are identified early and to
assure the overall health of the dogs are being monitored.
