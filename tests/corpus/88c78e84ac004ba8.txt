2.40(a)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
On inspection it was found that the licensee had not followed the Program of Veterinary Care in regards to rabies
vaccination. According to the Program of Veterinary Care, rabies will be administered by a veterinarian every 1 to 3
years. Rabies is a deadly disease that can affect both animals and humans. It is vital that you follow the Program of
Veterinary Care and veterinarian to ensure the animals are protected against rabies and other common preventable
diseases. Call a veterinarian to vaccinate your animals for rabies and from this day forward ensure all animals are
vaccinated according to your Program of Veterinary Care.
