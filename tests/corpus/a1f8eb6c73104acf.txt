2.10(c) REPEAT
LICENSEES WHOSE LICENSES HAVE BEEN SUSPENDED OR REVOKED.
The licensee has re-acquired the 10 month old white female wolf 'Dixie' back from the other USDA licensee he
'donated' it to on March 8, 2017, during the suspension period.
There are 10 black bear cubs that were Acquired and transported by the licensee on March 21, 2017, according to
Disposition form 7020(block 6, block 10)) provided by the donor. The licensee advised he was helping another
licensee during this process, however a suspension prohibits any brokering, exhibiting or transporting during the
time of suspension. The licensee currently has 4 of the bear cubs in his possession.
The license was suspended for 90 day beginning October 25, 2016, and continues until such time as the
respondents have demonstrated to APHIS that they are in compliance with the Animal Welfare Act regulations and
standards.

The licensee shall not be involved in any regulated activity until the suspension is lifted. This includes buying,
selling, donating/trading, transporting and exhibiting regulated animals.
Inspection and exit interview was conducted with the licensee.
