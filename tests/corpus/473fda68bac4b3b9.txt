3.76(b)
INDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
The warm room for 2 adult (lemur) primates is not sufficiently ventilated. Ventilation must be provided by windows,
doors, vents, fans or air conditioning. Adequate ventilation is needed to minimize odors, drafts, ammonia levels and
moisture condensation to protect the health and well-being of the primates. The licensee must provide adequate
ventilation as described above.
