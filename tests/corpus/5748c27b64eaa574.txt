Primary enclosures.
Space. Insufficient space was being provided to approximately 58 enclosures housing 20 weanlings in each enclosure.
Enclosures measure 30 inches by 28 inches or 840 square inches. Weanlings weighing up to 350 grams require a least
60 square inches. Can not house more than 14 weanlings in this size enclosure.

In addition, insufficient space was being provided to approximately 150 weanling females in a temporary enclosure on the
floor. Enclosure measured 52.5 inches by 48 inches or 2520 square inches. Can not house more than 42 weanlings in
this size enclosure.

Inadequate space can cause stress, crowding, and aggression in animals. Licensee must ensure that guinea pigs are
provided with at least the minimum space requirements to prevent unnecessary stress and discomfort to the animals.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
