ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
At time of the inspection, the manager stated that the rabbits and kangaroos are undergoing treatment for ear mites
. Treatment recommended by a facility employee was being used .A veterinarian was not consulted prior to
beginning treatment and there was no vet exam to determine the cause of the ear problem in the kangaroos. The
attending veterinarian must have appropriate authority to ensure the provision of adequate veterinary care and to
oversee the adequacy of other aspects of animal care.
