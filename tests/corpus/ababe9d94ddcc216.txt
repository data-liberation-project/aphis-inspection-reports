PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Section 3.28(c)(2)(iv) Space requirements: There are two enclosures with at least one dwarf Russian female
hamster and her litter that are housed with other dwarf Russian hamsters.
1) There is one enclosure housing one dwarf Russian male hamster and three dwarf Russian female hamsters, one
of the females has recently given birth to multiple pink colored babies.

2) There is another enclosure housing four dwarf Russian female hamsters and five small furred young juveniles
from one or multiple litters.

A nursing female dwarf hamster with her litter must be housed in an enclosure that contains no other hamsters and
that provides at least 25 square inches of floor space to comply with the Animal Welfare Act standards. To request
a variance from this standard for the dwarf Russian hamsters please send your written request to:
