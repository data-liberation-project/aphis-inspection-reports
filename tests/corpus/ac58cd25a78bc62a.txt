3.13(a)

Veterinary care for dogs.
The written program of veterinary care (PVC) care was not documented in writing and signed by the attending
veterinarian at the time of inspection. A written PVC for dogs must be established and maintained so that the facility has a
plan for providing adequate preventative care and treatments as necessary. The plan must include all of the required
components as listed in the Animal Welfare Act and Animal Welfare Regulations, Part 3, Subpart A, Section 3.13

Correct by 20 May 2022

This inspection was conducted with the onsite manager and the exit interview was conducted with the facility
representatives and the Attending Veterinarian.
