2.31(d)(1)(9)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The protocol for Feline Onchyectomy does not specify post-operative care for the animals as required. The protocol
refers to an unnamed SOP but no SOP covering post-operative care was available at the time of inspection.
Activities that involve surgery must include appropriate provision for pre-operative and post-operative care of the
animals in accordance with established veterinary medical and nursing practices to assure the well being of the
animals. Correct by assuring there is clearly written guidance specific for the procedure performed for the
post-operative care of all animals receiving surgery. To be corrected before performing surgical procedures.
An exit interview was conducted with the facility IACUC Chair.
