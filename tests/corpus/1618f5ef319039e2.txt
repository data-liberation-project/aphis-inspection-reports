2.27(a)
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF OPERATION.
(a) The facility was bought by another company for at least one month already but has not notified Animal Care
Regional Office on the change of ownership. Change of ownership, or other change in operations affecting its
status shall notify Animal Care within ten working days after making such change. Facility shall ensure that such
change is transmitted to Animal care in a timely manner.
