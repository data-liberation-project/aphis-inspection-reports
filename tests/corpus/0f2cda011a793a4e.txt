2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The licensee has a written program of veterinary care and an attending veterinarian for her facility. The program of
veterinary care states that the attending veterinarian will make an annual visit to provide adequate veterinary care to the
facility’s animals. It has been over a year since the last visit to the facility. The licensee last made contact with the
attending veterinarian in June 2019. The licensee must schedule a visit from the attending veterinarian in accordance with
the written program of veterinary care.

Correct by: May 16, 2021

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility manager.
