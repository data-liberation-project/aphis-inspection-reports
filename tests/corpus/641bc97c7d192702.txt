3.3(b)
SHELTERED HOUSING FACILITIES.
The indoor portion of the sheltered whelping facility did not have sufficient ventilation and smelled of ammonia. The
facility had two small fans in the side window of the building, only one of which was working. The window was
closed with a 1 inch opening at the bottom. Breathing air with high ammonia levels can lead to health
complications. The licensee shall provide sufficient ventilation to minimize odors and reduce ammonia levels.TO BE
