3.129(b)
FEEDING.
At the time of inspection, the wooden trough used for feed in the capybara's enclosure was worn and chewed down.
A nail was exposed on the right side of the food receptacle. The exposed nail can pose a risk of injury and harm to
the animal. Food receptacles must be kept clean and in good repair at all times.
Correct by: November 13, 2016
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility manager.
