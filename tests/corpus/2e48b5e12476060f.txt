ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The attending veterinarian's last annual visit was on May 4, 2016. This was affirmed with the attending veterinarian
via a telephone call. The veterinarian offered to make his annual visit in two days.

program of veterinary care (which licensee did have) and regularly scheduled visits (annually is the minimum) to the
premises of the dealer to ensure the provision of adequate veterinary care and to oversee the adequacy of other
aspects of animal care and use.
