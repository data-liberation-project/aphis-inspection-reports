2.131(d)(2)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
During the inspection it was found that in the exhibition of the kangaroos the responsible worker remained in the
entry while the public had free access to interact with the animals.
A responsible, knowledgeable, and readily identifiable employee or attendant must be present at all times during
periods of public contact, supervising the contact to prevent any type of incidents that may affect the health and
well-being of the animals and/or the safety of the public
Correct by: from now forward.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the park manager.
