2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The facility has had their veterinarian visit for animals needing care, however the written Program of Veterinary Care
(PVC) has not been completed. A written PVC is necessary to ensure that regulated animals receive appropriate
veterinary care. The facility must contact the attending veterinarian and ensure that a written PVC is completed and
made available if requested by APHIS officials.
