3.125(a) REPEAT
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals from
injury and to contain the animals.
5 goats,1 pig and 1 cow have access to the wooded pasture area that contains a large debris heap containing large
branches, sticks, wooden pallets, and assorted trash items. The trash items may be potential hazards that could
cause injury to the animals that have access to this area.
The hazards must be removed from the pasture or the animals precluded from entering the hazardous area.
Correct by November 8, 2017.
A shelter structure consisting of wood boards, wire fencing and metal sheeting is in severe disrepair such that it
does not provide adequate shelter and can be potentially injurious to the pig, goats, and cow that have access to
this structure. The structure needs to be repaired, secured, or removed in order to protect the animals from
potential injury.
Correct by November 1, 2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representatives.
