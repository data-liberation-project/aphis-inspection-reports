2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The following medications were found to be past their expiration dates:
Lidocaine 2% (2 bottles) 9-15
Sodium Acetate 1 Sep 2013
Heparin 5/2016
Glycopyrrolate 3/2015
Epinephrine 12-15
Dopram Injection 6/2016
Atropine Sulfate 6-16
These medications were maintained with the medications that are currently in date. The use of outdated
medications is not considered adequate veterinary care. Utilizing medications that are in date ensures that the
mammals involved are receiving adequate veterinary care for their health and well-being.
The outdated medications were removed and placed in a container labeled "EXPIRED" to prevent usage on exhibit
mammals.
