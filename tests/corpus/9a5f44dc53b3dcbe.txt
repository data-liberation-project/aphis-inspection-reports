3.131(d)
SANITATION.
One Bennet Wallaby & 2 Swamp Wallabies were killed by a rattlesnake(s) which were observed on the property.
These attacks were on 7/15/15, 12/20/15, & 12/24/15 and occurred in different pens. These snakes are still at
large.
These snakes pose a definite risk of harm (and death) for all of the marsupials on the property.
Consult with a professional reptile trapper for a property assessment and plan for trapping these snakes.
