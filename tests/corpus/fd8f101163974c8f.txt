2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
A current / updated written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) was not available for inspection. In order to comply
with this section of the Animal Welfare Act (AWA) a written PVC must be in place. The establishment shall identify
and employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements, who could write a PVC plan which would include
regularly scheduled visits to the premises where the animals are kept. The attending veterinarian should conduct at
a minimum an annual visit, to ensure adequate veterinary medical care to animals.
