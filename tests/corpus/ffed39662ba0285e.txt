3.130

Watering.
Two ferrets are in an indoor enclosure. Water was not accessible at the start of the inspection. Water was then provided
and both ferrets drank readily for approximately 10 minutes.

Water must be provided as often as necessary for the health and comfort of the animal and to prevent excessive thirst
and dehydration. Correct by providing access to drinkable water more often. Corrected during the inspection.
