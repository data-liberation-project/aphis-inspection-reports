3.53(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
One rabbit cages is stacked on top of another cage . The cage moves when touched . Stacking a cage directly on
top of each other is not a safe way to house rabbits as the top cage may fall .The rabbit in the top cage can not
move about the cage without it moving. The cage must be moved to a more secure location to prevent injury to the
animal.
