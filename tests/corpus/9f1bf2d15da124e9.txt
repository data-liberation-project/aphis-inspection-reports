2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
**At the time of inspection no program of veterinary care had been reviewed by the new attending veterinarian. The
licensee should ensure the new attending veterinarian has reviewed and provided a new written program of veterinary
care in addition to regularly scheduled visits to the premises. Formal arrangements including a written program of
veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the facility are required in order to ensure adequate veterinary care is
provided to the animals as provided by this section.
Correct by: From this date forward

This inspection and exit interview was conducted with the licensee.
