3.13(a)(3)

Veterinary care for dogs.
The written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) is not complete. There is no schedule approved by the Attending
Veterinarian (AV) addressing the sampling for and the treatment of parasites and other pests including fleas, worms,
coccidia, giardia, and heartworm. Lack of a complete and approved PVC does not ensure that proper veterinary care is
being provided to the animals. The AV must approve a schedule for sampling and treating parasites and other pests.
3.13(a)(4)

Veterinary care for dogs.
The written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) is not complete. The PVC must include measures for preventative care
and treatment to ensure healthy and unmatted hair coats, properly trimmed nails, and clean and healthy ears, eyes, skin
and teeth. Lack of a complete and approved PVC doesn't ensure that proper veterinary care is being provided to the
animals. The PVC must address preventative care and treatments.
