3.127(c)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Heavy rain several days ago has resulted in large standing puddles of water in the enclosure housing 2 warthogs .
There is very little dry area in the main exhibit. Standing water creates a breeding ground for pests such as
mosquitos and flies. The animals may suffer foot problems if they must remain in muddy wet areas for long periods
of time. A method must be develop to rapidly eliminate standing water to prevent pest and foot problems .
