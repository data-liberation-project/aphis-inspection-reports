3.28(b)(3)(iii)

Primary enclosures.
Space. Insufficient space was being provided in two weanling enclosures that measure 78 inches by 24 inches or 1872
square inches of floor space. The top weanling enclosure had approximately 43 weanlings and the bottom enclosure had
40 weanlings. Weanling guinea pigs weighing up to 350 grams required at least 60 inches of floor space each. Can not
house more than 31 weanlings in this size enclosure.

Insufficient space can cause stress, overcrowding, and aggression in guinea which can lead to injuries and increased
health risks. Licensee must ensure that guinea pigs are provided with at least the minimum amount of floor space per
animal to prevent stress and aggression. Correct by December 22, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
