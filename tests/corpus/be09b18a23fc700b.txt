FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The junction box in the coatimundi building is no longer functioning. According to staff, sparks occurred in this
junction box so the power was shut off. Currently, there is no light available inside of this building for proper
husbandry and observations. Malfunctioning junction boxes are extremely dangerous for the coatimundi as well as
the staff working in the building. This junction box must be repaired, by a licensed electrician, to ensure that the box
is functioning properly and for the safety of the coatimundi housed therein.
Additionally, there is no power in the feed storage room. A licensed electrician must also repair the power in this
room so that ample lighting is available. Ample lighting is necessary for the adequate inspection of feed supplies to
ensure that it is wholesome and free of pests.
