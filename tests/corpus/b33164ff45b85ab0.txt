2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The program of veterinary care (PVC) is incomplete. There is no information on the type, dosage, and route of
administration of ivermectin for the internal and external parasite control. The facility personnel must have adequate
guidance from the attending veterinarian on the concentration, instructions for use, dosage, frequency and
administration of all medications and drugs. The program of veterinary care must be complete to provide adequate
guidance to the facility personnel to maintain the health of the animals. The program of veterinary care must include
the type of ivermectin, the concentration, instructions for use, dosage, frequency and route of administration so that
animals receive the appropriate amount of parasite control. Correct by May 14, 2016.
An exit briefing was conducted with facility representative.
