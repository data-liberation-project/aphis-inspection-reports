2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11:30 a.m. on
04/08/2016. The inspector knocked on the front door of the residence multiple times. After no response, the
inspector called the licensee's phone number and left a message. The inspector also honked his vehicle's horn and
waited on site for 30 minutes.
