2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
On 19 March 2014, a responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS officials on an animal welfare
inspection. Availability for these inspections is important to assure the health and well-being of the animals. Correct
by: Calling 919-855-7100 and informing an Individual Licencing Assisant (ILA) if there are times or days of the week
when a responsible adult is likely to be present.
