2.40(b)(2)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The licensee obtained information from the Attending Veterinarian (AV) regarding the care of the 11-month-old red
kangaroo in response to the citation from the last inspection. These instructions for feeding this kangaroo specified Mazuri
Kangaroo pelleted diet, however, it was being fed pelleted rabbit diet. Also, the AV instructions did not contain
information regarding the feeding of the milk replacer, which was being fed according to label instructions.

Proper nutrition is necessary to maintain the health and well-being of animals in captivity.

The facility must seek guidance from the attending AV for the care, including nutrition, for new species or those with
special needs, such as young or geriatric animals, that are maintained. Currently, the facility must obtain and follow
further instructions from the AV related to the feeding of the kangaroo.

Correct by: 20 July 2021

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Licensee.
