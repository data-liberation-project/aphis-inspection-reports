FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Sec. 3.50 Facilities, general.
The pen, housing 4 young rabbits, is comprised of old worn wood and wiring with holes. Sharp points are protruding
into the enclosure with the rabbits and creating an unsafe enviroment. Facilities for rabbits shall be structurally
sound and shall be maintained in good repair and protect the animals from injury.
Correction was made at the time of this inspection.
This inspection and exit interview was conducted by the ACI, with the facility owner.
