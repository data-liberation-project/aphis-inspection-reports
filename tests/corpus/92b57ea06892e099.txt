3.6(a)(2)(1) REPEAT
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
(a) General requirements (2)(i)
The 2nd enclosure and the 3rd enclosure on the bottom of the outdoor portion of the white sheltered facility had
sections of wire coated flooring that was broken. There were sharp wires protruding in the dog area and sections of
flooring with wire missing. Broken wire and sharp points can cause injury to the animals. The licensee shall repair or
replace the flooring and ensure that there are no sharp points or edges on the surface of the primary enclosures
that could harm the animals.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the licensee.
