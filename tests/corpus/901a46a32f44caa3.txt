2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
Four female Nubian goats the facility acquired on 3/12/22 did not have the name of the person from whom the animals
were acquired, USDA license number or the vehicle license number with state, and the driver’s license number. Records
containing the required information for animals acquired are necessary to accurately track animals being used in
regulated activities to ensure their humane care and use. Licensee shall make, keep, and maintain records of acquisition
for all animals that fully and correctly disclose the required information.
To be corrected by: Next Inspection
