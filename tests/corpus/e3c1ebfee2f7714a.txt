3.31(b)
SANITATION.
Housekeeping. There are accumulations of excreta, shavings, and debris along the walls and window ledges in the
guinea pigs rooms. Animal facilities must be kept clean to ensure good quality of air for the animals and to prevent
unnecessary disease risks. Ineffective cleaning exposes the animals to disease hazards and health problems.
Correct by ensuring the premises are kept clean especially along walls and window ledges. Licensee must establish
a program to kept the floor and window ledges clean.
Correct by January 31, 2015
An exit briefing was conducted with the owner.
