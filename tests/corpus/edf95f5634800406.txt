3.56(b)(1)
SANITATION.
The 2 male rabbit enclosures had accumulation of waste material in the corners making it difficult to clean and
sanitize.
In order to prevent health issues, primary enclosures must be sanitized at least once every 30 days.
To be corrected by 8-15-2015
3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The white back board in one pig enclosure had chewed corners, The umbrellas providing shade to the pig and goat
in the back of the facility were bended out of shape due to rain and wind.
In order to provide proper shade and to avoid injury to the animals, facilities must be kept in good repair.
To be corrected by 8-15-2015
3.127(c)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Several enclosures with pigs, cow, and goats had accumulation of rain water creating a muddy environment without
providing an area that is dry if the animals decided to be out of the mud.
In order to avoid discomfort and health issues to the animals, enclosures shall be provided with a suitable method to
rapidly eliminate excess water.
To be corrected by 8-15-2015.
An exit interview was conducted with the facility representative.
