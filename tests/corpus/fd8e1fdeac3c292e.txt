3.81
ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
Section 3.81 Environment enhancement to promote psychological well-being: The facility written plan for
environment enhancement to promote psychological well-being of the nonhuman primates could not be located
during this inspection. If the plan cannot be found then another plan for environment enhancement must be written
in accordance with the currently accepted professional standards as cited in appropriate professional journals or
reference guides, and as directed by the attending veterinarian. This plan must be available to APHIS upon
request. Correct by June 24, 2016.
