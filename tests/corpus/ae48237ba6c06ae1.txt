2.33(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
A vial of expired buprenorphine (expiration 11/16; Lot/bottle B5-15) and a vial of expired xylazine (expiration 16 July;
Lot 4G025) were stored with other medications and confirmed to have been used in ferrets and pigs after the
expiration dates. The use of expired medications can cause adverse effects to animals. The facility must
ensure that expired medications are not used in animals and are properly disposed of.
Correct from this day forward.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
