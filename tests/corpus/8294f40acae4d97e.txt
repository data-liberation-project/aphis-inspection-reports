2.31(c)(7)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
In ongoing activities included in three protocols, the number of dogs utilized did not conform to the number of animals
justified in the protocols and approved by the IACUC. The protocols were approved for four dogs, but seven dogs were
used.
The IACUC must be able to review any modifications to the proposed use of animals in order to assure the proper care
and use of animals.
Proposed modifications to a protocol must be submitted to and approved by the IACUC.
Correct by: April 28, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
