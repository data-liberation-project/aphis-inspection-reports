3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The following structural items were present:
1) The petting zoo fence on the west side has protruding wire, bent wire, and loose wire strands.
2) The wooden goat platform by the playground has protruding screws on two ends and a chewed and broken wall
on the west side.
Outdoor housing facilities must be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals from
injury and to contain the animals. The fence on the west side and the wooded platform must be repaired or replaced
to prevent possible injury to the animals and to contain the animals securely. Correct by July 8, 2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the manager.
