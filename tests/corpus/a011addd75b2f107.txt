2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 4:30 pm on
02/17/2016.
The inspector was at the facility between 4:15pm and 5:10pm. No one answered the door. The inspector called
both phone numbers on file and there was no answer. A message was left at 4:50pm. Inspector left at 5:10pm.
The licensee returned the call at 5:30pm and let the inspector know times and dates in the future when they would
be available for an inspection.
