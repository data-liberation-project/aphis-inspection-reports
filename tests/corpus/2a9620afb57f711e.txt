3.13(b)(3)

Veterinary care for dogs.
The date(s) of the last parvovirus and distemper vaccines administered were not available for all the adult dogs. Failure to
list the names and dates of all vaccines and treatments administered makes it difficult the access the animal care
program. Licensee must ensure that medical records are kept with the names and dates of all vaccines and treatments
administered to the adult dogs. Correct by October 15, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
