2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection
process at 2pm-5pm on 03/04/2016. The inspector knocked on the residence door, called both listed phone
numbers, left voice messages and waited in attempt to contact the registrant. Registrants must provide valid contact
information and business hours within which they are available for inspection.
