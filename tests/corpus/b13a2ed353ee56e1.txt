3.1(b)

Housing facilities, general.
There are several items being stored inside the main housing area for the dogs in the center aisle and along the walls. In
addition to the regular husbandry supplies there are materials for building, boards stored over runs, tools, empty crates,
supply bags of dog food, etc, cluttering the center aisle. Cleaning supplies, medications, supplements, etc. are also
stored with these items. Animal areas inside of housing facilities must be kept neat and free of clutter. Building materials,
tools, etc shall not be stored openly in the housing area to prevent accumulations and clutter. Husbandry materials shall
be stored in a manner that will protect them from contamination to allow proper husbandry practices.

To be corrected by January 15, 2022
