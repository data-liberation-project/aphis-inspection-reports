3.28(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Several of the enclosures have areas were the white paint is worn or scratched off exposing the bare wood. The
enclosures with areas of bare wood must be resealed so that the surfaces are impervious to moisture. Primary
enclosures for guinea pigs must be impervious so that they can be repeatedly cleaned and disinfected. Correct by
resealing enclosures with any bare wood.
