2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The written program of veterinary care provided during the inspection does not include the goats. The goats must
be added to the written program of veterinary care on file. The written program of veterinary care must include all
animals so that the attending veterinarian can provide guidance to the facility to prevent and control diseases.
