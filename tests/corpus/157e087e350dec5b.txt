MISCELLANEOUS.
There was no one available during business hours to allow APHIS Officials to enter and inspect the facilities,
property, records, and animals on16-Apr-19 at 1:30pm.
The inspector arrived at the facility at 1:30 PM on April 16, 2019 and knocked on the door. After two minutes, the
inspector knocked again. No one answered the door. At 1:35 PM the inspector called the phone number on file for
the registrant. No one answered so the inspector left a voicemail. At 1:56 PM the registrant called back and
informed the inspector that he was at work and would not be available to accompany the inspector on an inspection
during normal business hours.
