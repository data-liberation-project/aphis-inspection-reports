2.32(a)
PERSONNEL QUALIFICATIONS.
***Protocol 10-045A, Section 19 D listed the individuals involved with the care of the animals, however, did not
indicate the experience of the personnel with the species and/or procedures involved. Qualifications of the
personnel involved with the care and treatment of the animals should be provided. It is the responsibility of the
research facility to ensure that all personnel involved in the care and use of the animals are qualified to perform their
duties as set forth in this section in order to promote the health and well-being of the animals.
