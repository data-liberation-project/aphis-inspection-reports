2.32(c)(3)
PERSONNEL QUALIFICATIONS.
On March 24th, 2015 the improper use of anesthesia equipment caused complications in an anesthetized pig. The
pig was humanely euthanized while still under anesthesia due to the complications. To prevent adverse effects to
animals, employees must be adequately trained on the proper use of anesthesia equipment prior to using it.
Note: The clinical veterinarian and IACUC were promptly notified of the incident and corrective actions were put in
place by the facility to ensure lab personnel are properly trained and qualified to use the anesthesia equipment.
Correction: Ensure corrective actions are properly followed.
Exit interview conducted with facility representatives.
