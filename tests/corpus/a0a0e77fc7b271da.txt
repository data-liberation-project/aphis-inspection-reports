2.126(a)(2)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Facility was unable to produce the program of veterinary care for inspection and confirmation of annual visit by
attending veterinarian. Required records under the Animal Welfare Act must be available for review during routine
inspections.
A filing system allowing retrieval of required records needs to be in place and maintained.
