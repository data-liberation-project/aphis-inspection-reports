2.76(a)(3)
RECORDS: OPERATORS OF AUCTION SALES AND BROKERS.
Every broker shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the following
information concerning each animal consigned for auction or sold, whether or not a fee or commission is charged:
(3) The USDA license or registration number of the person(s) selling, consigning, buying, or receiving the animals
if he or she is licensed or registered under the Act. The USDA number or Driver license number must be entered
into the records for sales of animals. Several sales do not have this required information listed.
Correct from this date forward.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
