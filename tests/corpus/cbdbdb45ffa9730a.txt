3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
The primary enclosure housing a male dromedary camel, and a male llama has a single shelter. The outside of this
shelter is constructed of metal. There are two areas, approximately 18" wide, in the front of the enclosure where the
metal is pulled out at the bottom.

The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the
animals from injury.

Repair, or replace the panels of metal that are pulled out at the bottom of the shelter for the male camel, and male llama
to prevent injury to the animals contained in the enclosure.

To be corrected by November 4, 2020.
2.75(b)(1)(iv)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The licensee sold a Grivet monkey to an individual and did not acquire the individuals address for the disposition record.


Every exhibitor shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the following
information concerning animals other than dogs and cats, sold - The name and address of the person to whom an animal
was sold or given.

Accurate records with the required information must be kept to ensure animals are not being used for regulated purposes,
and so that tracking and tracing can be completed for all animals acquired and sold in case of disease outbreak.

To be corrected from this day forward.
2.132(d)

Procurement of dogs, cats, and other animals; dealers.
The licensee has acquired 2 bactrian camels. The licensees spouse stated that they found that the individual they
acquired the camels from is not licensed with the USDA, APHIS, AC.

No exhibitor shall knowingly obtain animals from any person who is required to be licensed but who does not hold a
current, valid, and unsuspended license.

All animals must be acquired from USDA, APHIS, AC licensed facilities when a license is required. Animals acquired
must come from licensed facilities to ensure that the facility is not in violation the Animal Welfare Act and that animals
held and sold are being kept to meet the Animal Welfare Act regulations and standards.

To be corrected from this day forward.
