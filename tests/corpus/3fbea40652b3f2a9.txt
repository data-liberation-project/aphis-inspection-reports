3.1(a)

Housing facilities, general.
In the outdoor section, of one enclosure, the ramp leading down to the ground level had come unattached and was laying
on the ground. There was one dog that could not access the inside area of the enclosure.
Licensee must ensure that the housing facilities are be kept in good repair and structurally sound to protect the animals
from injury.
Licensee corrected this at the time of inspection..

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with licensee.
