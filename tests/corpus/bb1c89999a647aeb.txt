2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
--The Program of Vet Care was out of date and the last recorded site visit by the attending veterinarian was 9/13/16.
Good communication and consultation between the licensee and the attending veterinarian in the form of a written
program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises are necessary for the health of the
animals. The licensee must schedule a site visit with the attending veterinarian affecting 9 dogs. The licensee must
ensure that the program of veterinary care is updated with the current preventative practices to ensure that all
animals are provided adequate veterinary care. The program of veterinary care must be made available for review
during inspections.
To be corrected by: 11/8/2017.
