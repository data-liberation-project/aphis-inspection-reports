3.25(d)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Waste disposal. Water is leaking on the floor from the automatic water lines. Spilled and loose hay on the floor is
allowing the water to accumulate creating a foul and smelly condition. Provisions must be made for the removal
and disposal of animal waste, food waste, bedding and debris to minimize vermin infestations, odors, and disease
hazards. Correct by ensuring the water lines and gutters are operating properly and cleaned so that stagnant liquid
waste and debris are not accumulating on the floor to minimize infestations, odors and disease hazards. Correct by
November 2, 2018.
3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
There are at least 19 tubs housing approximately 202 guinea pigs with their offspring with accumulations of excreta
and soiled bedding. More excreta than bedding were present in these enclosures. Accumulations of excreta and
soiled bedding exposes the guinea pigs to potential infections with bacteria, fungal, and parasites. Correct by
ensuring that all primary enclosures are cleaned and sanitized often enough to prevent an accumulation of excreta
and soiled bedding, and that enclosures are sanitized at least once every 2 weeks. Correct by November 1, 2018.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner's spouse.
