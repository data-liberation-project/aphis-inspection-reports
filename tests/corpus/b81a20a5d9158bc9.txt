HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Enclosure #22 contains two (2) Jack Russell Terriers. Within this enclosure is a strip of wire sticking up from the
bottom, into the floor. The points of the wire are sharp, and in direct contact with the dogs. This condition of the wire
could cause injury to the dogs within the enclosure. Surfaces in contact with the animals must be free of jagged
edges or sharp points that might injure the animals.
