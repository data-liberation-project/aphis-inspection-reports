2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
At the time of inspection, the licensee did not have an animal inventory. The lack of inventory and the information
contained therein, can prevent thorough inspection and affect animal welfare. Every exhibitor shall make, keep, and
maintain records which fully and correctly disclose the following information concerning animals other than dogs and cats,
purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, or otherwise in his or her possession or under his or her control, or
which is transport, sold, euthanized, or other wise disposed by that exhibitor. The licensee must create and maintain a
complete and accurate animal inventory.
