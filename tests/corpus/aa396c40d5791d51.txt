3.56(a)(3)

Sanitation.
The large enclosure housing 8 weaned rabbits had more than one weeks accumulation of excreta under the wire flooring.
Troughs or pans under rabbit enclosures must be cleaned at least one each week. Failure to removed excreta from
troughs or pans weekly can increase the risk of disease, odors, and unsanitary conditions. Correct by cleaning excreta
and debris from troughs or pans at least weekly. Correct by January 28, 2023.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a family member.
