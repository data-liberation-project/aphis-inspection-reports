2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 3:45 pm on
05/10/2016.
Inspector arrived at the facility at 3:45pm and spoke to an individual at the facility who stated that the licensee had
just left. Inspector called the phone number, left a message, waited 30 minutes and then left the facility.
