3.131(a)
SANITATION.
Goat & sheep pens: The pens have a buildup of feces and the animals have no clean areas to rest. These animals
are not able to avoid the fecal material in their pens. Excreta shall be removed from primary enclosures as often as
necessary to prevent contamination of the animals contained therein and to minimize disease hazards and to
reduce odors. Facility was unable to clean the goat pen recently, and a system of adequate cleaning must be
established.
Correction date: 1 October, 2018
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
