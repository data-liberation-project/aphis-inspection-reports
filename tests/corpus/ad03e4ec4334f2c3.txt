3.138(a)
PRIMARY CONVEYANCES (MOTOR VEHICLE, RAIL, AIR, AND MARINE).
THE TRAILER USED TO TRANSPORT RIDE ANIMALS, (ZEBUS AND LLAMAS), HAS A RUSTED CRACKED
AREA ALONG THE WHEEL WELL. THIS AREA HAS JAGGED METAL EDGES THAT AN ANIMAL COULD GET
CUT ON. THIS WHEEL WELL NEEDS TO BE REPAIRED TO PREVENT INJURY TO AN ANIMAL.
