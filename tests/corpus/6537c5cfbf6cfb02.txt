3.31(b)
SANITATION.
Housekeeping. The floor contains accumulations of scattered hay, bedding and debris. There are numerous
cobwebs on the upper walls and ceiling in animal room. Animal facilities must be kept clean to ensure good quality
care and to prevent unnecessary disease risks. Ineffective cleaning and housekeeping can expose the animals to
disease hazards and health problems. Correct by ensuring the premises including the floor, walls, and ceiling are
kept clean by removing scattered hay, bedding, debris, and cobwebs from the animal room.
Correct by December 1, 2016.
This inspection was conducted with the owner's son and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
