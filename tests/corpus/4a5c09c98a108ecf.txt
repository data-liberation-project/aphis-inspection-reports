3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
Cleaning and Sanitation of primary enclosure.
The guinea pig enclosure next to the front desk had an excessive accumulation of feces inside the enclosure.
There were excessive amount of flies flying inside and around the enclosure. The accumulation of feces can cause
odor problems, attract pests, and other disease hazards. The enclosure must be cleaned as often as necessary to
prevent the accumulation of excreta.
Corrected at the time of inspection.
Inspection and exit interview was conducted by APHIS personnel with facility representative.
