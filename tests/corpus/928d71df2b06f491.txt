2.134(c)

Contingency planning.
This facility has developed a contingency plan however there is no indication that employees have been trained on the
plan . It is important that employees are trained on the plan so a proper response can occur in case of an emergency .
Dealers, exhibitors, intermediate handlers, and carriers must provide training for their personnel regarding their roles and
responsibilities as outlined in the plan. Newly hired employees must be trained on the plan within 30 days of their start
date. Any substantive changes to the plan as a result of the annual review must be communicated to employees through
training which must be conducted within 30 days of making the changes.
