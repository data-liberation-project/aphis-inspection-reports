2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC reviewed the facility's program of humane care in October 2017 and again September 2018. Eleven
months have elapsed between each review.The humane care and use program for animals shall be reviewed at
least once every six months to verify that any animal use at the facility meets the standards and requirements under
the Animal Welfare Act.
Correct by: Future program reviews should be conducted every six months.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the IACUC Chair and Program Chair.
