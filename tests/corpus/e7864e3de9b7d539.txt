SANITATION.
Housekeeping. There is a pile of empty feed bags by the wall and loose and scattered hay on the floor around the
feed pallets in the barn. Clutter and miscellaneous items are being stored on top of animal shelters in the barn.

bags, storing all trash properly, cleaning the barn floor of loose hay, and removing clutter and stored items from the
