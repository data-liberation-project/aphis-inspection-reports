3.29
FEEDING.
***** In the building housing the Gerbils, hamsters and hedgehogs there was a bin of Rodent block food that had
deteriorated to mainly powder with scattered whole blocks present. This powdered feed and scattered whole rodent
blocks had an excessive accumulation of live and dead insects present in the feed. Food must be nutritive,
palliative and wholesome for the health and well-being of the animals. The licensee must establish and maintain
food that is free from contamination, wholesome, palatable and of sufficient quantity and nutritive value to meet the
normal daily requirements for the size and species being fed.
To be corrected by: October 14, 2014
Inspection and exit interview was conducted with the licensee.
