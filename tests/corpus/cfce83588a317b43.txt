ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 4:30 PM on
18-Jan-18.
APHIS inspector arrived at the facility at 4:30 PM on Thursday, January 18. The licensee was not on site, however
the inspector spoke with another individual on the premises. She informed the inspector that the licensee was not
available for an inspection, as he was in town, and she would not conduct an inspection. She gave APHIS inspector
a copy of the licensee's work schedule. The inspector informed her that this would be an attempted inspection.
