2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 2:00 PM on
26-JUL-18.
The inspector contacted a couple of individuals in person at the facility. They informed the inspector that the
licensee was not home and would not be available for the rest of the day. They said there was no authorized person
available to accompany the inspector on an inspection. The inspector reached the licensee by phone and she
confirmed that this was true.
