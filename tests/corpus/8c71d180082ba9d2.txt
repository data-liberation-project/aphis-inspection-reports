3.26(d)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
Interior surfaces. The back door in the guinea pig room has peeling paint and unsealed wood. Unsealed wood in
animal rooms can become wet providing an optimal area for growth of bacteria and mold which can cause animals
to become ill. The back door must be resealed or replaced so that the surface is impervious to moisture to allow it to
be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized.
