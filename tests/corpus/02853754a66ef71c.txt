HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The following metal points and edges were observed:
-The ends of a wire panel were protruding into the new exercise yard.
-The small shed in the new exercise yard has bent metal panels with a jagged edge.
-Wire points turned out into the old exercise yard.
-Two nail heads protruding into an enclosure in the whelping building.
Metal points and edges can cause injury to the dogs. The licensee must ensure that any surfaces that come in
contact with dogs or cats must be free of jagged edges or sharp points that might injure the animals.
