ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
The plan for environment enhancement adequate to promote the psychological well-being of nonhuman primates
had not been reviewed by the new veterinarian. The current plan does not accurately reflect the significant human
interaction that Chloe receives during the day. The plan for environmental enhancement needs to be approved by
the current veterinarian and accurately reflect the environmental enhancement for Chloe.
