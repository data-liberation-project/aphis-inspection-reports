ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
** A seven year old, female, white Poodle, ID # 0A01450667. Had a build up of dark brown tartar on the canine and
cheek teeth. This can be a sign of dental disease which can be painful, cause tooth loss and other health problems.
The licensee has been instructed by the attending veterinarian on how to control and treat dental disease. The
licensee stated that he has not been following the instructions given by the veterinarian. The licensee must follow
the instruction of the veterinarian to properly treat the dog.
This inspection and exit briefing was conducted with the licensee.
