2.33(a)(3)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
The Attending Veterinarian is currently listed as a non-voting member of the IACUC. Without the presence of
another veterinarian with delegated program responsibility being appointed to the IACUC the Attending Veterinarian
shall be a voting member. To prevent any conflict of interest the Attending Veterinarian shall not vote on protocol in
which they are the investigator or co-investigator. Correct by conclusion of the next semi-annual inspection in the
fall of 2016.
The inspection and exit interview conducted with the Instructor.
