2.33(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care.
The facility has an attending veterinarian that has made visits and is an active member of the IACUC, however reports the
written program of veterinary care is in progress with a part-time attending veterinarian. To ensure that adequate
veterinary care is available for the animals at the facility, a written program of veterinary care is required to be in place
and available for review by APHIS officials. Ensure that the formal arrangements with a part-time attending veterinarian
include a written program of veterinary care.

Correct by October 26, 2021.
