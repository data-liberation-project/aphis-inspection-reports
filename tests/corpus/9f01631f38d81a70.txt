Housing facilities, general.

injure the dogs. The rusted surfaces can not be properly cleaned or sanitized.

** At least three enclosures had unsealed wood, wood that is not impervious to moisture can not be properly cleaned and

The rusted surfaces must be repaired and/or replaced. The unsealed wood must be made impervious to moisture. All
housing facilities must be kept in good repair, free from rust, sharp points and jagged edges. All surfaces must be easily
