2.131(d)(2)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
At the time of inspection, there were no employees present at location of the petting zoo. The general public was
observed interacting with the animals by giving feed that was available for purchase. A responsible, knowledgeable
and readily identifiable employee must be present at all times during period of public contact to protect both the
animals and the public.
Correct by: May 23, 2018
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility manager.
