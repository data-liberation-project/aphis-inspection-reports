2.1(b)(1)

Requirements and application
The capuchin monkey, Elvis, has been kept at the licensee's home since the pandemic shutdowns began. There was no
notification to the Agency of the move. The home site is not covered under the license.
A license is issued to specific persons and for approved sites. The licensee must notify Animal Care at least 90 days prior
to a substantial change in business, such as location of the animal.
Keeping Animal Care informed as to the location of the covered animal allows for the required unannounced inspections
to ensure compliance with the Animal Welfare Act.
Correct by immediately notifying Animal Care of the new location of Elvis.

Access and inspection of records and property; submission of itineraries.

Correct by ensuring records are available for inspection from this point forward.
