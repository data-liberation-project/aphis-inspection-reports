3.131(d)

Sanitation.
Excessive numbers of flies were observed in and around the armadillo and sloth enclosures. The flies were observed on
the armadillo’s bedding, near the food dishes, and on the sloth’s enclosure floor. Additionally, an excessive number of
flies were observed inside the light fixture, above the sloth’s enclosure. There were no fly control provisions in place
during the time of inspection. Flies can carry and spread diseases. Implement and maintain a program to effectively
control the flies.
Correct by: 10-4-21

The inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
