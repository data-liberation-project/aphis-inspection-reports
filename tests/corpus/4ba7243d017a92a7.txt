2.126(a)

Access and inspection of records and property; submission of itineraries.
**During the inspection, the Program of Veterinary Care was not available for review. The facility representative indicated
the person responsible for maintaining records was on vacation and they were unable to locate the Program of Veterinary
Care. Current records should be maintained in a manner that allows access by APHIS officials whenever unannounced
inspections are conducted. It is the responsibility of the licensee to ensure APHIS officials are able to examine records
required by the Animal Welfare Act in order to ensure the safety and health of the animals housed at the facility.
