3.25(c)

Facilities, general.
Storage. There is a large hole in the lid of the food container and an open bag of shavings on the floor of the animal room.
Food and bedding supplies must be stored in a manner that protects supplies from spoilage, deterioration, insects and
vermin. Food containers are required to have tightly fitted lids or covers. Licensee must ensure that open food supplies
are stored in containers with lids or covers and that open bedding/shavings are stored in a manner that protects supplies
from contamination. Correct by December 18, 2021.
3.30

Watering.
The water bottles have a layer of green algae growing on the inside surfaces. All water receptacles must be sanitized
when dirty, and receptacles must be sanitized at least once 2 weeks to ensure water is potable/drinkable. Licensee must
ensure that water bottles are sanitized when dirty and at least once every 2 weeks. Correct by December 14, 2021.
