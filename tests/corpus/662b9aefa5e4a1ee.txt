2.75(a)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The Record of Dogs and Cats on Hand (APHIS form 7005) does not correctly disclose all of the dogs present at the
facility. The facility currently has 25 adult dogs with only 21 adult dogs listed on the APHIS form 7005. Inaccurate or
incomplete records do not allow for APHIS officials to perform a complete inspection of the facility and does not facilitate
proper tracking of animals. Correct by ensuring that the records are fully and correctly disclosed for all dogs that are
purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, or otherwise in his or her possession or under his or her control.
3.1(c)(2)

Housing facilities, general.
Three dog houses that are provided for 5 dogs, located in building 3, have excessive chew damage present. The chew
damage is located around the entrance and protruding rim around each dog house. In the whelping area of building 2,
there is chew damage present on the surfaces on the front walls of the kennel around the top rim. The damage was
present on 5 kennel front walls. Chew damage can create sharp points that can cause injury to the animals and can not
be properly sanitized. Correct by ensuring that all surfaces including houses, dens, and other furniture type objects within
the facility that can not be readily cleaned and sanitized, are maintained or replaced.
