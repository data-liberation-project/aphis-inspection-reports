3.125(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Supplies of food and bedding shall be stored in facilities which adequately protect supplies against deterioration,
molding, or contamination. The food and bedding was stored in the animal room and supplies were open and
setting on top of animal caging or on the floor. These items left open can easily become contaminated by splashing
of food, urine or fecal waste as well as by insects or vermin. The containers for food and bedding need to be kept
covered and the trash can needs a lid.
