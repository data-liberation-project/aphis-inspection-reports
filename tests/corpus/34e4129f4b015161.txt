IACUC are required at least every six months to ensure that any activities involving animals at this facility are being
conducted in a manner to reduce pain and/or distress and they meet the current standards for the species involved.

animal facilities, including animal study areas, using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A- Animal Welfare, as a basis for
evaluation. The IACUC must also review the research facility's program for humane care and use of animals, at least
once every six months, using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A.
