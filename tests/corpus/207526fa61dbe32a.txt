3.1(e)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
An open bag of dog food was not kept in a leakproof container with a tightly fitting lid. Open supplies of food may
become contaminated by debris, pests or may deteriorate or spoil so should be stored in a manner to protect the
health of the dogs. All open supplies of food and bedding must be kept in leakproof containers with tightly fitting lids
to prevent contamination and spoilage.
