2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Numerous bottles of outdated drugs ( vitamins and antibiotics ) are present in the portable vet boxes . Two boxes of
outdated suture material are present in the Colony 1 hospital. Medications and veterinary supplies lose their
effectiveness when expired and this may effect treatment outcome. Supplies of drugs and other medical supplies
and must be maintained within expiration date to ensure that adequate veterinary care is provided.

3.84(d)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
There are many cockroaches in the light covers and along ceiling edges in the sheltered housing area of one
building in Colony 1. Pest such as roaches many contaminate feed and aid in the spread of diseases . Recent
inclement weather has prevented the routine pest treatment in animal holding areas however a method must be
developed to prevent a buildup pests. CORRECTED DURING INSPECTION
