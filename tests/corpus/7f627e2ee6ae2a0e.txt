ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
** The owner has an attending veterinarian and a written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) however, the written
PVC has not been signed in over a year. The program of veterinary care states that the veterinarian will examine
the animals annually. The signature of the veterinarian ensures that the animals are being provided adequate
veterinary care by the signed veterinarian and that the animals have been examined on an annual basis.The written
program of veterinary care will ensure that there is accurate communication between the applicant and the
attending veterinarian and ensure that all routine medications and vaccines provided to the animals by the applicant
are done with the direction and knowledge of the attending veterinarian. The program of veterinary care should be
sign by the appointed veterinarian for this facility. Correct by October 15, 2015

FACILITIES, GENERAL.
