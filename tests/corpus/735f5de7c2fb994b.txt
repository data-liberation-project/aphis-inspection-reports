3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
An enclosure fence housing two alpacas is electric. This fence was off and the fence was sagging in several areas.
This fence can not properly contain these animals to prevent injury caused by escape or being entangled in the
fence. This fence must be repaired or replaced to contain these animals.
Correct by: 8-11-17
3.127(b)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Two sheep are housed in an enclosure with no access to shelter during times of inclement weather. Shelter must
be provided for the comfort of these sheep and to prevent any possible effects on their health.
Correct by: 8-17-17
