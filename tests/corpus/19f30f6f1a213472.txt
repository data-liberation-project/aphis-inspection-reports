2.8
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME, ADDRESS, CONTROL, OR OWNERSHIP OF BUSINESS.
Inspector arrived to perform an inspection at the facility. Upon arrival, inspector saw a sign above the store with a
different business name. Inspector was notified by the new store owner, that the business had been sold to him on
December 7, 2016. No further facility inspection was performed once the change of ownership was identified.

USDA had not been notified about the change of ownership of the business by the licensee. The licensee is
required to promptly notify the AC Regional Director by certified mail of any change in the name, address,
management, or substantial control or ownership of is business or operation within ten days of any change.
The exit briefing was conducted over the telephone with facility representative and VMO Dominique Engel on
February 2, 2017 at 9:34am.
