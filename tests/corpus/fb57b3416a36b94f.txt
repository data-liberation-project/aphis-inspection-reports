2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at approximately
1:00 pm on 10/28/2014. The inspector attempted to contact the license holder by phone and by knocking on the
facility door. Both attempts were unsuccessful. The inspector then waited in the facility driveway for 1/2 of an hour
before leaving.
A responsible adult shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
Note: This inspection report was sent by mail.
