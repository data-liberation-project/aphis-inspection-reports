REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This is a search inspection. (cid:9)
The facility exhibits goats, chickens and racing pigs as part of a seasonal fall pumpkin patch/corn maze business
without a current USDA Animal Welfare Act license. The animals on exhibit are kept in small pens that allow the
public to see and interact with the animals. A new goat walk has been built. The facility is open on weekends to the
public and on weekdays by reservation.
A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations. The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities.
Exit conducted with undersigned facility representative.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
