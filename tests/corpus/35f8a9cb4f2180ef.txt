2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11:00 AM on
20-JUN-19.
The inspector arrived at 11:00 AM on 6/20/19 to conduct an inspection. Spoke with an adult on the premises and
learned that the Licensee was not on the premises and would not return until after 5PM. An inspection could not be
conducted because there was no adult with knowledge of the regulated activity available to facilitate the inspection.
Unannounced inspections are used to evaluate compliance with the AWA Regulations and Standards.
