3.129(a)
FEEDING.
The Mazuri feed for the bears and lion are kept in the garage in open bags on the floor.
The food shall be wholesome, palatable, and free from contamination and of sufficient quantity and nutritive value to
maintain all animals in good health.
Keep open bags of food in containers so that the food is not spoiled by rodent infestation or insects. Keep food in
bags off the floor so that moisture is not wicked into the food from the floors.
To be corrected by September 5, 2017.
Inspection and exit conducted with the licensee.
