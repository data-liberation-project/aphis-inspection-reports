REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Everglades Jacks LLC, has been exhibiting USDA covered animals to the public without a valid license since
September 17, 2013. The owner was unaware the previous facilities USDA license was canceled. This facility is in
the process of applying for a USDA license. Any person operating as an exhibitor must have a valid USDA license.
Until a USDA license is obtained, no USDA regulated animals can be exhibited to the public.
