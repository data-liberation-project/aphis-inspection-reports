Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The facility IACUC last completed a semi-annual review of the program of humane care and use of animals on February
23, 2021.
A review of the facilities program of humane care and use of animals by the IACUC, at least once every six months, is
critical in assuring that documented protocols and procedures, put in place to protect the health and welling of animals,
are bring followed and are effective and adequate.

program for humane care and use of animals, using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A--Animal Welfare, as a basis for
evaluation.
