3.1(e)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The kennel facility consists of one indoor housing building and an outdoor housing area. Next to the outdoor
housing area is a building which contains the stored food material. Inside of the building there are two metal barrels
for the dry food storage. During the inspection one of the barrels did not have it's lid. Supplies of food must be
stored in a manner that protects the supplies from spoilage, contamination, and vermin infestation. All open supplies
of food must be kept in leakproof containers with tightly fitting lids to prevent contamination and spoilage.
Affects twenty-two adults and two puppies over eight weeks of age. Correct by March 23, 2015
The inspection and exit briefing was conducted with the Representatives.
