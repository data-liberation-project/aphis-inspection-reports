2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 10:50 a.m. on
06/27/2016. The licensee was contacted by phone, stated she was at a veterinary clinic with a horse and did not
think she could be available within 30 minutes. The inspectors waited at the facility until 11:25 a.m. but did not make
contact with any person.
