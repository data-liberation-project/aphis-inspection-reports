3.26(c)

Facilities, indoor.
The indoor housing facility, housing 4 guinea pigs and 2 hamsters, did not have adequate lighting to permit routine
inspection. Inadequate lighting prevents thorough observation of animals and animal areas which could impact animal
health and well-being. Indoor housing facilities for guinea pigs and hamsters shall have ample light of good quality and
well distributed. Such lighting shall provide uniformly distributed illumination of sufficient light intensity to permit routine
inspection and cleaning. Primary enclosures shall be so placed as to protect the guinea pigs and hamsters from
excessive illumination.
To be corrected by 9/5/2022
3.126(c)

Facilities, indoor.
The indoor housing facility, housing 1 ferret, did not have adequate lighting to permit routine inspection. Inadequate
lighting prevents thorough observation of animals and animal areas which could impact animal health and well-being.
Indoor housing facilities shall have ample lighting of good quality, distribution, and duration as appropriate for the species
involved. Such lighting shall be uniformly distributed and of sufficient intensity to permit routine inspection and cleaning.


Lighting of primary enclosures shall be designed to protect the animals from excessive illumination.
To be corrected by 9/5/2022

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
