3.53(a)(4)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
The rabbit has a solid wood floor in it's enclosure but no litter. Litter helps to maintain cleanliness and to keep the
animal from getting wet or matted with it's feces. Litter shall be provided in all primary enclosures having solid
floors.
