3.77(g)
SHELTERED HOUSING FACILITIES.
There is no public barrier in front of the red ruffed lemur enclosure this date. The barrier for the ring tailed lemurs
consists of a bamboo fence with a top and bottom rail. There is a large enough space between and under the rails
that would allow a small child to slip thru the fence and possibly contact the animals. A barrier must be present
between the primary enclosure and the public at any time the public is present, that restricts physical contact
between the public and the nonhuman primates. CORRECT BY : 9/20/18
