2.38(h)(1)(i)

Miscellaneous.
The facility sent 21 non-human primates to another facility in TX in which the health certificates for the transport had a
Date Inspected that was more than 10 days prior to the day of transport on 9/8/2021. To ensure the health of the animals
prior to transport, the health certificate must certify that the licensed veterinarian inspected the animals no more than 10
days prior to delivery for transport. The research facility must ensure that prior to delivering animals over for transport that
the health certificate is properly issued as required by a licensed veterinarian and animals are inspected no more than 10
days prior to transport.
Correction: The facility reports corrective actions have been implemented to prevent further recurrences. Maintain correct
for all future transportations.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
