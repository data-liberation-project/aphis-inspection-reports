3.26(d)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
Interior surfaces. There is unsealed wood on the floor and window frames in the new section of the guinea pig
building. Interior surfaces of indoor guinea pig facilities must be constructed and maintained so that the surfaces are
impervious to moisture so that they can be readily sanitized. Correct by ensuring the wood floor and window frames
in the new section are covered with an impervious surface or sealed to maintain it in a substantially impervious
condition so that surfaces can be readily sanitized. Correct by August 15, 2019.
