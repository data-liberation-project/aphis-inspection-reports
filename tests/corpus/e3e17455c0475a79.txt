2.31(e)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The protocols reviewed had good description of the procedures as they are performed on the animals but were
lacking some of the required information. The five requirements of this subpart must all be addressed in the protocol
to assure that the investigator has considered the best methods and use to reduce animal suffering. The information
provides the IACUC a basis for approval and assurance that animal welfare is addressed. Correct by assuring
proposal to conduct an activity involving animals, or to make a significant change in an ongoing activity involving
animals, contains all of the subparts under this Paragraph of 9 CFR. To be corrected for all future protocol
approvals and annual reviews.
An exit interview was conducted with facility CEO.
