3.125(a) REPEAT
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to
protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
The wooden boards that form the climbing and shelter structure in the center of the goat enclosure contained two
loose boards that had tilted upwards exposing two nails. Additional areas had loose, rotted wood boards in need of
repair. Sharp protruding objects and loose, rotting boards can cause injury to the animals. The two wood boards
with sharp objects were removed immediately at the time of inspection.
The climbing and shelter structure must be repaired, replaced or the animals relocated to avoid potential injury to
the animals.
Correct by 11 October 2016.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
