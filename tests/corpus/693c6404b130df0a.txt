Records: Dealers and exhibitors.

Housing facilities, general.
Wood shavings are used in the dog enclosures to absorb waste. An open bag of wood shavings is located near the
outdoor dog runs. Open bags of shavings could be contaminated by pests such as flies and mice.

All open supplies of food and bedding must be kept in leakproof containers with tightly fitting lids to prevent contamination
and spoilage. The facility must place the open shavings into a container with a tightly fitted lid and ensure that all open
supplies are properly stored at all times.
