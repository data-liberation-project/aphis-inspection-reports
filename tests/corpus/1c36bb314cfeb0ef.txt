2.8
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME, ADDRESS, CONTROL, OR OWNERSHIP OF BUSINESS.
There has been a substantial change in control or ownership of the business or operation.
In order to provide a good customer service and have good communication with the new owners, the facility must
notify USDA of any change in the name, address, management, or ownership of the business within 10 days by
certified mail to the AC regional director.
