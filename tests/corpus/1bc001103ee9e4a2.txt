ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
**** This is a focused inspection in response to tiger cubs with skin lesions. At the time of inspection, a sixteen week
old male white tiger cub had three circular, hairless lesions. One lesion on the medial aspect of the right P2, one
lesion on the medial aspect of the left P2, and one lesion on the medial aspect of the right rear hock. The owner
stated that the lesions weren't present three days prior and the attending veterinarian had not been contacted. This
animal must be examined by a licensed veterinarian and appropriate treatment administrated.
To be corrected by: 13 September 2014
