2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The current program of veterinary care (PVC) was incomplete. The sections for vaccinations, parasite control, emergency
care, euthanasia, and additional topics were not completed for the mammals present. It is important to have this
information to ensure that the animals involved are provided with adequate veterinary care as required. Each dealer shall
employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. Formal arrangements shall include a written program of
veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises.
