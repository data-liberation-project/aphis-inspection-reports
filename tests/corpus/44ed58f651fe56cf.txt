2.40(a)(1) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
***The licensee uses a veterinarian under consulting arrangements to provide veterinary care for the animals but
does not have a formal agreement nor a written program of veterinary care. In order to ensure problems of animal
health are addressed adequately and timely, a written program of veterinary care must be maintained and followed.
Each licensee shall employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a part-time
attending veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written program of
veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the licensee.
2.75(b)(1) REPEAT
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
***The licensee did not have records that contained the required information regarding the correct number of
inventory, gender, age and location of animals on hand. Records containing the required information for animals
acquired for use in regulated activities, including those offspring born on premise, need to be made and maintained
in order to properly track the animal numbers and use in those activities.
3.81 REPEAT
ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
***An environmental enhancement plan to promote the psychological well-being of all non-human primates currently
housed at this facility has not been approved by the attending veterinarian. A written plan must be developed and
followed for all nonhuman primates. This plan must be in accordance with current accepted professional standards.
