2.31(c)(1) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Semi-annual Program Review.
1. The IACUC still has not reviewed the research facility's Program for Animal Care and Use at least once every 6
months as required. The last review of the Program was conducted almost 19 months ago on 4/20/15 and a review
of the Program has not been done since this issue was identified at time of the last inspection on 3/30/16.
Per this Section of the Regulations, the IACUC shall review the Program for Animal Care and Use at least once
every 6 months to ensure all aspects of the Program are in compliance with the AWA Regulations.
This item remains uncorrected.
2.31(c)(2) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Semi-annual Facility Inspection.
1. The IACUC still has not inspected the research facility's animal facilities at least once every 6 months as
required. The last inspection of the animal facilities was conducted almost 19 months ago on 4/20/15 and an
inspection of the animal facilities has not been done since this issue was identified at time of the last inspection on
3/30/16.
Per this Section of the Regulations, the IACUC shall inspect all of the research facility's animal facilities at least
once every 6 months to ensure the condition of the animal facility is in compliance with the AWA Regulations.
This item remains uncorrected.
2.31(c)(3) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).IACUC functions.
1. The IACUC still has not updated their reports to the Institutional Official (IO) at least once every six months as
required. The last Semi-annual IACUC Report submitted to the IO was dated 4/20/15 and pertained to the IACUC's
April 2015 semi-annual reviews of the program for animal care and animal facility inspection. An update of the
semi-annual report to the IO has not been done since this issue was identified at time of the last inspection on
3/30/16. Almost 19 months have elapsed since the IACUC prepared and updated their semi-annual report to the
IO.
Per this Section of the Regulations, reports of the IACUC semi-annual evaluations of the program for animal care
and animal facility inspection should be updated at least once every 6 months upon completion of the required
semiannual evaluations and the report should be submitted to the Institutional Official. Compliance with the
requirements outlined in this Section is important to ensure the IO is updated regarding the status of the animal use
program and the condition of the animal facilities to ensure that the research facility is adhering to the standards and
regulations specified under the Act.
This item remains uncorrected.
