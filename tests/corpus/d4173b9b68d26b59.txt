2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The owner of the kennel has more than 4 breeding female dogs and is selling their offspring to a pet store.
Any person who has 4 or more breeding female dogs and is selling the offspring to a pet store or broker is acting as
a dealer and therefore must obtain a USDA, APHIS, AC license.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Exit conducted with the kennel owner.
