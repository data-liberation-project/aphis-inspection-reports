2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
* There was one 100 ml bottle of Oxytocin with an expiration date of 03/2014 being stored in the whelp building.
This medication is currently being used to treat 96 dogs at the facility and is therefore ready for use. The usage of
expired medical materials on regulated animals is not considered to be an acceptable veterinary practice. The
effectiveness of the dosage level and expected results when expired drugs are administrated could be drastically
changed or may not work as anticipated. The licensee must ensure that all medications used have not gone past
the recorded expiration date. Correct by 12/9/14.
2.78(a)(1)
HEALTH CERTIFICATION AND IDENTIFICATION.
* The recorded inspection date by a licensed veterinarian on two health certificates has exceeded the 10 day time
limit for those inspected animals to be shipped. Thirteen puppies inspected on July 3, 2014 by a licensed
veterinarian were shipped on July 21, 2014. Seven puppies inspected on August 7, 2014 by a licensed veterinarian
were shipped on August 25, 2014. Health certificates provide information on the health status of all animals being
shipped within the 10 day time limit. The health status could change affecting the travel status if the shipment date
exceeds 10 days past recorded date on the health certificate. The licensee must ensure that all animals are
shipped within 10 days of the recorded date of inspection by a licensed veterinarian on the health certificate. To be
corrected from this date forward.
