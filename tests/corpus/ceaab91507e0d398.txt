3.131(d)
SANITATION.
An excessive amount of flies were observed in the kangaroo barn, the barn housing the education animals, and the
back barn. Flies were observed on food dishes within the enclosures. No measures had been taken to address the
fly issue. An excessive amount of flies can be a nuisance to animals leading to undo stress as well as transmit
disease. A safe and effective program for the control of insects, ectoparasites, and avian and mammalian pests
shall be established and maintained.
To be corrected within 7 days.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility owner.
