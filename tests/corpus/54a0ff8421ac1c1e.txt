2.75(b)(1) REPEAT
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Section 2.75(b)(1): Records: The facility inventory provided to the APHIS officials during this inspection did not have
a date and did not include guinea pigs, rabbits, gerbils and ferrets. There were also no previous weekly inventories
provided for the weeks from the last inspection conducted in December 2016. Records are required in order to
adequately verify the total number of animals housed at the facility and for evaluating the facility animal care
program. Correct by ensuring there are records for animal totals, including births and deaths, that are maintained
and available for review by APHIS officials during inspections.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility president.
