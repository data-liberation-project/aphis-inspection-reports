3.129(b)

Feeding.
Approximately 50% of the hard plastic feeders in use for the sheep and swine are in disrepair: chewed edges, cracked
corners, and broken rims are prominent. Damaged plastic feeders cannot be cleaned and sanitized effectively and can
harbor infectious agents that can harm the animals. Per the regulation, food receptacles shall be kept clean and sanitary
at all times. Correct by 01March2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
