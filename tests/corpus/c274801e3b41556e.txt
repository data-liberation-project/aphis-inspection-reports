2.132(d)

Procurement of dogs, cats, and other animals; dealers.
Records obtained from the October 8, 2021 exotic animal auction indicate the following animals were accepted for sale
from unlicensed customers: 2 Hedgehog, 1 camel, 3 prairie dogs and 3 water buffalo. No dealer shall obtain any wild or
exotic animal from any person who is required to be licensed but who does not hold a current, valid license.
Correct by December 1, 2021.
