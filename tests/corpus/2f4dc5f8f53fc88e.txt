2.33(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
Two bottles of ketamine were expired on 8-2017 and were still in the cabinet with medication currently used.
Expired medications are not considered appropriate for treatment of animals in any way.

2.38(f)(1) CRITICAL REPEAT
MISCELLANEOUS.
An incident was reported on May 2016 in which a guinea pig escaped while unpacking and housing the animals
delivered. After 3 days the guinea pig was found loose on the floor of the room. The animal appeared lethargic and
dehydrated and was humanely euthanized.
Corrective actions have been implemented by retraining personnel and updating the SOP for receiving animals
delivered at the facility.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative and facility personnel.
