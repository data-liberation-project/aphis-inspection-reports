REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
*The facility currently exhibits farm animals and small nondangerous animals at a county fair, Wilson, NC, without a
valid USDA Animal Welfare Act license. The animals are on display for people to observe but contact is not
permitted. Appropriate barriers to prevent contact are in place and two attendants are on site. The county fair is
open to the public on an entrance fee basis.
*A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations.
*The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Exit conducted with facility representative.
