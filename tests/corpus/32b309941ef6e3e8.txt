2.130
MINIMUM AGE REQUIREMENTS.
The licensee sold some puppies that were under 8 weeks of age. The disposition forms indicated that some of the
puppies were sold between 7 weeks to just under 8 weeks of age. The licensee indicated that she was not aware of
the requirement during the time of inspection. Puppies must be 8 weeks or older prior to their transport, sale, etc.,
to provide for their health and well-being.
Assure that all puppies are 8 weeks of age prior to transport and/or sale.
To be corrected: From this day forward.
Note the inspection exit briefing was conducted with the facility owner.
