RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Several records of acquisitions/dispositions were missing and not up to date. There are no records for five

2.131(d)(2)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
Several members of the public were observed feeding and petting the miniature zebu and llamas in the front
pasture without an attendant present. No attendant was observed anywhere in the walk-thru portion of the zoo.
Also, one adult was observed putting his small child on the roof of his car next to the giraffe exhibit so the child
could feed the giraffe. No attendant was present in this area. Employee escorting inspectors did stop and tell the
man to stay in his car, however a readily identifiable attendant should be present during any public contact in order
to prevent harm to the animals and the public. Correct by April 15, 2014.
