2.31(e)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The teaching protocols do not contain a complete description of the procedures, methods, & drugs utilized.
A proposal to conduct an activity involving animals must contain a complete description of the proposed use of
animals as well as a complete description of the procedures designed to assure that discomfort and pain to the
animals will be limited to that which is unavoidable.
These updated protocols must be approved by the IACUC.
