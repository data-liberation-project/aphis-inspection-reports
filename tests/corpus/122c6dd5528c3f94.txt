FACILITIES, INDOOR.
The drainage trench was backed up and filled with stagnant water and debris. This was observed in the pig
enclosure as well as the area of food storage. This could harbor bacteria and potentially cause the animals to
become ill. The facility needs to ensure that all excess water is eliminated from housing facilities.
