2.75(b)(2)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Records for animals at hand containing required information on acquisition/disposition information of these were not
available for inspection. The licensee shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly
disclose information on purchased or otherwise acquired animals, and that are in his or her possession/control. The
Record of Animals on Hand (APHIS Form 7019) and Record of Acquisition, Disposition, or Transport of Animals
(APHIS Form 7020) are forms which may be used by the licensee to keep and maintain the information required by
federal regulations. The licensee needs to obtain/document the required information on all recently acquired
animals as soon as possible.
