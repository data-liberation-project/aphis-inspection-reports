3.1(c)(1)(1)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
(c)(1)(i)
Thoughout the facility there were multiple areas with sharp points and or jagged edges. These areas include but are
not limited to; enclosure # 5 of the whelping facility had sections of metal framing surounding the dog door that had
been pulled away and bent leaving a sharp edge, enclosure # 5 of the adult housing area had sections of metal
along the base of the wall in both the indoor and outdoor area that had rusted through leaveing holes in the metal
with sharp edges, at least 3 of the plastic dog beds were chewed and had sharp edges. Sharp edges and or points
could injure the dog. The licensee shall repair, replace or cover these sections and ensure that all of the animal
areas are free from surfaces with jagged edges or sharp points.
