2.75(a)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The facility’s disposition records for the wolf-dog hybrids were not complete. Records for the time frame 4/25/18 to
7/9/18 were reviewed. Conversations with the owner and attending veterinarian revealed that two wolf-dog hybrid
pups died during this period. No written records were maintained documenting the death disposition of these
wolf-dogs.
Accurate and complete records are necessary to allow identification and tracking of ownership/health status of each
wolf-dog. Every dealer shall make, keep and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose all required
information concerning each dog which is transported, sold, euthanized sold or otherwise disposed of by that
dealer. Correct by 8/10/18
2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The facility’s acquisition and disposition records did not fully and correctly disclose all required information
concerning animals other than dogs and cats. Records for the time frame 4/25/18 to 7/9/18 were reviewed.
*Written records for offspring born at the facility included 8 raccoons and 2 opossums. The owner stated during the
inspection that the actual number of these species born at the facility was higher and that other species also had
offspring born at the facility during that time period. Written records were found that indicate the total number of
litters born during the time period but not the date of birth or the number of animals in each litter.
*Conversations with the owner and the attending veterinarian revealed that animals including one adult raccoon and
two adult opossums died during this time period. No written records were maintained documenting the death
disposition of these animals.
Accurate and complete records are necessary to allow identification and tracking of ownership/health status of the
animals. Every dealer shall make, keep and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose all required
information concerning animals other than dogs and cats, purchased or otherwise acquired or under his or her
control or which is transported, sold, euthanized or otherwise disposed of by that dealer. The records shall include
any offspring born. Correct by 8/10/18.This inspection was conducted by undersigned VMO and owner and with ACI.
