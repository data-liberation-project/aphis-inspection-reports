3.53(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
There are at least 6 rabbit enclosures with chewed edges on the front of the plastic J feeders. Primary enclosures
including self-feeders and accessories must be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to prevent injury to
the animals. The chewed J feeders must be replaced to prevent possible injury to the animals and so that feeders
can be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized.
