3.28(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
At least 6 enclosures have broken and/or detached wire on the doors or sides. Two bottom yellow enclosures and 4
white enclosures by the entrance door have broken or detached wire. All enclosures must be structurally sound and
maintained in good repair to protect guinea pigs from injury and to contain the animals securely. Enclosures with
broken and/or detached wire must be repaired or replaced to prevent injury to the guinea pigs. Correct by January
13, 2018.
