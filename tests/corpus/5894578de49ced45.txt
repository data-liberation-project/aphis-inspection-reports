2.33(b)(5)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
Item Veterinary Care 2.33 b 5
At the time of inspection outdated drugs were found in an investigator surgery to include dobutamine exp Apr 1,
2015, Atropine exp 12/14, Metoprolol exp 5/1/15, diltiazem exp Aug 1, 2012, Potassium Acetate exp June 1 2015,
Metoprolol exp exp 10/14, Solu Medrol exp 5/2014, Cefazolin exp 2/15
The use of outdated drugs is not proper veterinary care. In addition IACUC needs to be more vigilant during its
semi annual inspection.
Facility needs to review its guidelines concerning proper use drugs with its investigators.
