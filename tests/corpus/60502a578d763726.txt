2.31(p)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
**No method of euthansia was listed in Protocol #R14-01-01. Although the protocol prompts the principal
investigator to describe what method of euthansia will be utilized in case of emergency, no method is indicated. A
description of the method of euthansia to be utilized should be indicated. It is the responsibility of the IACUC to
review the protocol in order to ensure that a humane method of euthansia will be utilized according to established
standard veterinary practices as set forth in this section.

Inspection and exit interview conducted with facility representatives.
