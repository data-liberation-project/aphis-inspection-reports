3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The trees in the tiger enclosure have grown to the point that they could potentially provide a way for the tiger to
escape. Particularly when a large carnivore is involved, it is important to maintain enclosures in a highly secure
manner. Steps must be taken to secure the enclosure, either by trimming the trees or providing a cover for the
enclosure. Complete by 11 November 2014.
An exit interview was conducted with the licensee.
