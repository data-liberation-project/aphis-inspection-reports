2.33(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
(a)(1) The facility has not yet hired a new part time attending veterinarian and has no Written Program of Veterinary
Care (PVC) on file. The regular veterinarian has retired and the facility is still looking for a new attending
veterinarian. A new PVC must be prepared to ensure that adequate veterinary care guidline will be followed for their
animals based on current medical practice.
