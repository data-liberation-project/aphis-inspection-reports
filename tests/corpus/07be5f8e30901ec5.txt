ANNUAL REPORT.
Section 2.36(b) Annual report: No gerbils were reported in Column D on the 2016 Annual Report for the facility. The
gerbils were either reported in Column B or Column C. A review of animal records for gerbils on animal activity
proposal (protocol) 194 documented at least 40 gerbils were given anesthesia and used in a terminal procedure
from March 2016 to September 2016. These gerbils should have been reported in Column D (Number of animals
upon which experiments or surgery were conducted involving accompanying pain or distress to the animals and for
which appropriate anesthetics, analgesic, or tranquilizing drugs were used.). The 2016 Annual Report should have
reported gerbils in Column D, not just Column B and Column C. Research facilities must submit accurate annual
reports to comply with the Animal Welfare regulations and to provide the correct information to USDA, APHIS,
Animal Care. Correct by amending the 2016 Annual Report and submitting it to the Animal Care Raleigh, NC Office
to report the correct number of gerbils in Columns B, C and D.
