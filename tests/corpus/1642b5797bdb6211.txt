FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The truck used to transport 4 lions and 5 tigers functions as part of the housing facility for the animals at stationary
locations. The interior of the roof, doors and sides in the back cargo area are lined with wood that is damaged,
splintered and missing pieces. Housing facilities should be structurally sound and kept in good repair to ensure
animal containment, protect the animals from injury and to meet husbandry and housekeeping requirements as
directed by the Animal Welfare Act. The wood should be repaired or replaced.
Correct by: This non-compliance was corrected prior to delivery of the inspection report.
Exit interview conducted with the licensee and the authorized person.
The inspection was conducted in Rosenberg, TX.
