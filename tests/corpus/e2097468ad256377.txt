3.50(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The white wood rabbit hutch containing one female rabbit was in disrepair. The door did not close securely and had
an approximately 2-3" gap at the top and bottom, which could allow for rabbit to escape or predators to access the
hutch. Wire mesh on right door and cage bottom was unattached and exposed nail heads present on right door
These could allow for injury to the rabbit and serve as potential escape route / predator access.
Repair or replace affected areas of rabbit hutch.
To be corrected by July 16, 2015.

SANITATION.
Excessive feces were present in the four rabbit hutches containing eight rabbits. Feces can serve as a source of
diseases that can affect rabbits and other animals.
Remove the excessive feces and hutches should be cleaned frequently enough to prevent buildup of excreta, hair,
cobwebs and other debris.
To be corrected by July 16, 2015.

SANITATION.

veterinary care issues if not controlled.
