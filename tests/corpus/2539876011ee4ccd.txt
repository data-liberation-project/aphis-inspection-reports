3.53(c)(2)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Seven rabbits, each weighing less than 4.4 pounds, are housed in an enclosure that measures 25 inches by 19
inches. This space provides 475 square inches of floor space. Each of these rabbits require 216 square inches of
floor space. This enclosure only provides enough floor space for 2 rabbits weighing less than 4.4 pounds.
475 square inches divided by 216 square inches = 2.199
Facility shall provide rabbits with the minimum amount of floor space in their primary enclosure.
