ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Approximately 20% of the guinea pigs have poor, thin hair-coats and chewed ears .
Each dealer shall establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that include:
The use of appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries.
Poor hair coat, thinning hair and chewed ears can be caused by stress, external parasites and/or nutritional
deficiency.
