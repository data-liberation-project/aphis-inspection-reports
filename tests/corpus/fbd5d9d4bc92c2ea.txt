3.31(a)(1)

Sanitation.
There is a buildup of brown roughened scale and debris on the sides of the guinea pig enclosures. Inadequate cleaning of
scale and debris on sides of enclosures can increase the risk of disease and health problems. Correct by ensuring that
the primary enclosures are kept clean and sanitized often enough to prevent an accumulation of scale and debris. In
addition, enclosures must be sanitized at least once every 2 weeks.
Correct by March 12, 2021.
