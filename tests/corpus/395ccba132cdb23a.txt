3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
Four enclosures housing 14, 11, 14; 15 adult guinea pigs respectively with their offspiring had accumulaltions of
soiled bedding. Primary enclosures must be cleaned often enough to prevent an accumulation of feces, dirty
bedding, and debris. Inadequate cleaning and sanitiation exposes the animals to potential disease hazards such as
bacterial, fungal and viral diseases. Primary enclosures must be cleaned and sanitized at least once every two
weeks to maintain the health of the animals. Correct by ensuring that all primary enclosures are kept clean and
sanitized often enough to prevent and accumualtion of feces, dirty bedding and debris.
