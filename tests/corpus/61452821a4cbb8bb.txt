3.53(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Section 3.53(a)(1) Primary enclosure: The enclosure housing two rabbits has linoleum on the floor and extends
several inches up the back and right walls. The rabbits have chew a large hole in the linoleum in the back right
corner. Pieces of the linoleum are in the enclosure. The rabbits could ingest these pieces and become ill. The
enclosure must be repaired to be structurally sound and to protect the health of the rabbits.
Correct by October 6, 2014.
An exit briefing was conducted with the facility director.
