2.126(a) Critical

Access and inspection of records and property; submission of itineraries.
On Wednesday, August 31, 2022 at approximately 10:05 am, the inspectors arrived at the facility to complete an animal
welfare inspection. The licensee's wife informed the inspectors that the licensee had left for work. The inspectors traveled
to his employer's business location and spoke with the licensee at approximately 10:40 am. The licensee stated he
needed more time to correct the non-compliances from the previous report, dated August 24, 2022. The licensee
explained that he had not yet arranged a visit from the attending veterinarian and he needed additional time to correct the
other noncompliant items. The inspectors informed the licensee that he would be cited for a refusal. The licensee stated
that he needed more time to correct all of the non-compliances and that it was acceptable to him to be cited for a refusal.

The exit briefing was conducted with the licensee.
