2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11:59 a.m. on
12/29/2015. The inspector knocked at the front door of the residence and after no response called the licensee and
left a voicemail message. The licensee called back a few minutes later and stated he was not available for an
inspection today.
