2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This facility is exhibiting a rabbit to the public without a USDA license. This facility has been licensed years ago
mainly exhibiting birds. The management and staff are new and unaware of obtaining a USDA license. The
management during this inspection was issued a licensing packet and stated she will be applying for a USDA
license immediately.
Until a USDA is obtained, no animals regulated by USDA can be exhibited to the public.
This inspection and exit interview was conducted with the Director / Attending Veterinarian .
