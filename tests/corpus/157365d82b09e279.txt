2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The owner of the pet store has degus for sale at her pet store.
Any person operating as a dealer, except persons who are exempted from the licensing requirements of this
section, must have a valid license.
A USDA, APHIS, AC license must be obtained to sell degus through a pet store.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Exit conducted with the owner.
