2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
A current record of guinea pigs was not available during this inspection. A total of 1,005 adult guinea pigs, weanlings, and
newborns were counted during this inspection. There were no inventory records for this calendar year. Failure to maintain
these records can make it difficult to assess the health of the caviary by identifying animal losses by decreased number of
guinea pigs born or raised. The licensee must maintain these records containing the required information for animals
which disclose the number of guinea pigs held, owned, acquired or disposed of including births and deaths. Correct by
July 20, 2022.
3.31(b)

Sanitation.
Housekeeping. There were accumulations of hair, bedding , and debris adhering to the right sides of four racks of
enclosures on the west side of the building. The upper walls and ceiling had accumulations of cobwebs. Animal facilities
must be kept clean to ensure good quality of the air for the animals and to prevent unnecessary disease risks. Correct by
ensuring premises are kept clean, including: removing accumulations of hair, bedding, debris, and cobwebs; routinely
dusting off and cleaning surfaces; and maintaining a level of general cleanliness to support good animal husbandry and
health. Correct by July 13, 2022.
