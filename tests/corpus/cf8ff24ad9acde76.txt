3.26(d)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
Interior Surfaces. There is a 4 foot by 4 foot section of the ceiling in the middle of guinea pig room with exposed
insulation. Interior surfaces of indoor guinea pig buildings must be constructed and maintained so that surfaces are
substantially impervious to moisture. The section of exposed insulation must be covered or repaired so that the
indoor facility is impervious to moisture. Correct by June 8, 2019.
3.30
WATERING.
Ten enclosures housing a total of 85 adult guinea pigs and their offspring had water bottles with an algae buildup on
the inside of the water containers. Water bottles must be sanitized when dirty so that potable water is provided to
the guinea pigs to protect the health and well-being of the animals. In addition, water receptacles must be sanitized
at least once every 2 weeks. Water bottles with the algae buildup were sanitized and potable water was provided at
time of this inspection. Licensee must ensure that water receptacles are sanitized when dirty and at least once
every 2 weeks. Correct by May 31, 2019.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
