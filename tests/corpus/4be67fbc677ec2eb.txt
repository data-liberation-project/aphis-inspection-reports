2.40(a)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee is not following the direction noted in the Program of Veterinary Care. The program states that the
lemurs receive annual fecal floats, yearly rabies vaccines, yearly tests for blood parasites and yearly TB tests which
is not being done. The program also does not include care for the Black Cap Capuchin. The licensee is doing public
contact with the animals and must take all steps necessary to ensure public health per their attending veterinarian.
The licensee must provide the attending veterinarian with the appropriate authority to provide adequate veterinary
care to ensure optimal animal health.
Correct by May 23, 2018
3.81
ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
This facility does not have an approved Program of Environmental Enhancement for the primates that it maintains.
The licensee must develop, document, and follow a plan that is approved by the attending veterinarian. This will
ensure the promotion of psychological well-being for these animals. This plan must be in accordance with current
accepted practices and as directed by the attending veterinarian. Correct this by creating and following an
approved plan of environmental enhancement.
Correct by May 23, 2018
