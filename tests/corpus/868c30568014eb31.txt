2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Currently there are not complete records kept at the facility of the regulated animals that are present during the
inspection. There needs to be accurate records of all animals acquired and those that have been sold or died while
at the facility.
Complete and up to date records are important for proper management and care of the regulated animals.
The licensee must keep complete and up to date records of the regulated animals at the facility that can be
presented during an inspection.

Inspection and exit interview conducted with licensee.
