3.58(a)
CLASSIFICATION AND SEPARATION.
Two rabbits are being housed in an enclosure with other farm animals. Housing rabbits with other species may
affect their health and well-being and increase their risk of injury.
The facility should ensure the rabbits are not housed in the same primary enclosure with any other species.
Corrected during the inspection
An exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
