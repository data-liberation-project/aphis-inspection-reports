2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The licensee does not have records of disposition for 1 prairie dog, 1 stiped skunk, 2 domestic ferrets, and 1 white-nosed
coati mundi.

Each exhibitor shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the following information
concerning animals other than dogs and cats, animals that are transported, sold, euthanized, or otherwise disposed of by
the exhibitor.

Accurate record of animal disposition must be kept to trace and track animal movement in the case of disease outbreak,
and to ensure that the persons the animals were sold to are properly licensed if required.
