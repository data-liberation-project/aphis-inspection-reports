3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The cyclone fencing along the right side of the enclosure housing two adult goats is curled upward along the bottom
with a broken wire. The curled up fence and the broken wire with a sharp edge could potentially cause injuries to
the animals. Maintain housing facilities in good repair so that it protects the animals from injuries.

Inspection and an exit briefing were conducted with the licensee.
