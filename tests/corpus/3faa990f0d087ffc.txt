3.80(a)(2)(2)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
A juvenile baboon was killed when a guillotine door became dislodged and fell on the animal. The incident was
promptly reported to USDA and the facility took immediate corrective action which included the addition of clips on
the handles of all door cables. Primary enclosures must be constructed and maintained so that they protect the non
human primates from injury. CORRECTED AT TIME OF THE INSPECTION
This focal inspection and exit briefing was conducted with the attending veterinarian , colony manager and a facility
employee.
