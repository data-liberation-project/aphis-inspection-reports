2.31(c)(1)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC has not conducted a review of the facility’s program for humane care and use of animals at least once every
six months. The last program review occurred on January 5, 2022.
The IACUC must review the animal use program, at least once every six months, in order to assure the proper care and
welfare of the animals.
A review of the program for humane care and use of animals must be performed by the IACUC.
Correct by October 28, 2022.
2.31(c)(2)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC has not conducted an inspection of the animal facilities at least once every six months. The last facility
inspection occurred on January 5, 2022.
The IACUC must inspect the animal facilities, at least once every six months, in order to assure the proper housing and
general welfare of animals.
An inspection of the facility must be performed by the IACUC.
Correct by October 28, 2022.


2.38(l)

Miscellaneous.
The facility does not have a documented contingency plan.
If an emergency or natural disaster were to occur at the facility, the lack of a written contingency plan could impact animal
health if the staff are not prepared to respond to the situation appropriately.
Research facilities must develop, document, and follow an appropriate contingency plan to provide for the humane
handling, treatment, transportation, housing, and care of their animals in the the event of an emergency or disaster.
Correct by: October 28, 2022.
