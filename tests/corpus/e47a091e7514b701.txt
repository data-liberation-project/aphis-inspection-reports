Sanitation.
Numerous flies were present throughout the sugar glider housing area. Flies were observed around the sugar glider
enclosures, near food dishes, and catch pans. A couple of flies strips were observed in front of the window at the back of
the room. The fly strips had flies covering them and were not effective in controlling the amount of flies present. Flies can
contaminate food and spread diseases. A safe and effective method to control insects shall be established and
maintained.
