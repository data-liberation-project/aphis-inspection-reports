2.31(d)(1)(ii)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
Section 2.31 (d) (1) (ii) states - "The IACUC shall determine that the proposed activities or significant changes in ongoing
activities meet the following requirements: (ii) The principal investigator has considered alternatives to procedures that
may cause more than momentary or slight pain or distress to the animals, and has provided a written narrative description
of the methods and sources.....used to determine that alternatives were not available."
**During review of the teaching protocols, a written narrative of the methods and sources used to determine that
alternatives were not available to potentially painful and/or distressful procedures described in the protocol did not include
surgical procedures.
**Per this Regulation, the IACUC should determine during a review of proposed animal use activities that the proposed
activities meet the requirements as per Section 2.31(d)(1)(ii) to include the methods and sources used when determining
consideration of alternatives by the principal investigator to potentially painful and/or distressful procedures as described
in the protocol.
**The IACUC needs to address this item as identified for this protocol and ensure that all active animal use proposals are
in compliance with this section.

2.31(e)(1)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
**Section 2.31 (e)(1) states "A proposal to conduct an activity involving animals, or to make a significant change in an
ongoing activity involving animals, must contain the following:
(e)(1) Identification of the species and the approximate number of animals to be used."

**Upon review of the teaching protocols, there was no written description of the approximate number of animals to be
utilized.
**Per this Regulation the IACUC should determine during a review of proposed animal use activities that the proposed
activities meet the requirements as per Section 2.31(e)(1) that includes a description of the approximate numbers of
animals to be used in the protocol.
**The IACUC needs to address this item as identified for this protocol and ensure that all active proposals are in
compliance with Section 2.31 (e) (1).
