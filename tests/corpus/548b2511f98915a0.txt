2.132(d) Repeat

Procurement of dogs, cats, and other animals; dealers.
The licensee purchased camels on 2 different dates from 2 different unlicensed sources. One camel was purchased on
7/1/21 and 2 camels were purchased on 9/23/21 and neither seller was licensed as required.
Purchasing from unlicensed sources who are required to be licensed does not allow for inspections at those sources to
ensure compliance with the Animal Welfare Act regulations.
Exhibitors must not obtain animals from any person who is required to be licensed but does not hold a current valid
license.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
