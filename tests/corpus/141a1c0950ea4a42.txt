HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
An outdoor run housing a single dog had an excessive accumulation of feces. Multiple piles of fecal matter were
dried and white in color. Excessive waste is a source of contamination to the enclosed animals. This pen must be
cleaned, and must be spot cleaned daily or as often as necessary to prevent waste build-up.
Corrected during the inspection
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
