3.29(d)

Feeding.
The animal care records documented a lack of sanitization of the feeding containers every 2 weeks. The sanitization of
water bottles was documented on 2/5/21, 2/19/21, 4/2/21, 5/14/21, 5/21/21 and 6/4/21. The every two week sanitization
was not completed correctly from February 19 to May 21, 2021. At the time of inspection, the feed containers had been
sanitized 3 days prior on the correct schedule. Sanitization of water bottles every two weeks is required to minimize
disease hazards and ensure the welfare of the guinea pigs.

Correct from this day forward.

3.31(a)(1) Repeat

Sanitation.
The animal care records documented a lack of sanitization of the primary enclosures every 2 weeks. The sanitization of
enclosures was documented every two weeks except for between 2 May 21 and 21 May 21, when two weeks should
have been 16 May 21. At the time of inspection, the enclosures had been sanitized 4 days prior on the correct schedule.
Failure to sanitize surfaces in contact with the animals in a manner provided in Section 3.31(a)(4) may expose the guinea
pigs to disease hazards. Facility must ensure that all surfaces in contact with the animals are sanitized at least once
every 2 weeks.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Head of Vaccines.
