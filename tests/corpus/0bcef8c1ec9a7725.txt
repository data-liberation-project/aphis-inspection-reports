2.40(a)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
One bottle of expired antibiotic was found at the facility. Since you cannot be assured that expired drugs will still
elicit the responds that were intended to, they are not an adequate means of veterinary care. All expired drugs
should be removed from the facility drug stocks. A regular program of checking and removing expired drugs form
current stock needs to be in place and followed.
Correct by: was corrected during inspection.
An exit interview was conducted with a facility representative at close of inspection.
