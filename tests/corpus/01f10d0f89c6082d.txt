Sanitation.
At the time of inspection, there was a variety of miscellaneous items, metal debris, machinery parts and clutter in the front
enclosure of the facility. Metal debris and machinery parts can cause injury and can a pose a potential risk to animals. All
buildings and premises must be kept clean, cleared and in good repair in order to prevent animals from injury and to keep
them in good health.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with authorized representative and licensee.
