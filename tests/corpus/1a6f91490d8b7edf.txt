3.127(d) REPEAT
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Sec. 3.127 Facilities, outdoor
(d) Perimeter fence.
*** There is no perimeter fence around the main enclosure housing the male and female siberian lynx. A 6ft. high
perimeter fence must be constructed around the enclosure to restrict access from unauthorized animals and people
as well as to act as a secondary containment fence.
This inspection and exit interview was conducted by the ACI, with the facility owner.
