2.1(a)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This establishment is operating as an exhibitor of regulated mammals without a valid USDA Animal Welfare license.

You may not exhibit to the public any regulated mammals (including, but not limited to: rabbits, goats, pigs, sheep)
without a valid USDA Animal Welfare license. A pre license application packet will be mailed to you under separate
cover. If you wish to continue to exhibit regulated mammals, please complete the application for license and submit
it to the Raleigh Regional Office with the correct application fee. Once you application is received you will be
contacted to schedule a pre license inspection.
