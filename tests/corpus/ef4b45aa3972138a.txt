PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
In the main row of doe cages (built before 1990), measuring 30"x42"= 1,260 square inches of space, there were 5
rabbits all over 6 lbs in one cage. Each rabbit over 6 lbs requires 288 square inches of space, so 5 rabbits over 6
lbs requires (5x288=)1440 square inches. In this size of doe cage, a maximum of 4 rabbits over 6 lbs can be
housed in order to meet the space regulation.
One rabbit was immediately removed from the doe cage.
