2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 2:30 pm on
11-Sep-19. The inspector was unable to knock on the registrant's door as the address listed is in an limited access
apartment building. The contact number was called and the inspector waited thirty minutes in attempts to reach the
registrant. All attempts were unsuccessful.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
