REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This facility has held an exhibitors license since 2010. In June 2016 their license was cancelled for (cid:28)failure to
renew(cid:29).
They were contacted by the area inspector by phone and email on July 5, 2016 to let them know their license had
been cancelled and to encourage them to get their paperwork in for a new license immediately. The area inspector
talked with the facility again by phone on July 25, 2016 to encourage them to get their paperwork for a new license
into the office immediately if they were going to continue exhibiting animals.
On Aug 3, 2016, USDA inspectors Dr. Jeanne Kjos and Dr. Cindy DiGesualdo stopped by the facility. The animal
exhibits were open to the public. As of the morning of Aug 3, 2016 the Fort Collins regional office had not received
any paperwork from this facility.
Any person exhibiting exotic species to the public must have a valid USDA license.
Exit briefing conducted with office manager on August 3, 2016.
