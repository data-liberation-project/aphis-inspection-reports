2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
** A Yorkshire Terrier (# 072-046-296) had twisted, rope-like hair matts dangling off either side of the muzzle. This
matted hair can pull at the skin causing pain and irritate the skin. This dog also had extremely long toenails that
were curving around and would not allow the dog to stand in a normal postural stance. Licensee must trim the
toenails and remove the matted hair and maintain a regular grooming schedule as needed per dog.
* To be corrected by: 1/26/16
** A grey and white Shi Tzu (#0A02460431) had thickly matted hair to the skin on the inside of the hind legs,
clumps of matted hair hung off the head and face of the dog. These matts are interfering with vision and can pull on
the skin causing pain and irritation. Both of the eyes appeared to be bulging and conical in shape . They also have
a grey-green mucoid type film covering them. Licensee must remove the matted hair and have this dog examined
by a veterinarian for a diagnosis and appropriate treatment. Documentation must be available upon request of the
inspector.
* To be corrected by: 1/26/16
** A bottle of expired Albon (2/2014) was in the fridge and available for use on the dogs. Expired medications may
not work effectively. Licensee must remove the medication from use and ensure that all medications in use are not
expired.
* To be corrected: 1/22/16
