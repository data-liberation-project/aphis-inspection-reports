2.27(a)
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF OPERATION.
The registrant moved his business site used for inspection purposes and has not yet notified the regional office of
this address change. A registrant shall notify the Animal Care Regional Director by certified mail of any change in
business operations affecting its status as an intermediate handler, including address changes, within 10 days after
making such change.
Timely notification of changes are required to maintain communication between the registrant and Animal Care and
facilitate the inspection process.
