2.40(b)(3)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
*** A dog "Buster" has inter digital cysts on two feet. The licensee had not observed the cysts or started treatment.
These cysts could be painful and should be communicated to the AV in a timely manner for assessment and a
treatment plan.

hanging down and she did not seem responsive to people or her surroundings. Her condition should be
communicated to the AV for possible assessment and treatment if needed.
