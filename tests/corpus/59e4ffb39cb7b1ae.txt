2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 200pm on
04/01/2014.
The inspector knocked on the front door and called into a barn with negative results. A phone message was left on
the licensees' cell number advising that the inspector was currently at the residence and to call and advise what
days and times are best for inspections.
