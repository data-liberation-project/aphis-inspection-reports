3.106(b)(3)

Water quality.
Water quality records were reviewed, and it was identified that the facility is currently only checking pH levels for the pools
housing the manatees biweekly. It is important to sample and test water daily to maintain water quality standards. Water
in the manatee pools shall be sampled and tested for pH levels at least daily.
To be corrected by 3rd March 2021.
