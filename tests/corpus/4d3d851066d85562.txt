HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
In some of the sheltered housing enclosures, containing at least thirteen dogs, the outdoor flooring is constructed of
concrete and this concrete is cracked and broken. Dirt and debris has collected in these cracks. Cracked and
broken concrete cannot be readily cleaned and sanitized. All surfaces must be maintained on a regular basis and
those surfaces that cannot be cleaned and sanitized must be repaired or replaced when worn or soiled.
