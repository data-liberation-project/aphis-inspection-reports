OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
Next to the house on the east side in the exercise yard housing one Adult Basset Hound the wood surfaces that are
in contact with the dog is no longer impervious to moisture. This includes the door frame and stairs which no longer
has paint leaving exposed wood. Unsealed wood surfaces can harbor bacteria and other disease pathogens that
can be harmful to the dogs. The entrance to the plastic shelter in the exercise yard has an excess accumulation of
mud and bedding caked to the surface which shows it has not been properly cleaned and sanitized.
Ensure that surfaces in contact with the animals in outdoor facilities are impervious to moisture and can be properly
cleaned, sanitized or replaced.
