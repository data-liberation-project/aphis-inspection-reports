ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 3:00 pm on
13-Aug-19. The inspector knocked on the facility door, called the contact number, and waited for thirty minutes at
the facility in attempts to reach the registrant. All attempts were unsuccessful.
