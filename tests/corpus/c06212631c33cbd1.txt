3.131(a)
SANITATION.
Fecal accumulations were observed on the wire floor of some enclosures where the floor was supported by two inch
iron bars. This accumulation of feces may contaminate the animals, attract insects, or lead to disease. Correct by
assuring excreta is removed from primary enclosures as often as necessary to prevent contamination of the animals
contained therein and to minimize disease hazards and to reduce odors. To be corrected by 25 March 2016
An exit briefing was conducted with Dr. Geib, Dr. Oelberg and the facility owner.
