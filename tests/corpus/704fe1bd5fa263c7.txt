2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
A current record of guinea pigs on hand was not available for review during this inspection. There were no inventory
records besides the number of weanlings indicating the number of adults or newborns present for several months.
Records need to be kept and maintained on guinea pigs held including births and deaths to help keep track of animals
and for evaluating the animal care program. Licensee must ensure that records are kept and maintained for guinea pigs
on hand including births and deaths. Correct by January 17, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
