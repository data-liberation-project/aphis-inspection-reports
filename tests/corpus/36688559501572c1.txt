2.75(b)(2)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The facility does not currently have a method in place to document or maintain records (other then sale paperwork)
for any offspring born from their breeding stock (hedgehog and sugar gliders). In addition, the facility does not have
any disposition records for one of the breeding females,"peper" listed on the Acquisition record for the adult sugar
gliders. The Licensee stated that this animal had been sold because she did not get along with her cage mates.
However; no sales/disposition documentation was able to be provided at the time of inspection. Acquisition and
Disposition records must be maintained in order to properly ID animals on the premise and to preform proper
traceback and traceforward of all animals. The Licensee must ensure that Acquisition and Disposition records are
maintained for all animals purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, or otherwise in her possession or
under her control, or which is transported, sold, euthanized, or otherwise disposed of. The records shall also
include any offspring born of any animal while in her possession or under her control.
