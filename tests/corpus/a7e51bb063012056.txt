2.31(c)(3)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The most recent semi annual evaluation and report to the Institutional Official (IO) was not approved by a majority of the
Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC) at the time of inspection. The November 2022 semi annual
evaluation and report to the IO was completed, but not approved and signed by a majority of the IACUC. Reports to the
IO shall be reviewed and signed by a majority of the IACUC and updated at least once every six months upon completion
of the required semi-annual evaluations.

Corrected during inspection.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the IACUC representative.
