3.127(d)

Facilities, outdoor.
The adult male kangaroo was recently moved to a new enclosure. This enclosure has one side without a perimeter fence
or public barrier, allowing the possibility of public contact through the primary enclosure fence which is comprised of
square gap field fencing. Public barriers protect both the animals and people from injury. Ensure the animal is protected
from public contact. Correct by 09 September 2021.
3.130

Watering.
The back pasture which houses 3 adolescent bison, 3 eland, and 4 waterbucks contains a pond for the animals but does
not current contain a receptacle or trough in which the animals can have access to potable water. Access to clean
potable water is essential to ensure the health and welfare of the animals within. Provide animals with access to clean
and potable water as often as necessary for the health and comfort of the animals. Correct by 09 September 2021
