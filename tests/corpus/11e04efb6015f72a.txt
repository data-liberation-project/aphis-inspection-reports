2.40(b)(3)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
***There are three sheep (1 large white male, 1 white female and 1 black female) in the large pen that have hair
loss, dermatitis and reddened moist patches of skin. The large white male sheep has hair-loss above and between
his eyes, the white female has generalized hair-loss on the side of her abdomen with the skin appearing pink and
moist, the black female has generalized hair-loss with large areas of dry hair and flaky dermatitis. The male was
observed rubbing along the fence as if scratching. The veterinarian has not been notified of the sheep's condition.
The veterinarian shall be contacted and the sheep treated according to their condition. Documentation of treatment
shall be retained for review during follow-up inspection.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Director.
