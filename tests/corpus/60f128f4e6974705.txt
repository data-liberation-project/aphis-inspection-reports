2.40(d)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Bloody diarrhea was noted in the litter box in an enclosure with Sphinx cats. Licensee stated that these cats had a
recurring problem and have been under veterinary treatment. No documentation of veterinary evaluation or
treatment was available at the time of the inspection. Each exhibitor shall establish and maintain programs of
adequate veterinary care that include the use of appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat
diseases and injuries. A system of documentation of veterinary care must be present and maintained including for
the re-evaluation of existing chronic conditions.
