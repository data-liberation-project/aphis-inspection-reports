3.53(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Section 3.53(a)(1) Primary enclosures: In the enclosure housing two rabbits the ramp on the right side going up to
the shelter has a screw head that is protruding up out of the horizontal wood slat (2nd from the bottom). The slat is
no longer secured to the ramp on its right side and the right side of the slat has slid down to the next horizontal slat
on the ramp. The rabbits may be injured on the screw head or lose slat. Correct by reattaching the slat to the
ramp or remove the slat to protect the rabbits from injury. Correct by October 13, 2016.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility secretary.
