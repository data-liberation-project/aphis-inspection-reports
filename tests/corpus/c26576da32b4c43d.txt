2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
On January 22, 2016, a search was conducted in response to unlicensed activity being conducted at this exotic
animal pet store. This facility does not currently have an active U.S.D.A. license. The previous license was
cancelled on June 10, 2015. This facility has been advertising or offering to sell covered, exotic animals in-store as
well as on the facility(cid:25)'s internet site without a valid license. The regulations and standards have been discussed
with the facility owner and the process of obtaining a license with the USDA has been initiated.
No regulated activity may be conducted until a USDA license is issued.
Exit interview was conducted with facility owner.
