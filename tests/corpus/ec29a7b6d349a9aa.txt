3.13(c)
CONSIGNMENTS TO CARRIERS AND INTERMEDIATE HANDLERS.
An adult spayed female pit bull just received in the cargo area for transport does not have documentation from the
consignor that she was offered food and water within four hours of delivery for transport.
Documentation of time the animal was last fed and watered informs the transporters as to when next food and
water must be provided.
Correct by ensuring from now on that required documentation is complete for animals accepted in transit.
The inspection and discussion of the non-compliant item were conducted with cargo area employees.
