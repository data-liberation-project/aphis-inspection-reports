3.131(a) REPEAT
SANITATION.
The goat and llama barn contained excessive feces on the bird roosts and behind the dividing half wall. The five
goats and single llama share the barn with chickens. Excessive feces build up can cause diseases in animals.
Remove the feces and clean the affected areas. Repeat as necessary to prevent build up.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner. VMOs Dr. Sara Savage and Dr. Kristin Napoli
were also present during the inspection.
