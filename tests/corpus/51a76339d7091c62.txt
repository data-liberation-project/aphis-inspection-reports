3.13(a)

Veterinary care for dogs.
No Program of Veterinary Care for dogs, documented in writting and signed by the attending veterinarian, was avaliable
for review at time of inspection.

A Program of Veterinary Care for dogs addressing the requirements of adequate veterinary care is necessary to insure
the health and well being of the dogs at the facility.

The registrant will develope, document in writting and have signed by the attending veterinarian a Program of Veterinary
Care that meets all the requirements of section 3.13 - veterinary code for dogs.
