3.131(c)
SANITATION.
Section 3.131(c) Housekeeping: Empty feed bags were in the hall and left-over food/bedding remained on the floor
in a previously used animal room. Empty soiled enclosures and trays remained in the animal rooms. Bacteria and
fungi may grow in feces, old bedding and food and affect the health of the animals. The entry/exit and common
areas were not regularly cleaned and were soiled to the extent that people and equipment could track debris into
the animal rooms and affect the health of the animals. Premises must be kept clean to facilitate the prescribed
husbandry practices set forth in this subpart, prevent odors, deter pests, and to protect the health of the animals.
Correct by February 17, 2015.
This inspection was conducted on February 9-10, 2015 and covered locations 1 and 2.
