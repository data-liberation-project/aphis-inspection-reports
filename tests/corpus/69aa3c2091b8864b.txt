3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
There are accumulations of excreta and soiled bedding in at least 6 enclosures housing guinea pigs. These
enclosures were last cleaned over 2 weeks ago. Ineffective cleaning and sanitation exposes the animals to disease
hazards such as bacterial, fungal and viral diseases. Primary enclosures must be cleaned often enough to prevent
an accumulation of excreta and soiled bedding and must be sanitized at least once every 2 weeks. Correct by
ensuring that all primary enclosures are kept clean and that enclosures are sanitized at least once every 2 weeks.
Correct by January 23, 2016.
