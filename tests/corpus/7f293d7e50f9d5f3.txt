2.31(c)(1)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC reviewed the facility's program of human care and use of animals most recently on 28 Dec 2020. As the
review has not been completed since then, the interval between reviews will be longer than the required 6 months. The
IACUC is responsible for ensuring that the research facility's program uses and cares for animals in a humane manner
according to the Animal Welfare Act. An IACUC that does not meet to review the program in a timely manner does not
fulfill these responsibilities. The IACUC must review the research facility's program for humane care and use of animals at
least once every six months. To be corrected from this day forward.
2.31(c)(2)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
The IACUC reviewed the animal facility most recently on 28 Dec 2020. As the review has not been completed since then,
the interval between reviews will be longer than the required 6 months. The IACUC is responsible for ensuring that the
research facility houses and cares for animals in a humane manner according to the Animal Welfare Act. An IACUC that
does not meet to review the program in a timely manner does not fulfill these responsibilities. The IACUC must inspect all
of the research facility's animal facilities, including animal study areas at least once every six months. To be corrected
from this day forward.
