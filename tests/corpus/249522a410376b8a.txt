ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The facility has acquired hedgehogs for breeding purposes. The facility had the attending veterinarian visit recently,
however the written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) does not include hedgehogs. Formal arrangements set forth
in a written PVC and approved by the attending veterinarian assures APHIS officials that adequate veterinary care,
husbandry guidance, and regularly scheduled visits will be provided for each regulated animal species at the facility.
The licensee must have a PVC approved by a licensed veterinarian that outlines the care and husbandry for each
regulated species at the facility.
