2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
EACH EXHIBITOR SHALL MAINTAIN A WRITTEN PROGRAM OF VETERINARY CARE. THE CURRENT
PROGRAM OF VETERINARY CARE IS NOT UP TO DATE. THE PROGRAM DOES NOT INCLUDE ALL OF THE
SPECIES OF ANIMALS KEPT BY THE FACILITY. EACH SPECIES MUST BE INCLUDED AND ADDRESSED IN
THE WRITTEN PROGRAM OF VETERINARY CARE.
