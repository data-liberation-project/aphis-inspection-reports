Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The facility is switching to a new attending veterinarian as the previous attending veterinarian is retiring. The new
attending veterinarian completed the annual facility visit and preventative health procedures in the fall of 2021. The new
attending veterinarian has not yet completed a written program of veterinary care. A written program is required to
formally indicate the veterinary care arrangements for the animals at the facility. The facility shall ensure that a written
program of veterinary care is completed. Correct by May 31, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
