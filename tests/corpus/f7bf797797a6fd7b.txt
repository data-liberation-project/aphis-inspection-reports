3.130
WATERING.
The watering tub in the enclosure was dry upon inspectors' arrival. Workers at the facility reported that it had not
been filled in at least a day. At inspectors' request, the watering tub was filled and the animal was observed to drink
for about one minute. Infrequent watering can lead to health conditions and cause discomfort for the animal.
Licensee must ensure that the animal has regular and routine access to drinking water to provide for it's comfort
and health.
Item was corrected during inspection. Ensure water is provided regularly.
Exit interview conducted with facility representative.
