3.80(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Section 3.80(a)(1) Primary enclosures: In the Small Mammal House, enclosure E6 housing a pygmy marmoset has
the fiberglass detaching from the underlying wall to the left of the doorway to enclosure E7. The pygmy marmoset
may injure itself on the edge of the fiberglass or by trying to reach between the fiberglass and wall. Primary
enclosures must be kept in good repair to protect the animals from injuries. Correct by securing the fiberglass to the
wall. Correct by May 3, 2017.
This inspection was conducted with the facility director of animal health, facility animal curators, and the animal
collections manager. The exit interview was conducted with the facility director of animal health.
