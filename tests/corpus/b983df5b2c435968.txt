2.40(a)(1) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee has acquired capybara and they are not listed on the program of veterinary care and there are no
recommendations for routine care for these animals.
Each exhibitor shall have an attending veterinarian, in the case of a part-time veterinarian, the formal arrangements
shall include written instructions on routine veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits.
Have your veterinarian add any new species added to your collection at the time they are acquired.
3.128 REPEAT
SPACE REQUIREMENTS.
The licensee has three wallaby's that are housed in vari-kennels. The wallaby's are allowed access to the house
twice daily.
Enclosures shall provide sufficient space to allow each animal to make normal postural and social adjustments with
adequate freedom of movement.
Build an enclosure or move the wallaby to an enclosure that allows the animal to make normal postural movements
and allows freedom of movement.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
