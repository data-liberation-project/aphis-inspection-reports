2.31(d)(1)(iv)(A)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Protocols 2016-0050, 2015-0094, 2015-0092,2016-0029 and 2013-0121 did not contain scientific justification in
writing for the withholding of appropriate sedatives, analgesics or anesthetics. These protocols were placed in
category "E" by the principal investigator and this information is required to be included in the protocol. This
information is necessary in order for the IACUC to determine that these animals are being used in a humane and
judicious manner.
Correct by: August 10, 2017.
2.31(e)(4)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Protocol 2016-0050 did not contain a complete description of the use of analgesic drugs for one of the procedures
in the protocol. This information must be included in the protocol in order for the IACUC to determine that the
animals are being used in a humane manner.
Correct by: August 10, 2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with various facility employees including the attending
veterinarian and the manager of the IACUC and IBC.
