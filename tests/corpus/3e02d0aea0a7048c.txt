2.126(c)(1)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
During a routine inspection on September 18, 2014, the facility manager indicated that some of the staff members
were on travel with a collection of animals from their facility. The animals were on overnight travel in another state
yet there was no notification sent to the regional office beforehand. All exhibitors must notify the office with an
itinerary 48 hours before travel.

Exit interview conducted with facility manager.
