3.131(c)

Sanitation.
The kitchen and food prep area is in need of cleaning and organization. The stainless steel sink and work space is
covered in soap scum and hard water stains. The work/counter space on either side of the sink and the small ledge
above and behind the sink and counter space were completely covered with food preparation items, bowls, assorted
plastic containers, and cleaning supplies and implements.
This area needs to be cleaned and organized to ensure that food and food receptacles cannot be contaminated during
cleaning and/or preparation.

Correct by 2/18/2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
