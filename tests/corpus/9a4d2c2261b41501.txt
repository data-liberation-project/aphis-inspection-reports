3.26(d) REPEAT
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
***** The ceiling panels in the animal facility are not adequately affixed to the ceiling. The panels are bowed, leaving
gaps at the seams of the panels. There is a large opening around one of the vents in the ceiling . The surfaces can
not be readily cleaned and sanitized as required. The gap openings can provide access areas for the harborage of
insects, rodents, and/or other pests. The ceiling and wall panels are not in a good state of repair. Repair and/or
replace wall and ceiling panels to facilitate the prescribed husbandry practices and to promote the health and
well-being of the animals housed in the facility.
INSPECTION AND EXIT BRIEFING CONDUCTED WITH THE FACILITY REPRESENTATIVE.
