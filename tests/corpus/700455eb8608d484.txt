3.9(b)
FEEDING.
* There is a brownish looking greasy substance coating the interior of six metal food receptacles used by six dogs.
The brownish substance is covering an estimated 25 to 40 percent of the interior area of the receptacle. Unsanitary
conditions exist from a lack of cleaning of the interior area of the food receptacle. Food receptacles which are not
kept clean could harbor bacteria and other disease-causing agents that can affect the health of the dogs. The
licensee must ensure all food receptacles are kept clean and must be sanitized in accordance with 3.11 (b) of the
regulations. Correct by 11/23/15.
* The facility inspection and verbal exit briefing done on 11/9/15 were conducted with the licensee.
* Copy of the inspection report was sent by certified and regular mail.
