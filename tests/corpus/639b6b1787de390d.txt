3.50(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
One of the rabbit enclosures had jagged broken plastic edges. This can possibly cause injury to the rabbit and also
affect the ability of the surface to be sanitized. The licensee needs to ensure that enclosures are properly
maintained and structurally sound. The enclosure was replaced at the time of inspection.
