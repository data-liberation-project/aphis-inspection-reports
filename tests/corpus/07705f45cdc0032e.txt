3.58(a)

Classification and separation.
A male rabbit was housed with several peacocks. Rabbits shall only be housed with other rabbits in the same primary
enclosure to avoid dissemination of disease, social incompatibility, and stress that may adversely impact their welfare.
The licensee shall ensure that only rabbits are housed in the same primary enclosure.
