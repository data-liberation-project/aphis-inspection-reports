3.26(d)

Facilities, indoor.
Interior surfaces. There is bare wood on the window sills and windows frames in the new upper guinea pigs room.
Building surfaces in indoor guinea pig buildings must be constructed and maintained so that the surfaces are impervious
to moisture so that surfaces can be readily sanitized. The bare wood on the windows sills and window frames in the
upper guinea pig room must be sealed or covered with an impervious surfaces so that surfaces can be repeatedly
cleaned and sanitized. Correct by August 1, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
