2.1(a)(1)(iii)

Requirements and application.
There are animals being exhibited that are housed at a location approximately 60 miles from the licensed site. This
second site is not listed on the approved sites for regulated activity. These animals currently include rabbits, guinea pigs,
wallaby, chinchilla, and coati.
Facilities and animals at locations not approved for regulated activity are not available for inspection to ensure compliance
with the Animal Welfare Act.
Exhibitors must provide valid addresses for all locations, facilities, premise, or sites where animals, animal facilities,
equipment, and records are held, kept, or maintained.
Correct by: January 12, 2023
2.134(a)

Contingency planning.
There was no written contingency plan on file at the facility.
Failure to have a written contingency plan can result in lack of readiness for emergencies or disasters.
Facilities must develop, document, and follow an appropriate plan to provide for the humane handling, treatment,


transportation, housing, and care of their animals in the event of an emergency or disaster.
