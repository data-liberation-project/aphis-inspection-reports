2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The attending veterinarian has made a recent visit to the facility; however, the licensee does not have a written
program of veterinary care. A written program of veterinary care is required when the attending veterinarian is
employed on a part-time basis, in order to ensure the facility has a program to follow and all animals at the facility
have adequate veterinary care. A written program of veterinary care in consultation with the attending veterinarian
must be in place. To be corrected by 9/17/18.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the facility representative.
