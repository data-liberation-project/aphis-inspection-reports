Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
* Licensee had no documentation of facility inspection by the attending veterinarian. Regularly scheduled visits by the
attending veterinarian to the premise must be done where animals are kept, to assess and ensure the adequacy of
veterinary care and other aspects of animal care. The licensee must ensure that the attending veterinarian shall do
regularly scheduled visits to the premises. Correct by 1/1/23
