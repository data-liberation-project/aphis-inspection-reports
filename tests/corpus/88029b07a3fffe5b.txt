FACILITIES, GENERAL.
During this inspection it was noted that the food storage trailer was in need of attention. There was opened bags of
food present. There was spilled grain on the floor between the pallets. The freezer that was being used to store
frozen diet items had an accumulation of grim and frozen blood at the bottom. To ensure that the supplies are
protected from deterioration or contamination by vermin this trailer, and freezer, need to be addressed.
