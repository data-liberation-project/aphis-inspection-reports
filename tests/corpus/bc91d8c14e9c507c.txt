2.1
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The facility exhibits domestic animals including goats, rabbits and a pig, seasonally and at other times during the
year by appointment. The exhibition of these regulated animals to the public for compensation requires a valid
USDA license. A USDA license has not been issued to any person exhibiting animals at this facility.
The owners indicated willingness to comply with federal regulations and intend to apply for licensing. They are in
receipt of an application packet.
No regulated activities may be conducted until a valid USDA license is obtained.
