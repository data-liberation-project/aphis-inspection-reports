2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
There is no record of animals on hand.

Every exhibitor shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the following
information concerning animals other than dogs and cats, purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, or
otherwise in his or her possession or under his or her control, or which is transported, sold, euthanized, or otherwise
disposed of by that exhibitor. The records shall include any offspring born of any animal while in his or her possession or
under his or her control.

The licensee must ensure all animal are listed on the record of animals on hand in order to allow for tracing and tracking
of animals in case of disease outbreaks.
