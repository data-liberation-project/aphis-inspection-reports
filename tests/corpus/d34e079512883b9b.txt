2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The annual scheduled visit by the attending veterinarian is overdue. For the health and welfare of the animals, each
dealer needs to ensure that the attending veterinarian makes regularly scheduled visits to the premises according to
the written program of veterinary care.
