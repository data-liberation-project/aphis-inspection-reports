3.129(a)
FEEDING.
The wolves' diet is a non-commercially prepared mix consisting of beef, chicken, and fish with supplements. This
diet has not been reviewed by the Attending Veterinarian.
The non-commercially prepared diet must be reviewed by the Attending Veterinarian; to ensure the diet is of
sufficient quantity and nutritive value to maintain all animals in good health. Steps must be taken to follow the
Veterinarian reviewed dietary guideline for proper feeding of the wolves.
