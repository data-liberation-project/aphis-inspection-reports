3.30
WATERING.
There were accumulations of brown debris around and inside the drain pipe for the automatic water receptacles.
These accumulations around and inside the drain pipe expose the guinea pigs to potential disease infections with
bacterial, fungi, and parasites. Water receptacles including water valves and drain pipes must be cleaned and
sanitized whenever dirty and at least once every two weeks. Inadequate cleaning of the water valves and drain
pipes can result in contamination of the the guinea pigs in the same enclosure. Licensee must establish and
maintain a program to keep the water valves and drain pipes sanitized when dirty, and must be sanitized at least
once every two weeks. Correct by May 18, 2017.
