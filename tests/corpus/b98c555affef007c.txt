3.129(a)
FEEDING.
All food intended to be consumed by regulated animals was found stored in one main refrigerator, but collocated
with human food. The food stored for regulated animals shall be free from potential contamination to maintain all
animals in good health. Human and animal food should be segregated and stored separatedly in different
refrigerators/locations to avoid the potential of contamination and disease.
