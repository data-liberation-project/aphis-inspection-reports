2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The owner is operating a business that sells dogs to the public. More than more than 3 breeding female dogs are
currently present at the facility. A federal license with USDA Animal Care is required in order to conduct business in
this manner. The operator of this facility must cease conducting regulated activities until such time that she apply
for a license, successfully complete the prelicense process, and pay all fees associated with obtaining a license.
This interview was conducted with ACI 6021.
