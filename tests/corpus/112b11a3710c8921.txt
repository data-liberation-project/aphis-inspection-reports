2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The formal arrangements between the licensee and attending veterinarian do not include all of the species at the
facility. No information is provided for 24 hour emergency care. The methods of euthanasia inaccurately states that
the licensee is authorized to euthanize animals. The attending veterinarian has not made a formal visit in over one
year according to records. Without complete, accurate records and regular visits from the attending veterinarian,
there is no was to ascertain whether or not the animals are receiving adequate veterinary care. The licensee and
attending veterinarian must update and complete the formal arrangements, followed by a scheduled visit by the
attending veterinarian.
