NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME, ADDRESS, CONTROL, OR OWNERSHIP OF BUSINESS.

contact have been disconnected. The whelping facility and outdoor enclosures had been removed from the property
along with all the animals. The licensee in previous conversations had disclosed that they were moving. During
these conversations the licensee was informed of the ten day certified mail notification of change of address to the
regional office. Checking ACIS no notification had been sent to the regional office for a change of address. A
licensee shall promptly notify the AC Regional Director by certified mail of any change in the name, address,
management, or substantial control or ownership of his business or operation, or of any additional sites, within 10
