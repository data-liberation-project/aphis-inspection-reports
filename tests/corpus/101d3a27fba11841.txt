Sec. 3.6 (a)(2)(iii) Primary enclosures. General requirements
There are three enclosures that dogs have dug holes around the fencing and gate areas. The holes allow the dogs
to escape or other animals to enter the enclosures. All enclosures must be maintained on a regular basis to keep
the dogs secured or prevent other animals to enter.
