2.50(a)(1)
TIME AND METHOD OF IDENTIFICATION.
The licensee uses tags for identification. However, there are three Bichon dogs that are not properly identified by
an official USDA tag.
Lack of official identification does not allow for the identification and tracking of the dogs.
The three dogs must be tagged with an official USDA tag.
Correct by: March 26, 2014.
3.3(e)(1)(1)
SHELTERED HOUSING FACILITIES.
The concrete floor in the housing facility is not impervious to moisture. The adult dogs have occassional contact
with the concrete floor. Also, there is currently one puppy housed in an enclosure that sits directly on the floor.
Surfaces that are not impervious to moisture can absorb water, urine or other substances that can harbor odors.
Also, the floor cannot be properly sanitized and can affect the health, comfort and well-being of the animals.
Indoor floor areas in contact with the animals must be made impervious to moisture.
Correct by: June 30, 2014.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with licensee.
End of report.
