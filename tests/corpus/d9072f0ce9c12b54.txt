2.131(c)(1) REPEAT
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
There are currently no attendants, barriers or video feed in place to prevent public contact with the camel, reindeer
and Muntjac. Video has been added, but when electric water buckets are plugged in and the cameras are plugged
in the electrical circuit blows. The video feeds have been unplugged during the winter when electric water buckets
are in use. There is no camera feed for the muntjac.
During public exhibition, any animal must be handled so there is minimal risk of harm to the animal and to the
public, with sufficient distance and/or barriers between the animal and the general viewing public so as to assure
the safety of animals and the public.
Sufficient distance or barriers must be in place to prevent public contact with wild or exotic animals to ensure the
safety of the animals and the public.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
