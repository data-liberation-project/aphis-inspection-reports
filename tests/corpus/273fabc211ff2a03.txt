ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.

environment enhancement adequate to promote the psychological well-being of nonhuman primates. The plan must

reference guides, and as directed by the attending veterinarian. This plan must be made available to APHIS upon
request.
