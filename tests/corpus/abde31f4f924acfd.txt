3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
One sloth named "Syd " is housed in an enclosure that has electrical cords from an air conditioner and a humidifier in
reach of the sloth. These appliances were running at time of the inspection and pose a hazard to the sloth if he should
bite or claw into the cord. All electrical cords must be protected from reach of the sloth to prevent injury .

3.127(d)

Facilities, outdoor.
The recently acquired juvenile tiger is housed in an outdoor enclosure that is not surrounded by an 8 foot perimeter
fence. An 8 foot perimeter fence serves to restrict animals and keeps unauthorized persons out . All outdoor housing
facilities for potentially dangerous animals such as tigers and other large felines must be surrounded by an 8 foot
perimeter fence that is at least 3 feet from the primary enclosure.
