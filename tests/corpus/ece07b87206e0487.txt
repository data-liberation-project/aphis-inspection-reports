2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11:35 AM and
5:00 PM on 06/08/2016. The inspector arrived on the property at 11:35 AM and spoke to a resident who stated the
licensee was unavailable and wouldn't be home for a while. The inspector called the licensee's cell phone number
and left a message.
The inspector returned to the property again at 5:00 PM. A resident said the licensee was not home. The licensee
stated on the phone that he was not in the area and unavailable for an inspection.
