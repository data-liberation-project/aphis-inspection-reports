Facilities, general.
* Three different areas in the exterior tin wall located in the goat and sheep enclosure is showing missing, jagged tin
with a sharp edge. The affected areas are located on the south side of the outdoor housing area. Twenty three goats
and five sheep are in contact with the affected tin wall located in outdoor housing. Sharp, jagged edges could potentially
injure the goats and sheep. The licensee must ensure that all surfaces shall be maintained in good repair to protect the
animals from injury. Correct by 10/6/22.

* Protruding nails were observed in the exterior wood wall. Two potbelly pigs are in contact with the affected wall
located in outdoor housing. Protruding nails could cause potential injury. The licensee must ensure that all surfaces
shall be maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury. Correct by 10/6/22.
