Sanitation.
Housekeeping. The floor of the animal room had accumulations of boxes, bags, tools, supplies, dirt, debris, and spilled
feed scattered on the floor. Animal rooms must be kept clean and in good repair to protect the animals and to prevent
unnecessary disease risks. Ineffective cleaning can expose the guinea pigs to disease hazards and increase health
problems. Correct by ensuring the animal premises are kept clean, including: sweeping the floor on a regular basis,
removing clutter from the animal room, storing items properly for the care of the guinea pigs, and maintaining a general
cleanliness to support good animal husbandry and health. Correct by May 7, 2022.
