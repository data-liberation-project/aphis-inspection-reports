2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Since the previous inspection report, records of births and deaths were not available for review for the guinea pigs.
Records must be kept and maintained on guinea pig births, deaths, purchases, owned, held, transported,
euthanized, sold or otherwise disposed of by the licensee. Licensee must ensure that records are kept and
maintained on guinea pig births and deaths to document changes in ownership, to keep track of animals, and to
help assess the animal care program. Correct by October 12, 2019.
3.29(d)
FEEDING.
There are 23 self-feeders that are rusty and pitted. Rusty and pitted feeders cannot be repeatedly cleaned and
sanitized properly and may harbor bacteria and fungus. Rusty and pitted self-feeders must be repaired to replaced
so that they can be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized. Correct by October 26, 2019.
