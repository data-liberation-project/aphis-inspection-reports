3.125(a) REPEAT
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Structural strength.
The enclosure housing three pigs has several nails on the inside south wall of the shelter protruding from the wall.
The enclosure housing three rams also has several nails protruding outward from the shelter on the northwest
outside corner and the inside south wall. Sharp points present a physical hazard that could injure the animals.
Repair or remove any sharp points to prevent injury and maintain enclosures in good repair.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with the owner.
