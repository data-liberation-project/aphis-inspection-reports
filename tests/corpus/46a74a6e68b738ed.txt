3.80(a)(2)(2)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Nonhuman primates.
** The nonhuman primates have heat lamps hanging on the outside of the enclosures. The nonhuman primates
could grab the electric cords if they choose to do so, this could cause injury to the nonhuman primates. The electric
cords must be moved and/or covered so that the nonhuman primates can not access the electric cords. All
enclosures must be constructed and maintained to protect the nonhuman primates from injury. To be corrected by:
11-14-14.
3.127(b)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Shelter from inclement weather.
** The ambient temperature at the time of the inspection was 33.6 deg F. The hoof stock, raccoons, wolves and
cougars did not have bedding provided. Bedding must be provided during cold weather months to afford the animals
protection and to prevent discomfort to the animals.Clean dry bedding must be added to the outdoor shelters. To be
corrected by: 11-15-14.
