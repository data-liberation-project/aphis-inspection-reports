2.33(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
One bottle of warfarin tablets was found with an expiration date of 11/15. These tablets were currently in use as a
daily medication for three sheep. Expired medication can have compromised efficacy and potency. The facility
needs to ensure that medications are not being used past the expiration dates. This bottle of medication was
disposed of by the facility at the time of inspection.
