2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This is a search inspection of a petting zoo exhibit conducted at Jellystone Park in Luray, VA. Heavenly Heart Farm
and Mini Petting Zoo is currently exhibiting regulated animals without a valid USDA exhibitor license as required by
the USDA Animal Welfare Act and Regulations. The following animals were exhibited at the petting zoo: 2 sugar
gliders, 1 hamster, 2 gerbils, 1 hedgehog, 2 goats, 3 sheep, 2 rabbits, 1 ferret and 4 guinea pigs.
A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations.
This facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities.
No regulated activities may be conducted until a USDA license is obtained.
Exit interview conducted with the facility representative.
The inspector completed this inspection report as a word document and re-typed the inspection report into the
system thus the “prepared by” date on this inspection report is different than the date the inspector prepared the
original inspection report, which occurred on August 10, 2017.
