3.83
WATERING.
Inside the Mandrill enclosure, the watering receptacle in this enclosure has an excess of green material covering the
inside and along the edges of the watering receptacle. Dirty water may harbor disease hazards which may pose a
health risk for the animals. All water receptacles shall be kept clean and sanitary.
Corrected at the time of inspection.
3.84(d)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
Inside of the indoor portion of the Orangutan sheltered enclosure, there is an excessive amount of rat feces inside
the enclosure. Evidence of rat feces is an indicator of an ineffective pest control program. Rat droppings can
harbor disease causing organisms and could cause potential contamination in animal areas. The licensee should
ensure an effective pest control program is in place to control pests and reduce potential contamination.
To be corrected by February 25, 2015.
