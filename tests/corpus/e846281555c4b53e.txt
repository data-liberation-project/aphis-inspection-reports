2.40(a)(1) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Exhibitors with part time attending veterinarians must have a formal arrangement with the veterinarian that includes
a written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) and regularly scheduled visits to the premises. The facility does not
have a current arrangement in place with an attending veterinarian and no regularly scheduled visits by a
veterinarian have been conducted in the past 18 months. The licensee is currently in discussions with a prospective
veterinarian to enter into a formal arrangement to provide veterinary care for this facility.
The licensee must have an arrangement with an attending veterinarian and a current program of veterinary care in
place to ensure adequate veterinary care is provided.
This is to be corrected by March 31, 2017
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
