3.55
WATERING.
***There was a buildup of green debris, hair, and mud on the interior surfaces of the water receptacle in the
enclosure that contains one rabbit. Additionally, the water in the receptacle was brown in color.
Animals must be provided with clean, potable water to minimize disease hazards and to ensure that they maintain
adequate hydration. Sufficient potable water shall be provided daily and all watering receptacles shall be sanitized
when dirty, provided that such receptacles shall be sanitized at least once every two weeks.
The water receptacle was cleaned and fresh water was added to it at the time of inspection.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
