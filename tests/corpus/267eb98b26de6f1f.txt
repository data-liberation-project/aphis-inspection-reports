2.75(a)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors
The Record of Dogs and Cats on Hand was not complete as it did not include all dogs on hand.

This information is required to know with certainty the identification, acquisition and disposition of the dogs.

Records such as the APHIS From 7005 must be updated to fully and correctly disclose the required information for every
dog held or disposed of by the dealer.
