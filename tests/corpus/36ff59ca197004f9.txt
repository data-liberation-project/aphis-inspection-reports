Facilities, general.
Three of the vertical wooden boards of one outside wall of the goat barn above the hay rack had water damage near the
roof overhang. The wooden boards were soft compared to the other boards when touched and the paint was peeling.
There was also some damage to the same outside wall of the goat barn near the corner approximately 12"x18" in size.
The outermost layer of the wood had been removed by the goats rubbing their horns on the wall and the unpainted
interior layer of the wall board was exposed.

Outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair. The Licensee needs to
make repairs to the outside wall of the goat barn.
