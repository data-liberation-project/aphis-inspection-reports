3.31(a)(1) REPEAT
SANITATION.
Primary enclosures for guinea pigs are not being cleaned adequately or often enough to remove accumulations of
dust, dirt, cobwebs, soiled bedding, and debris. Majority of the enclosures have accumulations of excreta, dirt and
debris The cleaning process utilized is not effective in removing this accumulation. Ineffective cleaning and
sanitation exposes the animals to disease hazards and health problems. Primary enclosures must be cleaned often
enough to prevent an accumulation of dust, dirt, excreta, cobwebs, and debris and enclosures must be sanitized at
least once every 2 weeks. Correct by ensuring that all primary enclosures are kept clean and sanitized often enough
to prevent an accumulation of excreta, dirt and debris.
