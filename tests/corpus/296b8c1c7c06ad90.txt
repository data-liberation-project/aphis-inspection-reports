2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Dog with microchip #0A01402443, female Yorkie, was noticed to have a heavy accumulation of dark brown crusty
matter around her left eye. Licensee stated that his son had clipped the dog 2-3 days ago and he had nicked the
skin by the eye. Upon further examination, the skin had been nicked or torn by the upper portion of the dogs eye
lid. Licensee stated that his son applied a blood stopper agent at the time it happened. However; no further
treatment has been given.
Having eye/skin issues can be painful and irritating to the dog if left untreated. Licensee must have methods to
prevent, control, diagnose and treat all eye/skin issues. This dog must be seen by a licensed veterinarian.
Documentation of the examination, diagnoses and treatment must maintained by the licensee and made available
for inspection by APHIS officials.
