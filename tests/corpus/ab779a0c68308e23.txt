Facilities, general.
Item Facilities General 3.101 a
At the time of inspection the metal door in the off exhibit holding area for sea otters was completely rusted, this resulted
in sharp metal jagged edges .
The sharp metal edges of the door has the potential to injure the animals.
Facility needs to repair or replace the damaged door so as to insure the exhibit is maintained in good repair and prevent
the animals from injury.
Correct by 11/21/2020

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
