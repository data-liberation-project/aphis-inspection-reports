FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Storage: There are gas cans, loose insulation on floor, exposed insulation on one section of the ceiling, and clutter
against the walls in the food storage room. Food supplies must be stored in facilities which adequately protect such
supplies against spoilage, deterioration, infestation or contamination by vermin. Food supplies must be stored in
areas free of clutter, gas cans, and loose insulation. The section of exposed insulation in the ceiling must be
covered to prevent pest problems.
