REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Izzy the camel is being exhibited by the owners/facility at multiple venues, and has been for several years. Such
exhibition is regulated under the Animal Welfare Act (AWA), and requires a license with USDA APHIS Animal Care.
An application packet was sent in summer of 2013 when regulated activity was discovered; no application was
made.
A valid license is required to be in compliance with the AWA, and to help protect the health and safety of the
animals and the public.
The facility must obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activity immediately.
Correction date: IMMEDIATELY
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
