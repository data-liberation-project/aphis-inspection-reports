HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
One enclosure in the whelping building had an accumulation of dirt, grime, hair and wet bedding material. The
enclosure contained one female English bulldog. The accumulations of hair, old bedding and dirt must be removed
routinely from the enclosure. Providing a clean and sanitized environment for the dogs will help to protect the dogs
from disease and other health hazards and help to ensure their health and well-being. Licensee must thoroughly
clean and sanitize all areas of the kennel building inside and out. All enclosures must be cleaned and all surfaces in
contact with the dog be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized. Corrected after completion of inspection.
