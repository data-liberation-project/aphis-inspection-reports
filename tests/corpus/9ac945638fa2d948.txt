2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Several sugar gliders were on exhibit and offered for sale in this pet store at the time of the inspection. Store
personnel confirmed that hedgehogs were also recently sold and were expected back in stock soon for future sales
at the store.
The exhibition and sale of these regulated animals requires a valid USDA license. Management stated they were in
receipt of an application packet.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
