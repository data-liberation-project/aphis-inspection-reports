2.75(b)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
Records of acquisitions, dispositions, and animals animal on hand are not available for review. Records of births, sells,
donations, deaths and any animal used for exhibition must be maintained and kept at all times. Records of acquisitions,
dispositions, and animals on hand must be available to be viewed by a USDA inspector to assist with the enforcement of
the regulations, rules, and standards of the Animal Welfare Act. Correct by March 31, 2022
