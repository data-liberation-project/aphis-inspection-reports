3.131(d)

Sanitation.
The indoor facility had numerous flies present. Flies were noted in the food bowls, the bedding around the animals, along
the floor in the coatimundi enclosure, and in the food preparation area. Fly strips were present in the facility; however,
they were filled with flies. Excessive numbers of flies can facilitate disease transmission and cause discomfort to the
animals. A safe and effective program for the control of insects shall be established and maintained.
To be corrected by: 9/2/2022

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
