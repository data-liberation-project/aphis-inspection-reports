2.126(a) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 150pm on
02/20/2014.
There were messages left on the home phone that the inspector was there for an inspection, and asking for
information on better days to conduct inspections. The dog transport vehicle was home and a car but the door was
not answered. Both the home and garage doors were knocked on, and horn honked from the driveway. The
inspector waited 40 minutes before leaving with no contact made. Messages had been left in the past after the last
Attempted in December 2013 for the licensee to contact the inspector, however no contact has been made or calls
returned by the licensee.
