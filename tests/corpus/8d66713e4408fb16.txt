FACILITIES, GENERAL.
A skunk escaped from the facility and was not recovered. The skunks were allowed access into a facility room that
contained an unsecured doggy door. A single skunk was able to access the doggy door and escape from the
facility. Animals must be housed in species appropriate enclosures/areas that do not allow for their escape to
protect their health and well-being.
Assure that all animals are housed in species appropriate enclosures/areas to prevent their escape.

The inspection and a verbal exit briefing were conducted with the facility owner's daughter. A thorough verbal exit
briefing was conducted with the facility owner.
