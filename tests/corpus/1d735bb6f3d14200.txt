2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 2:35pm on
17-SEP-19.
Inspector arrived on site and rang the doorbell, waiting several minutes for a response. Several more attempts to
ring the bell were made with no response. Attempted to contact licensee at 2:45pm by contact number provided and
left a message. Inspector waited 40 additional minutes, with intermittent attempts to reach licensee by phone and at
the door of the building. With no response from licensee, inspector left the site at 3:25pm.
