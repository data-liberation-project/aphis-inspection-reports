REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
On Sept 5, 2014 this facility was conducting armadillo races at a public event at the Cowgirl Museum in Ft Worth,
TX. Any person/facility exhibiting covered species must have a valid USDA license in order for USDA to ensure the
animals are being cared for in a manner consistent with the Animal Welfare Act. This facility needs to apply for a
license immediately or discontinue exhibiting covered species.
Search conducted with facility owner.
