3.131
SANITATION.
There were a large number of flies observed in the commissary in the new animal building. Insects such as flies
may be an irritation to the animals housed there in addition the insects could contaminate food and are a potential
disease hazard for the animals. There must be a safe, effective program for the control of insect pests.
