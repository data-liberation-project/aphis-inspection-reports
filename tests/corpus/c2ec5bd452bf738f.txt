2.40(b)(3)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Two raccoons were injured in a fire. The attending veterinarian was not contacted concerning the animals and was
unaware that they had been euthanized by another veterinarian. A mechanism of direct and frequent
communication is required so that timely and accurate information on problems of animal health, behavior, and
well-being is conveyed to the attending veterinarian; The attending veterinarian shall be kept informed of the
animals in the collection at the facility.
Correct from this day forward.
An exit briefing was conducted with the President. The report is being delivered via email to the facility.
