2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The owner of the farm has animals on exhibit to the public.
Any person operating or intending to operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except persons
who are exempted from the licensing requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section, must have a valid license.
Acquire an exhibitors license if you wish to continue to exhibit animals to the public.
Exit conducted with the owner.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
