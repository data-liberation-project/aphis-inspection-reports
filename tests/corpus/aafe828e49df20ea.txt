2.31(b)(3)(ii)

Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC).
This facility has one community member who has attended only approximately 50% of the past year’s IACUC meetings. If
the community member is not present at the meetings, this member cannot effectively represent the general community
interests in the proper care and treatment of animals. Per the regulations, at least one member of the IACUC committee
shall not be affiliated in any way with the facility other than a member of the Committee. The Secretary intends that such
person will provide representation for general community interests in the proper care and treatment of animals. Correct by
January 10, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representatives.
