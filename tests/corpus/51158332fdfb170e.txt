Watering.
19 small water bottles were observed to have dark green material on the inside of the bottle. Buildup inside water bottles
can harbor bacteria, mold, or other organisms that may result in illness in hedgehogs. The facility must ensure that water
receptacles are clean and sanitary.

applicant.

All items must be in compliance within (2) more inspection(s) or by (12/17/21) or the applicant will forfeit the license fee
and must wait 6 months to reapply. Please contact your inspector to schedule your next Relicense Inspection.
