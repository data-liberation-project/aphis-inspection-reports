Facilities, indoor.
Interior Surfaces. There is bare wood on the cabinet, shelves and work bench in the guinea pig room. Building surfaces in
indoor guinea pig buildings must be constructed and maintained so that the surfaces are impervious to moisture. The bare
wood on the cabinet, shelves, and work bench must sealed or covered with an impervious surface so that these surfaces
can be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized. Correct by September 1, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
