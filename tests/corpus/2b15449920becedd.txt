3.125(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
In the rabbit/cavy shed area, the storage container for the cavy's(3 animals) food did not have a lid and was open to
the enviroment. Open feed containers may allow for contamination by pests, vermin or debris or deterioration in
quality or nutritional value. Food supplies need to be stored in a manner which provides adequate protection from
deterioration or contamination by vermin. To be corrected by October 20th, 2019.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
