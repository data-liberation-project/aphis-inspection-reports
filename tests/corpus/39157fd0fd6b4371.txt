2.27(a)
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF OPERATION.
*Licensee conducted regulated business by selling two young Bactrian camels to a zoo in July 2014. According to
health certificates, invoices, and facility representative, these camels were housed and transported from a location
in Lancaster, MO with zoo delivery in December 2014. This site is not listed as an approved site for the licensee.
Licensee did not inform the inspector nor notify the Regional Office of any change in operations such as acquiring a
new site (Lancaster, MO). Regulated activity cannot be conducted from an unlicensed site. APHIS officials must be
aware of all sites where regulated activity may occur in order to ensure the well-being of all animals through the
Animal Welfare inspection process. Licensee must notify the AC Regional Office by certified mail of any changes in
operations (including a site change or addition) within 10 days after making such a change.
