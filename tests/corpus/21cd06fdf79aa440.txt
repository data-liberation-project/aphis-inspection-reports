TIME AND METHOD OF IDENTIFICATION.
****
Puppy Identification
There are 2 litters of puppies that are not identified properly. The licensee is using cage cards as a means of
identifying the puppies but is missing the unique puppy identification number and the required USDA license
number. The licensee must complete the cage cards for the 2 litters of puppies to include the USDA license
number and a unique identification number for each puppy. The licensee must ensure that all required information
is listed on the cage cards at all times to help facilitate record keeping requirements.
