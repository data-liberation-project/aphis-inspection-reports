ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
An adult Yorkshire terrier male, (microchip number 094-791-592) was lame and not bearing any weight on its left
front leg during time of inspection. The front paw had an abnormal nail. The nail was approximately 3 to 4 times
larger in diameter than the rest of the nails and excessively long. The licensee indicated that this dog(cid:25)s condition
had not been reported to the veterinarian during the time of inspection. The licensee must have this animal
examined by the veterinarian to ensure that an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment plan are obtained.
The licensee must document this information and make it available to the inspector upon request. The licensee
must ensure that all animals receive adequate veterinary care at all times.
