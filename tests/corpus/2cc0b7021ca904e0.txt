2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 12:30 pm on
30-Jan-18. The inspector called the licensee's phone and knocked at the facility door in attempts to contact the
licensee. Both attempts failed. The inspector waited in the driveway for 30 minutes before leaving.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection.
