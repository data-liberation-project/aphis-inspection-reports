2.132(d)

Procurement of dogs, cats, and other animals; dealers.
The licensee acquired a red kangaroo from an individual that does not have a license to deal in exotic animals with the
USDA, APHIS, Animal Care.

No exhibitor shall knowingly obtain any dog, cat, or other animal from any person who is required to be licensed but who
does not hold a current, valid, and unsuspended license.

Ensure that when acquiring wild or exotic animals from individuals that they have a current and valid license with the
USDA, APHIS, Animal Care.

To be corrected from this day, March 11, 2022, forward.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
