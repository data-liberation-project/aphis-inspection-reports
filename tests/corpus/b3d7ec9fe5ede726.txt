Wire flooring less than 1/8 inch (9 gauge) must be covered with a plastic or fiberglass like material.
At least two outside runs on building #1 have wire flooring that is missing the plastic/fiberglass coating,
leaving bare wire flooring that is less that 1/8 inch in diameter. The owner must replace/repair these
areas of bare wire and any other area where the coating is missing leaving bare wire flooring that dogs
walk on. Having the correct floor wire size/coating will help dog(cid:25)s feet to be healthy and injury free.
