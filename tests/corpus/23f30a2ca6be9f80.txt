2.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
The applicant sold a young female red kangaroo on January 20th, 2022. On January 4th, 2022 the applicant was
informed that regulated activity including the sale of exotic mammals such as kangaroos requires a USDA license, and
that conducting regulated activity without a license is a violation of the Animal Welfare Act. The applicant has applied for a
license but is not yet licensed under the Animal Welfare Act.
No regulated activities may be conducted until USDA license/registration is obtained.

This inspection was conducted with the applicant.
