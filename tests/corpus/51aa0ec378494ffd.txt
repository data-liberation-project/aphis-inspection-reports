SANITATION.
The shelves in the small mammal building are very cluttered . There are discarded items stacked next to and with
items used in the daily care of the animals in this room . Allowing items not used in the daily care of the animals to
accumulate near the animals may lead to pest and cleaning problems which create an unhealthy environment for
the animals. Animal rooms must be maintained clutter free to maintain husbandry and protect the animals from
injury.
