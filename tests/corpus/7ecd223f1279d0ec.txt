3.127(d)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
-The entire perimeter fence for the Wallabies and the Cavies is four feet tall.
-The facility does not have a six feet tall perimeter fence that completely encloses the wallabies and the cavies; to
restrict animals and unauthorized person from going through it or under it and having contact with the animals in the
facility and to function as a secondary containment system for the animals. Steps must be taken to correct this
noncompliant and to ensure this noncompliant does not repeat.
-To be corrected by: August 1, 2015.
- Facilities that do not meet the requirement for perimeter fence can submit a written request to the Western Region
Office seeking approval from the administrator.
-Exit interview conducted with a facility representative.
