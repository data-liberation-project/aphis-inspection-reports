ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 3:30 pm on
22-AUG-17. The inspector contacted employees at another business that share the same address as the
registrant. The employees indicated that the registrant was not there and was unavailable. The provided contact
number was called. There was no answer and no voice mail available to leave a message.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
