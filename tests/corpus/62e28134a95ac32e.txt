2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
A bottle of expired ivermectin (expired 10/2016) was in the medicine cabinet and had been used in some animals at
the facility since the expiration date. The use of expired medications may not be effective or may cause reactions in
animals. For the health of the animals, ensure that expired medications are not used in animals and are properly
disposed of.
Correct by 1/5/2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
