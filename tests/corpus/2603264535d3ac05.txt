3.125(a) REPEAT
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
***The enclosure for the black bear "Nutty Buddy" has three tiered platforms made of plywood. The second tier of
the platform is in disrepair, the wood is rotten and broken. It has been chewed on the back wall of the platform.
There are boards running across the length of the platform that are worn and are splintering. The worn boards
compromise the structural strength which may allow the bear to become injured when climbing on it. This, and all of
the platforms shall be examined and all worn or broken boards replaced to protect the bears safety.
