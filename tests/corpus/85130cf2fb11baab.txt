2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Since March 2015, records of animal births and deaths were not maintained for the guinea pigs. Records must be
kept on animals for animal tracking, assessment of the animal care program and to comply with the regulations. The
licensee must ensure that records of animal births and death are kept and maintained. Correct by August 4, 2015.
An exit briefing was conducted with the owner.
