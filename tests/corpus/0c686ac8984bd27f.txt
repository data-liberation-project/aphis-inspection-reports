3.131(d)
SANITATION.
** The inspector observed cockroaches in one building. The licensee stated that she is working on a pest control
program but does not have one in place. Insects and pest can contaminate food, bedding and harbor health
hazards. The licensee must establish and maintain an effective pest control program to provide for the health and
well-being of the animals. Licensee's must have a safe and effective program to control insects, ectoparasites, and
avian and mammalian pests. To be corrected by: 10-15-15.
This inspection and exit briefing conducted with the licensee.
End of report.
