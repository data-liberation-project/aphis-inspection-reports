3.1(c)(3)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
There is a mixture of dirt, grease and other excreta accumulating on the fiberglass wall board in three enclosures
and on two metal entryway doors. Four dogs are in contact with the affected areas located in sheltered housing.
The affected area covers approximately 20 to 30 percent of the fiberglass wall board and metal entryway doors.
Cleaning and sanitization of surfaces that are in contact with dogs is necessary to prevent an accumulation of
excreta and reduce disease hazards. Unsanitary living conditions and potential transmission of diseases exists
when surfaces are not cleaned and sanitized. The licensee must ensure that all surfaces are spot cleaned on a daily
basis and sanitized in accordance with Sec. 3.11(b) of this subpart to prevent accumulation of excreta and reduce
disease hazards. Correct by 5/22/14.
