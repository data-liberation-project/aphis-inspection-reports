3.28(c)(1)(iii)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Two primary enclosures housing 12 weanlings each measured 27 in. length X 25 in. width for a total surface area of
675 sq. in. Each weaning only had 56.23 sq. in. which is less than the required 60 sq. in. for each. The interior
height requirement was met. Primary enclosurs shall be constructed and maintained so as to provide sufficient
space for each guinea pig containe therein to make normal postural adjustment with adequate freedom of
movement.
Do not house more than 11 weanlings per enclosure measuring 27 in. length X 25 in. width.
Correct by July 21, 2017.
The exit briefing was conducted with the licensee.
