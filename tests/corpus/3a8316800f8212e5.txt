2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 9:10 a.m. on
06/28/2016. The licensee was contacted by phone, stated she was at an appointment in Temple and couldn't be
available within 30 minutes.
The licensee was queried about optimal hours for inspection and replied there were normally few restrictions on her
hours of availability.
