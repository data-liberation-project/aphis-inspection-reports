2.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
The facility currently sells regulated animals (sugar gliders, hedgehogs) and exhibits a regulated animal (African crested
porcupine) to the public at Bargain Land Bait and Tackle in Roanoke Rapids, NC without a valid USDA license.

A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the federal Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations.

The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities.

No regulated activities may be conducted until USDA license is obtained.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
