ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
(2) The use of appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries, and the

* The licensee has 3 bottles of medication inside of her whelping facility that were recently used or ready for use on
her dogs and/or puppies. A transferred bottle of Oxytocin was found inside of the refrigerator that was labeled with
"use as directed". The licensee had recently used this medication on a female Yorkshire Terrier/Chihuahua, tag
#78. The licensee had no written instructions as to the dosage for this medication. A bottle of Bactracillin G
Benzathine, labeled for use in cattle, was found inside of the refrigerator. The licensee had recently used this
medication on a female Yorkshire Terrier, tag #265. The licensee had no written directions for appropriate use in
dogs from a veterinarian. An expired bottle of Albon (exp date: 2/2014) was found inside the whelping building and
ready for use on the dogs and/or puppies. Expired medications can lose its effectiveness and the ability to prevent
and control disease. The licensee must ensure that all medications used in the facility are not expired and labeled
properly in accordance with standard veterinary practices and manufacturer's recommendations.
