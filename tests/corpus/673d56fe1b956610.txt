3.127
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
There is standing water and wet muddy conditions throughout enclosure 17 housing 5 tigers which indicates
inadequate drainage. There is wet mud and water surrounding the 3 feeding platforms and around the shelter. The
tigers are unable to easily access their shelter and food without going through the puddles or soggy muddy areas.

3.131(c)
SANITATION.
The supply storage area, which is in close proximity to the food storage area is disorganized and cluttered. There
are tools, supplies and chemical products piled throughout the front and rear areas. Messy, unkept disorganized
areas may allow for accidental contamination of food and/or harbor vermin and insects. Vermin and insect pests
may harbor disease which is a hazard to the animals. Premises (buildings and grounds) shall be kept clean and in
good repair to protect the safety and well-being of the animals.
