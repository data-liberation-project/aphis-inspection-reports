FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* An estimated three inch wide by two foot long open area from missing wood was observed along the upper right
hand corner of the outdoor enclosure housing two bobcats. Deterioration of wood surfaces can affect the structural
integrity of the enclosure. This affects the ability of the enclosure to protect the animals from injury, contain the
animals securely and ensure the safety of the employees. The facility must ensure that all enclosures shall be
structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
* Broken, protruding wires with sharp points were observed on the secondary gate that is used by employees to
enter the bobcat enclosure. The gate is located on the west side of the bobcat enclosure housing two bobcats.
Broken, protruding wires with sharp points can affect the structural integrity of the enclosure. The facility must
ensure that all enclosures shall be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals from
injury and to contain the animals.
* Both NCI's to be corrected by 12/5/16.
