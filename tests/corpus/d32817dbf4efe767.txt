2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee has a part-time/consultant veterinarian. The licensee does not have a written program of veterinary
care and no regularly scheduled visits to the premises have been determined by the attending veterinarian.
Each dealer shall employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a part-time attending
veterinarian or consultant arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written program of veterinary care
and regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the dealer.
Have a veterinarian complete a written program of veterinary care and determine the frequency of visits to the
facility. The program of veterinary care is important to ensure all aspect of veterinary care are being followed by the
licensee and to ensure the animals are maintained in good health.
To be corrected by February 14, 2020.
