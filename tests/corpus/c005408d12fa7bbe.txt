2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Since the last inspection the dealer has changed attending veterinarians. The new veterinarian partially filled out a
new program of veterinary care form. However some areas were not filled in at all or only partially. For example
there are no vaccines identified to be administered to the adults or puppies. The intestinal parasite control schedule
is not complete and there is no form of euthanasia. It is the dealers responsibility of have a complete written
program of veterinary care to protect the health and welfare of the animals.
Affects fifty-three adults and twenty-one puppies. Correct by May 22, 2015.
2.50(a)(1)
TIME AND METHOD OF IDENTIFICATION.
The kennel facility consist of one whelping shelter type building and one outdoor housing area. In the whelping
building there was one enclosure which contained three weaned puppies from different mothers. One of the
puppies was nine weeks old and the other two were eight weeks old. None of the puppies had any form of
identification. All class ``A'' dealers (breeder) shall identify all live dogs on the premises shall be identified by an
official form of identification at all times.
Affects three puppies Correct by May 20, 2015.
3.3(b)
SHELTERED HOUSING FACILITIES.
The kennel facility consist of two types of housing. There is one sheltered type whelping building containing
mothers and puppies and a outdoor housing area. Upon entering the whelping building there was a strong
ammonia smell which burnt the eyes. The enclosed or sheltered part of sheltered housing facilities for dogs must be
sufficiently ventilated when dogs are present to provide for their health and well-being, and to minimize odors,
drafts, ammonia levels, and moisture condensation. The dealer must take measures to insure all fans and
ventilation is working properly at all times to protect the health and welfare of the contained animals.Affects twenty adults and twenty-one puppies. Correct by May 20, 2015
The inspection and exit interview was conducted with the facility representative.
