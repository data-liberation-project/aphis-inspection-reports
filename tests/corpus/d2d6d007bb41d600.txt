2.31(d)(5)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
A covered animal protocol inspected was not reviewed by the IACUC for the last year. There is no documentation
on file that the protocol and covered animal SOP's referred to in the protocol approved in 2009 have been reviewed
by the IACUC. A review of the protocol shall be done to record ongoing updates and other modifications that may
be necessary for the completion of the study. Facility shall ensure that continuing reviews are appropriately done as
determined by the IACUC, but not less than annually.
