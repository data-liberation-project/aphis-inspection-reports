3.10
WATERING.
Several of the water receptacles in the outdoor enclosures contained green or dark brown (not potable) water.
Potable water, if not continually available, must be offered to the dogs to ensure their health and well-being.
Moving forward, the licensee shall implement a proactive watering regimen to ensure potable water is available.
