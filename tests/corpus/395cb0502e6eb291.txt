2.31(c)(1) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The facility was unable to provide documentation indicating that the IACUC has been performing semiannual
reviews of the research facility’s program for humane care and use of animals. The facility representative informed
the inspector that the program has not been reviewed every six months.
IACUC review of the program for the humane care and use of animals is required at least every six months to
ensure that any activities involving animals at these facilities are being conducted in a manner to reduce pain and/or
distress and that they meet the current standards for all species. The IACUC, as an agent of the research facility
shall review, at least once every six months, the research facility's program for humane care and use of animals,
using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A
2.31(c)(2) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The facility was unable to provide documentation indicating that the IACUC as been performing a semiannual
inspection of the research facility’s animal facilities. The facility representative informed the inspector that a facility
inspection has not been performed within the last six months.
Inspection of the animal facilities by the IACUC is required at least every six months to ensure that any activities
involving animals at these facilities are being conducted in a manner to reduce pain and/or distress and ensure they
meet the current standards for all species. The IACUC, as an agent of the research facility, shall inspect, at least
once every six months, all of the research facility's animal facilities, including animal study areas, using title 9,
chapter I, subchapter A- Animal Welfare, as a basis for evaluation.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
