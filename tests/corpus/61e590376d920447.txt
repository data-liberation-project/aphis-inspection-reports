2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The facility does maintain and have available for review records that disclose information about animals other than
dogs and cats sold. However, records which fully disclose the required information about animals other than dogs
and cats acquired and currently in the facility's possession are not being made and maintained. Acquisition
records/records of animals on hand shall include offspring born at the facility and shall include all required
information: name and address of person from whom animal was acquired, their USDA number or if unlicensed their
drivers license and state and vehicle license number and state, date of acquisition/birth if born on property and
species of animal. Records are necessary to allow trace-ability of ownership. Correct by making and keeping
records with all required information. Correct by April 30, 2019.
