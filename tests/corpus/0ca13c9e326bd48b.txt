2.33(b)(2)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care.
Goat # 22 with an illness or sign of disease is not being detected/ documented by animal care staff during daily
observations. Inspector found a goat with moderate to significant hair loss. According to animal care staff the hair loss had
not been noticed or documented by the facility. The observation logs did not have any indication of hair loss on this
animal. The herd was treated for lice in January 2022 and for mites in March 2022. Veterinary intervention had not been
provided to goat #22 by facility employees prior to observations by the inspector. Daily observations are necessary for
the detection of illness or conditions that may require veterinary care.

Corrected June 16, 2022 facility called veterinarian and treated entire herd. Also, corrected by ensuring facility employees
are performing adequate daily observations, recognize and recording signs of illness, injury or disease in animals.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
