3.19(b)(2)
HANDLING.
Animal Care received a complaint indicating that a primary enclosure containing a puppy tipped over during normal
rigors of transport because it was not properly secured. During a formal unannounced inspection , the licensee
advised that the aforementioned information was correct. Primary enclosures containing live animals must be
positioned and secured in a manner that does not result in its falling. Moving forward, the licensee shall ensure that
all primary enclosures containing live animals within the transport vehicle are properly positioned and secured to
ensure it does not tip.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with Licensee.
