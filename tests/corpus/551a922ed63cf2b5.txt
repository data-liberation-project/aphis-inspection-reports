2.31(e)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Protocols 2015.11.ENDO & 2015.01.16 Lap-R contains several different surgical teaching skills within each of these
protocols.
The procedure for some of these skills within each protocol does not fully & completely describe the actual
procedure being taught.
A proposal to conduct an activity must contain a complete description of the proposed use of the animals.
These protocols (and all future ones) shall provide a complete & detailed description of the actual
procedure/technique being taught as discussed during the inspection. The revised protocols shall be reviewed and
approved by the IACUC.
