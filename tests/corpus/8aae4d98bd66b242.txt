2.78
HEALTH CERTIFICATION AND IDENTIFICATION.
(a) (1)
***** At time of inspection two animals were identified to have been transported from the facility across state lines to
meet a transporter for further shipment in commerce without a health certification. A health certification stating that
the animal had been inspected no more than 10 days prior to the shipment in commerce must be obtained on all
animals transported in commerce. This animal was accepted for transport in commerce by the transporter without
this health certification having been received from the licensee as required.
This health certification from the veterinarian is to insure that the animal is deemed to be free from any infectious
disease or physical abnormality which will endanger these animals or other animals or endanger the public health.
All future animals transported in commerce shall be accompianed by a health certification issued no more than 10
days prior to the animal being transported in commerce.
