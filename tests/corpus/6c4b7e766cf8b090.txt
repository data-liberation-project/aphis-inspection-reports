HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
One Pomeranian/Japanese Chin dog was observed during the inspection to have climbed to the top edge of the 4
feet tall enclosure fence. This dog was balanced in the corner standing on the strands of the panel fence. This dog
could be injured climbing up and down this fence or could escape from this enclosure. All facilities must be
constructed in a manner that protects the animals from injury and securely contains the animals. Correct by 27 July
2017.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
