2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The facility records do not include all required information concerning animals acquired and sold. Information that is
not on the records includes the name and address of the person from whom the animals were acquired, the USDA
license number of the person animals were acquired from for those licensed under the Act, the driver's license
number and state and vehicle license number and state of the person animals were acquired from for those persons
not licensed under the Act and the address of the person to whom the animals were sold.
Incomplete records does not allow for tracking of the animals or ensuring that they came from proper sources.
Maintain records that contain all required information. Correct from this point on.
Inspection and exit briefing conducted with the owner.
