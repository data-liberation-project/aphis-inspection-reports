2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 2:30 pm on
01/26/2016.
Inspector arrived at facility at 2:30pm. Inspector knocked on door and honked car horn. There are two phone
numbers provided for the licensee. The first number called would not allow the inspector to leave a message. The
second number did allow the inspector to leave a message. An individual within the home did come out and tell the
inspector that the licensee was unavailable for the inspection. The inspector then left the facility.
