2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 12:15 pm on
09-MAY-19. The inspector attempted to contact the licensee by ringing the facility door bell, knocking on the door,
and by calling and texting the licensee's cell phone upon arrival at the facility. All attempts were unsuccessful. The
inspector waited for thirty minutes in front of the facility and left.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
