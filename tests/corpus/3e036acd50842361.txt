2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Four enclosures contained at least one dead newborn guinea pig. One enclosure contained a dead weanling, and
one adult had been identified in the morning walkthrough by the facility. The group had been started on antibiotic
treatment in the feed this weekend as directed in the PVC based upon deaths noted by the facility. The use of
antibiotic in the feed may not be sufficient to treat or prevent disease in neonates. The veterinarian must be
contacted to assure the most appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries is
used based upon the observations by the facility. Correct by contacting the veterinarian by: 8 April 2015.
An Exit interview was conducted with facility owner.
