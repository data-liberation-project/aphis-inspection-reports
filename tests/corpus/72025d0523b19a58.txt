ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The written program of veterinary care is incomplete. There is no information on the type, dosage, and route of
administration of ivermectin. The licensee must have adequate guidance from the attending veterinarian on the use,

veterinary care must include the type of invermectin, dosage, frequency and route of administration so that animals
receive the appropriate amount of parasite control. Correct by September 23, 2016.
