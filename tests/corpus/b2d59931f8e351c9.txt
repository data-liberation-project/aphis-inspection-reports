2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC last reviewed the facility's program for humane care and use in July 2017. The humane care and use
program for animals shall be reviewed at least once every six months to verify that any animal use at the facility
meets the standards and requirements under the Animal Welfare Act. Ten months have elapsed since the last
program review by the IACUC.
Correct by: Future program reviews should be conducted every six months.
2.31(c)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC last inspected the facility's animal facilities and animal study areas in July 2017. Facility inspections
shall be conducted at least once every six months to verify that animal facilities and housing areas meet the
standards and requirements under the Animal Welfare Act. Ten months have elapsed since the last facility
inspection by the IACUC.
Correct by: Future facility inspections should be conducted every six months.
