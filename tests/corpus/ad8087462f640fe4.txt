2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 12:40 PM on
14-Aug-17.
APHIS inspector arrived at the facility at 12:40 PM on August 14, 2017. No one was present at the facility. APHIS
inspector called the phone number provided by the facility. The woman who answered the phone directed the
APHIS inspector to call another individual who identified herself as the facility representative's granddaughter. She
informed the inspector that no one was able to accompany the inspector on an inspection. APHIS inspector
explained that this would be considered an attempted inspection.
