2.38(f)
MISCELLANEOUS.
According to the April 2015 IACUC minutes, on April 3 2015, a technician was restraining a rabbit who jumped off
the exam table and as a result the rabbit had a fracture at L-7 and was euthanized. The incident was reported to
OLAW and the facility has taken steps to correct the situation by retraining the employees on animal restraint.
It has been corrected.
An exit interview was conducted with the facility representative.
