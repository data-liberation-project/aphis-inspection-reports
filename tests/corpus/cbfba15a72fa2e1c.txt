This is a focused inspection on the commissary/feeding reported on the Jan. 8, 2014 inspection report.
**** The commissary had fly strips over the stainless steel food preparation tables.The fly strips have been moved
and are no long over the food preparation table.
**** The walk in refrigerator in the commissary had moisture dripping from a pipe at the back of the unit. This
condensation could act as a source of contamination of food. The refrigerator unit now has tape around the pipe
and a catch tray welded under the entire unit to prevent food contamination from condensation.
