Contingency planning.
The contingency plan was developed but the training for the personnel has not been completed. Training of personnel on
their roles and responsibilities as outlined in the contingency plan must occur within 60 days of implementation for current
employees and within 30 days for new hires.

3.80(a)(2)(i)

Primary enclosures.
The enclosure for the capuchins had areas of hardware and chicken wire unsecured with sharp points in the enclosure or
accessible by the capuchins. The detached areas of cut or broken wire create sharp points and edges that could injure
the capuchins. Primary enclosures must be maintained so they have no sharp points or edges.
