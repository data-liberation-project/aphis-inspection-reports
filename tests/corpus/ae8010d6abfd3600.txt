2.40(b)(2) DIRECT REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
There is light tan adult ewe limping favoring her left front foot and rear right rear foot at the pasture in front of the
barn. During the inspection this sheep was limping each time she walked while eating grass. There are two billy
goats, one white with a white and brown head and one brown that have overgrown hooves that have not been
provided adequate veterinary care.
Without adequate veterinary care for these animals they may develop further medical problems and endure more
discomfort and pain. For the health and welfare of the animals at this facility this exhibitor shall establish and
maintain programs of adequate veterinary care to its animals that include the use of appropriate methods to
prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries. Noncompliance's for lame goats and sheep were cited
during previous inspections on December 19, 2017 and October 19, 2017, and for overgrown hooves December 19,
2107.
