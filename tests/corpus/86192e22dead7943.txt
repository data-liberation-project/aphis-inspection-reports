SANITATION.
The straw/hay litter covering the solid floor of the rabbit enclosure housing 20 rabbits is soiled. Rabbit excreta
pellets are mixed in/accumulated throughout all the litter covering the flooring. The rabbits cannot avoid the soiled
litter. Soiled litter shall be removed and replaced with clean litter at least once a week, more often if necessary with
many rabbits to prevent odors, pest accumulations, contamination of the rabbits and disease spread. Correct by
8/16/17.
