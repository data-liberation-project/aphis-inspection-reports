2.132(d)
PROCUREMENT OF DOGS, CATS, AND OTHER ANIMALS; DEALERS.
On April 13, 2016, the licensee purchased a porcupine from a person who does not possess a USDA license to sell
animals. The sale of large exotic or wild mammals requires a valid USDA dealer (or exhibitor) license
No exhibitor shall knowingly obtain any dog, cat, or other animal from any person who is required to be licensed but
who does not hold a current, valid, and unsuspended USDA Animal Welfare license.
You may not purchase such mammals from a person who does not possess either a USDA dealer or exhibitor
license.
