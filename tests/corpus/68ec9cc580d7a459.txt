3.1(c)(3)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
(3) Cleaning.
* Bird excrement was splattered on top of six dog houses (containing 6 dogs total) and along the enclosure railings.
Embedded dirt and grime could be seen on the edges of the doghouses and along the west wall. This types of
organic material can harbor pests, disease organisms and soil the dogs. Licensee must remove the dirt, grime and
organic matter, clean and sanitize. Licensee must ensure that all hard surfaces that come in contact with the dogs
are spot-cleaned daily and sanitized to prevent accumulations of any type, minimize pest attractions and reduce
disease hazards for the overall health of the dogs.
* To be corrected by: 7/16/14

OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
b) Shelter from the elements.
* An enclosure housing one dog, did not have four complete walls and does not protect the animal completely from
