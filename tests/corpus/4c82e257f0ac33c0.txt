3.58(b)
CLASSIFICATION AND SEPARATION.
At the time of this inspection it was noted that the rabbits were being housed with other species. There was 1 rabbit
in an enclosure with 2 cavies, another pen had 1 rabbit and 1 tortoise, and another had 4 rabbits and 1 tortoise.
Rabbits can not be housed with any other species. This situation will need to be addressed to ensure the health of
the rabbits and other species.
