2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 3:10pm on
15-OCT-19.
Inspector arrived on site and rang the doorbell, waiting several minutes for a response. Several more attempts to
ring the doorbell and knock were made with no response. Attempted to contact licensee at 3:15 pm by contact
number provided and left a message. Inspector waited 30 minutes, with intermittent attempts to reach licensee by
phone and at the door of the building. With no response from licensee, inspector left the site at 3:42pm.
