2.31(c)(1) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC has not performed the function of reviewing the facility's program for humane care and use of animals
at least once every six months as required. The facility’s last program review was conducted on April 16, 2018.
Regular reviews are necessary to ensure IACUC oversight of the facility's program for humane care and use of
animals. The IACUC must review the facility's program for humane care and use of animals at least once every six
months.
2.31(c)(2) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC has not been conducting facility inspections at least once every six months as required. The facility’s
last inspection of the animal facilities was conducted on April 16, 2018. Animal facility inspections by the IACUC
ensures optimal animal welfare by identifying, prioritizing, and making plans for correcting areas which may be
harmful to the health and well-being of the animals. The IACUC must inspect, at least once every six months, all of
the facility's animal use areas including housing and study areas.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with a facility representative.
