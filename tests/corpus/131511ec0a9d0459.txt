2.78(a)(1) CRITICAL
HEALTH CERTIFICATION AND IDENTIFICATION.
A ringtail lemur was transported from GW Zoo (USDA #73-C-0139) in Oklahoma, on or around May 3rd, 2016 by
Ms. Ringo to her facility (USDA# 92-C-0078) in Oregon without a valid health certificate. USDA regulations require
a health certificate executed and issued by a licensed veterinarian no more than 10 days prior to transport of any
nonhuman primate. A health certificate was provided by Dr. JoAnne Green, DVM in Oklahoma on June, 21 2016.
This health certificate was issued over three weeks post transport of the lemur and therefore invalid. All animal
transports must be accompanied by a health certificate issued by a licensed veterinarian before they are
transported in commerce.
Correct by: All future animal transports must have a valid health certificate before transportation begins.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility owner.
