SANITATION.
Section 3.131(c) Housekeeping: The room housing the skunk, chinchilla, two ferrets and rodents has rodent feces
and debris on the floor. Rodent feces may carry bacteria and parasites that could cause the animals to become ill.
The floor must be kept clean for the health of the animals and to be able to monitor the effectiveness of the facility's
pest control program.
