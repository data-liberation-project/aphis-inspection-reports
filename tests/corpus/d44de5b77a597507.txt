3.26(d)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
There is bare wood on some of the walls, drywall, support posts and part of the floor that is wood in the room
housing the guinea pigs in the barn. Interior surfaces of indoor guinea pig rooms must be constructed and
maintained so that all surfaces are impervious to moisture so that they can be readily clean and sanitized and won't
retain odors. The bare wood on walls, drywall, support posts and wooden floor must be sealed or covered with an
impervious surfaces so that all surfaces an be repeatedly cleaned and sanitized and won't retain odors. Correct by
October 1, 2017.
3.31(b)
SANITATION.
Housekeeping. There are accumulations of dust, dirt, cobwebs on the walls, ceiling, wire surfaces, furniture, and
items on the walls in the room housing the guinea pigs and fowl. Animal facilities must be kept clean to ensure
good quality of air for the animals and to prevent unnecessary disease risks. Ineffective cleaning exposes the
animals to diseases hazards and health problems. Correct by ensuring the premises are kept clean, including
removing accumulations of dust, dirt, cobwebs; routinely dusting off and sanitizing all surfaces on the inside of the
facility; removing unnecessary furniture and items from the animal room; and maintaining a level of general
cleanliness to support good animal husbandry and health. Correct by September 2, 2017.
3.127(c)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
The new outdoor enclosure housing the 3 African crested porcupines is excessively muddy and there are several
areas where the dirt has washed away exposing the metal fencing on the ground. Animals should not have to walk
through mud to get to their water, food, or shelter. Standing in mud can cause animals to have foot problems and
the exposed metal fencing on the ground can cause foot and leg injuries. A suitable method shall be provided to
rapidly eliminate excess water from the enclosure and the metal fencing on the ground must be covered to prevent
foot and leg injuries. Correct by August 31, 2017.This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
