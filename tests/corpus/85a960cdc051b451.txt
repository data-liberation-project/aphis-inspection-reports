2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This is a search inspection.
*The facility currently exhibits goats, pigs and a rabbit as part of a seasonal fall pumpkin patch/corn maze business
without a valid USDA Animal Welfare Act license. The facility advertises on websites and in print that their facility
would have animals on display including petting animals, a goat walk and racing pigs. The public is allowed to
interact with the animals via petting and feeding of the animals. The facility is open on weekends to the public on an
entrance fee basis. This facility hosts school groups on weekdays on a fee basis.
*A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations.
*The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities. Application packet left at facility.
Exit with facility representative.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
