3.125(c)

Facilities, general.
Deer feed used for guests are stored in large metal coated barrels. The interior of the barrels has coating flaking off and
could be a contaminant to the deer feed. There are rusted areas underneath and these barrels should be replaced with a
manner of storage that protects food supplies from deterioration or contaminates.

Correct by: June 26, 2021

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
