2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 10:30 am on
01/19/2016. APHIS inspectors knocked on the residence door, called residence phone number, and honked the
vehicle horn. A neighbor came out to speak to inspectors and verified that the licensee was out of town and
therefore unavailable.
