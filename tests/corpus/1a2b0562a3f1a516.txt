A focused inspection and exit briefing was authorized by the licensee and conducted with a facility representative on
June 25, 2014. This inspection was limited to the non-compliant items 2.40(b)(2) and 3.2 (b) identified during a
routine inspection on June 17,2014.
The facility representative provided written record of a diagnosis and treatment plan from the attending veterinarian
dated 6/18/14 for the adult, female Shih Tzu identified in 2.40(b)(2). As well as a written record of the treatments
provided. The animal has been moved to the isolation facility and the representative stated that the dog was
showing improvement and her appetite was improving.
Additionally, a ventilation fan has been installed in the isolation facility and there are no longer ammonia issues as
previously identified in 3.2(b).
