FACILITIES, GENERAL.
(a) structural strength. the facility must be constructed of such material and of such strength as appropriate for the
animals involved. the indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in
good repair to protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals.
The metal cap along the two foot high block wall in the macque cage is torn and jagged edges are exposed. This
creats sharp edges that can cut and trap animals legs or hands. This needs to be replaced or repaired in order to
ensure the safety of the animals and prevent injury. A regular program of maintenance needs to be in place to keep
all caging in good repair.
