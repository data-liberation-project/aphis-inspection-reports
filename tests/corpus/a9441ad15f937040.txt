HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Although the food bags were stored elevated off the floor the bags were in contact with the wall. Food must be
stored at least six inches away from the wall to prevent rodents from being able to use the wall to climb up the food
bags. This minimizes contamination and disease risks to the food and the dogs.
