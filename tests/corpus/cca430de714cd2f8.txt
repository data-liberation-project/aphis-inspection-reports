INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
IACUC reports dated 3 Dec 2015 and 22 May 2016 documenting the facility's program and facility review were
submitted to the Science Director but not to the Institutional Official. The IACUC must communicate timely and
accurate information to the Institutional Official who is ultimately responsible for the appropriate care and use of
animals in regulated activities at the institution. Without timely receipt of these reports, the Institutional Official may
not be aware of the activities of the IACUC and the conditions of the animals and facilities. The IACUC must
prepare and submit reports of its evaluations conducted as required by paragraphs (c)(1) and (2) of this section to
the Institutional Official of the research facility. These reports must be updated at least once every six months upon
completion of the required semiannual evaluations. To be corrected with the next review of the animal use facility
and program.
