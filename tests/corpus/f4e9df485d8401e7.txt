3.127(d)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
There is no 6 foot perimeter fence surrounding the enclosures for the camels , water buffalo or bison at the new site.
A perimeter fence at least 6 feet in height is need to protect the animals. A perimeter fence serves as a secondary
containment fence and must restrict persons or animals from going through it or under it to contact the animals , It
must be at least 3 feet from the primary enclosure fence to ensure persons on the outside of the perimeter fence
may not reach the animals. CORRECT BY : 4/11/16
Note : No covered activity to be conducted until the new site is in compliance.
