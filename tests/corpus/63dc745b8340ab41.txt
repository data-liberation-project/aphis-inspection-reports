3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
There is a area of broken metal on the climbing tube into the underground portion of the badger enclosure. This
broken metal is resulting in a jagged edge which could cause injury. Facilities must be structurally sound and
maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury. Correct by 29 June 2019.
3.127(c)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
There is standing water in the tiger enclosure and the pig enclosure. This standing water has been standing long
enough that it is green with algae. Standing may attract pests, become odorous and may contaminate the animals
inside the enclosures. A suitable method must be established to rapidly eliminate standing water. Correct by 4
June 2019
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
