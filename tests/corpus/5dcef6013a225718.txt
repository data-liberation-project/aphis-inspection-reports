2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
***During the inspection, there was no written program of veterinary care available. The lack of a written program of
veterinary care can lead to errors in treatment or veterinarian's instructions. The licensee must create and maintain a
written program of veterinary care with the attending veterinarian as required.
To be corrected by July 15, 2022.
3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
***Throughout the facility, including, but not limited to the pig, kangaroo and coatimundi enclosures, there were sharp
points, edges, detached fencing and an electrical cord laying on the ground. These objects create a potential hazard for
injury to the animals within the enclosures. The licensee must remove or repair these areas and maintain all areas to
protect the animals from injury as required.
To be corrected by July 15, 2022.
