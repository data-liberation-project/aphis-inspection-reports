2.77(b)
RECORDS: CARRIERS AND INTERMEDIATE HANDLERS.
During inspection of records from December & January, seven shipments had a signed checklist without a copy of
the Health Certificate. The dates of the shipments were 12/17/16, 12/23/16, 12/27/16, 12/30/16, 1/2/17, 1/2/17 and
1/7/17. The health certificate for interstate shipment of dogs, cats and nonhuman primates is a required record in
order to confirm the health status and the age of the animal by a licensed veterinarian within ten days of shipment.
Correct for all future shipments.
Exit interview conducted with Compliance Coordinator at the time of inspection at the passenger terminal.
