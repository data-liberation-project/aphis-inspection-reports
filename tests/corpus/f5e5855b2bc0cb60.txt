2.126(c)(1)(iii)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
At the time of this inspection, there is no written itinerary on file at the Animal Care Western Regional Office from
this licensee. It is necessary for any person who is subject to the Animal Welfare regulations and who intends to
exhibit any animal at any location other than the person's approved site to submit a written itinerary to the AC
Regional Director. The itinerary shall include the following: name, identification number, species, sex, and age of
each animal; and all anticipated dates and locations (with addresses) where the animals will be housed and
exhibited. To be corrected by: February 17, 2017.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with facility representatives at the El Paso Zoo.
