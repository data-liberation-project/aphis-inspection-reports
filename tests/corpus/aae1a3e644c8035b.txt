2.78(a)
HEALTH CERTIFICATION AND IDENTIFICATION.
Section 2.78(a) Health certification: The licensee purchased four Geoffrey marmosets on 5/7/15 and transported
them from Florida to Pennsylvania without a health certificate executed and issued by a licensed veterinarian. All
non-human primates transported in commerce must be accompanied by a health certificate to ensure that they
appear free of any infectious disease or physical abnormality which would endanger the animal or other animals or
endanger the public. The health certificate must state that the licensed veterinarian inspected the non-human
primate on a specified date which shall not be more than 10 days prior to the transportation of the non-human
primate. Correct by having all non-human primates transported in commerce accompanied by a health certificate
issued by a licensed veterinarian no more than 10 days prior to the transport. Correct by July 30, 2015.
An exit briefing was conducted with the owner.
