2.33(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
-Euthasol, with an expiration date of January 2016, was used in February/March 2016 on covered animals
according to the controlled drug log.
-Six bottles of expired artificial tears (11/15) were observed in student boxes/trays in the treatment area.
Utilizing expired medical materials on covered animals is not consistent with adequate veterinary care. Each
research facility shall establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that include the use of
appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries.

3.1(c)(1) REPEAT
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The outdoor dog runs have concrete floors with crevices and areas where the paint has cracked or flaked off,
especially in the last runs at the end of the building. The areas where the floor is no longer effectively sealed are
porous and cannot be cleaned and sanitized as required.
Fecal material and other debris can accumulate in small crevices increasing the potential of disease hazards in
these areas. The surfaces of housing facilities must be constructed and maintained in a manner and made of
materials that allow them to be readily cleaned and sanitized.
