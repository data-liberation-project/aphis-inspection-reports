2.75(a)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
Records of Disposition (form 7006) need to be completely and accurately filled out with all the required information in
blocks 3, 4, and 6. This information is required to assure the dogs are appropriate age when sold and information is
provided to track movement of puppies. Records of disposition should be filled out in a timely manner and maintained at
the facility for review.
