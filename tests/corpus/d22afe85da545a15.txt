2.75(b)(1)(3)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The licensee did not have all of the required information concerning the acquisition of numerous rabbits, hamsters,
gerbils, rats, and guinea pigs from a breeder for resale by the licensee between October 2013-May 2014. The
records maintained by the licensee had the name, address, and telephone number for this breeder but no USDA
License/Registration number, if they are licensed/registered with USDA, APHIS, Animal Care, or no driver's license
number and state and vehicle license number and state if they are not licensed or registered.
Records that contain all of the required information for the acquisition and disposition of animals for AWA regulated
purposes are necessary to ensure that animals are obtained legally and transported, handled, and disposed of
humanely.
Every dealer shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the required
information concerning animals other than dogs and cats, purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, or
otherwise in his or her possession or under his or her control, or which is transported, sold, euthanized, or otherwise
disposed of by that dealer.
To be corrected on all subsequent acquisitions of animals other than dogs and cats.
Records only inspection conducted at an alternate place of business for this licensee. Exit interview conducted by
telephone with the licensee on 7/2/2014.
