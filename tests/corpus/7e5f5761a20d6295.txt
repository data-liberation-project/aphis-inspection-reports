Nippon Cargo exported a giraffe from Dallas/Ft Worth International Airport (DFW) on Feb 7, 2018 and 10 zebra on
March 7, 2018. Nippon does not have an active registration with USDA for the DFW site. All carriers handling
regulated species must be registered with USDA Animal Care.
Nippon Cargo needs to register the DFW site with USDA or discontinue handling regulated species from this site.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
