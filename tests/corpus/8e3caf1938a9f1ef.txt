2.5(d)
DURATION OF LICENSE AND TERMINATION OF LICENSE.
The licensee currently stores and thaws her meats in the same freezer & thaw area of another licensee that is on
the same property.
If two licensees are on the same property, then each licensee's equipment, utensils, feed, bedding, cleaning
supplies, food storage, thawing, & preparation areas; as well as anything else that the licensee may use in
connection with the care of the animals must not be commingled and shall be physically separate and apart from
the other licensee's items and areas that are used for the care and feeding of that person's animals.
