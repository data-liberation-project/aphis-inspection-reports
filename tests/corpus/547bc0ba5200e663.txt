ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Required records and a documented Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) could not be located by the licensee during
this inspection. Required records and a current PVC must be made available during inspection for APHIS officials.
Current records and a written PVC can be important when evaluating animal health. Locate and maintain records
available for officials during inspections.
