2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The written program of veterinary in incomplete. There is no information on the type, dosage, and route of
administration for external and internal parasite control. The licensee must have adequate guidance from the
attending veterinarian on the concentration, use, dosage, frequency and administration of all medications and
drugs. The program of veterinary care must be complete to provide guidance to the licensee to maintain the health
of the animals. The program of veterinary care must include the type of parasite control used, dosage, and route of
administration so that animals receive the appropriate amount of parasite control. Correct by February 1, 2016.
An exit briefing was conducted with the supervisor.
