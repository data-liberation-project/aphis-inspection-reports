FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
The 3 remaining female guinea pigs are housed in a hutch located in a protected area with a fan but they are not
housed inside a building . Guinea pigs shall not be housed in outdoor facilities unless the facilities are located in an
appropriate climate and prior approval is obtained from the Deputy Administrator .

letter should describe the enclosure, location and climate . Additional information such as photos of the housing
area may be included and useful in determining if a variance is granted .
