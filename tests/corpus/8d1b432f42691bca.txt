2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC review of the facility’s program for humane care and use of animals is not occurring at the required
frequency of every six months. IACUC records reviewed during this inspection indicate that IACUC semi-annual
reviews of the facility’s program for the care and use of animals occurred on 06/03/2019 and on 05/11/2020 with no
other reviews taking place between these two dates. Regular reviews are necessary to ensure IACUC oversight of
the program and to ensure the welfare of the animals being used in research by the facility. The IACUC must review
the facility's program for humane care and use of animals at least once every six months.
Correct from this day forward.
2.31(c)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC has not been conducting facility inspections at least once every six months as required. IACUC records
reviewed during this inspection indicate that IACUC semi-annual inspections of animal facilities occurred on
06/03/2019 and on 05/27/2020 with no other inspections taking place between these two dates. Animal facility
inspections by the IACUC ensures optimal animal welfare by identifying, prioritizing, and making plans for correcting
areas which may be harmful to the health and well-being of the animals. The IACUC must inspect, at least once
every six months, all of the facility's animal use areas including housing and study areas.
Correct from this day forward.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
