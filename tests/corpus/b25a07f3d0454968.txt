2.126(c)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
There were 2 camels and 1 zebra in travel status from Apr 20-24, 2016 without an itinerary filed at the Eastern
Regional Office. Any person subject to the AWA that exhibits USDA covered species away from their approved
location extending overnight must submit an itinerary with the Regional Director. The itinerary must be received no
less than 2 days prior to the travel event and must also contain the complete information as required under AWA
2.126(c).
An exit briefing was conducted with a representative of the licensee. This inspection was conducted at the Shriner
Auditorium in Wilmington, MA on Apr 22, 2016.
