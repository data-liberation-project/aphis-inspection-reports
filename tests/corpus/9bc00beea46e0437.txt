3.14 PRIMARY ENCLOSURES USED TO TRANSPORT LIVE DOGS AND CATS.(g)(1)
The "White " transport vehicle had 2 enclosures that contained 5 puppies and the "Rental" transport vehicle had one
enclosure that contained 5 puppies Without adequate space these puppies cannot make normal postural
adjustments which are crucial to their health and well-being. No more than four live dogs or cats, 8 weeks of age or
older, that are of comparable size, may be transported in the same primary enclosure. TO BE CORRECTED BY :
6/5/2015
This inspection was conducted with transport employees and Konnie Plumlee, VMO. An exit briefing was conducted
with the licensee.
