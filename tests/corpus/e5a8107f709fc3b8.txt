2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Most of the medications in the veterinary care kit are expired. Medications present such as antibiotic,
anti-inflammatory, pain relieving, anti-diarrhea, and topical ointments are beyond their expiration date. Expired
medications may be ineffectual or harmful. Licensee must employ appropriate methods to prevent, control,
diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries. Medical materials including drugs and other treatment products used for
regulated animals must be in date.
Correct by: Corrected. Licensee disposed of the expired medications at the time of inspection
Exit interview conducted with Licensee.
