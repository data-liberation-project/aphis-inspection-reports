2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
A on site petting zoo and traveling petting zoo have been operating and conducting regulated activity since 2012
without a USDA license.
Without a USDA license APHIS officials do not have the necessary oversight to ensure compliance with the Animal
Welfare Act and applicable regulations. All persons conducting regulated activities must have a valid license.
Application packet was left with facility representative. No regulated activity may be conducted until a USDA license
is obtained.
This search inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
