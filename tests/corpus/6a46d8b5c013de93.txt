2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Sec. 2.40(b)(2) Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care
--The facility is not maintaining appropriate hoof care so as to prevent injuries. A fallow deer has hooves that are
too long and have lost the appropriate hoof angle. Flattened hoof angles, such as these, may lead to tendon
injuries. The facility shall establish and maintain an adequate program of veterinary care that includes adequate
hoof care and trimming when needed.
Correct by 4-15-14 by providing appropriate hoof trimming for any animals with hooves that are too long.
