2.134(b)

Contingency planning.
Facility had not reviewed their contingency plan in the past year and had not updated it recently to address emergent
situations. Contingency plans must be in place and reviewed annually to ensure it adequately addresses the criteria listed
in paragraph (a) of this section. The plan must provide for the humane handling, treatment, transportation, housing, and
care of their animals in the event of an emergency or disaster.
