2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The record of animals on hand does not include births, deaths or euthanasias.

Every exhibitor shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the following
information concerning animals other than dogs and cats, purchased or otherwise acquired, owned, held, leased, or
otherwise in his or her possession or under his or her control, or which is transported, sold, euthanized, or otherwise
disposed of by that dealer or exhibitor. The records shall include any offspring born of any animal while in his or her
possession or under his or her control.

Accurate records must be kept to trace and track animals in case of disease outbreak, and to ensure all parties that are
required to be licensed have a current, valid license.

To be corrected from November 22, 2021 forward.
3.27(b)

Facilities, outdoor.

The facility is housing guinea pigs outside. Low temperatures have fallen below 50 degrees F during October.

Guinea pigs shall not be housed in outdoor facilities unless such facilities are located in an appropriate climate and prior
approval for such outdoor housing is obtained from the Deputy Administrator.

Guinea pigs must be housed inside. Guinea pig facilities must be maintained between 60 and 85 degrees F to protect
them from the cold and provide for their health and comfort. Approval must be obtained to house guinea pigs outdoors.

To be corrected by November 26, 2021.
