2.126(c)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
The exhibitor did not submit a written itinerary of travel plans to the AHPIS Animal Care Regional Director prior to
exhibiting at a location other than the approved home site for a time that extended overnight and longer. The
itinerary allows APHIS to conduct inspections while the exhibited animals are away from the home site. The
licensee must submit a written itinerary to the AC Regional Director no fewer than 2 days in advance of travel and
which shall contain complete and accurate information concerning the whereabouts of any animal intended for
exhibition at any location other than the person's approved site. To be completed by 25 July 2015.
This inspection was conducted in Manhattan, Kansas. The inspection and exit interview were conducted with the
licensee and Margaret A Shaver, VMO in attendance.
