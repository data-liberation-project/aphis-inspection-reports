2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Although the IACUC has been meeting at least once every three months and conducting facility inspections and
program reviews, no reports have been sent to the Institutional Official for the last year. The reports to the
Institutional Official must describe the program's adhearance to the Animal Welfare Act, contain a description of any
deficiencies, identifying them as significant or minor and providing a correction plan and date for the deficiencies.
The reports need to also say if there were or were not any minority views to the report. A majority of the IACUC
members must sign the report.
To be corrected with future IACUC evaluations of the program and facility.
Exit briefing conducted with facility representative.
