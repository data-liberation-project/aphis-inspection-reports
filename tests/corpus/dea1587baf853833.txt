3.56(a)(1)
SANITATION.
Numerous rabbit enclosures had significant accumulations of feces, hair, and cobwebs. Inappropriate husbandry
practices and unsanitary conditions can have an impact on animal welfare and lead to animal disease. Each rabbit
enclosure must be cleaned as often as necessary to keep it reasonably free of excreta, hair, cobwebs, and debris.
To be corrected by 10/14/19
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
