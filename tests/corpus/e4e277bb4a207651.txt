2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The two reindeer were observed with overgrown hooves. Over long toes are prone to breaking and cracking, and
may alter the animal's gait and place undue stress on tendons and joint structures. The licensee must ensure that
routine hoof care is provided to the reindeer.
