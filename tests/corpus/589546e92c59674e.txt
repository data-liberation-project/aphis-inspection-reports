2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
On March 20, 2014, a search was conducted in response to unlicensed activity at Just Pets. The pet store currently
has degus and hedgehogs for sale and the store manager stated that they have sold sugar gliders in the past. The
regulations and standards were discussed with the store manager and an application packet was provided.
Selling of these species of animals is a regulated activity that requires a valid U.S.D.A. license.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITY MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL U.S.D.A. LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Search and exit briefing conducted with store manager.
