3.31(b)

Sanitation.
Housekeeping. There are numerous cobwebs, dust, and debris on the widows, walls, and empty animal enclosures.
Animal facilities must be kept clean to ensure good quality of the air for the guinea pigs and to prevent unnecessary
disease and health risks. Ineffective cleaning and housekeeping can expose the animals to health problems. Correct by
ensuring the premises are kept clean, including: removing cobwebs dust, and debris from windows, walls, and empty
enclosure; routinely dusting off and cleaning surfaces in the animal room; and maintaining a level of general cleanliness to
support good animal husbandry and health. Correct by March 13, 2021.

This inspection was conducted with the licensee and exit interview was conducted with a family member.
