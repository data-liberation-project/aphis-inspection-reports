Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
The facility acquired a hedgehog and they do not have any record of where the hedgehog came from.
Every exhibitor shall make, keep, and maintain records or forms which fully and correctly disclose the name and address

Ensure that records are made, and maintained to allow animals to be traced and tracked in the event of animal disease
