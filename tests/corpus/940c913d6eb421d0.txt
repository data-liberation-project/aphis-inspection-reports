2.32(a)
PERSONNEL QUALIFICATIONS.
After review of a protocol involving anesthetic concerns noted on the previous inspection report from 28-NOV-2018,
it was determined that the qualifications of the personnel involved with this protocol are inadequate.
It is the responsibility of the research facility to ensure that all personnel involved with the animals' care and use be
qualified for their specific duties.
Correct by ensuring that all personnel have proper training and experience before they are directly involved with
animal care and use from this point forward.
An additional exit interview was conducted with the attending veterinarian by telephone on 28-MAR-2019.
