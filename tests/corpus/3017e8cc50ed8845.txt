SANITATION.
Housekeeping. The trailer housing the golden hamsters and the Chinese dwarf hamsters has accumulations of
spilled feed and shavings on the floor. Animal facilities must be kept clean to ensure good quality care and to
prevent unnecessary diesease risks. Ineffective cleaning and housekeeping exposes the animals to disease
hazards and health problems. Correct by ensuring the premises are kept clean, including dusting off and sanitizing
all surfaces; and removing spilled feed and shavings from the floor.
