3.108(b)
EMPLOYEES OR ATTENDANTS.
No training records had been completed for staff working with the marine mammals. While some of the staff caring
for the animals had been doing so for as much as 10 years, the facility had not documented any of their training.
The facility should ensure that training records are maintained and made available for review by APHIS officials so
that said officials may assess the qualifications of facility staff.
Correct by April 15, 2015.
An exit briefing was conducted with a facility representative.
