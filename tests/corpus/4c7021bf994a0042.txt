3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
The kick in and top of the wolf enclosure fence was detached from several posts on one side of the enclosure, weakening
the structural integrity of the fence. A weakness in the integrity of the fence may not appropriately contain and protect the
animal. The facility shall ensure that the fence is maintained in good repair to contain and protect the animal.
Correct by June 30, 2022.
3.130

Watering.
The automatic waterer for the bears had a buildup of green material on the sides and bottom of the stainless steel bowl.
Water receptacles that are not kept clean and sanitary may be a source of contamination in the water the animals are
drinking, and adversely affect their welfare. The facility shall ensure that water receptacles are kept clean.
Correct by June 1, 2022
