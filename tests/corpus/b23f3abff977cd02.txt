2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Hunsader Farms is exhibiting goats, pigs, camel, lemurs, deer, sheep and other USDA animals to the general public
without a USDA license. This facility failed to renew their USDA license which was cancelled on February 17, 2017.
This facility received the certificate cancellation letter on February 23, 2017. During this inspection there were
numerous members of the public viewing the animals on exhibit at the petting zoo. The annual weekend festival
started on October 14, 2017, where a train take members of the public to view USDA covered animals. Any person
exhibiting USDA covered animals must have a USDA license.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with owner.
