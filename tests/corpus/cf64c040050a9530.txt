CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
* The primary enclosure housing one adult dog and three puppies has an accumulation of dog hair and fecal waste.
This indicates that daily spot-cleaning and sanitization is not happening. Therefore, increasing the potential for
disease hazards, insects, pests and odors.
All primary enclosures must be spot-cleaning daily to prevent an accumulation of feces and waste and to reduce the
potential for disease hazards, insects, pests and odors.
