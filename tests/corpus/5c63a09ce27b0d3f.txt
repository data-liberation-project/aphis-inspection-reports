2.33(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care.
The facility employs a part-time Attending Veterinarian (AV), but does not yet have a written program of veterinary care. A
written program of veterinary care is essential for ensuring animals have adequate veterinary care. Correct by 12/27/22 by
documenting a program of veterinary care with the AV.
2.38(l)

Miscellaneous.
The facility does not have a written contingency plan that contains situations that could adversely impact animals at the
facility. A complete written contingency plan must include scenarios in which animals would be adversely affected, in
order to allow for planning and preparation for those scenarios. Correct by 12/27/22 by documenting a contingency plan
that contains all of the required elements in 2.38(l).
