2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
Female Golden Retriever (M70, #145357750A) has an abnormal appearance to the left front foot. Closer
examination of this foot revealed that the outside toe is extremely swollen and enlarged. The swelling was round in
shape, a dark reddish color and firm to the touch. The dog is not currently receiving any treatment for this condition.
Female Golden Retriever (#032-063-574) has an abnormal appearance to the right hind foot. Closer examination
revealed a large, round swelling between the outside of toes of this foot. The swollen area is dark in color and firm
to the touch. A portion of the swelling had split open and a red fluid is coating the hair around the affected area.
The dog is not currently receiving any treatment for this condition.
Swollen areas of the feet could cause pain and discomfort to the dog. The licensee must have these two Golden
Retrievers evaluated by a licensed veterinarian for a proper diagnosis and to establish an effective treatment plan.
The licensee must ensure that all dogs receive adequate veterinary care at all times.
