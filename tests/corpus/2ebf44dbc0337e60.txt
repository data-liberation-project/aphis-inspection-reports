2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
There were no sale records present for guinea pigs sold to two buyers since the previous inspection report. Records
must be kept and maintained on all guinea pigs sold, transported, euthanized, or otherwise disposed of by the
dealer. Sale records must include the name and address of the buyer, the date of sale or disposal, the species
(guinea pig), and the number of animals in the shipment. Sale records must be complete to document changes in
ownership and to keep track of animal shipments. Correct by March 16, 2019.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
