2.40(b)(2) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
2.40 (b)(2)
An adult red tabby, male cat referred to as "King Kong" had yellow nasal discharge.The licensee was not providing
treatment to the cat nor was she aware that the cat was exhibiting these symptoms. Two other cats at the facility are
currently under treatment for similar symptoms. The licensee has a written protocol for treatment of upper
respiratory symptoms provided by her attending veterinarian on 7/30/14. The licensee shall provide treatment as
instructed by the attending veterinarian in the treatment protocol and use direct and frequent communication with
the veterinarian to discuss changes in animal's behavior, health or if the condition persists following treatment.
3.1(c)(3) REPEAT
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
(c) Surfaces (3) Cleaning
The white, plastic panels that line 3 sides of the enclosures still have brown and yellow areas of grime on their
surface, Adequate cleaning and sanitizing is a crucial aspect of animal husbandry which helps to prevent disease
and provide for the health of the animals. The licensee shall clean the hard surfaces in contact with the animals
daily and sanitize routinely to prevent accumulation of excreta and reduce disease hazards.
