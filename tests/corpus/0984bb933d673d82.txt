TIME AND METHOD OF IDENTIFICATION.

RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.

HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
There is an open bag of puppy food sitting on the floor that is not stored in a container with a lid. Improper storage

3.3(e)(1)
SHELTERED HOUSING FACILITIES.
The licensee has recently laid new cement floors in the inside and outside portions of the dog enclosures, but the
cement floors are not currently impervious to moisture. Surfaces that are not impervious to moisture cannot be
properly cleaned and sanitized. All cement surfaces in contact with the dogs must be made impervious to moisture.
Correct by September 11, 2015.
3.11(a)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
The outside portions of the middle enclosures have more than one days worth of feces present within them. There
is also an accumulation of soil and other debris present in the inside portions of the middle enclosures. Also the
enclosure currently housing an unidentified pregnant female Chihuahua has an accumulation of feces, soiled
bedding and food waste present in her primary enclosure area and within the collection tray beneath. Excessive
accumulations of feces, food waste, soiled bedding and other debris cannot prevent the soiling of the dogs housed
within, is a potential disease hazard, can attract pests and create odors. Excessive feces, waste and other soiled
materials must be cleaned daily from the enclosures daily and as often as necessary to prevent accumulation of
such materials. Correct by August 14, 2015.
