This focused inspection was conducted to verify corrective actions had been initiated by the licensee to help correct
deficiencies previously found under CFR 2.40 (b)(2) during USDA inspection on 24APRIL2019.
The Weimaraner identified as "Izzy" (USDA Tag J-17) was found to have been moved to a separate pen by the
licensee so that she could be individually fed. The dog was alert, active and appeared to have gained weight since
USDA inspection on 24APRIL2019.
An inspector (USDA VMO) initiated telephone call to the attending veterinarian on 29APRIL2019 confirmed that
the dog had been evaluated by the attending veterinarian for the weight loss. The attending veterinarian stated that
the dog had been examined by him at the the kennel facility following the USDA inspection on 24APRIL2019 and
no diagnostic tests were recommended.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
