2.75(b)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
There are no records available at the pet store. The owner believes his wife took the records home for tax
purposes.
Every dealer must make, keep and maintain records which fully and correctly disclose information concerning
animals purchased, acquired, owned, held, leased or otherwise in his possession or under his control,or which is
transported, sold, euthanized, or otherwise disposed of by that dealer. The records shall include any offspring born
of any animal while in his or her possession or under his or her control.
Ensure that records are available for the inspector at time of inspection.
