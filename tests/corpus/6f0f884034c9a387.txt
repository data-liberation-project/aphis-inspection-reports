FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The air conditioning unit in the mammal room is in disrepair. Water from the air conditioning unit was observed
dripping onto an active electrical outlet and down onto the floor. One metal tank containing ferrets was adjacent to
the pooling water on the floor. The water from the air conditioning unit could potentially injure the animals or
employees at the facility.
Repair or replace the air conditioning unit to provide for the safety and welling being of the animals and employees
at the facility.
A temporary fix must be implemented immediately. The air conditioning unit must be repaired or replaced by
11-27-15
The inspection report was sent by email. A thorough verbal exit briefing was conducted with the facility manager at
the time of inspection.
