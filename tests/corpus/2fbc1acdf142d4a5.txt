FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
At time of this inspection, January 05-2015, there were three guinea pigs housed in an outdoor enclosure. Guinea
pigs shall not be housed in outdoor facilities unless such facilities are located in an appropriate climate and prior
approval for such outdoor housing is obtained from the Deputy Administrator.
