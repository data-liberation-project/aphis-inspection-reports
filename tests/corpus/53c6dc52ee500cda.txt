REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
This is a search inspection.
(cid:9)
*The facility is exhibiting an alpaca as part of a booth at the Nashville Exotic Pet Expo this weekend without a valid
USDA Animal Welfare Act license. The facility advertises on-line and on a banner at their booth that their facility has
animals available for petting zoos and nativity scenes. The alpaca on exhibit is in a small pen indoors that allows
the public to see and feed it. Animal feed is available at the booth for the public to purchase food to feed the alpaca.
A valid license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations.
*The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Inspection conducted at Nashville Exotic Pet Expo at the Nashville Fairgrounds.
Exit conducted with undersigned facility representative.
