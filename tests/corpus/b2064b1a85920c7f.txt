2.126(b) REPEAT
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process on 03/15/2016.
The inspector and an IDALS official arrived at the property at 11:00 AM. The inspector knocked at the front door of
the residence and then called the licensee. The licensee stated that he was not available for an inspection. The
licensee also stated that none of the authorized people were available either.
