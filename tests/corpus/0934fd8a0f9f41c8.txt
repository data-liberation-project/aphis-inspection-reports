ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 10:07 AM on
15-Mar-18.
APHIS inspectors arrived at the gates of the facility at 10:07 AM. APHIS inspector spoke with the licensee via
telephone and was informed that she was not at the facility and unable to conduct an inspection. The inspector
informed her that this would be considered an attempted inspection. The licensee sent a text message to the
inspector with the phone number of someone who was as the facility and potentially able to lead an inspection.
APHIS inspector called the phone number provided at 10:13 AM and left a voice message. After waiting for thirty
minutes, the inspectors left the premises.
