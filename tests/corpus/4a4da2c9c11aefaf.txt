3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Was found in the fence that divides the llamas /goats enclosure and the water buffaloes enclosure a portion of the
mesh that was poorly repaired with sharp tips that can cause injury to animals in both sides of the enclosures.
Also was found that the primary enclosure fence for the bison and deer is in disrepair in many locations, broken,
very rust wire was found, and some of the pols are already in bad condition and needs to be replaced.
All enclosures shall be in good repair, and constructed and maintained so that it prevents unauthorized persons and
animals from going through it, and also protect the animals from injury, be structurally sound and contain the
animals.
