2.31(d)(1)(ii)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The searches conducted to insure that there are no alternatives available for the potentially painful or distressful
procedures in the proposed animal use Protocols #GR02P and #EM50D were incomplete. The searches do not
include terminology for the specific potentially painful procedures involved. IACUC review should determine that the
proposed activities meet the requirements in Section 2.31(d)(1)(ii) before a protocol is approved for use.
A complete search for alternatives, review and approval of the protocols shall be conducted as required in this
Section. September 25, 2016.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Director of Pre-Clinical Quality Assurance.
