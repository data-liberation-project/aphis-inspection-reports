Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
An approximately 7-year-old female Bichon, Chip, (id number 571) was found with a heavy substance on the teeth. On
both the left and right side the gums were reddened and appeared to be inflamed. All the teeth were mostly covered with
a dark brown substance. On the left side, the upper tooth behind the canine tooth was found to be extremely loose. The
licensee state that he hadn’t noticed this dog’s dental condition. This dog’s dental condition could result in pain and
discomfort as well as lead to further health issues. The licensee must consult with a licensed veterinarian for an accurate
diagnosis, develop and follow an effective treatment plan. All dogs must receive adequate veterinary care at all times.
