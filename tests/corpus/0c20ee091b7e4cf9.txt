3.11(b)(2)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
All the enclosures in the indoor and sheltered housing are spot had a build-up of brown greasy residue on the walls
and egress doors when present. The licensee stated that the whelping enclosures are disinfected between litters
and everything else is once or twice a year.
All of the feeders in all the enclosures had food debris and greasy brown buildup. The licensee stated that they are
cleaned and sanitized when the enclosures are done.
The build-up of organic material and absence of more frequent sanitization can cause disease hazards.
The licensee must clean and sanitize at least once every two weeks using one of the methods prescribed in
paragraph (b)(3) of this section, and more often if necessary to prevent an accumulation of dirt, debris, food waste,
excreta, and other disease hazards.
