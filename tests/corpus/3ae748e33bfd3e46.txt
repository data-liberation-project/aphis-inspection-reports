3.75(c)(1)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The enclosure housing the rhesus macaques had a broken piece of PVC pipe and and a broken plastic trash can
which appeared to have jagged edges.
Toys or furniture-type fixtures must be removed or replaced when worn or soiled and must be sturdily constructed
and must be strong enough to provide for the safe activity and welfare of nonhuman primates.
To be corrected by 11-15-2016.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
