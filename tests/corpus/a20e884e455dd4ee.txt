2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
During the inspection an 18 day old female Zebra foal appeared to be thin. The owner advised that the Zebra was a
recent purchase and was being bottle fed. Owner stated that the new foal had been having a loose stool. Young
animals are very sensitive to change and moving them away from their mother at an early age can be stressful.
Weight loss and loose stools can be an indication of serious health problems. Owner must have the Zebra foal
evaluated by a licensed veterinarian. To be corrected by 07-22-2015. During inspection owner was making phone
calls to attending veterinarian for an appointment. Owner advised the foal would be taken to a veterinarian.
Inspection conducted by R. Whiteley and the owner.
