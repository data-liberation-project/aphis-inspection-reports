ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 1:38 PM on
16-Nov-17.
APHIS inspector arrived at the facility at 1:38 PM on November 16, 2017. The inspector knocked on the door
several times, however there was no one on the premises. APHIS inspector called the phone number provided by
the licensee and spoke with her. The licensee told the inspector that no one was at the facility to conduct an
inspection. APHIS inspector informed her that this would be considered an attempted inspection.
