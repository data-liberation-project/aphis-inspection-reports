3.50(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Item Facilities General 3.50 a
At the time of inspection it was noticed that an area of the rabbit cage exhibit had broken and exposed wire.
Broken wires can injure an animal.
Housing facilities for rabbits shall be structurally sound and maintained in good repair to protect the animals from
injury.

3.127(b)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Item Outdoor Facilities 3.127 b
At the time of inspection it was noticed that for both the alpacas and llamas the amount of outdoor shelter was
inadequate for the number of animals in each pen.
Shelter for all animals kept outdoors must be provided so as to afford them protection from inclement weather.
