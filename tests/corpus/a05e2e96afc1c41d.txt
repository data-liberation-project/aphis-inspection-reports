3.127(a)

Facilities, outdoor.
The 4 red river hogs have minimal shade in their enclosure. They are forced to seek shade inside an enclosed stall .
Additional shade must be provided to prevent discomfort to the animals when daytime temperature are in the upper 90s.

3.131(a)

Sanitation.
There is a large muddy area on the floor inside the section of the giraffe barn used by the male giraffe. It is under the
feeding area inside the barn . Wet areas in animal housing areas create an unhealthy environment for the animals and
can lead to hoof conditions and other health issues . Excreta shall be removed as often as necessary to prevent
contamination, reduce disease hazards and reduce odors.
