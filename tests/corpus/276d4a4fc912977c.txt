3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Item Facilities General 3.125 a
At the time of inspection it was noticed that the African straw colored fruit bat holding area, had areas of the walls
that were peeling paint.
Facility needs to repair the damaged surfaces so as to maintain them in good repair and protect the animals.
Correct by 10/5/2017
3.127(c)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Outdoor Facilities 3.127 d
At the time of inspection it was noticed that the outdoor run for the babirusa swine has a drainage problem causing
a pooling of water in the back area of the run due to a sprinkle system which is used to cool the animals.
Facility needs to review the drainage of this area so as to prevent the pooling of water.
Correct by 9/30/2017
Inspection took place from 8/21-24/2017
The inspection and exit interview took place with the Curator of mammals and General Curator/Associate Director
