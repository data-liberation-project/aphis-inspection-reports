3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The material pouch hanging inside the enclosure housing two sugar gliders is ripped with fraying material. The
animals can become caught in the ripped frayed areas and become injured. Maintain all housing facilities so that
they are structurally sound and in good repair to protect the animals.
To be corrected by: Corrected during time of inspection
3.131(a)
SANITATION.
There is an excess accumulation of feces along the inside of the running wheel in the enclosure housing two sugar
gliders. Failure to remove and/or clean excreta can lead to contamination, disease hazards and odors which can
affect the health and well-being of the animals. Routine cleaning and sanitizing must be done frequently to protect
the health and well-being of the animals and to minimize odors.
To be corrected by: Corrected during time of inspection
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the manager.
