3.10
WATERING.
At least 4 water receptacles used for cats had a greenish buildup on the inside surfaces and are not being kept
clean or sanitized according to the AWA requirements. Water receptacles that are not kept clean or sanitized pose
a risk to the health of the animals due to potential buildup of bacteria, algae or other harmful agents. Water
receptacles must be kept clean and sanitized in accordance with Sec. 3.11(b) of this subpart, and before being used
to water a different cat or social grouping of cats.
Correct by 11/28/2016.This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with a facility representative.
