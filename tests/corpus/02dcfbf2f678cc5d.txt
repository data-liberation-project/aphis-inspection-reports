INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The IACUC has not conducted an inspection of all animal facilities (Sites 09 and 010) at least every six months. A
full inspection of facilites and review of the program of humane care and use occurred on 2/13/2013. The next
program review and facility review of Site 010 was conducted by the IACUC on 1/28/2014 which is greater than
eleven months since the last inspection and the facilities at Site 09 have not been inspected by the IACUC since
2/13/2013.
Inspection of the animal facilities by the IACUC is required at least every six months to ensure that any teaching
activities involving animals at these facilities are being conducted in a manner to reduce pain and/or distress and
that they meet the current standards for all species.
The IACUC, as an agent of the research facility, shall inspect, at least once every six months, all of the researchfacility's animal facilities, including animal study areas, using title 9, chapter I, subchapter A-Animal Welfare, as a
basis for evaluation.
