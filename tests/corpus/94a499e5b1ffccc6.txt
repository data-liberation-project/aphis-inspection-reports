3.4(b)(3)

Outdoor housing facilities.
There are two dogs housed in an outdoor enclosure. The shelter structure in this enclosure does not have a wind and rain
break at the entrance. Dog who are not provided a wind and rain break at the entrance to an outdoor shelter structure do
not have adequate protection from the elements. The licensee must ensure than all shelters in outdoor enclosures be
provided with a wind and rain break at the entrance. To be corrected by 16 April 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
