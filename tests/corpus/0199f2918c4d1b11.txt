3.133 Critical

Separation.
The facility has had approximately 15 blackbuck antelope killed by zebra. The facility has sold several of the zebra they
feel are responsible for the deaths, however the remaining zebra are still housed in a pasture with blackbuck.
Animals housed in the same primary enclosure must be compatible. Animals shall not be housed near animals that
interfere with their health or cause them discomfort.
The remaining zebra need to be separated from the blackbuck to ensure no more blackbuck deaths are caused by zebra.
To be corrected by: Sept 13, 2021

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
