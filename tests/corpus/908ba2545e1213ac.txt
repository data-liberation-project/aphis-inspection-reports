2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
(a)(1) Any person operating or intending to operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except
persons who are exempted from the licensing requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section, must have a
valid license.
The pet store sells hedgehogs to the public.
Any business that is selling hedgehogs to the public is operating as a dealer or regulated species and therefore
must have a valid USDA, APHIS, AC license.
A license must be obtained to sell regulated species.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
