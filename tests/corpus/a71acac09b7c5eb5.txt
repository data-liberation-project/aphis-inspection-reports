3.25(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The entrance part of two plastic igloos were exposing a sharp, jagged edge due to missing plastic. Five hamsters
are in contact with the affected igloos which are located in indoor housing. Jagged edges with sharp points could
cause potential injury. The licensee must ensure that indoor housing facilities for hamsters shall be maintained i
good repair to protect the animals from injury. Corrected during the inspection by replacing the affected igloos with
new ones.
3.50(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The top of one plastic igloo has an estimated two inch section of missing plastic that was exposing a sharp, jagged
edge. Three rabbits are in contact with the affected igloo which are located in indoor housing. Jagged edges with
sharp points could cause potential injury. The licensee must ensure that indoor housing facilities for rabbits shall be
maintained i good repair to protect the animals from injury. Corrected during the inspection by replacing the
affected igloo with a new one.
* The facility inspection and verbal exit briefing done on 1/18/18 were conducted with the licensee.
* Copy of the inspection report was sent by certified and regular mail.
