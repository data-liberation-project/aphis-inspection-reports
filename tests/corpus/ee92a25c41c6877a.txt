3.26(d)
FACILITIES, INDOOR.
Interior surfaces. There is bare wood on the floor where the paint is worn or scratched in the guinea pig building.
The interior surfaces including the floor must be constructed and maintained so that surfaces are impervious to
moisture so that they can be readily cleaned and sanitized. Correct by ensuring that the bare wood on floor is
resealed or covered with an impervious surface so ti can be repeatedly cleaning and disinfected. Correct by
November 15, 2016.
3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
Cleaning and sanitation of primary enclosures. There were three enclosures housing a total of 26 adult guinea pigs
with their offspring that had accumulations of feces and dirty bedding The accumulation of feces and dirty bedding
exposes the guinea pigs to potential infections with bacteria, fungi, and parasites. Correct by ensuring that all
primary enclosures are clean and sanitized often enough to prevent an accumulation of feces, dirty bedding, and
debris. Additionally, ensure that the primary enclosures are sanitized at least once every two weeks. Correct by
September 17, 2016.
