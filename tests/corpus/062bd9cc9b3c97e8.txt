3.27
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
Guinea pigs shall not be housed in outdoor facilities unless prior approval for such outdoor housing is obtained from
the Deputy Administrator. At time of this inspection, two guinea pigs were housed in the barn for the petting zoo.
The guinea pigs moved be moved to indoor housing.
Correct by: End of business day 08-27-14.
3.125
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The gate and fence section at the front right of the goat pen located outside the barn is giving easily when pushed
against. The support four by four needs to be repaired or replaced to provide strength for the fence and gate.
Facilities must be structurally sound and be maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury and to
contain the animals.
Correct by 08-31-14.
3.129(b)
FEEDING.
The animal feed shall be placed so as to minimize contamination. The hay in the tack room is placed next to open
shelves which have toxic liquids on it. Hay must be moved or the toxic materials moved away from the hay to
protect the feed from contamination.
Correct by: 08-31-14.
