2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
There is not a formal arrangement with the veterinarian that oversees the facility at this time. Without formal
arrangements, there is no assurance that the animal is receiving proper care from a veterinarian. The facility needs to
provide written documentation that they employ a veterinarian under formal arrangements for the regulated species on
site.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
