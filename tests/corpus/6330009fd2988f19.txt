2.27(a)
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF OPERATION.
Registrant moved since the previous inspection and failed to notify the AC Regional Director of the change in the
mailing address and site address. Registrant is required to notify the AC Regional Director by certified mail of any
change in the name, address, or ownership, or other change in operations affecting its status as a carrier within 10
days after making such change. Notifying the Regional Director of any change in the mailing and/or site address is
necessary to allow inspection of the carrier and so that the Regional Office has the current mailing address on file.
Correct by February 6, 2016.
An exit briefing was conducted with the owner.
