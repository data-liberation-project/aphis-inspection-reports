Requirements and application.
*This is a search inspection. The facility currently exhibits a kangaroo as part of a mobile children's animal educational
program without a valid USDA license.
*A valid USDA license is necessary to protect the health and well-being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act and Regulations.
*The facility needs to obtain a USDA license or discontinue regulated activities (exhibiting regulated species).

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
