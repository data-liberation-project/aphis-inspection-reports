2.1(a)

Requirements and application
The owner of the facility is exhibiting regulated species to the public. These animals include a spider monkey, and a red
kangaroo.

No person shall operate as an exhibitor without a valid license, except persons who are exempt from the licensing
requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section.

A USDA, APHIS, Animal Care license must be obtained to exhibit animals to the public.

NO REGULATED ACTIVITITES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA, APHIS, AC LICENSE IS OBTAINED.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner of the facility.

End Section
