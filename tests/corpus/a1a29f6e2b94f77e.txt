3.1(c)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Furniture including one platform & one of the den boxes in the primary enclosures for the canids were worn and had
sharp edges and holes in the surface. The surfaces of housing facilities, including dens, furniture type fixtures and
objects within the facility must be free of jagged edges or sharp points that might injure animals. All surfaces must
be maintained on a regular basis and must be replaced when worn or soiled.
Correction date 4 September 2015
Exit Interview conducted with facility personnel.
