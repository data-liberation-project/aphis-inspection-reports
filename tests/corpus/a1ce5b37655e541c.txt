2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The results of the May 8, 2014 facility inspection documented damaged tile walls in dog primary enclosures. This
minor deficiency/departure from the Animal Welfare Act was not assigned a correction date or specific plan forcorrection. At the time of this inspection the damaged walls have not been repaired or replaced. To ensure prompt
action and timely or appropriate correction of deficiencies the IACUC must assign a plan of action and correction
dates to identified deficiencies and departures from the Animal Welfare Act.
Correct the above by: Immediate.
3.6(a)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Three primary enclosures housing dogs have damaged walls with tile missing near the junction with the floor. The
resulting defects in the walls may prevent the enclosures from adequate cleaning and sanitizing. Primary
enclosures must be structurally sound and maintained in good repair. The walls should be repaired or replaced.
Correct by: September 18, 2014.
3.8
EXERCISE FOR DOGS.
The facility does not have a documented exercise plan for the six dogs housed individually.The plan must document
that individually housed dogs have no less than twice the required floor space and need no additional exercise
regimen or document the plan for exercise if this minimum requirement is not met. The plan must be approved by
the attending veterinarian and be available upon request by APHIS officials. A documented exercise program is
required to ensure provisions for the physical and psychological well-being of the dogs.
Correct by: September 18, 2014.
