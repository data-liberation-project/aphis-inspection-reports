3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Outdoor housing facilities.
1. The chain link wire fencing attached on the surface of the outdoor wall around the doorway of the wooden shed
housing 3 goats needs to be addressed.
There was at least one sharp point of wire noted above the doorway and one piece of wire along the right side of
the doorway that had come loose forming an elliptical shaped loop approximately 3 inches high by 8 inches wide
located several inches above the ground. The sharp pointed wire could cause an injury to the skin or eyes of the
goats and the elliptical shaped loop of wire could entrap a horn or a limb of the goats.
Per this Section of the Regulations, outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in
good repair to protect the animals from injury.
The licensee needs to repair or replace the chain link wire fencing for the safety and health of the animals.
Correct by 3/13/17.
