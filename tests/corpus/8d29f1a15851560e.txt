3.81
ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
The licensee does not have a written primate enrichment program for the lemurs housed at this facility. Exhibitors
must develop, document and follow an appropriate plan for environment enhancement adequate to promote the
psychological well-being of nonhuman primates. The plan must be directed and approved by the attending
veterinarian and made available to APHIS officials upon request.
