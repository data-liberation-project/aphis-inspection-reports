2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
A current / updated written Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) was not available for inspection. The licensee shall
employ an attending veterinarian under formal arrangements, who could write a PVC plan which would include
regularly scheduled visits to the premises where the animals are kept. The attending veterinarian should conduct at
a minimum, an annual visit to ensure appropriate medical care to animals.
