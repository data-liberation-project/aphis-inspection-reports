3.31(b)

Sanitation.
Housekeeping. There are accumulations of cobwebs, dust, and debris on walls, ceiling, windows, and animal racks.
Animal facilities must be kept clean to ensure good quality of the air for the animals and to prevent unnecessary disease
risks. Correct by ensuring the premises are kept clean, including: routinely dusting off and cleaning walls, ceiling,
windows, and racks; and maintaining a level of general cleanliness to support good animal husbandry and health. Correct
by January 5, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
