SANITATION.
Eleven of the twelve guinea pig enclosures had accumulations of excreta. These enclosures had more fecal
material than bedding. Animals standing in fecal materail are potentially exposed to parasites and diseases.
Enclosures were last cleaned more than two weeks ago. Correct by ensuring that all primary enclosures are
cleaned often enough to prevent an accumulation of excreta or debris and that enclosures are sanitzed at least
once every two weeks. Licensee must establish a program to keep enclosures clean.
