SANITATION.
Sec. 3.131 Sanitation.(a) Cleaning of enclosures.
***There is feed, feces and waste material on the floors and in the trays under the caging in the room housing the
82 sugar gliders. Poor housekeeping increases the risk of disease. The room shall be cleaned and items for use
on the sugar gliders shall be maintained in a manner that will protect from contamination and minimize disease
hazard.
This inspection and exit interview was conducted by the ACI, with the facility owner.
