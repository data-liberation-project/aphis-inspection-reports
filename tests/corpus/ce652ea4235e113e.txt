2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 3:45 pm on
21-Jun-17. The undersigned inspector knocked on the facility door, called two contact numbers, and waited at the
facility for 30 minutes in an attempt to reach the license holder. All attempts failed.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
