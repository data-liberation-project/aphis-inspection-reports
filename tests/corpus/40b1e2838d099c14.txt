3.127(d)

Facilities, outdoor.
A perimeter fence of sufficient height to keep animals and unauthorized persons out does not enclose the facility. All
outdoor housing facilities must be enclosed by a perimeter fence to protect the animals in the facility by restricting animals
and unauthorized persons from going through it or under it. Any alternatives to the regulations regarding perimeter fences
must be approved by the Administrator.
