2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
(b) Each dealer or exhibitor shall establish and maintain programs of adequate veterinary care that include:
(2) The use of appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries
Water is supplied to the kennel through a lixit system. The owner has modified the supply to add hydrogen peroxide
to the system at 30-50 ppm. The owner stated that he has added the peroxide to treat and prevent kennel cough.
The program of veterinary care does not include any guidance on the use of this additive for prevention of kennel
cough.
Any treatments or medications given to the dogs should only be given with the guidance and approval from the
attending veterinarian. Unapproved methods may be injurious to the dogs.
Discontinue using the additive until the veterinarian has reviewed the system and given written approval for the
system.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with facility representative.
