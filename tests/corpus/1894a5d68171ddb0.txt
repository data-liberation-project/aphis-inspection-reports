2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Section 2.31(c)(3) IACUC: The IACUC report of its April 21, 2015 review of the facility program for humane care and
use of animals and the inspection of the animal facility has not been sent to the Institutional Official. The IACUC
report of it October 24, 2014 review and inspection was sent to the Institutional Official signed by only two of the
seven IACUC members. The IACUC report to the Institutional Official must be updated at least once every six
months upon completion of the required semiannual evaluations to keep the Institutional Official adequately
informed and to comply with the regulatory requirements. The IACUC report must be reviewed and signed by a
majority of the IACUC members to assure a fully functioning and participatory committee and to comply with the
regulatory requirements. The IACUC report of its April 21, 2015 evaluations must be submitted to the Institutional
Official signed by a majority of IACUC members. Correct by October 1, 2015.
