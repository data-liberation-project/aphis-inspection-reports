Housing facilities, general.
Several of the wind/rain breaks on the shelters in the outdoor housing facility had dirt and grime on them. Dirt and grime
can harbor organisms that cause disease in dogs. The licensee must ensure that surfaces with which the dogs come in
contact are cleaned and sanitized when necessary to satisfy generally accepted husbandry standards and practices.

To be corrected by August 31, 2021.

Feeding

cleaned and sanitized.

To be corrected by August 31, 2021.
