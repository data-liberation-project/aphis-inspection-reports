2.131(c)(1)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
Photos and videos document that there was not sufficient distance and/or barriers in place to ensure the safety of a
juvenile tiger and the public on August 20, 2016 and October 9, 2016. When showed each of the photos and videos,
the licensee confirmed that he conducted the exhibitions. Because of the strength and speed of tigers, there is an
inherent danger present for both the viewing public and the exhibited animal(s) where there is any chance that the
public could come into direct contact with juvenile or adult tigers. Correct by ensuring that from this point forward all
animals (particularly dangerous animals such as tigers) are exhibited with barriers and/or sufficient distance from
the public that is appropriate for their species, age, strength, and speed.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
