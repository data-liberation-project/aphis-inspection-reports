3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
There are accumulations of wet and soiled bedding on the floor below the water bottles forming a wet pad.
According to the licensee, the bedding is removed every other Saturday. Accumulations of wet and soiled bedding
on the floor of enclosures can expose the guinea pigs to potential infections with bacteria, fungi, and parasites.
Correct by ensuring that all primary enclosures are cleaned and sanitized often enough to prevent accumulations of
excreta, wet and soiled bedding at that enclosures are sanitized at least once every 2 weeks. Correct by November
6, 2018.
