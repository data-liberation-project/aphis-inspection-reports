2.131(d)(2) REPEAT
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
"Gumball-type" animal feed dispensers are available at the facility for the public to feed the hoof stock on exhibition.
The facility is open to the public at all times. An attendant is only present at certain times, however, there is no
public barrier to prevent contact between the animals and the public when the attendant is not present.
No public barrier to prevent contact between the animals and public poses the potential for a safety risk.
The licensee must ensure there is a responsible and readily identifiable employee present at all times when there is
public contact with the animals.
Inspection and exit briefing conducted with the facility representatives.
