3.31(b)

Sanitation.
Several linoleum, tile squares have detached from the cement floor of the back holding room that currently houses 19
guinea pigs. The detached tiles leave the floor in disrepair and expose the unsealed cement floor. Unsealed flooring
cannot be adequately sanitized for the health of the guinea pigs housed in this room.
Facility should repair the damaged floor to maintain the floor in good repair and to facilitate the prescribed husbandry
practices for the health of the animals.

3.131(c)

Sanitation.
In the first section of the back holding room, which houses 63 sugar gliders, 13 ferrets and 11 hedgehogs, several
linoleum tile squares have detached from the cement floor. Detached tiles leave the floor of this room in disrepair and
exposes the unsealed cement floor.

Additionally, the ceiling of the secondary room in this holding area, is detached and hanging. The linoleum floor in this
room is torn and also exposes the cement floor. These damaged areas leave both rooms in disrepair and the floors

cannot be adequately sanitized for the health of the animals housed therein.

The facility should repair the floors and the ceiling to keep the room in good repair and to facilitate the prescribed
husbandry practices for the health of the animals.
