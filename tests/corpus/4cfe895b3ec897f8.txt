2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 1100 on
08/07/2014.
Two USDA-Animal Care Veterinary Medical Officers (VMOs) went to the address of record to conduct an
inspection. The VMO called the cell phone number provided by the licensee when their name did not appear on the
apartment building call box. The licensee stated that they had moved out of the state and did not plan to renew their
license.
