2.75(b)(1)

Records: Dealers and exhibitors.
During the review of paperwork and records it was noted that the acquisition records were incomplete. All of the required
information for whom the animals were purchased from or otherwise acquired was unavailable at the time of inspection.

Appropriate documentation of acquired regulated animals is essential for proper identification, tracing forward and trace
back of the animals.

The licensee must correctly document records of acquisition for all regulated animals, including animals born on the
premises, and assure that all records are kept as required. To be corrected by October 27, 2021.
3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
The inner wire panels on the western side of the raccoon enclosure are not tightly secured and bow in, creating gaps
running vertically up the wall up to 4 inches wide.

Gaps in the wire panels can result in entrapment or injury of the animal.



The indoor and outdoor facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the animals
from injury and to contain the animals. To be corrected by November 03, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
