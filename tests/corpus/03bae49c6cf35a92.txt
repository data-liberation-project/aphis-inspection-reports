2.8(h)
NOTIFICATION OF CHANGE OF NAME, ADDRESS, CONTROL, OR OWNERSHIP OF BUSINESS.
At the time of inspection, it was revealed that one of the two major sites (Pembroke Pines) listed under the
licensee's certificate was closed for business; licensee failed to notify this major change to USDA's Animal Care
(AC) Regional Director. A licensee shall promptly notify the AC Regional Director by certified mail of any change in
the name, address, management, or substantial control or ownership of his/her business or operation, or of any
additional sites, within 10 days of any change. The licensee must officially notify to the AC Regional Director (at
Raleigh, NC) the pertinent/required information on the closing of the Pembroke Pines site.
