3.131(c)
SANITATION.
Throughout the animal areas there was an accumulation of feed bags, baling twine and various items of trash.
Premises (buildings and grounds) shall be kept clean and in good repair in order to protect the animals from injury
and to facilitate the prescribed husbandry practices set forth in this subpart. Accumulations of trash shall be placed
in designated areas and cleared as necessary to protect the health of the animals. The areas were cleaned up and
the trash was properly moved out of the animal areas during this inspection.
Corrected during this inspection.
An exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
