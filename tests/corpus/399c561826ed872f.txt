Housing facilities, general.
The metal frame of the outdoor play yards in both capuchin buildings had areas that were significantly rusted. An
approximately 2-foot-long pipe in play yard A, located near the tunnel leading into Building C room A, was covered by a
thick layer of rust that was pitted and flaking. Capuchins were coming into contact with the rusted areas. Excessive rust
can affect the structural strength of surfaces and prevents the required cleaning and sanitization of enclosures. Correct by
ensuring that any significantly rusted surfaces that are in contact with the primates are repaired or replaced. Correct by:
11/10/2021
