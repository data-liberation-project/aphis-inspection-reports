3.130
WATERING.
Two tubs of water in the pasture housing goats, sheep, and a pig contained cloudy, green water.
Contaminated water may harbor harmful microorganisms that could cause illness in animals. All water receptacles
should be kept clean and sanitary.
Correct by October 10, 2015.
3.131(c)
SANITATION.
Two deteriorating plastic wading pools, what appeared to be an empty feed bag, and several pieces of metal were
found in the pasture containing goats, sheep, and a pig. Pieces of the wading pools could be seen on the ground.
Crumpled pieces of metal roofing with sharp edges and metal pipes were also found in the pasture.
Exposure to debris within enclosures may cause illness from ingestion and/or physical injury to the animals. The
facility should ensure that animal enclosures are monitored for debris and deteriorating items on a regular basis and
any potentially harmful objects should be removed in a timely manner.
Correct by October 11, 2015.
An exit briefing was conducted with a facility representative.
