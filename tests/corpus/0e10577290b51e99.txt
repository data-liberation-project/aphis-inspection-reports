2.40(b)(2) REPEAT
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The licensee had several expired medications at the facility. These medications were not currently being used on
any regulated species.
- A selenium-vitamin E injectable expired on November 2016.
- Dexamethasone solution expired on June 2012.
- A sterile saline solution expired on October 2015.
Medications lose their effectiveness and strength after the expiration date. Medications prescribed and/or
administered to animals must be used before the expiration date. Expired drugs should be disposed of in an
appropriate manner.
*** Corrected: May 11th, at the time of inspection.
The inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the licensee.
