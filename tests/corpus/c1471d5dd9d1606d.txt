3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
There are gaps and cracks in the cement flooring inside the giraffe barn . Debris and water can collect in the gaps
which may further deteriorate the floor . The gaps and cracks also create an uneven surface for the giraffe to walk
on. Flooring in this area must be repaired or replaced and maintained in good condition to prevent injury to the
animals. CORRECT BY : 10-20- 17
3.125(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Bags of dog food are stored in a large pile against the wall in the freezer. Several bags have torn open. There is an
open bag of oats in the storage shed by the bush buck holding barn. Open bags of feed must be stored in closed
containers to prevent contamination of the feed. Storage of bags of feed away from the walls of storage rooms and
freezers will prevent deterioration of the bags and allow for proper cleaning around food supplies. Food supplies
must be stored properly to prevent contamination. CORRECT BY : 9 - 22- 17
