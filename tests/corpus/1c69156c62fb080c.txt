2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
In the whelping area sitting on the counter with other items being utilized by the facility is a bottle of Equi Phar Protal
with an expiration date of 11/11. Licensee stated that this bottle had been recently utilized to deworm several of the
dogs at the facility. Expired medications can experience spoilage or have reduced efficacy. This could lead to
prolonged illness or suffering for the animals needing this drug. Ensure all medications used in the facility are not
expired.
