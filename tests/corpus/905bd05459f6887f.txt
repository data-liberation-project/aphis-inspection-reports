Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).

3.81(c)(4)

Environment enhancement to promote psychological well-being.
The facility has a singly housed, 3-year-old, Japanese snow macaque on exhibit. He is unable to hear or see other
nonhuman primates, which can affect his well-being. This macaque must be provided special attention regarding
enhancement of his environment, based on the needs of the individual species and in accordance with the instructions of
the attending veterinarian. The primate enrichment plan must be updated to address the singly housed, 3-year-old snow
macaque and it must be approved by the attending veterinarian.
