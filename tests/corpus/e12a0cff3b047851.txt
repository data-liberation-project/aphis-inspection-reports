2.134(a)

Contingency planning.
A contingency plan was not available at time of this inspection. If an emergency or incident occurred animal health could
be impacted if the facility was not prepared. Licensee must develop and document a contingency plan in the event of an
emergency or disaster that could affect the health and well-being of animals. Correct by July 25, 2022.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
