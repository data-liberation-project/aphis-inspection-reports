3.125(d)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* Fecal waste is collecting along the edge of three surfaces used by one Chinchilla. The affected surfaces are
located indoor housing. This creates an unsanitary condition that could lead to potential disease, contamination or
health risks. The licensee must provide for regular and frequent removal and disposal of all animal or food waste,
bedding, dead animals, trash and debris for the health and welfare of the Chinchilla. Correct by 5/18/15.
* The facility inspection done on 5/4/15 and exit briefing done on 5/5/15 were conducted with the licensee.
