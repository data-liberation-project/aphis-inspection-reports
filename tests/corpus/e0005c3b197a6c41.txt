3.25(c)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Storage. There is feed in an open container on the floor of the guinea pig room. Food supplies must be stored in
containers with tightly fitting lids or covers or in the original containers as received from the commercial sources of
supply. Open feed must be stored in containers with lids or covers to protect supplies from contamination, spoilage,
and pests. Correct by September 26, 2016.
