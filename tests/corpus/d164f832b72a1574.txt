2.32(a) CRITICAL
PERSONNEL QUALIFICATIONS.
An animal died under anesthesia on Feb 25, 2019 from inappropriate anesthesia for the species and inadequate
anesthesia monitoring.
The circumstances surrounding this death indicate the PI and his group were not fully qualified to perform this
procedure. After this incident all protocols with USDA covered species were closed for this PI.
It shall be the responsibility of the research facility to ensure that all scientists, research technicians, animal
technicians, and other personnel involved in animal care, treatment, and use are qualified to perform their duties.
The facility needs to ensure all personnel are fully trained and qualified to perform animal procedures.
Correct from this day forward.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
