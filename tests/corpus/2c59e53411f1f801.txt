3.130
WATERING.
The water receptacle for the elk herd has a buildup of algae and other organic material on the inside surfaces of the
the tank.
Unclean water receptacles can contaminate the drinking water and can pose a potential disease hazard to the
animals.
The water tank must be cleaned and maintained as often as necessary to prevent a buildup of organic materials
from developing in the water tank.
Correct by May 16, 2014.
Inspection and exit interview conducted with facility representatives.
