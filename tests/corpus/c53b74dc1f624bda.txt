2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The owner of the pet store has degus on hand for sale.
Any person operating or intending to operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except persons
who are exempted from the licensing requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section, must have a valid license.
A USDA, APHIS, AC license must be obtained to sell degus.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA, APHIS, AC LICENSE IS
OBTAINED.
Inspection and exit conducted with the owner of the store.
