3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
In the enclosure for the leopard "Conga", there are two boards on the upper resting platform that are damaged, with
splinters of wood visible on the end. The jagged edge of the wood has the potential to cause injury to the leopard,
and is not maintained in good repair.
The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to
protect the animals from injury and to contain the animals. A system of maintenance should be in place to maintain
the enclosures in good repair.
Correction date: 24 November 2016.
Exit Interview conducted with facility representative.
