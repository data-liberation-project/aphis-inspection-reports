2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The owner of this facility is exhibiting regulated mammals without a valid USDA license. His USDA license was
cancelled on January 14, 2014. The owner of this facility maintains a web site which advertises a petting zoo, tiger
show, llama rides and other animal activities. The public is also able to view the animals when they enter the
property to fish, go zip lining, go on a hay ride, as well as other non animal activities. This is considered an
exhibition of animals.
You may not exhibit or allow the public to (directly or indirectly) view any regulated mammals on your property
unless you possess a valid USDA exhibitor's license.
