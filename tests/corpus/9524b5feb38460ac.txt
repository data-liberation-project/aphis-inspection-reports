3.53(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Item Primary enclosures 3.53 a1
In Sept of 2015 a rabbit was able to wedge himself in the vertical grill openings of the front door panel of his cage
The facility had to cut the cage door to free the animal, but upon release the animal expired. No other rabbits
housed in similar rabbit cages were harmed.
Facility instituted a review of all rabbit cages and transferred rabbits to cages with different front door panels.
There have been no further incidents of this nature.
Facility needs to continue to monitor the primary enclosures.
Corrected before end of inspection
Inspection took place over 2 days 7/5-6/2016
Exit interview took place at the end of inspection
