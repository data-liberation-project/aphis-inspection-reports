3.76(c) REPEAT
INDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
***The shelter housing facility of the baboons, Patas, and african green monkey had deficient light supply making it
difficlut to conduct inspections and to observe the animals.
**** Lighting must be uniformly diffused throughout animal facilities and provide sufficient illumination to aid in
maintaining good housekeeping practices, adequate cleaning, adequate inspection of animals, and for the
well-being of the animals.
3.127(b)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
****The outdoor enclosure in which the porcupine was housed had no shelter from inclement weather.
****Exposure of the porcupine to direct sunlight, rain, etc., may cause discomfort to the animal.
****Artificial or natural shelter must be provided to animals kept outdoors in order to afford them protection and to
prevent discomfort.
****To be corrected by 4-19-2014.
3.129(a)
FEEDING.
****Multiple enclosures were observed with food on the floor which may cause the food to become contaminated.
**** Food shall be wholesome, palatable and with adequate nutritive value in order to maintain all animals in good
health, and free from contamination in order to prevent disease and health hazards.
****To be corrected by 4-22-2014.
****An exit interview was conducted with the facility representative.
