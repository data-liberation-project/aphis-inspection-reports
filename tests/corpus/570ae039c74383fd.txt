FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
There is a hole in the roof of the deer shed. Appropriate shelter must be provided for animals kept outdoors to
provide them protection and to prevent discomfort. The deer shelter roof must be repaired or replaced to protect the
animals from rain and snow entering the shed and to prevent discomfort. Correct by December 11, 2018.
