3.81
ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
There is no environmental enhancement plan approved by the attending veterinarian.
Dealers, exhibitors, and research facilities must develop, document, and follow an appropriate plan for environment
enhancement adequate to promote the psychological well-being of nonhuman primates. The plan must be in
accordance with the currently accepted professional standards as cited in appropriate professional journals or
reference guides, and as directed by the attending veterinarian.
Prepare a program of environmental ehancement for the primates and have it approved by the attending
veterinarian for the health of the animals.
To be corrected by February 25, 2015.
Inspection conducted with the licensee.
