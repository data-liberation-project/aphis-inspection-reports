2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 11:30 am on
02-Feb-17. The inspector knocked on the facility door, called the contact number for the license holder, and waited
in the driveway upon arrival. The license holder called back a couple of hours later in the day indicating that she
was at work and did not have access to her phone.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
