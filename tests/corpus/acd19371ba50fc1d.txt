3.127(d)

Facilities, outdoor.
The outdoor housing facilities are not enclosed by a perimeter fence. This could affect the animals housed at the facility
by allowing entrance of unwanted persons and animals. Perimeter fences of sufficient height are required to keep
animals and unauthorized persons out. In addition, fences must be constructed so that it protects the animals in the
facility and function as a secondary containment. The facility is currently in the preliminary stages of beginning the
perimeter fence construction.
