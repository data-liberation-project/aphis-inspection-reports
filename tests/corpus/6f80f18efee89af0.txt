3.130
WATERING.
The low pressure water jet that the provides continuous clean water for two pigs was clogged leaving the puddle on
the ground as the available water for the pigs. Lack of a potable source of drinking water can cause animal
sickness. The licensee cleaned the valve and a clean flow of water source was restored during inspection. To
promote the health of animals, ensure that they have access to clean water or that water receptacles holding their
water are clean and sanitary at all times.
Maintain correct from this day forward.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
