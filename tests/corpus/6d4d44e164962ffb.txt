2.33(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE.
A bottle of BOSE with an expiration date of 3/15 was found on a drug cart in the cow barn. In addition, a bottle of
Bupivicaine with an expiration date of Feb 2016 was found in the dental lab. Use of expired medications is not
considered appropriate veterinary care and can be detrimental to the animals health. Both medications were
discarded immediately.
