3.78(d) REPEAT
OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
***The perimeter fence is approximately one foot in distance from the outer wall of the outdoor primary enclosure of
the marmoset.
A perimeter fence must be of sufficient distance from the outside wall or fence of the primary enclosure to prevent
physical contact between animals inside the enclosure and both animals and humans outside the perimeter fence.
Such fences less than three feet in distance from the primary enclosure must be approved by the Administrator.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the Institutional Official and APHIS personnel.
