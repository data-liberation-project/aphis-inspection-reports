SANITATION.
Housekeeping. The floor in the lower room contains spilled hay, shavings, feces, and debris. Animal facilities must
be kept clean to ensure good quality air for the animals and to prevent unnecceary disease risks to the animals.
Ineffective cleaning and housekeeping exposes the animals to disease hazards and health problems. Correct by
ensuring the premises are kept clean, including removing spilled hay, shavings, feces, and debris on the floor and
sides of enclosures.
