3.28(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
There is bare wood where the paint is worn off on the wooden base for the water receptacles and food bowls for the
majority of the weaned guinea pig enclosures. Primary enclosures, including their racks, shelving and other
accessories, must be constructed of smooth material substantially impervious to liquids and moisture.The wooden
bases must be resealed, replaced, or removed so that surfaces in contact with the animals are impervious to liquids
and moisture so that they can repeatedly cleaned and disinfected. Correct by ensuring that all surfaces in the
primary enclosures for guinea pigs are impervious to liquids and moisture.
Correct by July 1, 2015
An exit briefing was conducted with the owner.
