3.129(a)

Feeding.
The produce and the meat thawing refrigerator had debris in the shelves and below the shelves of the fridge.
Refrigerators that are not clean can lead to contamination of food. Food fed to covered mammals shall be wholesome,
palatable, and free from contamination in order to ensure the welfare of the animals.
