SANITATION.
Sec. 3.56 Sanitation.
(c) Housekeeping.
As you approach the rabbitry, there is barrels, wood, tarps, wire caging, and other miscellaneous debris scattered
about. Although most of the debris is elements used in the animal husbandry, the way it is being stored is creating
living and breeding areas for rodents. The premise surrounding the facility, needs to be cleaned in order to create a
safe environment, and facilitate the husbandry practices. Premises shall remain free of accumulations of debris and
other materials not being used for the husbandry practice.
To be corrected by August 2, 2015.
This inspection and exit interveiw was conducted by the ACI, with the facility owner.
