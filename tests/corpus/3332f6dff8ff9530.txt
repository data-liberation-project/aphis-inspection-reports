2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
***The registrant failed to perform a review of the facility's program for humane care and use of animals. The IACUC
meeting took place on July 21, 2014 and then on April 27, 2015. These reviews are to be done every six months at
a minimum and are necessary to examine whether the institution is demonstrating compliance with good animal
care and use practices as defined by the Animal Welfare Act and regulations.
Correct from this day forward.
2.31(c)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
***The registrant failed to perform an inspection of the research facility's animal facilities every six months as
prescribed by the Animal Welfare Act. There was a facility inspection on July 21, 2014 and then on April 27, 2015.
These inspections are to be done every six months at a minimum and are necessary to examine whether the
institution's animal facilities are in compliance with the standards set forth in the Animal Welfare Act and regulations.
Correct from this day forward.
2.31(c)(3)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
***The facility has not had the required semi-annual meetings regarding evaluations of the animal care and use
program and facility inspections. For this reason no reports or evaluations were prepared and delivered to the
Institutional Official of the research facility. It is the responsibility of this institution to evaluate its program, conduct
facility inspections and prepare evaluative reports on the findings and present these reports to the Institutional
Official. Correct from this day forward.
