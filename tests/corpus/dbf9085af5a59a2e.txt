2.50(a)(1)
TIME AND METHOD OF IDENTIFICATION.
* There are eight dogs that have no official identification on them at time of inspection. Official identification is
needed to ensure each animal(cid:25)s identity can be known with certainty and to facilitate the ability to trace dogs. The
licensee must ensure that all dogs are officially identified by collar, tattoo or microchip means. If the licensee uses
microchips then a microchip reader must be made available to the inspector at time of facility inspection. Correct
by 12/4/14.
2.75(a)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
* Official identification of eight dogs were not listed on the official USDA (APHIS form 7005) dog on hand sheets.
Records provide a source of information that can be used to identify every dog on the premise. Complete and
accurate records are needed to ensure each animal's identity can be known with certainty and to facilitate the ability
to trace all dogs. The licensee must maintain complete and accurate records of all dogs at all times at their facility.
Correct by 12/4/14.
3.1(c)(2)
HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
* Gaps in the cement flooring were observed in four outdoor enclosures. Eight dogs are in contact with the
affected gaps estimated to be a half to one inch in width. Gaps in cement flooring can allow accumulations of dirt,
debris, food waste, excreta, and other disease hazards. The licensee must fill in all gaps and holes with surface
material that will allow the cement floor surface to be adequately cleaned and sanitized. Correct by 12/4/14.
3.10 REPEAT
WATERING.
* The interior rim of three metal water receptacles used by three dogs have a brownish looking deposit collecting on
an estimated 80 to 90 percent of the interior wall of these receptacles. The receptacles are located in shelter
enclosures. It indicates the water receptacles have not been adequately cleaned and sanitized. It could lead towater contamination affecting the health and well-being of the dogs. The licensee must ensure all water
receptacles are kept clean and sanitized.
* The facility inspection done on 11/4/14 and exit briefing done on 11/5/14 were conducted with the licensee.
