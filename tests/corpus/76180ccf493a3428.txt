SANITATION.
The license has started to use a botanical rodent repellent which is hung in a small sack from the ceiling in the
raccoon den. This sack is accessible to the raccoon, but at the time of the inspection the raccoon does not appear
to be interested in the sack of product While this product contains tree and other food grade oils, it may cause
gastric disturbances if ingested according to the manufacture.
This product should be installed in a fashion which would minimize the possibility of the animal ingesting it.
