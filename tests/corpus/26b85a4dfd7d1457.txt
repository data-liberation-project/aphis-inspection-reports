3.4(b)(3)
OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
One dog shelter in use for three dogs does not have a wind/rain break at its entrance. A shelter without a wind and
rain break may not provide adequate protection from the elements. All dog shelters must be provided with wind/rain
breaks at their entrances. Correct by 8 Feb. 2016.
This routine inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative and Kendall Lundy, ACI.
