3.53(a)(1)

Primary enclosures.
A minimum of 23 feeders were rusted through at the edges creating flakes and sharp points. Items in poor repair to which
the rabbits have access can cause injury. Primary enclosures shall be kept in good repair to protect the rabbits from
injury. Correct by 2/9/2023 by ensuring all surfaces including feeders are free of rust and sharp points.
3.54(b)

Feeding.
A food receptacle was found to have blue-green caked substance resembling mold smashed into the perforated panel.
Rabbits consuming mold or contaminated feed can become ill, perhaps leading to death. Measures must be taken to
prevent molding, deterioration and caking of feed when using self-feeders to feed rabbits. Correct by 2/2/2023.
