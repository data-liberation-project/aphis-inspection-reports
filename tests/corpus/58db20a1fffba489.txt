3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The fence surrounding the goat and sheep enclosure is in disrepair. The front fence of the enclosure has been
repaired with hog panels laid over another fence that has broken wires. There is also a gap between the two fences
that may allow the goats or sheep to become entangled and injured. The main gate to the enclosure is also in
disrepair. The gate and fence shall be repaired to protect the animals and keep them secured within their
enclosures.
Correct by October 1, 2014
The inspection and exit briefing conducted with the owner.
