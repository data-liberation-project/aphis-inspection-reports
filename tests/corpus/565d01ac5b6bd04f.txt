3.31(a)(1)
SANITATION.
One enclosure containing nine adult breeders and two enclosures containing weanlings had an accumulation of
excreta that was more than fifty percent of the amount of bedding in the enclosure. Enclosures shall be cleaned and
sanitized often enough to prevent an accumulation in order to reduce the transmission of disease and provide for
the comfort of the animals. Correct by: 24 November 2014
An exit interview was conducted with facility owner.
