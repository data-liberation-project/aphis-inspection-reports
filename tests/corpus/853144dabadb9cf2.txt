2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The owner of the facility has animals on exhibit to the public at the Village of Ashley Country Christmas in Ashley,
MI.
Any person operating or intending to operate as an exhibitor, except persons who are exempted from the licensing
requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section, must have a valid license.
A USDA, APHIS, AC license to exhibit animals to the public must be acquired prior to exhibiting animals to the
public.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
Exit conducted with the owner of the facility.
