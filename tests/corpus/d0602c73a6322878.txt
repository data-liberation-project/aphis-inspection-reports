2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
Section 2.126(b) - Access and inspection of records and property: A responsible adult shall be made available to
accompany APHIS officials during the inspection process.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 1:10 PM on
05/18/2016.
Inspector called the licensee by phone, left a message on the machine, approached the gate and called out, left a
card in the mailbox with the time & day of attempted inspection and waited a half an hour before leaving.
Licensee returned call later and optimal hours of inspection were discussed and documented.
