2.31(c)(1)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
IACUC meeting minutes and the semi-annual evaluation letter to the Institutional Official show that the IACUC
completed a facility inspection on Oct. 20, 2014 but there is no mention that a review of the facility's program of
humane care and use of animals was completed. The next facility inspection conducted was done on June 19, 2015
but again there are no records available to show that the facility's program for humane care and use of animals was
reviewed. Review of a facility's program of humane care and use of animals shall be done at least every six months
to ensure that the IACUC is familiar with the program and the use of animals at the facility and can adequately
address any issues identified in a timely manner. The IACUC shall complete a review of the facility's program of
humane care and use at least once every six months. Correct from this day forward.

INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
