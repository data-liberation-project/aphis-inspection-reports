FACILITIES, GENERAL.
A giraffe became entangled in an enrichment device during the night of March 6, 2017 and died. The facility took
immediate steps to remove all similar enrichment devices and has developed a revised standard operating
procedure regarding enrichment. All housing facilities, including enrichment items, should be structurally sound to
prevent injury. Corrected by facility prior to inspection.
Oryx #1939 received appropriate veterinary treatment.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the facility representative.
