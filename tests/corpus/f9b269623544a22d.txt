HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
An excess accumulation of fecal material was seen on the outdoor cement pad of sheltered housing for at least 9
enclosures housing 11 dogs. Owner states that he normally removes fecal material every other day.
Excessive build up of waste is unsanitary and can lead to health problems in the dogs and pest problems.
Correction should include daily spot cleaning of fecal material from surfaces that the dogs come in contact with and
sanitizing when necessary to prevent build up of fecal matter. Spot cleaning to be corrected by 6 pm, 12/7/2016.

with facility representative.
