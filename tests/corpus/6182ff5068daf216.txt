3.131(b)
SANITATION.
The licensee has not been properly sanitizing the enclosure's as required. The enclosures must have a proper
disinfectant and detergent used to prevent diseases and reduce odors on a regular basis. When enclosures are
cleaned by hosing or flushing, adequate measures shall be taken to protect the animals confined in such enclosures
from being directly sprayed with the stream of water or wetted involuntarily. The licensee must develop a plan for
proper cleaning and sanitization for all of the chinchilla enclosures.

licensee).
