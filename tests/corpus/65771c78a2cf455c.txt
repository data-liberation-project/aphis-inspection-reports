3.131(c)

Sanitation.
Around the lower sorting area and the maintenance shed is an accumulation of broken equipment, household items,
construction debris, and tree branches. Premises shall be kept clean in order to protect the animals’ health and wellbeing.
These conditions promote pests, both insect and rodents that can relay diseases to the wolves. A working program of
cleaning around the building and grounds as necessary to protect the health of the animals must be implemented.
To be corrected by: August 31, 2021

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representative.
