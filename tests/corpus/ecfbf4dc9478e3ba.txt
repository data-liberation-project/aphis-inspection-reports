2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The breeder is currently unlicensed. The inspector met with the breeder and discussed licensing requirements.
The breeder was found to be conducting regulated activities and requires a license. Any person operating or
intending to operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except persons who are exempted from
the licensing requirements under paragraph (a)(3) of this section, must have a valid license. The application was
completed while the inspector was there.
