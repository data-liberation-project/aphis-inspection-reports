2.131(d)(2)

Handling of animals.
Employees have no distinguishing clothing or other means to to identify them as employees of the petting zoo.
Having readily-identifiable employees is important to enable customers to know who to approach with questions and
concerns, and to confer authority to the employees for enforcing rules in the facility.
