3.6(a)(2)(xii)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
Flooring that is constructed of metal strands/wire, must be greater in diameter than 9 gauge or covered/coated with
a material such as plastic or fiberglass. The outside runs of the main kennel area have wire flooring where the
coating is worn, cracked, or missing. The thin bare wire flooring is smaller than regulations allow. The owner must
repair or replace these areas flooring. This will help dogs be more comfortable, less chance of foot injury, and
reside in a safe environment. Correct by 04-15-2017.
This inspection and exit briefing were conducted with the owner and Karl Thornton, ACI.
