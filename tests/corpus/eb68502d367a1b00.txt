3.4(b)(4)

Outdoor housing facilities.
In the outdoor enclosures, affecting 30 dogs, the shelters did not have sufficient bedding provided. Sufficient bedding is
necessary during cold weather for the dogs to keep warm. Bedding must be provided when the ambient temperature is
below 50 °F (10 °C). Additional clean, dry bedding is required when the temperature is 35 °F (1.7 °C) or lower. The
licensee must provide sufficient bedding to all of the outdoor shelters. The licensee must ensure that when the
temperature drops below 50 degrees Fahrenheit bedding is provided and additional bedding when temperatures drop
below 35 degrees Fahrenheit.
