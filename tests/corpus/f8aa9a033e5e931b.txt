3.13(c)(1)
CONSIGNMENTS TO CARRIERS AND INTERMEDIATE HANDLERS.
A dog arriving at SFO from Seoul on United Flight 892, baggage tag # 4016-804369, had an incomplete consignor's
certification on the crate:
- Although the owner's name was present on the certification document, there was no address given.
Failure to provide complete information on the consignor's certification could result in delays in transport, which
could have a negative impact on the well-being of the animal. The airline should ensure that complete information is
provided by the consignor, prior to taking possession of the animal.
To be corrected from this time forward.
