2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
(b)(2)
The licensee had a bottle of Specto Gaurd labeled as an oral treatment for scours in pigs and 2 bottles of antibiotics
labeled for treatment of fish with the kennel medications. The program of vet care did not list these medication to be
used in the treatment of dogs. The licensee shall seek guidance from the attending veterinarian to identify off label
medications that are safe and effective when treating dogs. The licensee shall obtain written instructions which
include the route, dosage and treatment the medication is used for. The bottle should also be labeled with these
dosage instructions.
