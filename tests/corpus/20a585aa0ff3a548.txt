2.40(a)(1)

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The licensee has an attending veterinarian who has provided veterinary care for the animals. However, there is no
documentation of formal arrangements with the veterinarian. There is also no written program of veterinary care. Formal
arrangements with a veterinarian that includes the frequency of visits to premises and a written program of veterinary
care, ensures animals will receive consistent and timely diagnoses and treatment of diseases, illnesses, and injuries. The
formal arrangement with the veterinarian must be documented and a written program of veterinary care must be
developed by the attending veterinarian for the health and well-being of the animals. Correct By May 14, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
