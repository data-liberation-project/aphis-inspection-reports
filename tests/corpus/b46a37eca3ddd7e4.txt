2.40(b)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The 2 year old, male, Yorkshire Terrier dog with USDA number 136 has an accumulation of hard, greyish/brown
material on the upper incisors and cheek teeth on both sides of the mouth. The gums associated with these teeth
are dark red and inflamed. Licensee must use appropriate methods to prevent, control, diagnose, and treat
diseases and injuries to ensure the health and well-being of the dogs. This dog must be examined by a licensed
veterinarian. Documentation of the examination, diagnosis, and treatment must be maintained by the Licensee and
made available to APHIS officials.
