3.103(b) REPEAT
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
A number of the cetacean enclosures (Dolphin Cove, Aloha Discovery and Backstage tanks) do not have shaded
areas to protect the animals from direct sunlight. Direct sunlight can be uncomfortable for the animals and can
adversely affect their health, especially eye health.
To be corrected by original correction deadline.
Inspection and exit briefing was conducted on-site with facility representative.
