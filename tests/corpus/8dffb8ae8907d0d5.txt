3.31(b)
SANITATION.
The area of the loft where the guinea pigs are being kept is cluttered with cages, wood, store supplies, animal
containers, crates and a variety of other items. The unorganized accumulation of items and debris nearby the
animal housing, may encourage pests. Vermin pose a parasite and disease hazard to the guinea pigs. Premises
must be kept clean and organized to discourage pests and protect the animals.
