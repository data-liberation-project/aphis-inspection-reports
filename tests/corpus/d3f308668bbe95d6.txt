2.31(e)(2)
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
One IACUC approved protocol involving covered species does not contain an adequate rationale for the
appropriateness for the numbers of animals to be used.There is not an explanation for the range in numbers stated,
90 (cid:19)to 120 animals, needed for statistical validation.
The rationale for the appropriateness of the number of animals to be used in a study is an important component of
protocols that utilize animals. The rationale approved by the IACUC should provide assurance that the appropriate
number of animals is being used to obtain the information the activity is designed to provide.
The IACUC should ensure all proposals to conduct an activity involving animals contains an adequate rationale for
the numbers of animals to be used.
To be corrected on all active and future protocols.
An exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
