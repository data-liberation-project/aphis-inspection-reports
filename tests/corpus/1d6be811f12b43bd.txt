2.75(a)(1)
RECORDS: DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS.
The Record of Acquisition and Dogs and Cats on Hand (APHIS Form 7005) has not been recently updated. There
are several dogs listed on the inventory that are no longer at the facility. The dates of disposition have not been
updated to indicate when the dogs were transferred, sold, or died.
Complete and accurate records must be kept to facilitate the tracking of animals.
The licensee must update the Record of Acquisition and Dogs and Cats on Hand to include the dates of disposition
for each animal that is no longer at the facility. Complete and accurate records must be kept at all times.
To be corrected by May 23, 2016.
