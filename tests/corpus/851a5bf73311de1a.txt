2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
** The facility has documentation to verify monthly site visits by the attending veterinarian, but does not have a
written program of veterinary care. The facility and attending veterinarian must establish a written program of
veterinary care to ensure that the animals at the facility are receiving adequate veterinary care and to provide for the
health and well-being of the animals at the facility. All licensed facilities with a part time veterinarian must have a
written program of veterinary care. To be corrected by: 06-20-16.
3.81
ENVIRONMENT ENHANCEMENT TO PROMOTE PSYCHOLOGICAL WELL-BEING.
** The facility does provide environmental enrichment for the non-human primates, but does not have a written plan
or documentation to support the environmental enhancement provided. The facility along with the attending
veterinarian must establish an environment enhancement plan and document the enhancement provided to
promote the psychological well-being of the non-human primates at the facility. All licensed facilities with non-human
primates must have a plan to provide environmental enhancement to promote psychological well-being and
document the enhancement provided. To be corrected by: 06-20-16.
