3.127(d)

Facilities, outdoor.
There is no perimeter fence present on two sides of the outside enclosures for the nonhuman primates. A perimeter
fence measuring six feet in height and standing no closer than 3 ft to the enclosure is required to prevent contact between
the nonhuman primates and animals or unauthorized persons outside of the enclosures. A perimeter fence shall be
constructed along the two sides of the outside enclosure to protect the animals held within. Correct by October 15, 2022.
