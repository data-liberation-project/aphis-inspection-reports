2.1(a)(1)

Requirements and application.
At the time of this inspection it was noted that the following species were being exhibited to the general public along with a
variety of other domestic farm animals: guinea pigs, dromedary camel, red kangaroo, wallaby, zedonk, two-toed sloth,
tamandua, ring-tailed lemur, Patagonian cavy, muntjac deer, African-crested porcupine, fox, and mini zebu. These
species are not exempt, or excluded, from the definition of an Animal Welfare exhibitor. Therefore, a USDA Class "C"
exhibitor license is needed. An application will need to be submitted to the USDA - Animal Care office.

No regulated activities can be conducted until a USDA license is obtained.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with facility representatives.
