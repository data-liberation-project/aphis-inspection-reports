2.31(e)(5) REPEAT
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
Protocols # 57,151, 171, 178, 179, 180, 176, and 177 do not indicate a method of euthanasia to be utilized. The
proposed activities must provide a complete description of the method of euthanasia to be used. The IACUC should
review proposed activities to ensure there is a complete description of the method of euthanasia to be utilized that is
in accordance with established standard veterinary practices and regulatory requirements of the Act.
Exit interview conducted with facility representative.
