3.4(b)
OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
***Shelter from the elements.****
An outside enclosure containing two adult cocker spaniels is provided a dog shelter that is not large enough to allow
enough room for both dogs to enter and make normal postural adjustments simultaneously. All outside shelters
must be large enough to allow all dogs room to enter, turn and lie down freely. To be corrected by: 05/24/15
