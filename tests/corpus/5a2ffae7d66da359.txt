ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).

3.50(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Several cages located at the facility's D barn were found to be in need of repair. The mesh wire of these cages was
rusted, deteriorated and with sharp ends. Housing facilities for rabbits shall be structurally sound and shall be
maintained in good repair, to protect the animals from injury, to contain the animals, and to restrict the entrance of
other animals. All deteriorated wire cages intended to house regulated animals must be repaired or replaced to
avoid potential injuries to rabbits.

PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
The roof at the facility's D barn was found to be in need of repair. Several areas of the wood roof were deteriorated

enable the rabbits to remain dry, clean and protected from rain and other weather elements. To provide adequate
