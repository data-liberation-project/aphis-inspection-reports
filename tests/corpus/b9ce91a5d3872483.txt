HOUSING FACILITIES, GENERAL.
The kennel facility consists of three sheltered buildings and one outdoor housing area. In the whelping building
there are five occupied enclosures whose exterior wall surfaces have a build up of dirt, hair and body oils which
need to be removed. On the exterior of the sheltered building with raised wire enclosures the exterior wall surfaces
need to be cleaned to remove the discoloration and body oils. There are five enclosures on the east side and four
enclosures on the western side. On the interior of the Bull dog building there are six occupied enclosures whose
wall surfaces need to be cleaned to remove the dirt and body oils. The surfaces of housing facilities--including
houses, dens, and other furniture-type fixtures and objects within the facility be readily cleaned and sanitized, or
removed or replaced when worn or soiled.
Affects sixteen adults. Correct by September 5, 2014
The inspection and exit briefing was conducted with the facility representative.
