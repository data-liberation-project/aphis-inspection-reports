3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
One of the fennec fox slips through the openings in her enclosure mesh. She ends up loose in the fox room. Outside of
her enclosure she may become ill or injured as she encounters stored items or gets too close to the other animals and
their enclosures. Housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the
animals from injury and to contain the animals.
