3.13(a)(3)

Veterinary care for dogs.
Fecal testing has not been performed in accordance with the schedule that was approved by the Attending Veterinarian.
Failure to follow a written and Attending Veterinarian approved schedule for sampling and treatment of parasites and
other pests can lead to a delay in treatment for the animals in the facility. Correct by ensuring that sampling and treatment
of parasites and other pests in accordance with a schedule approved by the Attending Veterinarian is followed.

applicant.

fee and must wait 6 months to reapply. Please contact your inspector to schedule your next Re-License Inspection.
