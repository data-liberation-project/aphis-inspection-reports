TIME AND METHOD OF IDENTIFICATION.
***Three litters of puppies, dates of birth of 2/13/17, 2/16/17, and 2/24/17, were housed in the newest sheltered

3.2(d)
INDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
***A new construction was observed on this inspection. This new building will be utilized as an indoor puppy rearing
building for weaned puppies. The building floor surface is comprised of compressed gravel flooring. The puppies
will have direct access to this gravel flooring surface. This surface is not impervious to moisture. Surfaces in
contact with the puppies must be impervious to moisture as well as all floor surfaces in indoor kennel areas.
Surfaces not impervious to moisture are not easily cleaned and sanitized and pose disease, odor and pest risks to
the dogs. To be corrected by:04/15/17
3.4(b)(4)
OUTDOOR HOUSING FACILITIES.
***The northwestern builidng contained 14 adult dogs. The dog shelters provided for these pens did not contain an
adequate amount of bedding and at the time of inspection the ambient temperature in these enclosures was
approximately 40 degrees F. The facility does make use of electric heating lamps to provide auxillary heat. At the
time of inspection the outdoor temperature was approximately 28 degrees F. Bedding must be provided to the
shelters to allow the dogs to nest in to conserve body heat. Bedding must be maintained clean and dry. To be
corrected by: 03/16/17
