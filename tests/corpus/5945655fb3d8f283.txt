3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
In several areas of the goat and alpaca pens, the fence had areas of damage and loose wires with sharp points that could
potentially injure the covered animals in the enclosures. Outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and
maintained in good repair to protect the animals from injury.

Correct by 15 March 2022

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the IACUC Chair.
