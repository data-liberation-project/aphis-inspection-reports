3.28(a)(1)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES.
A small amount of metal lids for the hamsters are rusty. All hamster enclosures shall be maintained in good repair
that includes no rust. Rusty galvanized lids are not constructed of smooth material that is impervious to moisture
and liquids. The licensee shall replace all rusty hamster enclosure lids so they are maintained in good repair to
protect the hamsters.
Correct by 5-8-2016
