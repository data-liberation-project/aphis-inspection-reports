Male chocolate/tan Dachshund (microchip #105-560-337) has a buildup of brown tartar on the teeth. The majority

the touch with round clumps of brown debris trapped within them.
Male black/tan Dachshund (microchip #0A01480502) has a buildup of brown tartar on the teeth. The entire surface

buildup of dark brown tartar. A line of thick, grey material is located along the gum line above the upper right teeth.
Nearly the entire surface of the upper left canine tooth and upper left cheek teeth are covered in a buildup of brown
tartar. The gums are moderately reddened.
The licensee stated that the dogs had tartar removed from their teeth 6 months ago by a dog groomer.
Male Shih tzu (microchip #105568855) has an abnormal appearance to the right eye. The eye is cloudy and a
brown, dry, crusty material is attached to the hair along the upper and lower eyelids. A yellow discharge is also
located along the inner and outer corner of the eye. The white of the eye is slightly reddened. The dog is not
currently receiving any treatment for the eye, but the licensee put some ointment in the dog's eye at the time of the
inspection.
Poor eye and dental health could cause pain and discomfort to the dog, as well as lead to further health issues.

effective treatment plan. The licensee must ensure that the facility's dental plan maintains the dog's teeth in a
healthy condition.
