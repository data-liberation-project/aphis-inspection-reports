3.14(a)(6)
PRIMARY ENCLOSURES USED TO TRANSPORT LIVE DOGS AND CATS.
***The enclosures currently being used for animal transport do not have the required Live Animal stickers. Live
Animal stickers with arrows or other indicators to show the correct upright position must be attached to the side and
top of the primary enclosure to prevent accidental mishandling.
Correct by February 1, 2017.
3.19(b) CRITICAL
HANDLING.
***During an overnight stay while on route for transport, the registrant removed a dog from it's primary enclosure to
allow the dog exercise in the hotel room. Upon receiving a personal delivery at the hotel room, the door to the room
was opened and the dog was able to escape. A search was conducted, however, the dog was not found. All dogs
shall be handled in a manner that will avoid physical harm or distress.
Correct from this day forward.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the owner.
