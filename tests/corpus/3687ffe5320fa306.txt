2.40(a)(1)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The attending veterinarian had not visited the facility in over one year at the time of inspection. The animals were
receiving care and treatment as outlined in the signed Program of Veterinary Care (PVC) and prescription lables.
Veterinary visits more than a year apart are not considered regularly scheduled visits to the premises of the dealer
and conditions could change that would adversely affect the animals even following previously determined best
practices. Correct By: 20 March 2015
An exit interview was conducted with facility owner.
