2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
Any person operating or intending to operate as a dealer, exhibitor, or operator of an auction sale, except persons
who are exempted from the licensing requirements must have a valid license.
The owner of the facility exhibits both domestic and exotic animals to the public without a USDA, APHIS, AC
license.
Any person operating as an animal exhibitor must have a valid license,
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A VALID USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
