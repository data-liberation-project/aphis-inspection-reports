Facilities, general.
There are two adult fox and 5 fox kits in an enclosure. The fox den is set up on a shelf approximately 2 feet off the floor.
There is an approximately 4" board missing directly in front of the den. The board missing in front of the fox den may
cause an injury to the fox kits if they fall out of the fox den.
The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the
animals from injury.
The licensee must ensure enclosures are maintained in good repair to prevent injury to the animals contained.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
