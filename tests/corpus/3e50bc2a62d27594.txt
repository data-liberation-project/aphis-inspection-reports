2.1(a)(1)
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The facility advertises via website that it is operating as a photographer that allows photos with regulated animals
(rabbits) for a fee.
This type of activity is considered an exhibition of animals as defined by the Animal Welfare Act. A valid USDA
Class C license is necessary to protect the health and well being of the animals and to comply with the Animal
Welfare Act.
You may not exhibit regulated animals (included but not limited to rabbits) without a Class C license.
A pre license application package will be mailed to the facility.
