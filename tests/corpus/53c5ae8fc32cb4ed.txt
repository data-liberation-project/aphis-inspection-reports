REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
The facility is operating without a license. The facility has a petting zoo and other covered farm animals including
one alpaca, nine rabbits, one cow, one pig, and nine goats on exhibit to the public. The owner of the facility was
contacted and notified of the licensing requirements needed to exhibit the animals to the public. The owner
indicated that she was not aware of the licensing requirements prior to the search/inspection today. The licensing
requirements were explained to the owner during the time of inspection.
Note: The facility owner has requested an application from the Animal Care Office between the time of inspection
and the exit briefing.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES ALLOWED UNTIL A LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
The inspection exit briefing was conducted with the facility owner.
