3.127(d)
FACILITIES, OUTDOOR.
The opossum enclosure has no surrounding perimeter fence. A perimeter fence protects the animal from
unauthorized people and animals and serves as a secondary containment system. Correct by constructing an
appropriate perimeter fence or by obtaining an approved variance by submitting a written request for a variance as
determined by the Director of the Regional Office.
This is a pre-use inspection for the new, permanent location.
No regulated activity may occur until the facility has passed an inspection and is in complete compliance.
