3.125(a)

Facilities, general.
The sliding door that locks the 2 lynx in or out of their sheltered area runs on a metal track. The bottom of the metal track
is severely rusted and has sharp points. The raised platform in the smaller stall is supported by two metal supports. The
metal support on the right side is very rusted.

The indoor and outdoor housing facilities shall be structurally sound and shall be maintained in good repair to protect the
animals from injury and to contain the animals.

Repair or replace the rusted areas in the enclosure to prevent injury to the lynx contained in the enclosure.

To be corrected by March 11, 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
