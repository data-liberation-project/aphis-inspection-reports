2.31(a) CRITICAL
INSTITUTIONAL ANIMAL CARE AND USE COMMITTEE (IACUC).
The Chief Executive Officer (CEO) has not appointed an Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC),
according to a conversation with the CEO. An IACUC is necessary to review and approve the proposed activities for
the care and use of animals and to provide the proper oversight of the care and use of animals by the research
facility. The CEO shall appoint an IACUC which shall be composed of a Chairman and at least two additional
members. Of these members one shall be a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine and one shall not be affiliated in any way
with the facility.
There are currently rabbits being utilized on an antibody production protocol that has not been reviewed or
approved by the IACUC. The facility is in the process of acquiring additional covered species. An IACUC should be
in place to approve all animal care and use as well as study protocols prior to animal use.
Animal facility was inspected on March 27, records were inspected on March 28, 2017 with facility representative.
Exit interview conducted with CEO on March 28, 2017.
