2.126(b)
ACCESS AND INSPECTION OF RECORDS AND PROPERTY; SUBMISSION OF ITINERARIES.
A responsible adult was not available to accompany APHIS Officials during the inspection process at 4:00 pm on
19-Nov-19. The inspector contacted an employee upon arriving at the facility. The employee indicated the licensee
was gone and would not be returning for a couple of hours. The employee also indicated she would be leaving the
facility very soon. The inspector called the licensee and left a message.
A responsible person shall be available to accompany inspection during business hours.
