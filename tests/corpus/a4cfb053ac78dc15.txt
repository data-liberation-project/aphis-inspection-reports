3.11(c)
CLEANING, SANITIZATION, HOUSEKEEPING, AND PEST CONTROL.
Housekeeping for premises. Veterinary products are being stored in various locations in the workshop, near stacked
feed, on tops of whelping enclosures, and on a table with miscellanous items. Storing veterinary products on tops
of whelping boxes or near feed may cause contamination or injury to the dogs if the products fall or spill into
enclosures. Veterinary products must be stored in a safe and clean manner such as in a cabinet or on shelves so
the products remain relatively clean and safely away from animals, feed, tools, and clutter. Correct by storing
veterinary products properly away from animals, feed, tools, and clutter.
