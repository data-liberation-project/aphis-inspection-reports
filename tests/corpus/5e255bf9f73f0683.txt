3.125(a)
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
Section 3.125(a) Structural strength: The following enclosures must be repaired.
1) The shelter for the enclosure housing 8 Jacob four-horned sheep and some donkeys has two vertical metal
panels of siding curling up along the sides away from the building to the left side of the right doorway. Animals
rubbing up against the building may be injured on the metal edges of the siding. The metal siding must be repaired
or replaced to protect the animals from injury.

2) The enclosure housing 21 Aoudad has fencing inside the enclosure between the swan pond and enclosure fence
that has numerous broken wires that protrude out away from the fencing. Animals may be injured/punctured by the
ends of the wire. The fencing must be removed, repaired or replaced to protect the animals from injury.

Correct both items by July 5, 2016.
An exit briefing was conducted with a facility farm manager.
