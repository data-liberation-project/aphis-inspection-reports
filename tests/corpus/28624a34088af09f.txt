2.40

Attending veterinarian and adequate veterinary care (dealers and exhibitors).
The facility has not had a regularly scheduled visit from the attending veterinarian since 2019. A new veterinarian may be
employed. If a new veterinarian is acquired the written program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits needs to
be completed. It the original attending veterinarian is kept then a site visit is needed. Each exhibitor shall employ an
attending veterinarian under formal arrangements. In the case of a part-time attending veterinarian or consultant
arrangements, the formal arrangements shall include a written program of veterinary care and regularly scheduled visits
to the premises.
