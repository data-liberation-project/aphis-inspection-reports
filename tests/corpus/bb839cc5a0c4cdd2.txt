2.40(a)(2)
ATTENDING VETERINARIAN AND ADEQUATE VETERINARY CARE (DEALERS AND EXHIBITORS).
The program of veterinary care does not include the newly acquired lemurs. The licencee must have an adequate
program of veterinary care for all regulated species at the time of acquisition. Contact your attending veterinarian
and create a plan to ensure optimal animal health.
