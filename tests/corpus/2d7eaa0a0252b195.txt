3.125(a) CRITICAL
FACILITIES, GENERAL.
A female elephant (Kelly) escaped from the Circus World Museum premises and wandered into a residential
neighborhood. The elephant was housed in a barn with another female elephant (Isla) overnight. Isla removed
Kelly's leg restraints as per the elephant handler. The elephant (Kelly) then left the barn, crossed a river bordering
the premises, and walked into residential neighborhood. The elephant could have been injured or caused injury as
it wandered unsupervised.
Elephants must be housed in enclosures that secure them and prevent escape.
To be corrected from this day forward.
Note: The elephant handler has taken additional steps to secure the elephants while in the barn.
This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the elephant handler.
