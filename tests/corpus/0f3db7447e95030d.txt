2.1(a)(1) REPEAT
REQUIREMENTS AND APPLICATION.
**The facility is conducting regulated activities without a license. The facility is open to the public and has petting
zoo animals including goats, pot-bellied pigs, llamas, camel, kinkajou, Patagonian cavies, marmosets, red kangaroo
and domestic cats on exhibit. The facility was open to the public during October 2015. The facility owner had
previously been contacted on July 9, 2015 and made aware that a USDA license is required in order to exhibit the
animals to the public. The facility owner indicated at that time she would contact the Regional Office in Raleigh, NC
to apply for a license.
NO REGULATED ACTIVITIES MAY BE CONDUCTED UNTIL A USDA LICENSE IS OBTAINED.
