2.131(a)
HANDLING OF ANIMALS.
The licensee acquired a 6.5 month old female cougar without being approved to handle dangerous animals by the
Regional Office. The licensee must demonstrate adequate experience and knowledge of this species. The licensee
must document his experience and forward this information to the Animal Care Specialist at the Regional Office for
approval to handle this animal.
