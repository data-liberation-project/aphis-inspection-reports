3.131(d)

Sanitation.
Rodent droppings were observed along windowsills and counter tops in the kitchen and in the corner of one storage room.
The facility has a pest control program in place for rodents. However, the program is no longer effective. Pests can carry
disease and lead to animal health concerns. A safe and effective program for the control of mammalian pests shall be
established and maintained. The licensee needs to enhance their current pest control program to address the presence of
rodents within the facility.
To be corrected by 10/6/2022

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with a facility representative.
