3.31(c)

Sanitation.
An excessive amount of flies were present in the guinea pig and hamster housing area. The pest control program in place
is unable to handle the number of flies present. Excessive numbers of flies pose a health risk to the animals due to fecal
transmission between animals and contamination of foodstuff. An effective program for the control of insects,
ectoparasites, and avian and mammalian pests shall be established and maintained. Correct by July 29 , 2021.

This inspection and exit interview were conducted with the licensee.
