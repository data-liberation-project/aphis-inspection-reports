2.78(c)
HEALTH CERTIFICATION AND IDENTIFICATION.
***There are no health certificates maintained for the for the animal transports reviewed. Health certificates are
required for dogs and cats transported in commerce. The health certificate must be issued by a licensed
veterinarian in accordance with paragraph (a) of this section, or an exemption issued by the Secretary in
accordance with paragraph (b) of this section. All dogs and cats accepted for transport shall be accompanied by a
health certificate. Copies shall be maintained with facility transportation records. To be corrected by next
scheduled transport.
Exit briefing conducted with the owner.
